【4352】求抛物面壳 $z = \frac{1}{2} (x^2 + y^2) (0 \leqslant z \leqslant 1)$ 的质量，此壳的面密度按规律 $\rho = z$ 而变.

解 质量为

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \iint_ {S} \rho d S = \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant 2} z \sqrt {1 + x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} d x d y = \frac {1}{2} \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant 2} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) \sqrt {1 + x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} d x d y \\ = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r ^ {3} \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} d r = \pi \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r ^ {3} \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} d r = \frac {\pi}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r ^ {2} \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} d (r ^ {2}) \\ = \frac {\pi}{2} \left[ \frac {2}{5} (1 + r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \left| _ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} - \frac {2}{3} (1 + r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \right| _ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} \right] = \frac {2 \pi (1 + 6 \sqrt {3})}{1 5}. \\ \end{array}
$$
