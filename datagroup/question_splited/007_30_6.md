30.6 $\int \frac{x^3 + 1}{x(x + 3)(x + 2)(x - 1)} dx.$

$\frac{x^3 + 1}{x(x + 3)(x + 2)(x - 1)} = \frac{A}{x} + \frac{B}{x + 3} + \frac{C}{x + 2} + \frac{D}{x - 1}$ . So $x^3 + 1 = A(x + 3)(x + 2)(x - 1) + Bx(x + 2)(x - 1) + Cx(x + 3)(x - 1) + Dx(x + 3)(x + 2)$ . Let $x = 0$ . Then $1 = -6A$ , $A = -\frac{1}{6}$ . Let $x = -3$ . Then $-26 = -12B$ , $B = \frac{13}{6}$ . Let $x = -2$ . Then $-7 = 6C$ , $C = -\frac{7}{6}$ . Let $x = 1$ . Then $2 = 12D$ , $D = \frac{1}{6}$ . Thus, $\frac{x^3 + 1}{x(x + 3)(x + 2)(x - 1)} = -\frac{1}{6}\frac{1}{x} + \frac{13}{6}\frac{1}{x + 3} - \frac{7}{6}\frac{1}{x + 2} + \frac{1}{6}\frac{1}{x - 1}$ , and $\int \frac{x^3 + 1}{x(x + 3)(x + 2)(x - 1)} dx = -\frac{1}{6}\ln |x| + \frac{13}{6}\ln |x + 3| - \frac{7}{6}\ln |x + 2| + \frac{1}{6}\ln |x - 1| + C_1 = \frac{1}{6}\ln \left|\frac{(x + 3)^{13}(x - 1)}{x(x + 2)^7}\right| + C_1$ .
