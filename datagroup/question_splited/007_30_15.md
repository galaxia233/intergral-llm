30.15 $\int \frac{x^4 + 1}{x^3 + 9x} dx.$

1 Dividing $x^4 + 1$ by $x^3 + 9x$ , we obtain $x + \frac{1 - 9x^2}{x^3 + 9x}$ . So $\int \frac{x^4 + 1}{x^3 + 9x} dx = \frac{1}{2} x^2 + \int \frac{1 - 9x^2}{x^3 + 9x} dx$ . Now, $\frac{1 - 9x^2}{x^3 + 9x} = \frac{1 - 9x^2}{x(x^2 + 9)} = \frac{A}{x} + \frac{Bx + C}{x^2 + 9}$ . Then $1 - 9x^2 = A(x^2 + 9) + x(Bx + C)$ . Let $x = 0$ . Then $1 = 9A$ , $A = \frac{1}{9}$ . Equate coefficients of $x^2$ : $-9 = A + B$ , $B = -9 - A = -\frac{82}{9}$ . Equate coefficients of $x$ : $0 = C$ . Thus, $\frac{1 - 9x^2}{x(x^2 + 9)} = \frac{1}{9} \frac{1}{x} - \frac{82}{9} \frac{x}{x^2 + 9}$ , and $\int \frac{1 - 9x^2}{x(x^2 + 9)} dx = \frac{1}{9} \ln |x| - \frac{41}{9} \ln (x^2 + 9) + C_1$ . The complete answer is, therefore, $\frac{1}{2} x^2 + \frac{1}{9} \ln |x| - \frac{41}{9} \ln (x^2 + 9) + C_1$ .
