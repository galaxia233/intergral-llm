32.37 Evaluate $\int_0^1 x\ln xdx$

By integration by parts, $\int x\ln x dx = \frac{1}{4} x^2 (2\ln x - 1)$ (Take $u = \ln x, v = x dx.$ ) Then $\int_{0}^{1}x\ln x dx = \lim_{u\to 0^{+}}\frac{1}{4} x^{2}(2\ln x - 1)]_{u}^{1} = \lim_{u\to 0^{+}}[-\frac{1}{4} -\frac{1}{4} u^{2}(2\ln u - 1)] = -\frac{1}{4}.$
