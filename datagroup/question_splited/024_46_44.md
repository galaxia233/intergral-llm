46.44 Solve the equation

$$
y ^ {\prime} = - x ^ {5} + \frac {y}{x} + x ^ {3} y ^ {2} \tag {1}
$$

This is a Riccati equation (see Problem 46.43). Note that $y_{p} = x$ is a particular solution. Consider the corresponding Bernoulli equation $z' - (1/x + 2x^4)z = x^3z^2$ . By Problem 46.42, its general solution is $z = -\frac{2x}{1 + Ke^{-2x^5/5}}$ . Hence, by Problem 46.43, a general solution of (1) is $y = x - \frac{2x}{1 + Ke^{-2x^5/5}}$ .
