【3995】 $\sqrt[4]{\frac{x}{a}} + \sqrt[4]{\frac{y}{b}} = 1; x = 0, y = 0.$

提示 作变换 $x = a r \cos^8 \varphi, y = b r \sin^8 \varphi$ ，则方程化为 $r = 1 (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2})$ .

解 令 $x = \arccos^8\varphi, y = br\sin^8\varphi$ ，则方程化为

$$
r = 1 \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}).
$$

于是，曲线所围的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \iint_ {S} 8 a b r \cos^ {7} \varphi \sin^ {7} \varphi d \varphi = 4 a b \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {7} \varphi \sin^ {7} \varphi d \varphi = 4 a b \int_ {0} ^ {1} u ^ {7} (1 - u ^ {2}) ^ {3} d u \\ = 4 a b \int^ {1} \left(u ^ {7} - 3 u ^ {9} + 3 u ^ {1 1} - u ^ {1 3}\right) d u = 4 a b \left(\frac {1}{8} - \frac {3}{1 0} + \frac {1}{4} - \frac {1}{1 4}\right) = \frac {a b}{7 0}. \\ \end{array}
$$
