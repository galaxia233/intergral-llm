19.49 Find $\int \left(\frac{x^2 + 1}{x}\right)^2 dx.$

$$
I \int \left(\frac {x ^ {2} + 1}{x}\right) ^ {2} d x = \int \frac {x ^ {4} + 2 x ^ {2} + 1}{x ^ {2}} d x = \int \left(x ^ {2} + 2 + x ^ {- 2}\right) d x = \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} + 2 x - x ^ {- 1} + C = \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} + 2 x - \frac {1}{x} + C.
$$
