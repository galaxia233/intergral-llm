【4038】求球面 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}$ 包含在柱面 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1(b\leqslant a)$ 内那部分的面积.

解 因为 $\sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right)^2} = \sqrt{1 + \frac{x^2}{z^2} + \frac{y^2}{z^2}} = \sqrt{\frac{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}{z^2}} = \frac{a}{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2 - y^2}},$

又积分域 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2}\leqslant 1$ 位于第一象限部分为

$$
0 \leqslant x \leqslant a, \quad 0 \leqslant y \leqslant \frac {b}{a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}.
$$

于是，利用对称性知，所求的面积为

$$
S = 2 \cdot 4 \int_ {0} ^ {a} d x \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {b}{a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} \frac {a}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} d y = 8 a \int_ {0} ^ {a} \arcsin \frac {y}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} \left| \begin{array}{l} \frac {b}{a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} \\ \end{array} \right. d x = 8 a ^ {2} \arcsin \frac {b}{a}.
$$
