【4415】证明：若函数 $u = u(x,y,z)$ 在凸区域 $\Omega$ 内可微，且 $\left|\operatorname{grad} u\right| \leqslant M$ ，其中 $M$ 为常数，则对于 $\Omega$ 中任意两点 $A, B$ 有：
$$
| u (A) - u (B) | \leqslant M _ {\rho} (A, B),
$$
式中 $\rho(A, B)$ 为 $A$ 与 $B$ 两点之间的距离.
证 由于 $\Omega$ 为凸区域，故线段 $AB$ 整个属于 $\Omega$ . 设 $B$ 的坐标为 $(x_0, y_0, z_0)$ , $A$ 的坐标为 $(x_1, y_1, z_1)$ , 且令 $x_1 - x_0 = \Delta x, y_1 - y_0 = \Delta y, z_1 - z_0 = \Delta z$ , 并考虑一元函数 $f(t) = u(x_0 + t\Delta x, y_0 + t\Delta y, z_0 + t\Delta z) (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 1)$ , 显然 $f(0) = u(B), f(1) = u(A)$ , 且 $f(t)$ 在 $[0, 1]$ 上可微，并且
$$
\begin{array}{l} f ^ {\prime} (t) = u _ {x} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x, y _ {0} + t \Delta y, z _ {0} + t \Delta z\right) \Delta x + u _ {y} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x, y _ {0} + t \Delta y, z _ {0} + t \Delta z\right) \Delta y \\ + u _ {z} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + t \Delta x, y _ {0} + t \Delta y, z _ {0} + t \Delta z\right) \Delta z. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，由微分学中值定理知
$$
\begin{array}{l} u (A) - u (B) = f (1) - f (0) = f ^ {\prime} (\xi) \\ = u _ {x} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + \xi \Delta x, y _ {0} + \xi \Delta y, z _ {0} + \xi \Delta z\right) \Delta x + u _ {y} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + \xi \Delta x, y _ {0} + \xi \Delta y, z _ {0} + \xi \Delta z\right) \Delta y + \\ u _ {z} ^ {\prime} \left(x _ {0} + \xi \Delta x, y _ {0} + \xi \Delta y, z _ {0} + \xi \Delta z\right) \Delta z \\ = \left[ \operatorname {g r a d} u \left(x _ {0} + \xi \Delta x, y _ {0} + \xi \Delta y, z _ {0} + \xi \Delta z\right) \right] \cdot B A, \\ \end{array}
$$

由此可知
$$
\begin{array}{l} | u (A) - u (B) | = \left[ \operatorname {g r a d} u \left(x _ {0} + \xi \Delta x, y _ {0} + \xi \Delta y, z _ {0} + \xi \Delta z\right) \right] \cdot B A \\ \leqslant \left| \operatorname {g r a d} u \left(x _ {0} + \xi \Delta x, y _ {0} + \xi \Delta y, z _ {0} + \xi \Delta z\right) \right| \cdot | B A | \leqslant M _ {\rho} (A, B). \\ \end{array}
$$
