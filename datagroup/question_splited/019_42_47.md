42.48 If $z = e^{ax}\sin ay$ ，show that $\frac{\partial^2z}{\partial x^2} +\frac{\partial^2z}{\partial y^2} = 0.$

$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = ae^{ax}\sin ay,\frac{\partial^2z}{\partial x^2} = a^2 e^{ax}\sin ay,\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = ae^{ax}\cos ay,\frac{\partial^2z}{\partial y^2} = -a^2 e^{ax}\sin ay.$
