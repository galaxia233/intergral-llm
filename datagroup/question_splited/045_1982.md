【1982】 $\int \frac{\sqrt{x}}{(1 + \sqrt[3]{x})^2}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 令 $x = z^6$

解 $\frac{\sqrt{x}}{(1 + \sqrt[3]{x})^2} = x^{\frac{1}{2}}(1 + x^{\frac{1}{3}})^{-2}$ . $m = \frac{1}{2}, n = \frac{1}{3}, p = -2$ ; $p$ 为整数，这是二项微分式的第一种情形.

设 $x = z^6$ ，则 $\mathrm{d}x = 6z^{5}\mathrm{d}z,\sqrt{x} = z^{3},\sqrt[3]{x} = z^{2}$ .代人得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\sqrt {x}}{(1 + \sqrt [ 3 ]{x}) ^ {2}} d x = 6 \int \frac {z ^ {8}}{(z ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}} d z = 6 \int \left[ z ^ {4} - 2 z ^ {2} + 3 - \frac {4}{z ^ {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{(z ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}} \right] d z \\ = \frac {6}{5} z ^ {5} - 4 z ^ {3} + 1 8 z - 2 4 \arctan z + 6 \left[ \frac {z}{2 \left(z ^ {2} + 1\right)} + \frac {1}{2} \arctan z \right] ^ {*} + C \\ = \frac {6}{5} x ^ {\frac {5}{6}} - 4 x ^ {\frac {1}{2}} + 1 8 x ^ {\frac {1}{6}} + \frac {3 x ^ {\frac {1}{6}}}{1 + x ^ {\frac {1}{3}}} - 2 1 \arctan \left(x ^ {\frac {1}{6}}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用 1921 题的结果.
