34.102 Find the radius of curvature of $y = \ln x$ at $x = e$ .

$y^\prime = 1 / x,\quad y^{\prime \prime} = -1 / x^2,\quad \mathrm{and}\quad [1 + (y^\prime)^2 ]^{3 / 2} = [1 + (1 / x^2)]^{3 / 2} = (x^2 +1)^{3 / 2} / x^3.$ By Problem 34.97,

$$
\kappa = \frac {- (1 / x ^ {2})}{(x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {3 / 2} / x ^ {3}} = - \frac {x}{(x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {3 / 2}}
$$

When $x = e, \kappa = -e / (e^2 + 1)^{3/2}$ , and $\rho = (e^2 + 1)^{3/2} / e$ .
