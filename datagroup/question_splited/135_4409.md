【4409】计算：(1)gradr；(2)gradr²；(3)grad $\frac{1}{r}$ ，其中 $r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$ .
解（1） $\frac{\partial r}{\partial x} = \frac{x}{r}, \frac{\partial r}{\partial y} = \frac{y}{r}, \frac{\partial r}{\partial z} = \frac{z}{r}$ 于是， $\operatorname{grad} r = \frac{r}{r}$ ，其中 $r = x + yj + zk$
(2) $\operatorname{grad}(r^2) = 2r\operatorname{grad}r = 2r\frac{r}{r} = 2r,$   
(3) $\operatorname{grad} \frac{1}{r} = -\frac{1}{r^2} \operatorname{grad} r = -\frac{r}{r^3}$ .
