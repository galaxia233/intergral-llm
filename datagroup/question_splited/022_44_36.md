44.36 Find the area of the region enclosed by the cardioid $r = 1 + \cos \theta$

$$
\begin{array}{l} A = \iint_ {\mathcal {R}} 1 d A = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {1 + \cos \theta} r d r d \theta = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {1}{2} r ^ {2} ] _ {0} ^ {1 + \cos \theta} d \theta = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (1 + 2 \cos \theta + \cos^ {2} \theta) d \theta = \\ \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (1 + 2 \cos \theta + \frac {1 + \cos 2 \theta}{2}) d \theta = \frac {1}{2} (\frac {3}{2} \theta + 2 \sin \theta + \frac {1}{2} \sin 2 \theta) ] _ {0} ^ {2 \pi} = \frac {1}{2} (\frac {3}{2} \cdot 2 \pi) = \frac {3 \pi}{2}. \end{array}
$$
