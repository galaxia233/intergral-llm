【2493】 $y = a\mathrm{ch}\frac{x}{a} (|x|\leqslant b)$ ：（1）绕 $Ox$ 轴；（2）绕 $Oy$ 轴

解 (1) $\sqrt{1 + y'^2} = \sqrt{\sin^2\frac{x}{a} + 1} = \cosh \frac{x}{a}$ .

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P _ {x} = 2 \pi a \int_ {- b} ^ {b} \operatorname {c h} ^ {2} \frac {x}{a} \mathrm {d} x = 2 \pi a \int_ {0} ^ {b} \left(1 + \operatorname {c h} \frac {2 x}{a}\right) \mathrm {d} x = \pi a \left(2 b + a \operatorname {s h} \frac {2 b}{a}\right).
$$
