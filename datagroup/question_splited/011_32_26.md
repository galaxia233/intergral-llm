32.26 For what values of $k$ , with $k \neq 1$ and $k > 0$ , does $\int_0^1 \frac{1}{x^k} dx$ converge?

$\int_{0}^{1}\frac{1}{x^k} dx = \lim_{u\to 0^{+}}\int_{u}^{1}\frac{1}{x^k} dx = \lim_{u\to 0^{+}}\frac{1}{-k + 1} x^{-k + 1}\bigg]_{u}^{1} = \lim_{u\to 0^{+}}\frac{1}{1 - k}\left(1 - \frac{1}{u^{k - 1}}\right).$ If $k > 1$ , this limit is $+\infty$ whereas, if $k <   1$ , the limit is $1 / (1 - k)$
