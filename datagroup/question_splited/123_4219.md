【4219】计算半径为 $R$ ，密度为 $\rho_0$ 的均质球对自身的引力势，即求积分

$$
u = \frac {\rho_ {0} ^ {2}}{2} \iiint_ {x _ {1} ^ {2} + y _ {1} ^ {2} + z _ {1} ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x _ {1} \mathrm {d} y _ {1} \mathrm {d} z _ {1} \mathrm {d} x _ {2} \mathrm {d} y _ {2} \mathrm {d} z _ {2}}{r _ {1 , 2}},
$$

式中 $r_{1,2} = \sqrt{(x_1 - x_2)^2 + (y_1 - y_2)^2 + (z_1 - z_2)^2}$

解 我们有

$$
u = \frac {\rho_ {0} ^ {2}}{2} \iiint_ {x _ {1} ^ {2} + y _ {1} ^ {2} + z _ {1} ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} d x _ {1} d y _ {1} d z _ {1} \iiint_ {x _ {2} ^ {2} + y _ {2} ^ {2} + z _ {2} ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} \frac {d x _ {2} d y _ {2} d z _ {2}}{r _ {1 , 2}}.
$$

由 4155 题的结果可知 $\iint_{x_2^2 + y_2^2 + z_2^2 \leqslant R^2} \frac{\mathrm{d}x_2 \mathrm{d}y_2 \mathrm{d}z_2}{r_{1,2}} = 2\pi R^2 - \frac{2}{3}\pi r_1^2,$

其中 $r_1 = \sqrt{x_1^2 + y_1^2 + z_1^2}$ . 于是（利用球坐标），

$$
\begin{array}{l} u = \frac {\rho_ {0} ^ {2}}{2} \iiint_ {x _ {1} ^ {2} - y _ {1} ^ {2} + z _ {1} ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} \left(2 \pi R ^ {2} - \frac {2}{3} \pi r _ {1} ^ {2}\right) d x _ {1} d y _ {1} d z _ {1} = \frac {\rho_ {0}}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos \psi d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {R} \left(2 \pi R ^ {2} - \frac {2}{3} \pi r ^ {2}\right) r ^ {2} d r \\ = \frac {1 6}{1 5} \pi^ {2} \rho_ {0} ^ {2} R ^ {5}. \\ \end{array}
$$
