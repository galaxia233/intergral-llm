20.7 Evaluate $\sum_{i = 1}^{n}(3i^{2} + 4)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \left(3 i ^ {2} + 4\right) = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} 3 i ^ {2} + \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} 4 = 3 \cdot \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} i ^ {2} + 4 n = 3 \frac {n (n + 1) (2 n + 1)}{6} + 4 n \\ = \frac {n}{2} [ (n + 1) (2 n + 1) + 8 ] = \frac {n (2 n ^ {2} + 3 n + 9)}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
