46.41 Solve $xy' - 2y = 4x^3 y^{1/2}$ .

The given equation is equivalent to

$$
y ^ {\prime} - \frac {2}{x} y = 4 x ^ {2} y ^ {1 / 2} \tag {1}
$$

Use the method of Problem 46.40, with $n = \frac{1}{2}$ . We get the equation

$$
v ^ {\prime} - \frac {1}{x} v = 2 x ^ {2} \tag {2}
$$

Let $\rho = \exp \left(\int -\frac{1}{x} dx\right) = e^{-\ln x} = \frac{1}{x}$ . Hence, $v = x(\int 2x dx + C) = x(x^2 + C)$ . Then, $y = v^2 = x^2 (x^2 + C)^2$ .
