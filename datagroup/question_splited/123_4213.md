【4213】 计算 $\iint_{x_1^2 + x_2^2 + \dots + x_n^2 \leqslant 1} \frac{\mathrm{d}x_1 \mathrm{~d}x_2 \cdots \mathrm{d}x_n}{\sqrt{1 - x_1^2 - x_2^2 - \cdots - x_n^2}}.$

提示 利用 4211 题的结果，

解 $\iint_{r_1^2 + r_2^2 + \dots + r_n^2 \leqslant 1} \frac{\mathrm{d}x_1 \mathrm{~d}x_2 \cdots \mathrm{d}x_n}{\sqrt{1 - x_1^2 - x_2^2 - \cdots - x_n^2}}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \iint_ {x _ {1} ^ {2}, r _ {2} ^ {2}, \dots + r _ {n - 1} ^ {2} \leqslant 1} \mathrm {d} x _ {1} \mathrm {d} x _ {2} \dots \mathrm {d} x _ {n} \int_ {- \sqrt {1 - x _ {1} ^ {2} - \cdots - x _ {n - 1} ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - x _ {1} ^ {2} - \cdots - x _ {n - 1} ^ {2}}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x _ {n}}{\sqrt {1 - x _ {1} ^ {2} - x _ {2} ^ {2} - \cdots - x _ {n} ^ {2}}} \\ = \pi \iint_ {x _ {1} ^ {2} + x _ {2} ^ {2} + \dots + x _ {n - 1} ^ {2} \leqslant 1} \mathrm {d} x _ {1} \mathrm {d} x _ {2} \dots \mathrm {d} x _ {n - 1} = \pi \cdot \frac {\pi^ {\frac {n - 1}{2}}}{\Gamma \left(\frac {n - 1}{2} + 1\right)} = \frac {\pi^ {\frac {n + 1}{2}}}{\Gamma \left(\frac {n + 1}{2}\right)}. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用 4211 题的结果.
