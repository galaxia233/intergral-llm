【4037】求以曲面 $x^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}$ 和 $y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}$ 为界的物体的表面积.

解如图8.48所示，两曲面的交线在 $Oyz$ 平面上的投影为圆 $y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2},x = 0.$

![](images/40c948d35dcea27dabece8f0aa2ceeac2281b151c1da408ce838c85e8c7c6864.jpg)  
图8.48

于是，利用对称性知，所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = 4 \iint_ {z ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2}} \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial z}\right) ^ {2}} d y d z = 4 \cdot 4 \int_ {0} ^ {a} d z \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} \sqrt {1 + 0 ^ {2} + \left(- \frac {z}{x}\right) ^ {2}} d y \\ = 1 6 \int_ {0} ^ {a} d z \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} \sqrt {\frac {x ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}{x ^ {2}}} d y = 1 6 a \int_ {0} ^ {a} d z \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} \frac {d y}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} \\ = 1 6 a \int_ {0} ^ {a} \frac {1}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}} d z = 1 6 a ^ {2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
