19.67 Find the equation of the curve passing through (9, 18) and whose tangent line at $(x, y)$ has slope $\sqrt{x}$ .

$y^\prime = \sqrt{x}$ So, $y = \int \sqrt{x} dx = \frac{2}{3} x^{3 / 2} + C$ Substituting (9, 18) for $(x,y)$ , we obtain $18 = \frac{2}{3} (\sqrt{9})^3 +C,$ $C = 0$ Hence, $y = \frac{2}{3} x^{3 / 2}$
