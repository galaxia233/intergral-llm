40.45 Find the volume of the parallelepiped formed by the vectors $\overrightarrow{PQ}$ and $\overrightarrow{PR}$ of Problem 40.42 and the vector $\overrightarrow{PS}$ , where $S = (3,5,7)$ .

The volume of the parallelepiped determined by noncoplanar vectors $\mathbf{A}$ , $\mathbf{B}$ , $\mathbf{C}$ is $|\mathbf{A} \cdot (\mathbf{B} \times \mathbf{C})|$ . Hence, in this case, the volume is $|PS \cdot N| = (2, 4, 10) \cdot (8, 4, 4) = 16 + 16 + 40 = 72$ .
