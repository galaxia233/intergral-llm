40.37 For a unit cube, find the angle $\theta$ between a diagonal $\overrightarrow{OP}$ and the diagonal $\overrightarrow{OQ}$ of an adjacent face. (See Fig. 40-5.)

$\begin{array}{r}\overrightarrow{OP} = (1,1,1),\quad \overrightarrow{OQ} = (1,1,0). \end{array}$ $\overrightarrow{OP}\cdot \overrightarrow{OQ} = |\overrightarrow{OP} ||\overrightarrow{OQ} |\cos \theta ,\quad 2 = \sqrt{3}\sqrt{2}\cos \theta ,\quad \cos \theta = \sqrt{6} /3.$ Hence, $\theta \approx 35^{\circ}16^{\prime}$
