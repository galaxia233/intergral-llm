【2238】 计算下列积分，并作出这些积分对参数 $\alpha$ 的函数关系 $I = I(\alpha)$ 的图像：
(2) $I = \int_{0}^{\pi} \frac{\sin^{2} x}{1 + 2\alpha \cos x + \alpha^{2}} \mathrm{d}x;$

解 (2) 分两种情况：

（i）若 $|\alpha| \leqslant 1$ ，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} I = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin^ {2} x}{1 + 2 \alpha \cos x + \alpha^ {2}} d x = \frac {1}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {4 \alpha^ {2} (1 - \cos^ {2} x) d x}{(1 + \alpha^ {2}) + 2 \alpha \cos x} \\ = \frac {1}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\left[ (1 + \alpha^ {2}) ^ {2} - 4 \alpha^ {2} \cos^ {2} x \right] + \left[ 4 \alpha^ {2} - (1 + \alpha^ {2}) ^ {2} \right]}{(1 + \alpha^ {2}) + 2 \alpha \cos x} d x \\ = \frac {1}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} [ (1 + \alpha^ {2}) - 2 \alpha \cos x ] d x - \frac {(1 - \alpha^ {2}) ^ {2}}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {d x}{(1 + \alpha^ {2}) + 2 \alpha \cos x} \\ = \frac {1}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \left[ (1 + \alpha^ {2}) x - 2 \alpha \sin x \right] \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} - \frac {(1 - \alpha^ {2}) ^ {2}}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \cdot \frac {2}{1 - \alpha^ {2}} \arctan \left(\sqrt {\frac {1 + \alpha^ {2} - 2 \alpha}{1 + \alpha^ {2} + 2 \alpha}} \tan \frac {x}{2}\right) ^ {*}) \right| _ {0} ^ {\pi} \\ = \frac {(1 + a ^ {2}) \pi}{4 a ^ {2}} - \frac {(1 - a ^ {2}) \pi}{4 a ^ {2}} = \frac {\pi}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$

（ii）若 $|\alpha| > 1$ ，则同上述情况类似有

$$
I = \frac {(1 + \alpha^ {2}) \pi}{4 \alpha^ {2}} - \frac {(\alpha^ {2} - 1) ^ {2}}{4 \alpha^ {2}} \cdot \frac {2}{\alpha^ {2} - 1} \arctan \left(\sqrt {\frac {1 + \alpha^ {2} - 2 \alpha}{1 + \alpha^ {2} + 2 \alpha}} \tan \frac {x}{2}\right) \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\pi} = \frac {(1 + \alpha^ {2}) \pi}{4 \alpha^ {2}} - \frac {(1 - \alpha^ {2}) \pi}{4 \alpha^ {2}} = \frac {\pi}{2 \alpha^ {2}}.
$$

于是， $\int_0^\pi \frac{\sin^2x\mathrm{d}x}{1 + 2\alpha\cos x + \alpha^2} = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}\frac{\pi}{2}, & |\alpha |\leqslant 1,\\ \frac{\pi}{2\alpha^2}, & |\alpha | > 1. \end{array} \right.$ （图 4.10）

\*) 利用 2028 题 (1) 的结果.
