30.27 $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{x} - x}$   
Make a substitution to eliminate the radical. Let $x = z^3$ , $dx = 3z^2 dz$ . Then $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{x} - x} = \int \frac{3z^2dz}{z - z^3} = 3\int \frac{zdz}{1 - z^2} = 3\cdot (-\frac{1}{2})\ln |1 - z^2| + C = -\frac{3}{2}\ln |1 - z^2| + C = -\frac{3}{2}\ln |1 - x^{2 / 3}| + C.$
