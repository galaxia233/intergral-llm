35.32 Sketch the graph of $r^2 = \cos 2\theta$

The construction of the graph, Fig. 35-5, is indicated in the table of values. Note that some values of $\theta$ yield two values of $r$ , and some yield none at all (when $\cos 2\theta$ is negative). The graph repeats from $\theta = \pi$ to $\theta = 2\pi$ . The graph is called a lemniscate.
