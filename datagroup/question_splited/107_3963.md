【3963】 $\iint_{x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1} f(ax + by + c) \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \quad (a^2 + b^2 \neq 0).$

提示 作变换 $\frac{ax + by}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}} = u, \frac{bx - ay}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}} = v$ ，则有 $x = \frac{au + bv}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}, y = \frac{bu - av}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}$ 及 $x^2 + y^2 = u^2 + v^2$ 。故区域 $x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1$ 变为 $u^2 + v^2 \leqslant 1$ ，且有 $|I| = 1$ 。

解 作变换 $\frac{ax + by}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}} = u, \frac{bx - ay}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}} = v$ ，则有 $x = \frac{au + bv}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}, y = \frac{bu - av}{\sqrt{a^2 + b^2}}$ 及 $x^2 + y^2 = u^2 + v^2 \leqslant 1$ ，故区域 $x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1$ 变为 $u^2 + v^2 \leqslant 1$ ，且有 $|I| = 1$ 。于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {a ^ {2} + b ^ {2} = 1} f (a x + b y + c) d x d y = \iint_ {u ^ {2} + v ^ {2} = 1} f (\sqrt {a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} u + c) d u d v = \int_ {1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {\sqrt {1 - u ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - u ^ {2}}} f (\sqrt {a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} u + c) d v \\ = \int_ {1} ^ {1} f \left(\sqrt {a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} u + c\right) d u \int_ {\sqrt {1 - u ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - u ^ {2}}} d v = 2 \int_ {1} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 - u ^ {2}} f \left(\sqrt {a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} u + c\right) d u. \\ \end{array}
