19.9 Find $\int \left(\frac{1}{x^3} -\frac{1}{x^5}\right)dx.$

$$
I \int \left(\frac {1}{x ^ {3}} - \frac {1}{x ^ {5}}\right) d x = \int \left(x ^ {- 3} - x ^ {- 5}\right) d x = - \frac {1}{2} x ^ {- 2} - \left(- \frac {1}{4}\right) x ^ {- 4} + C = - \frac {1}{2 x ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{4 x ^ {4}} + C = \frac {1}{4 x ^ {4}} \left(1 - 2 x ^ {2}\right) + C.
$$
