42.44 Is there a function $f(x, y)$ such that $f_x = e^x \cos y$ and $f_y = e^x \sin y$ ?

Assume that there is such a function. Then $f_{xy}$ and $f_{yx}$ will be continuous everywhere. Hence, $f_{xy} = f_{yx}$ . Thus, $-e^{x}\sin y = e^{x}\sin y$ , or $\sin y = 0$ for all $y$ , which is false. No such function exists.
