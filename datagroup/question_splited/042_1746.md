【1746】 $\int \sin \left(2x - \frac{\pi}{6}\right)\cos \left(3x + \frac{\pi}{4}\right)\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \sin \left(2x - \frac{\pi}{6}\right)\cos \left(3x + \frac{\pi}{4}\right)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \left[\sin \left(5x + \frac{\pi}{12}\right) - \sin \left(x + \frac{5\pi}{12}\right)\right]\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= - \frac {1}{1 0} \cos \left(5 x + \frac {\pi}{1 2}\right) + \frac {1}{2} \cos \left(x + \frac {5 \pi}{1 2}\right) + C.
$$
