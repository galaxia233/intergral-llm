【2434】 $y = e^{x}$ $(0\leqslant x\leqslant x_0)$

解 所求的弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {0}} \sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x}} d x = \left(\sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x}} + \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {\sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x}} - 1}{\sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x}} + 1}\right) \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {x _ {0}} \\ = \sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x _ {0}}} - \sqrt {2} + \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {\sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x _ {0}}} - 1}{\sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x _ {0}}} + 1} - \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {\sqrt {2} - 1}{\sqrt {2} + 1} \\ = x _ {0} - \sqrt {2} + \sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x _ {0}}} - \ln \frac {1 + \sqrt {1 + e ^ {2 x _ {0}}}}{1 + \sqrt {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$
