$\begin{array}{l}\int_{-1}^{2}|x - 1|dx = \int_{-1}^{1}|x - 1|dx + \int_{1}^{2}|x - 1|dx = \int_{-1}^{1}(1 - x)dx + \int_{1}^{2}(x - 1)dx = (x - \frac {1}{2} x^2)]_{-1}^{1} + (\frac {1}{2} x^2 -x)]_1^2\\ = [(1 - \frac {1}{2}) - (-1 - \frac {1}{2})] + [(2 - 2) - (\frac {1}{2} -1)] = 2 + \frac {1}{2} = \frac {5}{2}. \end{array}$
