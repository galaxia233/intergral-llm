【2512】求曲线 $r = a(1 + \cos \varphi)$ 所围图形的质心的坐标

解计算时，将小扇形的重量集中在其质心 $(\frac{2}{3} r\cos \varphi, \frac{2}{3} r\sin \varphi)$ 处。由对称性知 $\eta = 0$ ，而

$$
\begin{array}{l} \xi = \frac {\int_ {(t)} x y \mathrm {d} x}{\int_ {(t)} y \mathrm {d} x} = \frac {\frac {2}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} r \cos \varphi \frac {1}{2} r ^ {2} \mathrm {d} \varphi}{\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {1}{2} r ^ {2} \mathrm {d} \varphi} = \frac {2}{3} \frac {\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} a ^ {3} (1 + \cos \varphi) ^ {3} \cos \varphi \mathrm {d} \varphi}{\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} a ^ {2} (1 + \cos \varphi) ^ {2} \mathrm {d} \varphi} \\ = \frac {2 a}{3} \frac {\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} (1 + 3 \cos \varphi + 3 \cos^ {2} \varphi + \cos^ {3} \varphi) \cos \varphi d \varphi}{\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} (1 + 2 \cos \varphi + \cos^ {2} \varphi) d \varphi} = \frac {5 a}{6}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，质心的极坐标为 $\varphi_0 = 0$ ， $r_0 = \frac{5a}{6}$
