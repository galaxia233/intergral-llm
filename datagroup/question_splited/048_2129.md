【2129】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x} + \sqrt[3]{x}}.$

提示 令 $\sqrt[6]{x} = t$

解设 $\sqrt[6]{x} = t$ ，则 $\sqrt{x} = t^3, \sqrt[3]{x} = t^2, \mathrm{d}x = 6t^5\mathrm{d}t.$ 代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {x} + \sqrt [ 3 ]{x}} = 6 \int \frac {t ^ {3} \mathrm {d} t}{t + 1} = 6 \int \left(t ^ {2} - t + 1 - \frac {1}{t + 1}\right) \mathrm {d} t = 2 t ^ {3} - 3 t ^ {2} + 6 t - 6 \ln (1 + t) + C \\ = 2 \sqrt {x} - 3 \sqrt [ 3 ]{x} + 6 \sqrt [ 6 ]{x} - 6 \ln (1 + \sqrt [ 6 ]{x}) + C \quad (x > 0). \\ \end{array}
$$
