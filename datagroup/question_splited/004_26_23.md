26.23 When a condenser is discharging electricity, the rate at which the voltage $V$ decreases is proportional to $V$ . If the decay constant is $K = -0.025$ , per second, how long does it take before $V$ has decreased to one-quarter of its initial value?

$\begin{array}{r}V = V_{0}e^{-0.025t}\end{array}$ . When $V$ is one-quarter of its initial value, $\frac{1}{4} V_0 = V_0e^{-0.025t},\quad \frac{1}{4} = e^{-0.025t},\quad -0.025t =$ $\ln {\frac{1}{4}} = -\ln 4 = -2\ln 2\approx -1.3862.$ Hence, $t\approx 55.448$
