【2180】设 $f^{\prime}(\ln x) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} 1, & 0 < x \leqslant 1, \\ x, & 1 < x < +\infty, \end{array} \right.$ 且 $f(0) = 0$ ，求 $f(x)$ .

提示 令 $\ln x = t$

解设 $t = \ln x$ ，则 $f^{\prime}(t) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}1, & -\infty <  t\leqslant 0,\\ e^{t}, & 0 <   t <   + \infty . \end{array} \right.$ 于是，

$$
f (x) = \int f ^ {\prime} (x) d x = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} x + C _ {1}, & - \infty <   x \leqslant 0, \\ e ^ {x} + C _ {2}, & 0 <   x <   + \infty , \end{array} \right.
$$

其中 $C_1, C_2$ 是两个常数。由假定 $f(0) = 0$ , 得 $C_1 = 0$ . 再由 $f(x)$ 在 $x = 0$ 的连续性知， $f(0) = \lim_{x \to 0^+} f(x)$ , 由此得 $C_2 = -1$ . 于是，

$$
f (x) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} x, & - \infty <   x \leqslant 0, \\ \mathrm {e} ^ {x} - 1, & 0 <   x <   + \infty . \end{array} \right.
$$
