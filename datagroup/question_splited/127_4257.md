【4257】 $\oint_{\Omega mA nO}\arctan \frac{y}{x}\mathrm{d}y - \mathrm{d}x$ ，其中 $OmA$ 为抛物线段 $y = x^{2},OnA$ 为直线段 $y = x$

解 如图 8.62 所示，我们有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \oint_ {(m A n)} \arctan \frac {y}{x} d y - d x = \int_ {(m A} \arctan \frac {y}{x} d y - d x + \int_ {A n O} \arctan \frac {y}{x} d y - d x \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {1} 2 x \arctan x d x - \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x + \int_ {1} ^ {\infty} (\arctan 1 - 1) d x \\ = x ^ {2} \arctan x \left| _ {0} ^ {1} - \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {x ^ {2}}{1 + x ^ {2}} d x - 1 + \left(\frac {\pi}{4} - 1\right) x \right| _ {1} ^ {0} \\ = \frac {\pi}{4} - (x - \arctan x) \left| _ {0} ^ {1} - 1 - \left(\frac {\pi}{4} - 1\right) = \frac {\pi}{4} - 1 \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

注 原题为 $\int_{(m, m)} \mathrm{d}y \arctan \frac{y}{x} - \mathrm{d}x$ ，若把它理解为

$$
\int_ {(m A n O)} \mathrm {d} \left(y \arctan \frac {y}{x}\right) - \mathrm {d} x,
$$

则其值为零，与原答案不符.
