【1921】试导出用于计算积分 $I_{n} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(ax^{2} + bx + c)^{n}} (a\neq 0)$ 的递推公式。利用这个公式计算

$$
I _ {3} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x ^ {2} + x + 1) ^ {3}}.
$$

解题思路 首先，注意

$$
4 a (a x ^ {2} + b x + c) = (2 a x + b) ^ {2} + (4 a c - b ^ {2}) = t ^ {2} + \Delta ,
$$

其中 $t = 2ax + b, \Delta = 4ac - b^2$. 这样，原积分就变形为 $2^{2n-1}a^{n-1}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{(t^2 + \Delta)^n}$.

其次，当 $\Delta \neq 0$ 时，对积分 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{(t^2 + \Delta)^n}$ 使用分部积分法，并注意 $t^2 = t^2 +\Delta -\Delta$ ，经运算即可得递推公式

$$
I _ {n} = \frac {1}{(n - 1) \Delta} \cdot \frac {2 a x + b}{\left(a x ^ {2} + b x + c\right) ^ {n - 1}} + \frac {2 n - 3}{n - 1} \cdot \frac {2 a}{\Delta} I _ {n - 1}.
$$

当 $\Delta = 0$ 时，易获解。

解 由于 $4a(ax^{2} + bx + c) = (2ax + b)^{2} + (4ac - b^{2}) = t^{2} + \Delta$ ，其中 $t = 2ax + b, \Delta = 4ac - b^2.$ 于是，

$$
I _ {n} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\left(a x ^ {2} + b x + c\right) ^ {n}} = \int \frac {(4 a) ^ {n} \mathrm {d} x}{\left[ (2 a x + b) ^ {2} + \Delta \right] ^ {n}} = 2 ^ {2 n - 1} a ^ {n - 1} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n}}.
$$

当 $\Delta \neq 0$ 时，对于积分 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{(t^2 + \Delta)^n}$ 施用分部积分法，即有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {d t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n}} = \frac {t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n}} + 2 n \int \frac {t ^ {2} d t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n + 1}} = \frac {t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n}} + 2 n \int \frac {(t ^ {2} + \Delta) - \Delta}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n + 1}} d t \\ = \frac {t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n}} + 2 n \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n}} - 2 n \Delta \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n + 1}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

若令 $\bar{I}_n = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{(t^2 + \Delta)^n}$ ，则得 $\bar{I}_n = \frac{t}{(t^2 + \Delta)^n} + 2n\bar{I}_n - 2n\Delta\bar{I}_{n+1},$

或 $\overline{I}_{n+1} = \frac{1}{2n\Delta} \cdot \frac{t}{(t^2 + \Delta)^n} + \frac{2n - 1}{2n} \cdot \frac {1}{\Delta} \overline{I}_n$ ，从而，

$$
\bar {I} _ {n} = \frac {1}{2 (n - 1) \Delta} \cdot \frac {t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n - 1}} + \frac {2 n - 3}{2 n - 2} \cdot \frac {1}{\Delta} \bar {I} _ {n - 1}.
$$

代入 $I_{n}$ ，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {n} = 2 ^ {2 n - 1} a ^ {n - 1} \left\{\frac {1}{2 (n - 1) \Delta} \cdot \frac {t}{(t ^ {2} + \Delta) ^ {n - 1}} + \frac {2 n - 3}{2 n - 2} \cdot \frac {1}{\Delta} I _ {n - 1} \right\} \\ = 2 ^ {2 n - 1} a ^ {n - 1} \left\{\frac {1}{2 (n - 1) \Delta} \cdot \frac {2 a x + b}{(4 a) ^ {n - 1} (a x ^ {2} + b x + c) ^ {n - 1}} + \frac {2 n - 3}{2 n - 2} \cdot \frac {1}{\Delta} \right. \\ \cdot \frac {2 a}{(4 a) ^ {n - 1}} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(a x ^ {2} + b x + c) ^ {n - 1}} \Bigg \} = \frac {1}{(n - 1) \Delta} \cdot \frac {2 a x + b}{(a x ^ {2} + b x + c) ^ {n - 1}} + \frac {2 n - 3}{n - 1} \cdot \frac {2 a}{\Delta} I _ {n - 1}, \\ \end{array}
$$

最后得递推公式

$$
I _ {n} = \frac {1}{(n - 1) \Delta} \cdot \frac {2 a x + b}{\left(a x ^ {2} + b x + c\right) ^ {n - 1}} + \frac {2 n - 3}{n - 1} \cdot \frac {2 a}{\Delta} I _ {n - 1}.
$$

当 $\Delta = 0$ 时，则有

$$
I _ {n} = \int \frac {(4 a) ^ {n} d x}{(2 a x + b) ^ {2 n}} = 2 ^ {2 n - 1} a ^ {n - 1} \int \frac {d (2 a x + b)}{(2 a x + b) ^ {2 n}} = \frac {1}{a ^ {n} (1 - 2 n)} \left(x + \frac {b}{2 a}\right) ^ {1 - 2 n} + C.
$$

对于 $I_{3}, \Delta \neq 0$ ，两次运用上述递推公式，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {3} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\left(x ^ {2} + x + 1\right) ^ {3}} = \frac {2 x + 1}{6 \left(x ^ {2} + x + 1\right) ^ {2}} + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\left(x ^ {2} + x + 1\right) ^ {2}} = \frac {2 x + 1}{6 \left(x ^ {2} + x + 1\right) ^ {2}} + \frac {2 x + 1}{3 \left(x ^ {2} + x + 1\right)} + \frac {2}{3} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {2} + x + 1} \\ = \frac {2 x + 1}{6 (x ^ {2} + x + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {2 x + 1}{3 (x ^ {2} + x + 1)} + \frac {4}{3 \sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 x + 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
