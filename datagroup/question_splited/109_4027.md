【4027】 $z^2 = xy, x + y = a, x + y = b (0 < a < b)$

解 由于 $z = \pm \sqrt{xy}$ ，又所界立体在 $Oxy$ 平面上的投影域 $\Omega$ 由直线 $x + y = a, x + y = b, x = 0$ 及 $y = 0$ 围成。利用对称性知，曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 2 \iint_ {\Omega} \sqrt {x y} d x d y = 2 \left(\int_ {0} ^ {a} d x \int_ {a - x} ^ {b - r} \sqrt {x y} d y + \int_ {a} ^ {b} d x \int_ {0} ^ {b - x} \sqrt {x y} d y\right) \\ = \frac {4}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left[ \sqrt {x (b - x) ^ {3}} - \sqrt {x (a - x) ^ {3}} \right] d x + \frac {4}{3} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \sqrt {x (b - x) ^ {3}} d x \\ = \frac {4}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {b} (b - x) \sqrt {x (b - x)} d x - \frac {4}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {a} (a - x) \sqrt {a (a - x)} d x. \\ \end{array}
$$

令 $x = b\sin^2 t$ ，可得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {b} (b - x) \sqrt {x (b - x)} d x = 2 b ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {4} t \sin^ {2} t d t \\ = 2 b ^ {3} \left(\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {4} t d t - \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {6} t d t\right) = 2 b ^ {3} \left(\frac {3 \cdot 1}{4 \cdot 2} - \frac {5 \cdot 3 \cdot 1}{6 \cdot 4 \cdot 2}\right) \frac {\pi}{2} = \frac {1}{1 6} \pi b ^ {3}; \\ \end{array}
$$

同理，有 $\int_0^a (a - x)\sqrt{x(a - x)}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{16}\pi a^3.$

于是，所求的体积为 $V = \frac{4}{3}\left(\frac{\pi b^3}{16} -\frac{\pi a^3}{16}\right) = \frac{\pi}{12} (b^3 -a^3).$
