41.10 Describe and sketch the surface obtained by rotating the curve $z = |y|$ (in the $yz$ -plane) about the $z$ -axis.

By Problem 41.5, an equation is $z = \left| \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \right|$ , which is equivalent to $z^2 = x^2 + y^2$ for $z \geq 0$ . This is a right circular cone (with a $90^\circ$ apex angle); see Fig. 41-7.
