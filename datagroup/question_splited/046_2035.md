【2035】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^4 x + \cos^4 x}$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^4 x + \cos^4 x} = \int \frac{2\mathrm{d}x}{2 - \sin^2 2x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\tan 2x)}{2\sec^2 2x - \tan^2 2x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\tan 2x)}{2 + \tan^2 2x} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \arctan \left( \frac{\tan 2x}{\sqrt{2}} \right) + C.$
