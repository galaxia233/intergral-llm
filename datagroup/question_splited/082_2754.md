【2754】 $f_{n}(x) = n\left(\sqrt{x + \frac{1}{n}} -\sqrt{x}\right);\quad 0 <   x <   + \infty .$

解当 $0 <   x <   + \infty$ 时，

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} f _ {n} (x) = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {n \left[\left(x + \frac {1}{n}\right) - x \right]}{\sqrt {x + \frac {1}{n}} + \sqrt {x}} = \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {x}} = f (x).
$$

若取 $x = \frac{1}{n}$ ，则有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| f _ {n} \left(\frac {1}{n}\right) - f \left(\frac {1}{n}\right) \right| = \left| n \left(\sqrt {\frac {2}{n}} - \sqrt {\frac {1}{n}}\right) - \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {\frac {1}{n}}} \right| \\ = \sqrt {n} \left| \sqrt {2} - 1 - \frac {1}{2} \right| = \sqrt {n} \frac {\sqrt {2} - 1}{2 (\sqrt {2} + 1)} = \frac {\sqrt {n}}{2 (\sqrt {2} + 1) ^ {2}} > \frac {1}{1 8} \sqrt {n}. \\ \end{array}
$$

当 $n$ 充分大时，它就可以大于指定的 $\varepsilon_0 > 0$ 因此， $f_n(x)$ 在 $(0, +\infty)$ 上收敛而不一致收敛。
