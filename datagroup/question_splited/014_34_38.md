34.40 Find the arc length of $x = u, y = u^{3/2}, 0 \leq u \leq \frac{4}{3}$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {I} d x / d u = 1, \quad d y / d u = \frac {3}{2} u ^ {1 / 2}. \quad \text {H e n c e}, \quad s = \int_ {0} ^ {4 / 3} \sqrt {1 + \frac {9}{4} u} d u = \frac {4}{9} \cdot \frac {2}{3} (1 + \frac {9}{4} u) ^ {3 / 2} ] _ {0} ^ {4 / 3} = \frac {8}{2 7} (8 - 1) = \frac {5 6}{2 7}. \end{array}
