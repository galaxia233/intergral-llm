【3968】 $\iint_{x^4 + y^4 \leq 1} (x^2 + y^2) \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y.$

提示 作变换 $x = r\cos \varphi, y = r\sin \varphi$ ，利用对称性及1712题的结果。

解 作变换 $x = r\cos \varphi, y = r\sin \varphi$ ，并利用对称性，则有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {x ^ {4} - y ^ {4} \leqslant 1} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) d x d y = 8 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\left(\frac {1}{\cos^ {4} \varphi + \sin^ {4} \varphi}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{4}}} r ^ {3} d r = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \frac {d \varphi}{\cos^ {4} \varphi + \sin^ {4} \varphi} = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \frac {\sec^ {2} \varphi d \tan \varphi}{1 + \tan^ {4} \varphi} \\ = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {1 + t ^ {2}}{1 + t ^ {4}} d t = \frac {2}{\sqrt {2}} \arctan \left. \frac {t ^ {2} - 1}{t \sqrt {2}} \right| _ {0} ^ {1 *}) = \frac {\pi}{\sqrt {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

*）利用1712题的结果.
