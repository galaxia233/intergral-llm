【2845】 $f(x) = \sin (u\arcsin x)$

解 $\arcsin x = \int_{0}^{x}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{1 - t^2}} = \int_{0}^{x}\left(1 + \frac{1}{2} t^2 +\frac{1\cdot 3}{2\cdot 4} t^4 +\dots\right)\mathrm{d}t = x + \frac{1}{2\cdot 3} x^3 +\frac{1\cdot 3}{2\cdot 4\cdot 5} x^5 +\dots \quad (|x| <   1),$

$$
\begin{array}{l} f (x) = u \arcsin x - \frac {u ^ {3}}{3 !} (\arcsin x) ^ {3} + \frac {u ^ {5}}{5 !} (\arcsin x) ^ {5} - \dots \\ = u x + \frac {u \left(1 ^ {2} - u ^ {2}\right)}{3 !} x ^ {3} + \frac {u \left(1 ^ {2} - u ^ {2}\right) \left(3 ^ {2} - u ^ {2}\right)}{5 !} x ^ {5} + \dots , \\ \end{array}
$$

收敛区间为 $(-1, 1)$ .
