【1955】 $\int \frac{x^3}{(1 + x)\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{x^3}{(1 + x)\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{(x^3 + 1) - 1}{(1 + x)\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int \frac {x ^ {2} - x + 1}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} d x - \int \frac {d x}{(1 + x) \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} \\ = - \int \frac {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} d x + \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {(2 x - 2) d x}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} + 3 \int \frac {d x}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} - \int \frac {d x}{(x + 1) \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} \\ = - \int \sqrt {2 - (x - 1) ^ {2}} d (x - 1) - \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {d (1 + 2 x - x ^ {2})}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} + 3 \int \frac {d (x - 1)}{\sqrt {2 - (x - 1) ^ {2}}} - I _ {1} \\ = \frac {1 - x}{2} \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} - \arcsin \left(\frac {x - 1}{\sqrt {2}}\right) - \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} + 3 \arcsin \left(\frac {x - 1}{\sqrt {2}}\right) - I _ {1} \\ = - \frac {x + 1}{2} \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} + 2 \arcsin \left(\frac {x - 1}{\sqrt {2}}\right) - I _ {1}. \\ \end{array}
$$

对于 $I_{1}$ ，设 $x + 1 = \frac{1}{t}$ ，可得

$$
I _ {1} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x + 1) \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \arcsin \left(\frac {x \sqrt {2}}{x + 1}\right) + C _ {1}.
$$

于是，最后得到

$$
\int \frac {x ^ {3}}{(x + 1) \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} d x = - \frac {1 + x}{2} \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} - 2 \arcsin \left(\frac {1 - x}{\sqrt {2}}\right) - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \arcsin \left(\frac {x \sqrt {2}}{1 + x}\right) + C.
$$
