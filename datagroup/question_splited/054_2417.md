【2417】 $x = \frac{c^2}{a}\cos^3 t, y = \frac{c^2}{b}\sin^3 t (c^2 = a^2 - b^2)$ （椭圆的渐屈线）.

解如图 4.28 所示，所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {c ^ {2}}{b} \sin^ {3} t \frac {3 c ^ {2}}{a} \cos^ {2} t \sin t d t = \frac {1 2 c ^ {4}}{a b} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {4} t (1 - \sin^ {2} t) d t \\ = \frac {3 \pi c ^ {4}}{8 a b}. \\ \end{array}
$$
