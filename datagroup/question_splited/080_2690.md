【2690】 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{\sin n\cdot\sin n^{2}}{n}.$

解 记 $a_{n} = \frac{1}{n}, b_{n} = \sin n \cdot \sin n^{2}$ . 显然 $\{a_{n}\}$ 单调下降趋于零，且

$$
\left| \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} b _ {n} \right| = \left| \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{2} [ \cos n (n - 1) - \cos n (n + 1) ] \right| = \left| \frac {1}{2} [ \cos 0 - \cos N (N + 1) ] \right| \leqslant 1,
$$

有界 $(N = 1,2,\dots)$ ，故由狄利克雷判别法知级数收敛.
