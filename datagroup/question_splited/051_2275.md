【2275】 $\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(2 + \cos x)(3 + \cos x)}.$

解 $\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(2 + \cos x)(3 + \cos x)} = \int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{2 + \cos x} -\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{3 + \cos x} = \int_0^{\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{2 + \cos x} +\int_0^{\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{2 - \cos x} -\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{3 + \cos x}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{4 - \cos^ {2} x} - 6 \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{9 - \cos^ {2} x} = 8 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{4 \sin^ {2} x + 3 \cos^ {2} x} - 1 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{9 \sin^ {2} x + 8 \cos^ {2} x} \\ = 8 \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {3}} \arctan \frac {2 \tan x}{\sqrt {3}} \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} - 1 2 \frac {1}{3 \sqrt {8}} \arctan \frac {3 \tan x}{\sqrt {8}} \right| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \pi \left(\frac {2}{\sqrt {3}} - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
