45.13 If $\mathbf{G}'(t)$ is perpendicular to $\mathbf{G}(t)$ for all $t$ , show that $|\mathbf{G}(t)|$ is constant, that is, the point $\mathbf{G}(t)$ lies on the surface of a sphere with center at the origin.

Since $\mathbf{G}'(t)$ is perpendicular to $\mathbf{G}(t)$ , $\mathbf{G}'(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}(t) = 0$ . Then $\frac{d}{dt} \left[ |\mathbf{G}(t)|^2 \right] = \frac{d}{dt} \left[ \mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}(t) \right] = \mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}'(t) + \mathbf{G}'(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}(t) = 0 + 0 = 0$ . Therefore, $|\mathbf{G}(t)|^2$ is constant, and hence $|\mathbf{G}(t)|$ is constant.
