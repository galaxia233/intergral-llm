【4133】求以下列曲面为界的均匀物体的质心坐标：
$\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} = \frac{z^2}{c^2},\quad z = c.$

提示 作变量代换 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = brsin\varphi, z = z$

解 若令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi, z = z$ ，则质量为

$$
M = a b \int_ {0} ^ {c} d z \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {z}{c}} r d r = \frac {\pi a b c}{3}.
$$

设质心坐标为 $x_0, y_0, z_0$ ，由对称性知 $x_0 = y_0 = 0$ ，而

$$
z _ {0} = \frac {a b}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {c} z d z \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {z}{c}} r d r = \frac {3}{\pi a b c} \cdot \frac {\pi a b c ^ {2}}{4} = \frac {3 c}{4}.
$$

于是，质心为点 $\left(0,0,\frac{3c}{4}\right)$
