【1836】+ $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{a + bx^2}\quad (ab\neq 0).$

解 当 $ab > 0$ 时，

$$
\int \frac {d x}{a + b x ^ {2}} = \operatorname {s g n} a \frac {1}{\sqrt {| b |}} \int \frac {d (\sqrt {| b |} x)}{(\sqrt {| a |}) ^ {2} + (\sqrt {| b |} x) ^ {2}} = \operatorname {s g n} a \frac {1}{\sqrt {a b}} \arctan \left(x \sqrt {\frac {b}{a}}\right) + C;
$$

当 $ab < 0$ 时， $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{a + bx^2} = \operatorname{sgn}a\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{|a| - |b|x^2} = \operatorname{sgn}a\frac{1}{\sqrt{|b|}}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\sqrt{|b|}x)}{(\sqrt{|a|})^2 - (\sqrt{|b|}x)^2}$

$$
= \frac {\operatorname {s g n} a}{2 \sqrt {- a b}} \ln \left| \frac {\sqrt {| a |} + x \sqrt {| b |}}{\sqrt {| a |} - x \sqrt {| b |}} \right| + C.
$$
