【4139】 $\left(\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2}\right)^2 = \frac{xyz}{abc} (x > 0,y > 0,z > 0).$

解 作代换 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos \psi, y = b r \sin \varphi \cos \psi, z = c r \sin \psi$ ，则物体的质量为

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {\cos \varphi \sin \varphi \cos^ {2} \psi \sin \psi} a b c r ^ {2} \cos \psi d r = \frac {1}{3} a b c \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {3} \varphi \sin^ {3} \varphi d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {7} \psi \sin^ {3} \psi d \psi \\ = \frac {1}{3} a b c \cdot \frac {1}{2} B (2, 2) \cdot \frac {1}{2} B (4, 2) = \frac {1}{1 2} a b c \cdot \frac {\Gamma (2) \Gamma (2)}{\Gamma (4)} \cdot \frac {\Gamma (4) \Gamma (2)}{\Gamma (6)} = \frac {a b c}{1 4 4 0}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} a ^ {2} b c \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {\cos \varphi \sin \varphi \cos^ {2} \psi \sin \psi} r ^ {3} \cos^ {2} \psi \cos \varphi d r = \frac {a ^ {2} b c}{4 M} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {5} \varphi \sin^ {4} \varphi d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {1 0} \psi \sin^ {1} \psi d \psi \\ = \frac {a ^ {2} b c}{4 M} \cdot \frac {1}{4} B \left(3, \frac {5}{2}\right) B \left(\frac {1 1}{2}, \frac {5}{2}\right) = \frac {a ^ {2} b c}{4 M} \cdot \frac {1}{4} \cdot \frac {\Gamma (3) \Gamma \left(\frac {5}{2}\right)}{\Gamma \left(\frac {1 1}{2}\right)} \cdot \frac {\Gamma \left(\frac {1 1}{2}\right) \Gamma \left(\frac {5}{2}\right)}{\Gamma (8)} \\ = \frac {1 8 a ^ {2} b c \pi}{1 6 \cdot 1 6 \cdot 7 !} \cdot \frac {1 4 4 0}{a b c} = \frac {9 \pi}{4 4 8} a. \\ \end{array}
$$

由对称性知，质心的坐标为 $x_0 = \frac{9\pi}{448} a,\quad y_0 = \frac{9\pi}{448} b,\quad z_0 = \frac{9\pi}{448} c.$
