【3087】 证明：若级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \alpha_n$ 绝对收敛，则乘积 $\prod_{n=1}^{\infty} \tan \left( \frac{\pi}{4} + \alpha_n \right) (|\alpha_n| < \frac{\pi}{4})$ 收敛.

证 由于级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \alpha_{n}$ 收敛，故 $\lim_{n \to \infty} \alpha_{n} = 0$ . 此时有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \tan \left(\frac {\pi}{4} + \alpha_ {n}\right) = \frac {1 + \tan \alpha_ {n}}{1 - \tan \alpha_ {n}} = (1 + \tan \alpha_ {n}) (1 + \tan \alpha_ {n} + \tan^ {2} \alpha_ {n} + \dots) \\ = 1 + 2 \tan \alpha_ {n} + 2 \tan^ {2} \alpha_ {n} + \dots = 1 + 2 \alpha_ {n} + o (\alpha_ {n}). \\ \end{array}
$$

由于级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left[2\alpha_{n}+o\left(\alpha_{n}\right)\right]$ 收敛，而且级数

$$
\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left[ 2 \alpha_ {n} + o (\alpha_ {n}) \right] ^ {2} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} 4 \alpha_ {n} ^ {2} + 4 \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left[ \alpha_ {n} \cdot o (\alpha_ {n}) \right] + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} o ^ {2} (\alpha_ {n})
$$

也收敛，故无穷乘积 $\prod_{n=1}^{\infty} \tan \left( \frac{\pi}{4} + \alpha_n \right) \left( |\alpha_n| < \frac{\pi}{2} \right)$ 收敛.

$\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \alpha_{n}$ 绝对收敛，故 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \alpha_{n}^{2} \leqslant \sum_{s=1}^{\infty}\left(|\alpha_{i_{s}}| \cdot |\alpha_{k_{s}}|\right)$ ，因而， $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \alpha_{n}^{2}$ 也收敛。又当 $n$ 充分大时，有

$$
\left| \alpha_ {n} \cdot o \left(\alpha_ {n}\right) \right| \leqslant \left| \alpha_ {n} \right|, \quad \left| o ^ {2} \left(\alpha_ {n}\right) \right| \leqslant \left| \alpha_ {n} \right|,
$$

故 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}\left[\alpha_n\cdot o(\alpha_n)\right]$ 及 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}o^{2}(\alpha_{n})$ 均收敛.
