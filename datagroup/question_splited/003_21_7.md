21.7 The region under the curve $\sqrt{x} +\sqrt{y} = 1$ and in the first quadrant.

See Fig. 21-7. The region has its base on the $x$ -axis. The area is given by $\int_0^1 (1 - \sqrt{x})^2 dx = \int_0^1 (1 - 2x^{1/2} + x) dx = (x - \frac{4}{3} x^{3/2} + \frac{1}{2} x^2) \bigg]_0^1 = 1 - \frac{4}{3} + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{6}$ .
