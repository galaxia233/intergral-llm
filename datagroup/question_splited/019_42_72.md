42.73 Let $z = u^3 v^5$ , where $u = x + y$ and $v = x - y$ . Find $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$ (a) by the chain rule, (b) by substitution and explicit computation.

(a) $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial u}\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial v}\frac{\partial v}{\partial y} = 3u^2v^5 - 5u^3v^4 = u^2v^4(3v - 5u) = u^2v^4(-2x - 8y)$

$$
= - 2 (x + 4 y) (x + y) ^ {2} (x - y) ^ {4}.
$$
