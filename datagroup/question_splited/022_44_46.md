44.45 If the depth of water provided by a water sprinkler in a given unit of time is $2^{-r}$ feet at a distance of $r$ feet from the sprinkler, find the total volume of water within a distance of $a$ feet from the sprinkler after one unit of time.

$V = \int_{0}^{2\pi}d\theta \int_{0}^{a}e^{-r\ln 2}rdr = 2\pi \left[\frac{e^{-r\ln 2}}{(\ln 2)^{2}} (-r\ln 2 - 1)\right]_{0}^{a} = \frac{2\pi}{(\ln 2)^{2}} [1 - e^{-a\ln 2}(1 + a\ln 2)]$

$$
= \frac {2 \pi}{(\ln 2) ^ {2}} \left(1 - \frac {1 + a \ln 2}{2 ^ {a}}\right).
$$
