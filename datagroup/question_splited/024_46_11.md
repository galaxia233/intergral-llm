46.11 Solve $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{1 + y^2}{1 + x^2}$ .

$$
I \int \frac {d y}{1 + y ^ {2}} = \int \frac {d x}{1 + x ^ {2}}, \quad \tan^ {- 1} y = \tan^ {- 1} x + C _ {1}, \quad y = \tan (\tan^ {- 1} x + C _ {1}) = \frac {\tan (\tan^ {- 1} x) + \tan C _ {1}}{1 - \tan (\tan^ {- 1} x) \tan C _ {1}},
$$

$$
y = \frac {x + C}{1 - C x}, \quad \text {w h e r e} \quad C = \tan C _ {1}.
$$
