【1907】 $\int \frac{x^4 - 3}{x(x^8 + 3x^4 + 2)}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{x^4 - 3}{x(x^8 + 3x^4 + 2)}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\left(1 - \frac{3}{x^4}\right)\mathrm{d}x}{x^5\left(1 + \frac{3}{x^4} + \frac{2}{x^8}\right)} = \int \frac{-\frac{1}{4}\left(1 - \frac{3}{x^4}\right)\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{x^4}\right)}{\frac{2}{x^8} + \frac{3}{x^4} + 1}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {1}{4} \int \left(\frac {5}{\frac {2}{x ^ {4}} + 1} - \frac {4}{\frac {1}{x ^ {4}} + 1}\right) d \left(\frac {1}{x ^ {4}}\right) = - \frac {5}{8} \ln \left(\frac {2}{x ^ {4}} + 1\right) + \ln \left(\frac {1}{x ^ {4}} + 1\right) + C \\ = \frac {5}{8} \ln \frac {x ^ {4}}{x ^ {4} + 2} - \ln \frac {x ^ {4}}{x ^ {4} + 1} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
