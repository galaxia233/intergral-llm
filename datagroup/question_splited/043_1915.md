【1915】 $\int \frac{1 - x^7}{x(1 + x^7)}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{1 - x^7}{x(1 + x^7)}\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(\frac{1}{x} -\frac{2x^6}{1 + x^7}\right)\mathrm{d}x = \ln |x| - \frac{2}{7}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(1 + x^7)}{1 + x^7} = \ln |x| - \frac{2}{7}\ln |1 + x^7 | + C$

$$
= \frac {1}{7} \ln \frac {\left| x \right| ^ {7}}{\left(1 + x ^ {7}\right) ^ {2}} + C.
$$
