27.74 If $y = \frac{1}{ab} \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{b}{a} \tan x\right)$ , find $y'$ .

$$
y ^ {\prime} = \frac {1}{a b} \cdot \frac {1}{1 + \left[ (b / a) \tan x \right] ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {b}{a} \sec^ {2} x = \frac {\sec^ {2} x}{a ^ {2} + b ^ {2} \tan^ {2} x} = \frac {1}{a ^ {2} \cos^ {2} x + b ^ {2} \sin^ {2} x}.
$$
