【2130】 $\int x^{2}\sqrt{\frac{x}{1 - x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 令 $\sqrt{\frac{1 - x}{x}} = t$ ，并利用 1921 题的递推公式，

解设 $\sqrt{\frac{1 - x}{x}} = t$ ，则 $x = \frac{1}{1 + t^2}$ $\mathrm{dx} = -\frac{2t}{(1 + t^2)^2}\mathrm{dt}$ 代人得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int x ^ {2} \sqrt {\frac {x}{1 - x}} d x = - 2 \int \frac {d t}{(t ^ {2} + 1) ^ {4}} \\ = - 2 \left[ \frac {t}{6 (t ^ {2} + 1) ^ {3}} + \frac {5 t}{2 4 (t ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {5 t}{1 6 (t ^ {2} + 1)} + \frac {5}{1 6} \arctan t \right] ^ {*}) + C _ {1} \\ = - \frac {1}{2 4} (8 x ^ {2} + 1 0 x + 1 5) \sqrt {x (1 - x)} - \frac {5}{8} \arctan \sqrt {\frac {1 - x}{x}} + C _ {1} \\ = - \frac {1}{2 4} (8 x ^ {2} + 1 0 x + 1 5) \sqrt {x (1 - x)} + \frac {5}{8} \arcsin \sqrt {x} + C \quad (0 <   x <   1). \\ \end{array}
$$

\*）利用 1921 题的递推公式.
