【4343】 $\iint_{S} (x + y + z) \, \mathrm{d}S$ ，式中 $S$ 为曲面 $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = a^2, z \geqslant 0$ .

解 由于

$$
\sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2}} = \sqrt {1 + \frac {x ^ {2}}{z ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{z ^ {2}}} = \frac {a}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}},
$$

故有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {S} (x + y + z) \mathrm {d} S = \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {- \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} \frac {a}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} (x + y + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}) \mathrm {d} y \\ = \int_ {- a} ^ {a} (\pi a x + 2 a \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}) d x = 4 a \int_ {0} ^ {a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = 4 a \frac {\pi a ^ {2}}{4} = \pi a ^ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$
