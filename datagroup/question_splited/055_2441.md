【2441】 $x = \frac{c^2}{a}\cos^3 t,\quad y = \frac{c^2}{b}\sin^3 t,\quad c^2 = a^2 -b^2$ （椭圆的渐屈线）.

解 $\sqrt{x_{t}^{\prime 2} + y_{t}^{\prime 2}} = \frac{3c^{2}}{ab}\sin t\cos t\sqrt{b^{2}\cos^{2}t + a^{2}\sin^{2}t}$ 所求的弧长为

$$
s = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {3 c ^ {2}}{a b} \sin t \cos t \sqrt {b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t + a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} t} d t = \frac {1 2 c ^ {2}}{3 a b \left(a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}\right)} \left\{b ^ {2} + \left(a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}\right) \sin^ {2} t \right\} ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {4 \left(a ^ {3} - b ^ {3}\right)}{a b}.
$$
