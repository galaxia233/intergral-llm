【2606】证明：若 $a_{n} > 0 (n = 1, 2, \dots)$ ，且 $\lim_{n \to \infty} n\left(\frac{a_{n}}{a_{n+1}} - 1\right) = p$ ，则 $a_{n} = o\left(\frac{1}{n^{p-\epsilon}}\right) (\epsilon > 0)$ .

证下面记 $\alpha_{n},\alpha_{n}^{\prime},\beta_{n},\beta_{n}^{\prime},\beta_{n}^{\prime \prime},\varepsilon_{n}$ 为无穷小量，即

$$
\alpha_ {n} = o (1), \alpha_ {n} ^ {\prime} = o (1), \beta_ {n} = o (1), \beta_ {n} ^ {\prime} = o (1), \beta_ {n} ^ {\prime \prime} = o (1), \epsilon_ {n} = o (1) (n \rightarrow \infty).
$$

由题设知，当 $n \to \infty$ 时有 $\frac{a_n}{a_{n+1}} = 1 + \frac{p}{n} + \frac{a_n}{n}$ 。取对数，即得

$$
\ln a _ {n} - \ln a _ {n + 1} = \ln \left(1 + \frac {p}{n} + \frac {a _ {n}}{n}\right) = \frac {p}{n} + \frac {a _ {n}}{n} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right) = \frac {1}{n} \left(p + a _ {n} ^ {\prime}\right).
$$

令 $n = 1,2,\dots ,N - 1$ 并求和，则得

$$
\ln a _ {1} - \ln a _ {N} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {1}{n} \left(p + a _ {n} ^ {\prime}\right).
$$

由143题（在其中令 $x_{N} = \sum_{n = 1}^{N - 1}\frac{\alpha_{n}^{\prime}}{n},y_{N} = \sum_{n = 1}^{N - 1}\frac{1}{n})$ 知

$$
\lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \frac {\left(\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {\alpha_ {n} ^ {\prime}}{n}\right)}{\left(\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {1}{n}\right)} = \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \alpha_ {N} ^ {\prime} = 0.
$$

又由146题知 $\sum_{n = 1}^{N - 1}\frac{1}{n} = C + \ln (N - 1) + \varepsilon_n$ ，其中 $C$ 是欧拉常数， $\varepsilon_N\to 0$ .于是，令

$$
\beta_ {N} = \frac {\left(\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {\alpha_ {n} ^ {\prime}}{n}\right)}{\left(\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {1}{n}\right)},
$$

有

$$
\ln a _ {1} - \ln a _ {N} = (p + \beta_ {N}) \sum_ {n - 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {1}{n} = (p + \beta_ {N}) [ C + \ln (N - 1) + \varepsilon_ {N} ] = (p + \beta_ {N}) \ln (N - 1) + k + \beta_ {N} ^ {\prime},
$$

其中 $k = Cp$ 为常数.于是，

$$
\ln a _ {N} = - (p + \beta_ {N}) \ln (N - 1) + k ^ {\prime} - \beta_ {N} ^ {\prime},
$$

其中 $k^{\prime} = \ln a_{1} - k$ 为常数，从而，

$$
a _ {N} = \mathrm {e} ^ {k ^ {\prime} - \beta_ {N}} (N - 1) ^ {- (p + \beta_ {N})} = \mathrm {e} ^ {k ^ {\prime} - \beta_ {N}} \left(\frac {N - 1}{N}\right) ^ {- (p + \beta_ {N})} N ^ {\beta_ {N}} N ^ {- p}.
$$

其中 $\beta_N'' = -\beta_N$ 由于 $\beta_N'' = o(1)$ ，故对于任给的 $\varepsilon >0$ ，当 $N$ 充分大时，有 $|\beta_N^{\prime}| < \frac{\varepsilon}{2}$ ，从而， $N^{\beta_N} < N^{\frac{\varepsilon}{2}}$ .再注意到

$$
\lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \left(\frac {N - 1}{N}\right) ^ {- (p + \beta_ {N})} = 1,
$$

即知：当 $N$ 充分大时，有

$$
0 <   a _ {N} \leqslant k ^ {\prime \prime} N ^ {\frac {\varepsilon}{2}} N ^ {- p} = O \left(\frac {1}{N ^ {p - \frac {\varepsilon}{2}}}\right),
$$

其中 $k''$ 是常数.于是，得 $a_{N} = o\left(\frac{1}{N^{p - \varepsilon}}\right)$ 本题获证
