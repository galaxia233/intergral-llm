【4327】 计算单层的对数势 $u(x,y) = \oint_{C}\kappa \ln \frac{1}{r}\mathrm{d}s,$

式中 $\kappa =$ 常数， $r = \sqrt{(\xi - x)^2 + (\eta - y)^2}$ ，设围线 $C$ 是圆周 $\xi^2 +\eta^2 = R^2$

解 由对称性知，单层的对数势为

$$
\begin{array}{l} u (x, y) = 2 \kappa \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \ln \frac {1}{r} \cdot R \mathrm {d} \theta = 2 R \kappa \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \ln \frac {1}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} - 2 R _ {\rho} \cos \theta + \rho^ {2}}} \mathrm {d} \theta \\ = - R \kappa \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \ln R ^ {2} \left[ 1 - 2 \frac {\rho}{R} \cos \theta + \left(\frac {\rho}{R}\right) ^ {2} \right] d \theta , \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $\rho = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2},\xi x + \eta y = R\rho \cos \theta$ ，而 $\theta$ 是向量 $r = x\dot{1} +y\dot{j}$ 与 $\pmb {r}_i = \pmb {\xi}_i + \pmb {\eta}_j$ 的正向夹角.

利用 3733 题（或 2192 题）的结果，可得

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \ln \left[ 1 - 2 \frac {\rho}{R} \cos \theta + \left(\frac {\rho}{R}\right) ^ {2} \right] d \theta = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & \rho \leqslant R, \\ 2 \pi \ln \frac {\rho}{R}, & \rho > R. \end{array} \right.
$$

于是，我们有 $u(x,y) = -2R\kappa \int_{0}^{\pi}\ln R\mathrm{d}\theta -R\kappa \int_{0}^{\pi}\ln \left[1 - 2\frac{\rho}{R}\cos \theta +\left(\frac{\rho}{R}\right)^{2}\right]\mathrm{d}\theta = \begin{cases} 2\pi R\kappa \ln \frac{1}{R}, & \rho \leqslant R,\\ 2\pi R\kappa \ln \frac{1}{\rho}, & \rho >R. \end{cases}$
