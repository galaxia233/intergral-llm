【4119】 $z = x^{2} + y^{2}, z = 2(x^{2} + y^{2}), xy = a^{2}, xy = 2a^{2}, x = 2y, 2x = y (x > 0, y > 0)$ .

解题思路 作变量代换 $z = u(x^{2} + y^{2}),xy = v,x = yw$ ，则

$$
x = \sqrt {v w}, y = \sqrt {\frac {v}{w}}, z = u \left(v w + \frac {v}{w}\right) \quad \text {及} \quad | I | = \frac {v}{2} + \frac {v}{2 w ^ {2}},
$$

且区域 $V$ 为 $1 \leqslant u \leqslant 2, a^{2} \leqslant v \leqslant 2a^{2}, \frac{1}{2} \leqslant w \leqslant 2.$

解令 $z = u(x^{2} + y^{2}),xy = v,x = yw$ ，则

$$
x = \sqrt {v w}, \quad y = \sqrt {\frac {v}{w}}, \quad z = u \left(v w + \frac {v}{w}\right).
$$

变换的雅可比行列式为

$$
I = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & \frac {\sqrt {w}}{2 \sqrt {v}} & \frac {\sqrt {v}}{2 \sqrt {w}} \\ 0 & \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {v w}} & - \frac {\sqrt {v}}{2 \sqrt {w ^ {3}}} \\ v w + \frac {v}{w} & u \left(w + \frac {1}{w}\right) & u \left(v - \frac {v}{w ^ {2}}\right) \end{array} \right| = - \left(\frac {v}{2} + \frac {v}{2 w ^ {2}}\right),
$$

于是，体积为

$$
V = \int_ {1} ^ {2} d u \int_ {u ^ {2}} ^ {2 a ^ {2}} d v \int_ {\frac {1}{2}} ^ {2} \left(\frac {v}{2} + \frac {v}{2 w ^ {2}}\right) d w = \frac {3 a ^ {4}}{4} \int_ {\frac {1}{2}} ^ {2} \left(1 + \frac {1}{w ^ {2}}\right) d w = \frac {9 a ^ {4}}{4}.
