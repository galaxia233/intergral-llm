【1970】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{[1 + \sqrt{x(1 + x)}]^2}$

解设 $\sqrt{x(1 + x)} = z + x$ ，则 $x = \frac{z^2}{1 - 2z},\mathrm{d}x = \frac{2z(1 - z)}{(1 - 2z)^2}\mathrm{d}z,1 + \sqrt{x(1 + x)} = \frac{1 - z - z^2}{1 - 2z}.$

代入得 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{[1 + \sqrt{x(1 + x)}]^2} = 2\int \frac{z(1 - z)}{(1 - z - z^2)^2}\mathrm{d}z = 2\int \frac{(1 - z - z^2) + (2z + 1) - 2}{(1 - z - z^2)^2}\mathrm{d}z$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = 2 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{1 - z - z ^ {2}} - 2 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (1 - z - z ^ {2})}{(1 - z - z ^ {2}) ^ {2}} - 4 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(1 - z - z ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \\ = 2 \int \frac {d (z + \frac {1}{2})}{\frac {5}{4} - (z + \frac {1}{2}) ^ {2}} + \frac {2}{1 - z - z ^ {2}} - 4 \left\{\frac {2 z + 1}{5 (1 - z - z ^ {2})} + \frac {2}{5} \int \frac {d (z + \frac {1}{2})}{\frac {5}{4} - (z + \frac {1}{2}) ^ {2}} * \right\} \\ = \frac {2}{5 \sqrt {5}} \ln \left| \frac {\frac {\sqrt {5}}{2} + z + \frac {1}{2}}{\frac {\sqrt {5}}{2} - z - \frac {1}{2}} \right| + \frac {2}{1 - z - z ^ {2}} - \frac {4 (2 z + 1)}{5 (1 - z - z ^ {2})} + C = \frac {2}{5 \sqrt {5}} \ln \left| \frac {\sqrt {5} + 2 z + 1}{\sqrt {5} - 2 z - 1} \right| + \frac {2 (3 - 4 z)}{5 (1 - z - z ^ {2})} + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $z = \sqrt{x(1 + x)} -x.$

\*) 利用 1921 题的递推公式.
