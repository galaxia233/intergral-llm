【4247】求螺线 $x = a\cos t, y = a\sin t, z = \frac{h}{2\pi} t$ （ $0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2\pi$ ）的一支对坐标轴的转动惯量.

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{a^2\sin^2t + a^2\cos^2t + \frac{h^2}{4\pi^2}}\mathrm{dt} = \frac{\sqrt{4\pi^2a^2 + h^2}}{2\pi}\mathrm{dt}.$

于是，转动惯量为

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {x} = \int_ {C} (y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}) d s = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \left(a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} t + \frac {h ^ {2}}{4 \pi^ {2}} t ^ {2}\right) \frac {\sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}}}{2 \pi} d t \\ = \frac {a ^ {2}}{2 \pi} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}} \cdot \pi + \frac {h ^ {2}}{4 \pi^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{2 \pi} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{3} (2 \pi) ^ {3} = \left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {h ^ {2}}{3}\right) \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {y} = \int_ {c} (x ^ {2} + z ^ {2}) d s = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \left(a ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t + \frac {h ^ {2}}{4 \pi^ {2}} t ^ {2}\right) \cdot \frac {1}{2 \pi} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}} d t \\ = \frac {a ^ {2}}{2 \pi} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}} \cdot \pi + \frac {h ^ {2}}{4 \pi^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{2 \pi} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{3} (2 \pi) ^ {3} = \left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {h ^ {2}}{3}\right) \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
I _ {z} = \int_ {c} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) d s = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} a ^ {2} \cdot \frac {1}{2 \pi} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}} d t = a ^ {2} \sqrt {4 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + h ^ {2}}.
$$
