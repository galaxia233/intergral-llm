【1794】 $\int \sqrt{x}\ln^2 x\mathrm{d}x$

解 $\int \sqrt{x}\ln^2 x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{2}{3}\int \ln^2 x\mathrm{d}(x^{\frac{3}{2}}) = \frac{2}{3} x^{\frac{3}{2}}\ln^2 x - \frac{4}{3}\int x^{\frac{3}{2}}\ln x\frac{1}{x}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {2}{3} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \ln^ {2} x - \frac {8}{9} \int \ln x d \left(x ^ {\frac {3}{2}}\right) = \frac {2}{3} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \ln^ {2} x - \frac {8}{9} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \ln x + \frac {8}{9} \int x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \frac {1}{x} d x \\ = \frac {2}{3} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \left(\ln^ {2} x - \frac {4}{3} \ln x + \frac {8}{9}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
