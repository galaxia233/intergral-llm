【1993】 $\int \cos^6 x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \cos^6 x\mathrm{d}x = \int \sin^6\left(x - \frac{\pi}{2}\right)\mathrm{d}\left(x - \frac{\pi}{2}\right)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {5}{1 6} \left(x - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) - \frac {1}{4} \sin 2 \left(x - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) + \frac {3}{6 4} \sin 4 \left(x - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) + \frac {1}{4 8} \sin^ {3} 2 \left(x - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) ^ {*}) + C _ {1} \\ = \frac {5 x}{1 6} + \frac {1}{4} \sin 2 x + \frac {3}{6 4} \sin 4 x - \frac {1}{4 8} \sin^ {3} 2 x + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用1992题的结果.
