26.42 If money is invested at 5 percent, compounded continuously, in how many years will it double in value?

$2y_{0} = y_{0}e^{0.05t}$ , by Problem 26.9. So $2 = e^{0.05t}$ , $0.05t = \ln 2$ , $t = 20\ln 2 \approx 20(0.6931) = 13.862$ . Thus, the money will double in a little less than 13 years and 315 days.
