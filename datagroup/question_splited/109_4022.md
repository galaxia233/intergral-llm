【4022】 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} -\frac{z^2}{c^2} = -1,\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1.$

提示 令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = brsin\varphi$ ，则曲面方程化为

$$
z = \pm c \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant 2 \pi , 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1).
$$

解 若令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi$ ，则曲面方程化为

$$
z = \pm c \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant 2 \pi , 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1).
$$

于是，曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \iint_ {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leqslant 1} 2 c \sqrt {1 + \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}} d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} 2 a b c r \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} d r = 2 a b c \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \int_ {0} ^ {1} r \sqrt {1 + r ^ {2}} d r \\ = \frac {4 \pi}{3} a b c (2 \sqrt {2} - 1). \\ \end{array}
$$
