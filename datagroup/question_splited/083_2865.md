【2865】 $\frac{x\mathrm{sh}\alpha}{1 - 2x\mathrm{ch}\alpha + x^2}.$

解 $\frac{x\mathrm{sh}\alpha}{1 - 2x\mathrm{ch}\alpha + x^2} = \frac{1}{2}\left[\frac{\mathrm{ch}\alpha + \mathrm{sh}\alpha}{x - (\mathrm{ch}\alpha + \mathrm{sh}\alpha)} -\frac{\mathrm{ch}\alpha - \mathrm{sh}\alpha}{x - (\mathrm{ch}\alpha - \mathrm{sh}\alpha)}\right]$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} \left[ \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {\alpha}}{x - \mathrm {e} ^ {\alpha}} - \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {- \alpha}}{x - \mathrm {e} ^ {- \alpha}} \right] = \frac {1}{2} \left[ - \frac {1}{1 - x \mathrm {e} ^ {- \alpha}} + \frac {1}{1 - x \mathrm {e} ^ {\alpha}} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left[ - \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {n} e ^ {- n \alpha} + \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {n} e ^ {n \alpha} \right] = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {n} \operatorname {s h} n \alpha , \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $|x| < \min(e^{-\alpha}, e^{\alpha}) = e^{-|\alpha|}$ .
