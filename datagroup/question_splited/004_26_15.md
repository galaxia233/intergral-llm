26.15 Assume that a quantity $y$ decays exponentially, with a decay constant $K$ . The half-life $T$ is defined to be the time interval after which half of the original quantity remains. Find the relationship between $K$ and $T$ .

$y = y_{0}e^{\kappa t}$ . By definition, $\frac{1}{2} y_0 = y_0e^{\kappa T}$ So $\frac{1}{2} = e^{\kappa T},\quad KT = \ln \frac{1}{2} = -\ln 2.$
