【2014】 $\int \cos x\cos 2x\cos 3x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \cos x\cos 2x\cos 3x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \cos 2x(\cos 4x + \cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4}\int (\cos 6x + \cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x + \frac{1}{4}\int (1 + \cos 4x)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{2 4} \sin 6 x + \frac {1}{8} \sin 2 x + \frac {1}{1 6} \sin 4 x + \frac {x}{4} + C.
