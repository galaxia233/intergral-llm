【3052】 $\prod_{n = 2}^{\infty}\frac{n^{3} - 1}{n^{3} + 1} = \frac{2}{3}.$

证 记 $p_n = \frac{n^3 - 1}{n^3 + 1}$ . 由于部分乘积

$$
P _ {n} = \prod_ {i = 2} ^ {n} p _ {i} = \frac {2 ^ {3} - 1}{2 ^ {3} + 1} \cdot \frac {3 ^ {3} - 1}{3 ^ {3} + 1} \dots \frac {n ^ {3} - 1}{n ^ {3} + 1} = \frac {2}{3} \cdot \frac {n ^ {2} + n + 1}{n (n + 1)} \rightarrow \frac {2}{3} \quad (\text {当} n \rightarrow \infty \text {时}),
$$

故 $\prod_{n = 2}^{\infty}\frac{n^{3} - 1}{n^{3} + 1} = \frac{2}{3}.$
