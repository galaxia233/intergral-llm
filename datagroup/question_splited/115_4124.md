【4124】 $\frac{x}{a} +\frac{y}{b} +\frac{z}{c} = \ln \frac{\frac{x}{a} + \frac{y}{b} + \frac{z}{c}}{\frac{x}{a} + \frac{y}{b}},x = 0,z = 0,\frac{y}{b} +\frac{z}{c} = 0,\frac{x}{a} +\frac{y}{b} +\frac{z}{c} = 1.$

提示 仿 4123 题的解法.

解 令 $u = \frac{x}{a}, v = \frac{x}{a} + \frac{y}{b}, w = \frac{x}{a} + \frac{y}{b} + \frac{z}{c}$ ，则 $\frac{D(u, v, w)}{D(x, y, z)} = \frac{1}{abc}$

且区域 $V$ 变为

$$
0 \leqslant u \leqslant w, \quad w e ^ {u} \leqslant v \leqslant w, \quad 0 \leqslant w \leqslant 1.
$$

于是， $\frac{D(x,y,z)}{D(u,v,w)} = abc$ ，且体积为

$$
V = a b c \int_ {0} ^ {1} d w \int_ {0} ^ {w} d u \int_ {u v ^ {- w}} ^ {w} d v = a b c \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left(w ^ {2} - w ^ {2} e ^ {- w}\right) d w = a b c \left(\frac {1}{3} - 2 + 5 e ^ {- 1}\right) = 5 a b c \left(\frac {1}{e} - \frac {1}{3}\right).
$$
