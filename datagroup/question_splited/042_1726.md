【1726】 $\int \frac{(2 - x)^2}{2 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{(2 - x)^2}{2 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{(x^2 - 2) - 4x + 6}{2 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(-1 - \frac{4x}{2 - x^2} +\frac{6}{2 - x^2}\right)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= - x + 2 \ln | 2 - x ^ {2} | + \frac {3}{\sqrt {2}} \ln \left| \frac {\sqrt {2} + x}{\sqrt {2} - x} \right| + C.
