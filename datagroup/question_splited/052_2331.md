【2331】设函数 $\varphi (x)$ 及 $\psi (x)$ 和它们的平方在区间 $[a,b]$ 上可积.证明柯西一布尼亚科夫斯基不等式：
$$
\left\{\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi (x) \psi (x) d x \right\} ^ {2} \leqslant \int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi^ {2} (x) d x \int_ {a} ^ {b} \psi^ {2} (x) d x.
$$

提示 考虑积分 $\int_{a}^{b}[\varphi (x) - \lambda \psi (x)]^{2}\mathrm{d}x$ ，其中 $\pmb{\lambda}$ 为任意实数.

证 证法 1：我们有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi^ {2} (x) d x\right) \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \psi^ {2} (x) d x\right) - \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi (x) \psi (x) d x\right) ^ {2} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi^ {2} (y) d x\right) \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \psi^ {2} (y) d x\right) + \frac {1}{2} \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \psi^ {2} (y) d x\right) \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi^ {2} (y) d y\right) - \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi (x) \psi (x) d x\right) \left(\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi (y) \psi (y) d y\right) \\ = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \left\{\int_ {a} ^ {b} [ \varphi (x) \psi (y) - \psi (x) \varphi (y) ] ^ {2} d x \right\} d y \geqslant 0, \\ \end{array}
$$

故 $\left\{\int_{a}^{b}\varphi (x)\psi (x)\mathrm{d}x\right\}^{2}\leqslant \int_{a}^{b}\varphi^{2}(x)\mathrm{d}x\int_{a}^{b}\psi^{2}(x)\mathrm{d}x.$

证法 2：考虑积分 $\int_{a}^{b}\left[\varphi (x) - \lambda \psi (x)\right]^{2}\mathrm{d}x,$

其中 $\lambda$ 为任意实数.从而有

$$
\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi^ {2} (x) d x - 2 \lambda \int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi (x) \psi (x) d x + \lambda^ {2} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \psi^ {2} (x) d x \geqslant 0.
$$

这是关于变量 $\lambda$ 的不等式，左端是二次三项式.于是，其判别式

$$
\left\{\int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi (x) \psi (x) \mathrm {d} x \right\} ^ {2} - \int_ {a} ^ {b} \varphi^ {2} (x) \mathrm {d} x \int_ {a} ^ {b} \psi^ {2} (x) \mathrm {d} x \leqslant 0,
$$

即 $\left\{\int_{a}^{b}\varphi (x)\psi (x)\mathrm{d}x\right\}^{2}\leqslant \int_{a}^{b}\varphi^{2}(x)\mathrm{d}x\int_{a}^{b}\psi^{2}(x)\mathrm{d}x.$
