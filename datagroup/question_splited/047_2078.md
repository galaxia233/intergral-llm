【2078】 $\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}\sin^{2}x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}\sin^{2}x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}(1 - \cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}\mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{2}\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}\cos 2x\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} e ^ {x} (x - 1) - \frac {1}{2} \int x \cos 2 x d \left(e ^ {x}\right) = \frac {1}{2} e ^ {x} (x - 1) - \frac {1}{2} x e ^ {x} \cos 2 x + \frac {1}{2} \int e ^ {x} (\cos 2 x - 2 x \sin 2 x) d x \\ = \frac {1}{2} e ^ {x} (x - 1) - \frac {1}{2} x e ^ {x} \cos 2 x + \frac {e ^ {x}}{2} \cdot \frac {\cos 2 x + 2 \sin 2 x ^ {*})}{5} - \int x e ^ {x} \sin 2 x d x, \\ \end{array}
$$

而 $\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}\sin 2x\mathrm{d}x = \int x\sin 2x\mathrm{d}(\mathrm{e}^{x}) = x\mathrm{e}^{x}\sin 2x - \int \mathrm{e}^{x}(\sin 2x + 2x\cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = x \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \sin 2 x - \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{5} (\sin 2 x - 2 \cos 2 x) ^ {* *}) - 2 \int x \mathrm {e} ^ {x} (1 - 2 \sin^ {2} x) \mathrm {d} x \\ = x \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \sin 2 x - \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{5} (\sin 2 x - 2 \cos 2 x) - 2 (x - 1) \mathrm {e} ^ {x} + 4 \int x \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \sin^ {2} x \mathrm {d} x. \\ \end{array}
$$

代入得 $\int x\mathrm{e}^{x}\sin^{2}x\mathrm{d}x = \mathrm{e}^{x}\left[\frac{x - 1}{2} -\frac{x}{10} (2\sin 2x + \cos 2x) + \frac{1}{50} (4\sin 2x - 3\cos 2x)\right] + C.$

\*) 利用 1828 题的结果.

\*\*）利用 1829 题的结果.
