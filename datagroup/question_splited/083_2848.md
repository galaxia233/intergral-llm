【2848】写出函数 $f(x) = (1 + x)^{\frac{1}{x}} (x \neq 0)$ 和 $f(0) = e$ 按变量 $x$ 的非负整数次幂展开式的前三项.

解 $f(x) = (1 + x)^{\frac{1}{x}}(x\neq 0),\qquad f(0) = \mathrm{e};$

$$
\begin{array}{l} f ^ {\prime} (x) = (1 + x) ^ {\frac {1}{x}} \left[ - \frac {1}{x ^ {2}} \ln (1 + x) + \frac {1}{x (1 + x)} \right] = (1 + x) ^ {\frac {1}{x}} \left[ - \frac {1}{x ^ {2}} \left(x - \frac {x ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {x ^ {3}}{3} - \dots\right) + \frac {1}{x} - \frac {1}{1 + x} \right] \\ = (1 + x) ^ {\frac {1}{x}} \left[ \frac {1}{2} - \frac {x}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + o (x) \right] \quad (x \neq 0), \\ \end{array}
$$

由微分学中值定理知 $\frac{f(x) - f(0)}{x - 0} = f'(\xi)$ ，其中 $\xi$ 介于0与 $x$ 之间，从而，

$$
\begin{array}{l} f ^ {\prime} (0) = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} \frac {f (x) - f (0)}{x - 0} = \lim  _ {x \rightarrow 0} f ^ {\prime} (\xi) = \lim  _ {\xi \rightarrow 0} f ^ {\prime} (\xi) = - \frac {\mathrm {e}}{2}; \\ f ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = (1 + x) ^ {\frac {1}{x}} \left\{\left[ \frac {1}{2} - \frac {x}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + o (x) \right] ^ {2} + \frac {2}{x ^ {3}} \ln (1 + x) - \frac {1}{x ^ {2} (1 + x)} - \frac {1}{x ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{(1 + x) ^ {2}} \right\} \\ = (1 + x) ^ {\frac {1}{x}} \left\{\left[ \frac {1}{2} - \frac {x}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + o (x) \right] ^ {2} + \frac {2}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + \frac {1}{(1 + x) ^ {2}} + o _ {1} (x) \right\} \quad (x \neq 0), \\ \end{array}
$$

仿上可得

$$
f ^ {\prime \prime} (0) = \frac {1 1}{1 2} \mathrm {e};
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} f ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = (1 + x) ^ {\frac {1}{x}} \left\{\left[ \frac {1}{2} - \frac {x}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + o (x) \right] ^ {3} \right. \\ + 3 \left[ \frac {1}{2} - \frac {x}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + o (x) \right] \cdot \left[ \frac {2}{3} - \frac {1}{1 + x} + \frac {1}{(1 + x) ^ {2}} + o _ {1} (x) \right] \\ \left. + \left[ - \frac {6}{x ^ {4}} \ln (1 + x) + \frac {2}{x ^ {3} (1 + x)} + \frac {4}{x ^ {3}} + \frac {1}{(1 + x) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{x ^ {2}} - \frac {2}{(1 + x) ^ {3}} \right] \right\} \quad (x \neq 0), \\ \end{array}
$$

同理可得

$$
f ^ {\prime \prime} (0) = - \frac {2 1}{8} e.
$$

于是，展开式的前三项为 $\mathrm{e}\left(1 - \frac{1}{2} x + \frac{11}{24} x^2 -\frac{7}{16} x^3 +\dots\right)$ ，收敛区间为（-1,1).
