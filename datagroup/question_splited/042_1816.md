【1816】 $\int \frac{x^2}{(1 + x^2)^2}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $\frac{x^2}{(1 + x^2)^2} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{x}{(1 + x^2)^2} \mathrm{d}(1 + x^2) = -\frac{1}{2} x \mathrm{~d}\left(\frac{1}{1 + x^2}\right)$ .

解 $\int \frac{x^2}{(1 + x^2)^2} \, \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2} \int \frac{x}{(1 + x^2)^2} \, \mathrm{d}(1 + x^2) = -\frac{1}{2} \int x \, \mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{1 + x^2}\right) = -\frac{x}{2(1 + x^2)} + \frac{1}{2} \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + x^2}$

$$
= - \frac {x}{2 \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right)} + \frac {1}{2} \arctan x + C.
$$
