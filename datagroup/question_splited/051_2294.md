【2294】 $\int_0^\pi \sin^n x\sin nx\mathrm{d}x.$

解 解法 1：

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \sin^ {n} x \sin n x d x = \frac {1}{(2 i) ^ {n + 1}} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \left[ \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {n} ^ {k} e ^ {i (n - 2 k) x} \left(e ^ {i n x} - e ^ {- i n x}\right) \right] d x \\ = \frac {1}{(2 \mathrm {i}) ^ {n + 1}} \left[ \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {n} ^ {k} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} (2 n - 2 k) x} \mathrm {d} x - \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {n} ^ {k} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {- \mathrm {i} 2 k x} \mathrm {d} x \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 ^ {n + 1} \mathrm {i} ^ {n + 1}} [ (- 1) ^ {n} C _ {n} ^ {n} \pi - (- 1) ^ {0} C _ {n} ^ {0} \pi ] = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & n \text {为 偶 数 ,} \\ \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n}} (- 1) ^ {\frac {n + 1}{2} + 1}, & n \text {为 奇 数 .} \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

由于 $\sin \frac{n\pi}{2} = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}0, & n\text{为偶数，}\\ (-1)^{\frac{n + 1}{2} +1}, & n\text{为奇数，} \end{array} \right.$

于是， $\int_0^\pi \sin^n x\sin nx\mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi}{2^n}\sin \frac{n\pi}{2}.$

解法 2：设 $x = \frac{\pi}{2} - t$ ，则

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \sin^ {n} x \sin n x d x = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {n} t \sin \left(\frac {n \pi}{2} - n t\right) d t = \sin \frac {n \pi}{2} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {n} t \cos n t d t - \cos \frac {n \pi}{2} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {n} t \sin n t d t
$$

$$
= \sin \frac {n \pi}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos^ {n} x \cos n x d x = \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n}} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2}.
$$
