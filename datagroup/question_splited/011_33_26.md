33.25 Find a vector of length 5 that has the direction opposite to the vector $\mathbf{B} = 7\mathbf{i} + 24\mathbf{j}$ .

$|\mathbf{B}| = \sqrt{49 + 576} = 25$ . Hence, the unit vector in the direction of $\mathbf{B}$ is $\mathbf{C} = \frac{7}{25}\mathbf{i} + \frac{24}{32}\mathbf{j}$ . Thus, the desired vector is $-5\mathbf{C} = -\frac{7}{3}\mathbf{i} - \frac{24}{3}\mathbf{j}$ .
