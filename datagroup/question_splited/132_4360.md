【4360】 计算积分 $F(t) = \iint_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = t^2} f(x, y, z) \, \mathrm{d}S,$

式中 $f(x,y,z) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}x^{2} + y^{2}, & z\geqslant \sqrt{x^{2} + y^{2}},\\ 0, & z <   \sqrt{x^{2} + y^{2}}. \end{array} \right.$

解 由球面方程 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = t^{2}$ 知

$$
\frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac {- x}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}}, \quad \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - x }}, \quad \sqrt {1 - \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2}} = \frac {| t |}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})}},
$$

而由 $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x = 1, \\ z^2 = x^2 + y^2 \end{array} \right.$

可得 $x^{2} + y^{2} = \frac{t^{2}}{2} = \left(\frac{t}{\sqrt{2}}\right)^{2}.$

于是，积分

$$
\begin{array}{l} F (t) = \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2} = t ^ {2}} f (x, y, z) d S = \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant \left(\frac {t}{\sqrt {2}}\right) ^ {2}} \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) \frac {| t |}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right)}} d x d y \\ = | t | \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {| t |}{\sqrt {2}}} \frac {r ^ {3}}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - r ^ {2}}} d r d \varphi . \\ \end{array}
$$

因为 $\int \frac{r^3}{\sqrt{t^2 - r^2}}\mathrm{d}r = \frac{1}{2}\int \frac{t^2 - r^2 - t^2}{\sqrt{t^2 - r^2}}\mathrm{d}(t^2 -r^2) = \frac{1}{3} (t^2 -r^2)^{\frac{3}{2}} - t^2\sqrt{t^2 - r^2} +C,$

所以， $\int_{0}^{\frac{|t|}{\sqrt{2}}}\frac{t^{3}}{\sqrt{t^{2} - r^{2}}}\mathrm{d}r = \left[\frac{1}{3} (t^{2} - r^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}} - t^2\sqrt{t^2 - r^2}\right]\Bigg|_{0}^{\frac{|t|}{\sqrt{2}}} = \frac{-5\sqrt{2}}{12} |t|^{3} + \frac{2}{3} |t|^{3} = \frac{8 - 5\sqrt{2}}{12} |t|^{3}.$

于是，最后得 $F(t) = |t| \int_{0}^{2\pi} \frac{8 - 5\sqrt{2}}{12} |t|^3 \, \mathrm{d}\varphi = \frac{(8 - 5\sqrt{2})\pi}{6} t^4.$
