【4342】计算 $\iint_{S} z \, \mathrm{d}S,$

式中 $S$ 为曲面 $x^{2} + z^{2} = 2az(a > 0)$ 被曲面 $z = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$ 所割下的部分.

解 作变换

$$
x = a r \sin \theta , \quad y = y, \quad z = a + a r \cos \theta ,
$$

则两曲面分别化为

$$
r = 1, \quad \text {和} \quad y ^ {2} = 2 a ^ {2} \cos \theta (1 + \cos \theta).
$$

两曲面交线的参数方程为

$$
x = a \sin \theta , \quad y = \pm \sqrt {2} a \sqrt {\cos \theta (1 + \cos \theta)}, \quad z = a + a \cos \theta \quad (- \frac {\pi}{2} \leqslant \theta \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}).
$$

于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {S} z \mathrm {d} S = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \mathrm {d} \theta \int_ {- \sqrt {2} a \sqrt {\cos \theta (1 + \cos \theta)}} ^ {\sqrt {2} a \sqrt {\cos \theta (1 - \cos \theta)}} (a + a \cos \theta) a \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} 2 \sqrt {2} a ^ {3} \sqrt {\cos \theta} \sqrt {(1 + \cos \theta) ^ {3}} \mathrm {d} \theta \\ = - 4 \sqrt {2} a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sqrt {\cos \theta} \sqrt {(1 + \cos \theta) ^ {3}}}{\sin \theta} d (\cos \theta) = - 4 \sqrt {2} a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sqrt {\cos \theta} (1 + \cos \theta)}{\sqrt {(1 - \cos \theta)}} d (\cos \theta) \\ = 4 \sqrt {2} a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left[ t ^ {\frac {1}{2}} (1 - t) ^ {\frac {1}{2}} + t ^ {\frac {3}{2}} (1 - t) ^ {- \frac {1}{2}} \right] d t = 4 \sqrt {2} a ^ {3} \left[ B \left(\frac {3}{2}, \frac {1}{2}\right) + B \left(\frac {5}{2}, \frac {1}{2}\right) \right] = \frac {7}{2} \sqrt {2} \pi a ^ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$
