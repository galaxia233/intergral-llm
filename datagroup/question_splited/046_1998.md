【1998】 $\int \frac{\cos^4x}{\sin^3x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{\cos^4 x}{\sin^3 x} \mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\cos^3 x}{\sin^3 x} \mathrm{d}(\sin x) = -\frac{1}{2} \int \cos^3 x \mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{\sin^2 x}\right)$

$$
= - \frac {\cos^ {3} x}{2 \sin^ {2} x} - \frac {3}{2} \int \frac {\cos^ {2} x \sin x}{\sin^ {2} x} d x = - \frac {\cos^ {3} x}{2 \sin^ {2} x} - \frac {3}{2} \int \frac {1 - \sin^ {2} x}{\sin x} d x
$$

$$
= - \frac {\cos^ {3} x}{2 \sin^ {2} x} - \frac {3}{2} \ln \left| \tan \frac {x}{2} \right| - \frac {3}{2} \cos x + C
$$
