【2165】 $\int \frac{1 + \sin x}{1 + \cos x} \mathrm{e}^x \, \mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{1 + \sin x}{1 + \cos x} \mathrm{e}^x \mathrm{d}x = \int \left( \frac{1 + 2\sin \frac{x}{2}\cos \frac{x}{2}}{2\cos^2 \frac{x}{2}} \right) \mathrm{e}^x \mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^x}{2\cos^2 \frac{x}{2}} \mathrm{d}x + \int \mathrm{e}^x \tan \frac{x}{2} \mathrm{d}x.$

$$
= \int e ^ {x} d \left(\tan \frac {x}{2}\right) + \int \tan \frac {x}{2} d \left(e ^ {x}\right) = e ^ {x} \tan \frac {x}{2} - \int \tan \frac {x}{2} d \left(e ^ {x}\right) + \int \tan \frac {x}{2} d \left(e ^ {x}\right) = e ^ {x} \tan \frac {x}{2} + C.
$$
