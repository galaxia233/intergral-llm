【4142】求形状为立方体 $0 \leqslant x \leqslant 1, 0 \leqslant y \leqslant 1, 0 \leqslant z \leqslant 1$ 的物体的质心坐标，设此物体在点 $(x, y, z)$ 的密度等于

$$
\rho = x ^ {\frac {2 \beta - 1}{1 - \alpha}} y ^ {\frac {2 \beta - 1}{1 - \beta}} z ^ {\frac {2 \gamma - 1}{1 - \gamma}},
$$

其中 $0 < \alpha < 1, 0 < \beta < 1, 0 < \gamma < 1$ .

解 物体的质量为

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \int_ {0} ^ {1} x ^ {\frac {2 \alpha - 1}{1 - \alpha}} d x \int_ {0} ^ {1} y ^ {\frac {2 \beta - 1}{1 - \beta}} d y \int_ {0} ^ {1} z ^ {\frac {2 \gamma - 1}{1 - \gamma}} d z = \left. \frac {1 - \alpha}{\alpha} x ^ {\frac {\alpha}{1 - \alpha}} \right| _ {0} ^ {1} \cdot \left. \frac {1 - \beta}{\beta} y ^ {\frac {\beta}{1 - \beta}} \right| _ {0} ^ {1} \cdot \left. \frac {1 - \gamma}{\gamma} z ^ {\frac {\gamma}{1 - \gamma}} \right| _ {0} ^ {1} \\ = \frac {(1 - \alpha) (1 - \beta) (1 - \gamma)}{\alpha \beta \gamma}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，质心的坐标为

$$
x _ {1} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {a} ^ {1} x ^ {\frac {2 a - 1}{1 - a} - 1} d x \int_ {a} ^ {1} y ^ {\frac {2 b - 1}{1 - b}} d y \int_ {b} ^ {1} z ^ {\frac {2 \gamma - 1}{1 - \gamma}} d z = \frac {\alpha \beta \gamma}{(1 - \alpha) (1 - \beta) (1 - \gamma)} (1 - \alpha) \frac {(1 - \beta) (1 - \gamma)}{\beta \gamma} = \alpha .
$$

同理可求得 $y_{11} = \beta, z_{11} = \gamma$ .
