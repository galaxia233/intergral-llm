【4244】求摆线 $x = a(t - \sin t)$ ， $y = a(1 - \cos t)(0\leqslant t\leqslant \pi)$ 的弧的质心.

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{a^2(1 - \cos t)^2 + a^2\sin^2t}\mathrm{dt} = 2a\sin \frac{t}{2}\mathrm{dt}.$

质量为 $M = 2a\rho_{0}\int_{0}^{\pi}\sin \frac{t}{2}\mathrm{d}t = 4a\rho_{0}$ .于是，质心的坐标为

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \rho_ {0} a (t - \sin t) \cdot 2 a \sin \frac {t}{2} d t = \frac {a}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} t \sin \frac {t}{2} d t - \frac {a}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \sin t \sin \frac {t}{2} d t \\ = - a t \cos \frac {t}{2} \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} + a \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos \frac {t}{2} d t + \frac {a}{4} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \left(\cos \frac {3 t}{2} - \cos \frac {t}{2}\right) d t = \frac {4 a}{3}; \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
y _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \rho_ {0} a (1 - \cos t) \cdot 2 a \sin \frac {t}{2} d t = \frac {a}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \sin \frac {t}{2} d t - \frac {a}{4} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \left(\sin \frac {3 t}{2} - \sin \frac {t}{2}\right) d t = \frac {4 a}{3}.
$$
