【1771】+ $\int \cos^5 x\sqrt{\sin x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $\sin x = t$ ，则 $\cos^5 x\mathrm{d}x = (1 - \sin^2 x)^2\mathrm{d}(\sin x) = (1 - t^2)^2\mathrm{d}t,$

故得 $\int \cos^5 x\sqrt{\sin x}\mathrm{d}x = \int (1 - t^2)^2 t^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}t = \int (t^{\frac{1}{2}} - 2t^{\frac{5}{2}} + t^{\frac{9}{2}})\mathrm{d}t = \frac{2}{3} t^{\frac{3}{2}} - \frac{4}{7} t^{\frac{7}{2}} + \frac{2}{11} t^{\frac{11}{2}} + C$

$$
= \left(\frac {2}{3} - \frac {4}{7} \sin^ {2} x + \frac {2}{1 1} \sin^ {4} x\right) \sqrt {\sin^ {3} x} + C.
$$
