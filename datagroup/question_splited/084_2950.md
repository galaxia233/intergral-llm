【2950】在区间 $(-\pi, \pi)$ 内展开 $f(x) = x \sin x$ .

解 因为 $f(x)$ 为偶函数，从而， $b_n = 0$ ，且

$$
a _ {0} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x \sin x d x = \frac {2}{\pi} \left(- x \cos x \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\pi} + \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos x d x\right) = 2,
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {n} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x \sin x \cos n x d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x [ \sin (n + 1) x - \sin (n - 1) x ] d x \\ = \frac {1}{\pi} \left[ - \frac {x \cos (n + 1) x}{n + 1} + \frac {\sin (n + 1) x}{(n + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {x \cos (n - 1) x}{n - 1} - \frac {\sin (n - 1) x}{(n - 1) ^ {2}} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\pi} \\ = \frac {2 (- 1) ^ {n + 1}}{n ^ {2} - 1} \quad (n = 2, 3, \dots), \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
a _ {1} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x \sin x \cos x d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x \sin 2 x d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \left[ - \frac {x}{2} \cos 2 x + \frac {1}{4} \sin 2 x \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\pi} = - \frac {1}{2},
$$

故按展开定理， $f(x)$ 可展开为 $1 - \frac{1}{2}\cos x + 2\sum_{n = 2}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{n + 1}}{n^2 - 1}\cos nx = x\sin x$ （ $-\pi < x < \pi)$
