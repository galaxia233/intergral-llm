【1749】 $\int \sin^4 x\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $\sin^4 x = \left(\frac{1 - \cos 2x}{2}\right)^2 = \frac{1}{4}\left(1 - 2\cos 2x + \frac{1 + \cos 4x}{2}\right) = \frac{1}{8} (3 - 4\cos 2x + \cos 4x)$ .

解 $\int \sin^4 x\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(\frac{1 - \cos 2x}{2}\right)^2\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4}\int \left(1 - 2\cos 2x + \frac{1 + \cos 4x}{2}\right)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{8} \int (3 - 4 \cos 2 x + \cos 4 x) d x = \frac {3}{8} x - \frac {1}{4} \sin 2 x + \frac {1}{3 2} \sin 4 x + C.
$$
