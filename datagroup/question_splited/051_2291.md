【2291】 $\int_0^\pi \frac{\sin nx}{\sin x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $u = \frac{\sin nx}{\sin x}$ ，利用欧拉公式得 $u = \frac{e^{inx} - e^{-inx}}{e^{ix} - e^{-ix}}.$

当 $n = 2k$ 时，

$$
\begin{array}{l} u = \left(e ^ {i k x} + e ^ {- i k x}\right) \left(e ^ {i (k - 1) x} + e ^ {i (k - 3) x} + \dots + e ^ {- i (k - 3) x} + e ^ {- i (k - 1) x}\right) \\ = e ^ {(2 k - 1) i x} + e ^ {(2 k - 3) i x} + \dots + e ^ {i x} + e ^ {- i x} + \dots + e ^ {- (2 k - 1) i x} \\ = 2 \left[ \cos (2 k - 1) x + \cos (2 k - 3) x + \dots + \cos x \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\int_{0}^{\pi} u \mathrm{d}x = 2\left[\frac{\sin(2k - 1)x}{2k - 1} + \frac{\sin(2k - 3)x}{2k - 3} + \dots + \sin x\right] \Bigg|_{0}^{\pi} = 0.$

当 $n = 2k + 1$ 时，同上得 $u = 2[\cos 2kx + \cos 2(k - 1)x + \dots +\cos 2x] + 1,$

于是， $\int_0^\pi u\mathrm{d}x = \pi .$

最后得到 $\int_0^\pi \frac{\sin nx}{\sin x}\mathrm{d}x = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}0, & n\text{为偶数，}\\ \pi , & n\text{为奇数.} \end{array} \right.$
