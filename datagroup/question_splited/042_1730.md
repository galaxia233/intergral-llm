【1730】 $\int x\sqrt{2 - 5x}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $x = -\frac{1}{5} (2 - 5x) + \frac{2}{5}$ .

解 $\int x\sqrt{2 - 5x}\mathrm{d}x = \int \left[-\frac{1}{5} (2 - 5x) + \frac{2}{5}\right](2 - 5x)^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \left[-\frac{1}{5} (2 - 5x)^{\frac{3}{2}} + \frac{2}{5} (2 - 5x)^{\frac{1}{2}}\right]\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {2}{1 2 5} (2 - 5 x) ^ {\frac {5}{2}} - \frac {4}{7 5} (2 - 5 x) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} + C = - \frac {8 + 3 0 x}{3 7 5} (2 - 5 x) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} + C.
$$
