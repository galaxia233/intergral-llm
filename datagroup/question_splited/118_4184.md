【4184】 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\varphi(x,y)}{|x - y|^p}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y.$

解 由于 $\frac{m}{|x - y|^p} \leqslant \frac{|\varphi(x, y)|}{|x - y|^p} \leqslant \frac{M}{|x - y|^p}$，并注意到广义重积分收敛必绝对收敛，可知

积分 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\varphi(x,y)}{|x - y|^p}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y$ 与积分 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p}$

同时收敛或同时发散. 由对称性及被积函数的非负性可知，

$$
\int _ {0} ^ {a} \int_ {0} ^ {a} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{| x - y | ^ {p}} = 2 \iint_ {\substack {0 \leqslant x \leqslant a \\ 0 \leqslant y \leqslant x}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}}. \tag {1}
$$

当 $p < 1$ 时，

$$
\iint_ {\substack {0 \leqslant x \leqslant a \\ 0 \leqslant y \leqslant z}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}} = \int_ {0} ^ {a} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {0} ^ {x} \frac {\mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}} = \int_ {0} ^ {a} \frac {x ^ {1 - p}}{1 - p} \mathrm {d} x = \frac {a ^ {2 - p}}{(1 - p)(2 - p)}.
$$

从而，由 (1) 式知 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p} = \frac{2a^{2 - p}}{(1 - p)(2 - p)}.$ 因此，当 $p < 1$ 时积分 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p}$ 收敛.

现设 $p \geqslant 1$ . 首先，我们有

$$
\iint_ {\substack {0 \leqslant x \leqslant a\\0 \leqslant y \leqslant x}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}} = \lim_ {c \rightarrow + c} \iint_ {\substack {0 \leqslant x \leqslant a\\0 \leqslant y \leqslant x - c}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}}. \tag {2}
$$

若 $p = 1$ ，则 $\iint_{\substack{0 \leq x \leq a \\ 0 \leq y \leq x - \varepsilon}} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y}{(x - y)^p} = \int_{\varepsilon}^{a} \mathrm{d}x \int_{0}^{x - \varepsilon} \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{x - y} = \int_{\varepsilon}^{a} (\ln x - \ln \varepsilon) \mathrm{d}x$

$$
= a \ln a - a + \varepsilon - a \ln \varepsilon ,
$$

故 $\lim_{\epsilon \to +0}\iint_{\substack{\epsilon \leqslant x \leqslant a \\ 0 \leqslant y \leqslant x - \epsilon}}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{(x - y)^p} = \lim_{\epsilon \to +0}(a\ln a - a + \epsilon - a\ln \epsilon) = +\infty.$

由此可知，此时 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p}$ 发散；若 $p = 2$ ，则

$$
\iint_ {\substack {0 \leqslant y \leqslant x - \varepsilon}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}} = \int_ {\varepsilon} ^ {a} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {0} ^ {x - \varepsilon} \frac {\mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {2}} = \int_ {\varepsilon} ^ {a} \left(\frac {1}{\varepsilon} - \frac {1}{x}\right) \mathrm {d} x = \frac {a}{\varepsilon} - 1 - \ln a + \ln \varepsilon ,
$$

故 $\lim_{\epsilon \to +0}\iint_{\substack{\epsilon \leqslant x \leqslant a \\ 0 \leqslant y \leqslant x - \epsilon}}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{(x - y)^p} = \lim_{\epsilon \to +0}\left(\frac{a + \epsilon\ln\epsilon}{\epsilon} -1 - \ln a\right) = +\infty.$

由此可知，此时积分 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p}$ 发散；最后，若 $p > 1,p\neq 2$ ，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {\substack {\varepsilon \leqslant x \leqslant a \\ 0 \leqslant y \leqslant x - \varepsilon}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}} = \int_ {\varepsilon} ^ {a} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {0} ^ {x - \varepsilon} \frac {\mathrm {d} y}{(x - y) ^ {p}} = \frac {1}{p - 1} \int_ {\varepsilon} ^ {a} (\varepsilon^ {1 - p} - x ^ {1 - p}) \mathrm {d} x \\ = \frac {1}{(p - 1) \varepsilon^ {p - 1}} \left(a - \frac {p - 1}{p - 2} \varepsilon\right) + \frac {1}{(p - 1) (p - 2) a ^ {p - 2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

从而， $\lim_{\epsilon \to +0}\iint \limits_{\substack{\epsilon \leqslant x\leqslant a\\ 0\leqslant y\leqslant r - \epsilon}}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{(x - y)^p} = +\infty .$

由此可知，此时积分 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p}$ 发散.

综上所述，可知积分 $\int_0^a\int_0^a\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{|x - y|^p}$ 当 $p < 1$ 时收敛， $p\geqslant 1$ 时发散.
