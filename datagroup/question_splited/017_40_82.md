40.82 Find an equation for the plane through the points $P(1,3,5)$ , $Q(-1,2,4)$ , and $R(4,4,0)$ .

$\mathbf{N} = \overrightarrow{PQ} \times \overrightarrow{PR}$ is normal to the plane. $\mathbf{N} = (-2, -1, -1) \times (3, 1, -5) = (6, -13, 1)$ . So, the equation has the form $6x - 13y + z = d$ . Since $R$ is in the plane, $6(4) - 13(4) + 0 = d$ , $d = -28$ . Thus, the equation is $6x - 13y + z = -28$ .
