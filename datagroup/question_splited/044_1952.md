【1952】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)^2\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}.$

解 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)^2\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)^2\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}} +\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}.$

设 $x - 1 = \frac{1}{t}$ 则 $\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{t^2}\mathrm{d}t.$ 不妨设 $t > 0$ ，则有 $\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2} = \frac{\sqrt{2t^2 - 1}}{t}.$

代入得 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)^2\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}} = -\int \frac{t\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{2t^2 - 1}} -\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{2t^2 - 1}}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {1}{2} \sqrt {2 t ^ {2} - 1} - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \ln | \sqrt {2} t + \sqrt {2 t ^ {2} - 1} | + C \\ = \frac {\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}}{2 (1 - x)} - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \ln \left| \frac {\sqrt {2} + \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}}{1 - x} \right| + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
