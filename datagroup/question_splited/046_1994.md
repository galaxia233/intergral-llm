【1994】 $\int \sin^2 x\cos^4 x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \sin^2 x\cos^4 x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4}\int \sin^2 2x\cos^2 x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{8}\int \sin^2 2x(1 + \cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{8} \int \frac {1 - \cos 4 x}{2} d x + \frac {1}{1 6} \int \sin^ {2} 2 x d (\sin 2 x) = \frac {x}{1 6} - \frac {1}{6 4} \sin 4 x + \frac {1}{4 8} \sin^ {3} 2 x + C.
$$
