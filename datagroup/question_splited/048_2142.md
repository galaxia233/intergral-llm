【2142】 $\int \frac{\arcsin x}{x^2} \cdot \frac{1 + x^2}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} \, \mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{\arcsin x}{x^2} \cdot \frac{1 + x^2}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} \mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\arcsin x}{x^2 \sqrt{1 - x^2}} \mathrm{d}x + \int \frac{\arcsin x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} \mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = (\operatorname {s g n} x) \int \frac {\arcsin x \mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {3} \sqrt {x ^ {- 2} - 1}} + \int \arcsin x \mathrm {d} (\arcsin x) = - (\operatorname {s g n} x) \int \arcsin x \mathrm {d} (\sqrt {x ^ {- 2} - 1}) + \frac {1}{2} (\arcsin x) ^ {2} \\ = - (\operatorname {s g n} x) \left(\frac {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}{| x |} \arcsin x - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{| x |}\right) + \frac {1}{2} (\arcsin x) ^ {2} = - \frac {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}{x} \arcsin x + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x} + \frac {1}{2} (\arcsin x) ^ {2} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
= - \frac {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}{x} (\arcsin x) ^ {2} + \ln | x | + \frac {1}{2} (\arcsin x) ^ {2} + C (0 <   | x | <   1).
$$
