39.6 Find the Taylor series for $\ln x$ around 2.

Let $f(x) = \ln x$ . Then, $f'(x) = \frac{1}{x}$ , $f''(x) = -\frac{1}{x^2}$ , $f'''(x) = \frac{2}{x^3}$ , $f^{(4)}(x) = -\frac{2 \cdot 3}{x^4}$ , and, in general, $f^{(n)}(x) = (-1)^{n+1} \frac{(n-1)!}{x^n}$ . So $f(2) = \ln 2$ , and, for $n \geq 1$ , $f^{(n)}(2) = (-1)^{n+1} \frac{(n-1)!}{2^n}$ . Thus, the Taylor series is $\sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{f^{(n)}(2)}{n!} (x-2)^n = \ln 2 + \frac{1}{2}(x-2) - \frac{1}{8}(x-2)^2 + \frac{1}{24}(x-2)^3 - \cdots$ .
