40.80 Find an equation of the plane containing the point $P_{1}(3, -2, 5)$ and perpendicular to the vector $\mathbf{N} = (4, 2, -7)$ .

For any point $P(x, y, z)$ , $P$ is in the plane if and only if $\overrightarrow{P_1P} \perp \mathbf{N}$ , that is, if and only if $\overrightarrow{P_1P} \cdot \mathbf{N} = 4(x - 3) + 2(y + 2) - 7(z - 5) = 0$ , which can also be written as $4x + 2y - 7z = -27$ .
