【2095】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{2x}}{x^2 - 3x + 2}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{2x}}{x^2 - 3x + 2}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{2x}}{(x - 2)(x - 1)}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{2x}}{x - 2}\mathrm{d}x - \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{2x}}{x - 1}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \mathrm {e} ^ {4} \int \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {2 (x - 2)} \mathrm {d} (x - 2)}{x - 2} - \mathrm {e} ^ {2} \int \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {2 (x - 1)} \mathrm {d} (x - 1)}{x - 1} = \mathrm {e} ^ {4} \operatorname {l i} (\mathrm {e} ^ {2 x - 4}) - \mathrm {e} ^ {2} \operatorname {l i} (\mathrm {e} ^ {2 x - 2}) + C.
$$
