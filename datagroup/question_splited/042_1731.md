【1731】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[3]{1 - 3x}}.$

解 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[3]{1 - 3x}} = -\frac{1}{3}\int \frac{(1 - 3x) - 1}{(1 - 3x)^{\frac{1}{3}}}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{3}\int [(1 - 3x)^{\frac{2}{3}} - (1 - 3x)^{-\frac{1}{3}}]\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{1 5} (1 - 3 x) ^ {\frac {5}{3}} - \frac {1}{6} (1 - 3 x) ^ {\frac {2}{3}} + C = - \frac {1 + 2 x}{1 0} (1 - 3 x) ^ {\frac {2}{3}} + C.
$$
