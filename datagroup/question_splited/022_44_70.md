44.69 Describe the solid whose volume is given by the integral $\int_0^3\int_0^{3 - x}\int_0^{3 - x - y}dzdydx.$   
See Fig. 44-38. The solid lies under the plane $z = 3 - x - y$ and above the triangle in the $xy$ -plane bounded by the coordinate axes and the line $x + y = 3$ . It is a tetrahedron, of volume $\frac{1}{6} (3)^3 = \frac{9}{2}$ .
