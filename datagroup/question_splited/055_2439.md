【2439】 $y^{2} = \frac{x^{3}}{2a - x}\left(0\leqslant x\leqslant \frac{5}{3} a\right)^{*})$

解 如图 4.36 所示，设 $y = tx$ ，得 $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x = \frac{2at^2}{1 + t^2}, \\ y = \frac{2at^3}{1 + t^2}. \end{array} \right.$

当 $0 \leqslant x \leqslant \frac{5}{3} a$ 时， $0 \leqslant t \leqslant \sqrt{5}$ （一半弧长）.

$$
x _ {t} ^ {\prime} = \frac {4 a t}{(t ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}}, y _ {t} ^ {\prime} = \frac {2 a t ^ {4} + 6 a t ^ {2}}{(t ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}}, \sqrt {x _ {t} ^ {\prime 2} + y _ {t} ^ {\prime 2}} = \frac {2 a t \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4}}{t ^ {2} + 1}.
$$

所求的弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {5}} \frac {2 a t \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4}}{t ^ {2} + 1} d t = 3 2 a \int_ {0} ^ {\arctan \frac {\sqrt {5}}{2}} \frac {\sin \theta d \theta}{\cos^ {2} \theta (1 + 3 \sin^ {2} \theta)} ^ {* *}) \\ = \frac {3 2 a}{3} \int_ {1} ^ {\frac {2}{3}} \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{z ^ {2} \left(z ^ {2} - \frac {4}{3}\right)} ^ {* * *}) = \frac {3 2 a}{3} \left\{\frac {3}{4} \cdot \frac {1}{z} + \frac {3 \sqrt {3}}{1 6} \ln \frac {z - \frac {2}{\sqrt {3}}}{z + \frac {2}{\sqrt {3}}} \right\} \Bigg | _ {1} ^ {\frac {2}{3}} \\ = 4 a \left(1 + 3 \sqrt {3} \ln \frac {1 + \sqrt {3}}{\sqrt {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

\*）原题误为 $y^{2} = \frac{x^{2}}{2a - x}$ 现按原答案予以改正.

$\star \star)$ 设 $t = 2\tan \theta$

\*\*\*）设 $z = \cos \theta$
