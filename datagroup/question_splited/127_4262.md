【4262】 $\int_{(0,0)}^{(a,b)}f(x + y)(\mathrm{d}x + \mathrm{d}y)$ ，其中 $f(u)$ 为连续函数.

解题思路 令 $F(x, y) = \int_{0}^{x + y} f(u) \mathrm{d}u$ . 注意

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y) = F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y) = f (x + y), \quad \text {及} \quad \mathrm {d} F (x, y) = f (x + y) (\mathrm {d} x + \mathrm {d} y),
$$

即可获解.

解 令 $F(x, y) = \int_{0}^{x + y} f(u) \mathrm{d}u$ . 由于 $f(u)$ 连续，故

$$
F _ {r} ^ {\prime} (x, y) = f (x + y), \quad F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y) = f (x + y),
$$

并且它们都是 $x, y$ 的连续函数。因此， $F(x, y)$ 可微，且

$$
\mathrm {d} F (x, y) = F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y) \mathrm {d} x + F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y) \mathrm {d} y = f (x + y) (\mathrm {d} x + \mathrm {d} y),
$$

故 $f(x + y)(\mathrm{d}x + \mathrm{d}y)$ 是全微分，并且

$$
\int_ {(0, 0)} ^ {(a, b)} f (x, y) (\mathrm {d} x + \mathrm {d} y) = F (a, b) - F (0, 0) = \int_ {0} ^ {a + b} f (u) \mathrm {d} u.
$$
