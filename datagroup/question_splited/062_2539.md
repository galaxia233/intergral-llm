【2539】取 $n = 10$ ，计算卡塔兰常数. $G = \int_{0}^{1}\frac{\arctan x}{x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $h = \frac{1}{10}$

$$
x _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 1; \quad x _ {1} = 0. 1, \quad y _ {1} = 0. 9 9 6 6 9;
$$

$$
x _ {2} = 0. 2, \quad y _ {2} = 0. 9 8 6 9 8; \quad x _ {3} = 0. 3, \quad y _ {3} = 0. 9 7 1 5 2;
$$

$$
x _ {4} = 0. 4, \quad y _ {4} = 0, 9 5 1 2 7; \quad x _ {5} = 0. 5, \quad y _ {5} = 0. 9 2 7 3 0;
$$

$$
x _ {6} = 0. 6, \quad y _ {6} = 0. 9 0 0 7 0; \quad x _ {7} = 0. 7, \quad y _ {7} = 0. 8 7 2 4 7;
$$

$$
x _ {8} = 0. 8, \quad y _ {8} = 0. 8 4 3 4 3; \quad x _ {9} = 0. 9, \quad y _ {9} = 0. 8 1 4 2 4;
$$

$$
x _ {1 0} = 1, \quad y _ {1 0} = 0. 7 8 5 4 0.
$$

按辛普森公式，得

$$
G \approx \frac {h}{3} \left[ \left(y _ {0} + y _ {1 0}\right) + 4 \left(y _ {1} + y _ {3} + y _ {5} + y _ {7} + y _ {9}\right) + 2 \left(y _ {2} + y _ {4} + y _ {6} + y _ {8}\right) \right]
$$
