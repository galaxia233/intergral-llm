40.40 Write the formula for the cross product $\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B}$ , where $\mathbf{A} = (a_{1}, a_{2}, a_{3})$ and $\mathbf{B} = (b_{1}, b_{2}, b_{3})$ .

$\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B} = (a_{2}b_{3} - a_{3}b_{2}, a_{3}b_{1} - a_{1}b_{3}, a_{1}b_{2} - a_{2}b_{1})$ . In pseudo-determinant form,

$$
\mathbf {A} \times \mathbf {B} = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} \mathbf {i} & \mathbf {j} & \mathbf {k} \\ a _ {1} & a _ {2} & a _ {3} \\ b _ {1} & b _ {2} & b _ {3} \end{array} \right|
$$

If we expand along the first row, we obtain

$$
\left| \begin{array}{l l} a _ {2} & a _ {3} \\ b _ {2} & b _ {3} \end{array} \right| \mathbf {i} - \left| \begin{array}{l l} a _ {1} & a _ {3} \\ b _ {1} & b _ {3} \end{array} \right| \mathbf {j} + \left| \begin{array}{l l} a _ {1} & a _ {2} \\ b _ {1} & b _ {2} \end{array} \right| \mathbf {k} = (a _ {2} b _ {3} - a _ {3} b _ {2}) \mathbf {i} + (a _ {3} b _ {1} - a _ {1} b _ {3}) \mathbf {j} + (a _ {1} b _ {2} - a _ {2} b _ {1}) \mathbf {k}.
$$
