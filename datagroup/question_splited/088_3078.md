【3078】 $\prod_{n = 1}^{\infty}\left(1 - \frac{x}{c + n}\right)\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{n}}$ ，其中 $c > 0$

解 对任意 $x$ ，考虑通项

$$
\begin{array}{l} p _ {n} = \left(1 - \frac {x}{c + n}\right) e ^ {- \frac {x}{n}} = \left(1 - \frac {x}{c + n}\right) \left[ 1 + \frac {x}{n} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right) \right] = 1 - \frac {x}{c + n} + \frac {x}{n} - \frac {x ^ {2}}{n (c + n)} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right) \\ = 1 - \frac {x ^ {2} - c x}{n (c + n)} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right) = 1 + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

易知级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \ln p_n$ 收敛，故原无穷乘积收敛.
