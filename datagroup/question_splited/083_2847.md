【2847】写出函数 $f(x) = x^x$ 按差 $x - 1$ 的非负整数次幂展开式的前三项.

解 $f(x) = x^{x}$ ， $f(1) = 1;$

$$
f ^ {\prime} (x) = x ^ {x} (1 + \ln x), \quad f ^ {\prime} (1) = 1;
$$

$$
f ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = x ^ {x} (1 + \ln x) ^ {2} + x ^ {x - 1}, \quad f ^ {\prime \prime} (1) = 2;
$$

$$
f ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = x ^ {x} (1 + \ln x) ^ {3} + 2 x ^ {x - 1} (1 + \ln x) + x ^ {x - 1} \left(\ln x + \frac {x - 1}{x}\right), \quad f ^ {\prime \prime} (1) = 3.
$$

于是，展开式的前三项为

$$
1 + (x - 1) + (x - 1) ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} (x - 1) ^ {3} + \dots ,
$$

收敛区间为 $|x - 1| < 1$ ，即 $0 < x < 2$ .
