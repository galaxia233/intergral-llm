28.32 Let $\mathcal{R}$ be the region under one arch of the curve $y = \sin x$ , above the $x$ -axis, between $x = 0$ and $x = \pi$ . Find the volume of the solid obtained by revolving $\mathcal{R}$ about the $y$ -axis.

By the cylindrical shell formula and Problem 28.5, $V = 2\pi \int_{0}^{\pi} x \sin x \, dx = 2\pi \{ (\pi + 0) - (0 + 0) \} = 2\pi^{2}$ .
