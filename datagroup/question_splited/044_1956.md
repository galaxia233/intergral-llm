【1956】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 - 3x + 2)\sqrt{x^2 - 4x + 3}}.$

解 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 - 3x + 2)\sqrt{x^2 - 4x + 3}} = \int \left(\frac{2}{x - 2} -\frac{1}{x - 1}\right)\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x^2 - 4x + 3}}$

$$
= \int \frac {2 \mathrm {d} x}{(x - 2) \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}} - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x - 1) \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}} = 2 I _ {1} - I _ {2}.
$$

对于 $I_{1}$ ，设 $x - 2 = \frac{1}{t}$ 可得

$$
I _ {1} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x - 2) \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}} = - \arcsin \left(\frac {1}{| x - 2 |}\right) + C _ {1};
$$

对于 $I_{2}$ ，设 $x - 1 = \frac{1}{t}$ 可得

$$
I _ {2} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x - 1) \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}} = \frac {\sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}}{x - 1} + C _ {2}.
$$

于是，最后得到

$$
\int \frac {x \mathrm {d} x}{(x ^ {2} - 3 x + 2) \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}} = - 2 \arcsin \left(\frac {1}{| x - 2 |}\right) - \frac {\sqrt {x ^ {2} - 4 x + 3}}{x - 1} + C,
$$

其中 $x < 1$ 或 $x > 3$ .
