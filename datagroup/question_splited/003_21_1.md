21.1 Sketch and find the area of the region to the left of the parabola $x = 2y^2$ , to the right of the $y$ -axis, and between $y = 1$ and $y = 3$ .

See Fig. 21-1. The base of the region is the $y$ -axis. The area is given by the integral $\int_{1}^{3} 2y^2 dy = \frac{2}{5} y^3\bigg]_1^3 = \frac{2}{3}(27 - 1) = \frac{52}{3}$ .
