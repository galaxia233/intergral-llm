28.24 $\int x^{2}\ln xdx.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {L e t} \quad u = \ln x, \quad d v = x ^ {2} d x, \quad d u = (1 / x) d x, \quad v = \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3}. \quad \text {T h e n} \quad \int x ^ {2} \ln x d x = \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} \ln x - \frac {1}{3} \int x ^ {2} d x = \\ \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} \ln x - \frac {1}{3} \cdot \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} + C = \frac {1}{9} x ^ {3} (3 \ln x - 1) + C. \end{array}
