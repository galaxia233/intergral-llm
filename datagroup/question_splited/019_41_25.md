41.91 Find the rectangular coordinates of the point with spherical coordinates $(4,2\pi /3,\pi /3)$

$$
x = 4 \sin \frac {2 \pi}{3} \cos \frac {\pi}{3} = 4 \left(\frac {\sqrt {3}}{2}\right) \left(\frac {1}{2}\right) = \sqrt {3} \quad y = 4 \sin \frac {2 \pi}{3} \sin \frac {\pi}{3} = 4 \left(\frac {\sqrt {3}}{2}\right) \left(\frac {\sqrt {3}}{2}\right) = 3 \quad z = 4 \cos \frac {2 \pi}{3} = 4 \left(\frac {1}{2}\right) = 2
$$
