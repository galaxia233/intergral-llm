【2998】 $\sum_{n = 1}\frac{1}{n^2(n + 1)^2(n + 2)^2}.$

解 首先，注意到

$$
\frac {1}{(n + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{(n + 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {2}{n ^ {2}} + \frac {6}{n (n + 1) (n + 2)} = - \frac {3}{(n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {1 2 n + 8}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}}, \tag {1}
$$

$$
2 \left[ \frac {1}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{n ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {2}{(n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} \right] = \frac {1 2 n + 1 0}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}}, \tag {2}
$$

$$
\frac {1}{n ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{(n + 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {2}{n (n + 2)} = \frac {4}{n ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} \tag {3}
$$

将（1）、（2）、（3）三式相加，合并整理可得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{(n + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {3}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{(n + 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {3}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{n ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{n (n + 2)} + \frac {6}{n (n + 1) (n + 2)} - \frac {1}{(n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} + \frac {2}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {2}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

其次，先后利用2961题、2990题、2987题和2997题的结果，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left\{\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{(n + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {3}{2} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{(n + 2) ^ {2}} - \frac {3}{2} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n ^ {2}} - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n (n + 2)} + 6 \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n (n + 1) (n + 2)} \right. \\ - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{(n + 1) ^ {2} (n + 2) ^ {2}} + 2 \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n ^ {2} (n + 1) ^ {2}} \Bigg \} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left\{\left(\frac {\pi^ {2}}{6} - 1\right) + \frac {3}{2} \left(\frac {\pi^ {2}}{6} - 1 - \frac {1}{4}\right) - \frac {3}{2} \cdot \frac {\pi^ {2}}{6} - \frac {1}{2} \left(1 + \frac {1}{2}\right) + 6 \cdot \frac {1}{4} - \left(\frac {\pi^ {2}}{3} - 3 - \frac {1}{4}\right) + 2 \left(\frac {\pi^ {2}}{3} - 3\right) \right\} \\ = \frac {\pi^ {2}}{4} - \frac {3 9}{1 6}. \\ \end{array}
$$
