【4157】求密度 $\rho_0$ 恒定的圆柱体 $\xi^2 +\eta^2\leqslant a^2,0\leqslant \zeta \leqslant h$ 在点 $P(0,0,z)$ 的牛顿引力势.

解 若引用柱坐标，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} u (x, y, z) = \rho_ {0} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {h} d \zeta \int_ {0} ^ {a} \frac {r d r}{\sqrt {r ^ {2} + (\zeta - z) ^ {2}}} = 2 \pi \rho_ {0} \int_ {0} ^ {h} \sqrt {r ^ {2} + (\zeta - z) ^ {2}} \left. \right| _ {0} ^ {a} d \zeta \\ = 2 \pi \rho_ {0} \int_ {0} ^ {h} \left[ \sqrt {a ^ {2} + (\zeta - z) ^ {2}} - | \zeta - z | \right] d \zeta \\ = 2 \pi \rho_ {0} \left[ \frac {\zeta - z}{2} \sqrt {a ^ {2} + (\zeta - z) ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{2} \ln | (\zeta - z) | + \sqrt {a ^ {2} + (\zeta - z) ^ {2}} - \frac {(\zeta - z) | \zeta - z |}{2} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {h} \\ = \pi \rho_ {0} \left\{(h - z) \sqrt {a ^ {2} + (h - z) ^ {2}} + z \sqrt {a ^ {2} + z ^ {2}} - [ (h - z) | h - z | + z | z | ] + a ^ {2} \ln \left| \frac {h - z + \sqrt {a ^ {2} + (h - z) ^ {2}}}{- z + \sqrt {a ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}} \right| \right\}. \\ \end{array}
$$
