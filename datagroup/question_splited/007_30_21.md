30.21 $\int \frac{dx}{1 + e^x}.$

Let $u = 1 + e^{x}$ , $du = e^{x} dx$ . Then $\int \frac{dx}{1 + e^{x}} = \int \frac{du}{u(u - 1)}$ . Let $\frac{1}{u(u - 1)} = \frac{A}{u} + \frac{B}{u - 1}$ . Then $1 = A(u - 1) + Bu$ . Let $u = 0$ . Then $1 = -A$ , $A = -1$ . Let $u = 1$ . Then $1 = B$ . So $\frac{1}{u(u - 1)} = -\frac{1}{u} + \frac{1}{u - 1}$ , and $\int \frac{du}{u(u - 1)} = -\ln |u| + \ln |u - 1| + C = -\ln (1 + e^{x}) + \ln e^{x} + C = -\ln (1 + e^{x}) + x + C$ .
