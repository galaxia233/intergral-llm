【2088】 $\int \sqrt{\frac{e^x - 1}{e^x + 1}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \sqrt{\frac{e^x - 1}{e^x + 1}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{e^x - 1}{\sqrt{e^{2x} - 1}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{e^x\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{e^{2x} - 1}} -\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{e^{2x} - 1}}$

$$
= \int \frac {d \left(e ^ {x}\right)}{\sqrt {\left(e ^ {x}\right) ^ {2} - 1}} + \int \frac {d \left(e ^ {- x}\right)}{\sqrt {1 - \left(e ^ {- x}\right) ^ {2}}} = \ln \left(e ^ {x} + \sqrt {e ^ {2 x} - 1}\right) + \arcsin \left(e ^ {- x}\right) + C.
$$
