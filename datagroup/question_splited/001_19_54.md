19.54 Find $\int \frac{x^3}{\sqrt{x^2 + 25}} dx.$

Let $u = x^2 + 25$ , $du = 2x \, dx$ . Note that $x^2 = u - 25$ . Then $\int_{\frac{1}{2}} \frac{x^3}{\sqrt{x^2 + 25}} \, dx = \int_{\frac{1}{2}} \frac{u - 25}{\sqrt{u}} \cdot \frac{1}{2} \, du = \frac{1}{2} \int (u^{1/2} - 25u^{-1/2}) \, du = \frac{1}{2} (\frac{2}{3} u^{3/2} - 50u^{1/2}) + C = \frac {1}{3} u ^ {1 / 2}(u - 75) + C = \frac {1}{3} \sqrt{x^2 + 25}(x^2 - 50) + C.$
