26.40 What is the present value of a sum of money which if invested at 5 percent interest, continuously compounded, will become $1000 in 10 years?

Let y be the value of the money at time t, and let y0 be its present value. Then, by Problem 26.9, y = y0e0.05t. In 10 years, 1000 = y0e0.05(10) = y0e0.5. So y0 = 1000/e0.5 ≈ 1000/1.64872 ≈ 606.53. Hence, the present value is about $606.53.
