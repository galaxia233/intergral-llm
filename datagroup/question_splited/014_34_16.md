34.18 Find $dy / dx$ and $d^2 y / dx^2$ for $x = t^3 +t,\quad y = t^7 +t + 1.$

$\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy/dt}{dx/dt} = \frac{7t^6 + 1}{3t^2 + 1}$ . Further,

$$
\frac {d ^ {2} y}{d x ^ {2}} = \frac {d}{d x} \left(\frac {d y}{d x}\right) = \frac {d}{d t} \left(\frac {d y}{d x}\right) / \frac {d x}{d t}
$$

But, $\frac{d}{dt}\left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right) = \frac{(3t^2 + 1)(42t^5) - (7t^6 + 1)6t}{(3t^2 + 1)^2} = \frac{t(84t^6 + 42t^4 - 6)}{(3t^2 + 1)^2} = \frac{6t(14t^6 + 7t^4 - 1)}{(3t^2 + 1)^2}$

Hence $\frac{d^2y}{dx^2} = \frac{6t(14t^6 + 7t^4 - 1)}{(3t^2 + 1)^3}$ .
