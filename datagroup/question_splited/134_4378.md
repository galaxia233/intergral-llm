【4378】 $\iint_{S} \frac{x \cos \alpha + y \cos \beta + z \cos \gamma}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}} \mathrm{d} S.$

解 由于 $P = \frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}}$ ， $Q = \frac{y}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}}$ ， $R = \frac{z}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}}$

从而 $\frac{\partial P}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z} = \frac{2}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}}.$ 于是，

$$
\iint_ {S} \frac {x \cos \alpha + y \cos \beta + z \cos \gamma}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}} d S = 2 \iiint_ {V} \frac {d x d y d z}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}}.
$$
