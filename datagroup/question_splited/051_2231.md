【2231】 求： $\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x,\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}a}\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x,\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}b}\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = 0,\quad \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}a}\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}a}\int_{b}^{a}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = -\sin a^{2},\quad \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}b}\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \sin b^{2}.$
