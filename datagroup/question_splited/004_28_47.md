28.47 Prove the reduction formula $\int \frac{x^2 dx}{(a^2 + x^2)^n} = \frac{-x}{(2n - 2)(a^2 + x^2)^{n - 1}} + \frac{1}{2n - 2}\int \frac{dx}{(a^2 + x^2)^{n - 1}}.$

$$
\text {L e t} u = x, d v = \frac {x d x}{\left(a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}\right) ^ {n}}, d u = d x, v = \frac {- 1}{(2 n - 2) \left(a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}\right) ^ {n - 1}}. \tag {I}
$$
