42.32 The plane $y = 3$ intersects the surface $z = 2x^2 + y^2$ in a curve. Find equations of the tangent line to this curve at the point (2,3,17).

The slope of the tangent line is the derivative $\partial z / \partial x = 4x = 8$ . Hence, a pair of equations for the tangent line is $(z - 17) / (x - 2) = 8$ , $y = 3$ , or, equivalently, $z = 8x + 1$ , $y = 3$ .
