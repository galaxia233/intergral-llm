【2247】 $\int_0^{0.75}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x + 1)\sqrt{x^2 + 1}}.$

提示 令 $\frac{1}{x + 1} = t$

解设 $t = \frac{1}{x + 1}$ ，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {0. 7 5} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x + 1) \sqrt {x ^ {2} + 1}} = \int_ {\frac {4}{7}} ^ {1} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {2 t ^ {2} - 2 t + 1}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \ln (2 t - 1 + \sqrt {4 t ^ {2} - 4 t + 2}) \int_ {\frac {4}{7}} ^ {1} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {1 + \sqrt {2}}{\frac {1}{7} + \sqrt {\frac {5 0}{4 9}}} \\ = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {7 + 7 \sqrt {2}}{1 + 5 \sqrt {2}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {9 + 4 \sqrt {2}}{7}. \\ \end{array}
$$
