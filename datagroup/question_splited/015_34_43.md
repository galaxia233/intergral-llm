34.85 Show that when arc length $s$ is chosen as the curve parameter, the principal unit normal vector has the simple expression $\mathbf{N}(s) = \mathbf{R}''(s) / |\mathbf{R}''(s)|$ .

By Problem 34.47, $\mathbf{T}(s) = \mathbf{R}'(s)$ , and so $\mathbf{T}'(s) = \mathbf{R}''(s)$ and $\mathbf{N}(s) = \mathbf{T}'(s) / |\mathbf{T}'(s)| = \mathbf{R}''(s) / |\mathbf{R}''(s)|$ [ex-cept where $\mathbf{R}''(s) = \mathbf{0}$ ].
