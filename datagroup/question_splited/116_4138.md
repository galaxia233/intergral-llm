【4138】 $x^{2} + y^{2} = 2z, x + y = z.$

解由 $x^{2} + y^{2} = 2z$ ， $x + y = z$ 所围成的区域在平面 $z = 0$ 上的投影为圆 $(x - 1)^{2} + (y - 1)^{2} = 2.$

若引用代换 $x = 1 + r\cos \theta ,y = 1 + r\sin \theta ,$

则质量为 $M = \int_{0}^{2\pi}\mathrm{d}\theta \int_{0}^{\sqrt{2}}r\mathrm{d}r\int_{1 - r(\cos \theta +\sin \theta) + \frac{r^2}{2}}^{2 - r(\cos \theta +\sin \theta)}\mathrm{d}z = 2\pi \int_{0}^{\sqrt{2}}(1 - \frac{r^2}{2})r\mathrm{d}r = \pi .$

于是，

$$
x _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r d r \int_ {1 \cdot r (\cos \theta - \sin \theta) - \frac {r ^ {2}}{2}} ^ {2 \cdot r (\cos \theta - \sin \theta)} (1 + r \cos \theta) d z = \frac {1}{M} \left[ \pi + \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \cos \theta d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r ^ {2} \left(1 - \frac {r ^ {2}}{2}\right) d r \right] = \frac {\pi}{M} = 1.
$$

同理可得 $y_0 = 1$ ，而

$$
\begin{array}{l} z _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r d r \int_ {1 + r (\cos \theta + \sin \theta) \cdot \frac {r ^ {2}}{2}} ^ {2 - r (\cos \theta + \sin \theta)} z d z = \frac {1}{2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} \left[ 3 + (\sin \theta + \cos \theta) (2 r - r ^ {3}) - \frac {1}{4} r ^ {4} - r ^ {2} \right] r d r \\ = \frac {1}{2 \pi} \cdot \frac {1 0 \pi}{3} = \frac {5}{3}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，质心的坐标为 $x_0 = y_0 = 1, z_0 = \frac{5}{3}$
