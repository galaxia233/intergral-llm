【2324】 $\int_{0}^{1}\frac{x^9}{\sqrt{1 + x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 由于 $\frac{x^9}{\sqrt{2}} \leqslant \frac{x^9}{\sqrt{1 + x}} \leqslant x^9$ $(0 \leqslant x \leqslant 1)$ ，从而，

$$
\frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {1} x ^ {9} d x \leqslant \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {x ^ {9}}{\sqrt {1 + x}} d x \leqslant \int_ {0} ^ {1} x ^ {9} d x,
$$

即 $\frac{1}{10\sqrt{2}} \leqslant \int_{0}^{1} \frac{x^9}{\sqrt{1 + x}} \mathrm{d}x \leqslant \frac{1}{10}$ .
