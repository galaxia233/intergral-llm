【2260】引入新变量 $t = x + \frac{1}{x}$ ，计算积分

$$
\int_ {\frac {1}{2}} ^ {2} \left(1 + x - \frac {1}{x}\right) e ^ {x + \frac {1}{x}} d x.
$$

解设 $t = x + \frac{1}{x}$ ，则 $t^2 - 4 = \left(x - \frac{1}{x}\right)^2, x = \frac{1}{2} (t \pm \sqrt{t^2 - 4}).$

于是， $\int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{2}\left(1 + x - \frac{1}{x}\right)\mathrm{e}^{x + \frac{1}{x}}\mathrm{d}x = \int_{1}^{2}\left(1 + x - \frac{1}{x}\right)\mathrm{e}^{x + \frac{1}{x}}\mathrm{d}x + \int_{\frac{1}{2}}^{1}\left(1 + x - \frac{1}{x}\right)\mathrm{e}^{x + \frac{1}{x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int_ {2} ^ {\frac {5}{2}} (1 + \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) e ^ {t} d \left[ \frac {1}{2} (t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) \right] + \int_ {\frac {5}{2}} ^ {2} (1 - \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) e ^ {t} d \left[ \frac {1}{2} (t - \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {2} ^ {\frac {5}{2}} (1 + \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) e ^ {t} \left(1 + \frac {t}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}}\right) d t - \frac {1}{2} \int_ {2} ^ {\frac {5}{2}} (1 - \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) e ^ {t} \left(1 - \frac {t}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}}\right) d t \\ = \int_ {2} ^ {\frac {5}{2}} e ^ {t} \left[ \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4} + \frac {t}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}} \right] d t = \int_ {2} ^ {\frac {5}{2}} \left[ \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4} d (e ^ {t}) + e ^ {t} d \sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4} \right] \\ = (\sqrt {t ^ {2} - 4}) e ^ {t} \left| _ {2} ^ {\frac {5}{2}} = \frac {3}{2} e ^ {\frac {5}{2}}. \right. \\ \end{array}
$$
