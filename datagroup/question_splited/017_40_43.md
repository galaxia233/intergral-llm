40.43 Find the area of $\triangle PQR$ of Problem 40.42.

Recall that $|\overrightarrow{PQ} \times \overrightarrow{PR}| = |\overrightarrow{PQ}| |\overrightarrow{PR}| \sin \theta$ is the area of the parallelogram formed by $\overrightarrow{PQ}$ and $\overrightarrow{PR}$ . So, the area is $|N| = |(8, 4, 4)| = \sqrt{64 + 16 + 16} = \sqrt{96} = 4\sqrt{6}$ . The area of $\triangle PQR$ is half the area of the parallelogram, that is, $2\sqrt{6}$ .
