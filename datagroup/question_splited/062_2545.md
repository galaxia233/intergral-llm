【2545】取 $\Delta x = \frac{\pi}{3}$ ，描点作出函数 $y = \int_{0}^{x} \frac{\sin t}{t} \, \mathrm{d}t$ （ $0 \leqslant x \leqslant 2\pi$ ）的图像.

解 取 $n = 2k = 6$ 计算函数 $y = \int_{0}^{x}\frac{\sin t}{t}\mathrm{d}t$ 的值. 先计算 $y = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{3}}\frac{\sin t}{t}\mathrm{d}t.$ 由于 $h = \frac{\pi}{18}$ , 且

$$
t _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 1; \quad t _ {1} = \frac {\pi}{1 8}, \quad 4 y _ {1} = 3. 9 8 0; \quad t _ {2} = \frac {\pi}{9}, \quad 2 y _ {2} = 1. 9 6 0;
$$

$$
t _ {3} = \frac {\pi}{6}, \quad 4 y _ {3} = 3. 8 2 0; \quad t _ {4} = \frac {2 \pi}{9}, \quad 2 y _ {4} = 1. 8 4 1; \quad t _ {5} = \frac {5 \pi}{1 8}, \quad 4 y _ {5} = 3. 5 1 1;
$$

$$
t _ {6} = \frac {\pi}{3}, \quad y _ {6} = 0. 8 2 7.
$$

按辛普森公式，得

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{3}} \frac {\sin t}{t} d t \approx \frac {\pi}{5 4} (1 + 0. 8 2 7 + 3. 9 8 0 + 3. 8 2 0 + 3. 5 1 1 + 1. 9 6 0 + 1. 8 4 1) \approx 0. 9 9.
$$

再计算 $y = \int_{0}^{\frac{2\pi}{3}}\frac{\sin t}{t}\mathrm{d}t.$ 由于 $h = \frac{\pi}{9}$ 且

$$
t _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 1; \quad t _ {1} = \frac {\pi}{9}, \quad 4 y _ {1} = 3. 9 1 9; \quad t _ {2} = \frac {2 \pi}{9}, \quad 2 y _ {2} = 1. 8 4 1;
$$

$$
t _ {3} = \frac {\pi}{3}, \quad 4 y _ {3} = 3. 3 0 8; \quad t _ {4} = \frac {4 \pi}{9}, \quad 2 y _ {4} = 1. 4 1 1; \quad t _ {5} = \frac {5 \pi}{9}, \quad 4 y _ {5} = 2. 2 5 7;
$$

$$
t _ {6} = \frac {2 \pi}{3}, \quad y _ {6} = 0. 4 1 3.
$$

所以，

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {2 \pi}{3}} \frac {\sin t}{t} d t \approx \frac {\pi}{2 7} (1 + 0. 4 1 3 + 3. 9 1 9 + 3. 3 0 8 + 2. 2 5 7 + 1. 8 4 1 + 1. 4 1 1) \approx 1. 6 5.
$$

选取适当的 $n$ ，类似地可求得

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin t}{t} d t \approx 1. 8 5, \quad \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {4 \pi}{3}} \frac {\sin t}{t} d t \approx 1. 7 2, \quad \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {5 \pi}{3}} \frac {\sin t}{t} d t \approx 1. 5 2, \quad \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {\sin t}{t} d t \approx 1. 4 2.
$$
