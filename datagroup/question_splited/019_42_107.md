42.107 Find an equation of the tangent plane to $z = xy$ at $(2, \frac{1}{2}, 1)$ .

$\partial z / \partial x = y = \frac{1}{2}, \quad \partial z / \partial y = x = 2.$ Thus, a normal vector to the tangent plane is $(\frac{1}{2}, 2, -1)$ , and an equation of that plane is $\frac{1}{2}(x - 2) + 2(y - \frac{1}{2}) - (z - 1) = 0$ , or, equivalently, $x + 4y - 2z = 2$ .
