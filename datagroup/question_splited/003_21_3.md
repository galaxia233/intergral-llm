21.3 Sketch and find the area of the region between the curve $y = x^3$ and the lines $y = -x$ and $y = 1$ .

See Fig. 21-3. The lower boundary of the region is $y = -x$ and the upper boundary is $y = x^3$ . Hence, the area is given by the integral $\int_0^1 [y^{1/3} - (-y)] dy = \left( \frac{3}{4} y^{4/3} + \frac{1}{2} y^2 \right) \bigg]_0^1 = \frac{3}{4} + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{5}{4}$ .
