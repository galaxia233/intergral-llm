27.41 $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{25 - x^2}}.$   
Let $x = 5u$ , $dx = 5du$ . Then $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{25 - x^2}} = \int \frac{5du}{\sqrt{25 - 25u^2}} = \int \frac{du}{\sqrt{1 - u^2}} = \sin^{-1}u + C = \sin^{-1}\frac{x}{5} + C$ . This is a special case of the formula $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}} = \sin^{-1}\frac{x}{a} + C$ for $a > 0$ .
