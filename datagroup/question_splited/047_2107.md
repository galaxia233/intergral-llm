【2107】 $\int x\arcsin (1 - x)\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x\arcsin (1 - x)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \arcsin (1 - x)\mathrm{d}(x^2)$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} \arcsin (1 - x) + \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {x ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 - (1 - x) ^ {2}}} d x.
$$

对于积分 $\int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{1 - (1 - x)^2}}\mathrm{d}x$ ，设 $1 - x = t$ ，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 - (1 - x) ^ {2}}} d x = - \int \frac {1 - 2 t + t ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}}} d t = \int \frac {- t ^ {2} + 1}{\sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}}} d t - 2 \int \frac {d t}{\sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}}} + 2 \int \frac {t d t}{\sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}}} \\ = \int \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} d t - 2 \arcsin t - 2 \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} = \frac {t}{2} \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} \arcsin t - 2 \arcsin t - 2 \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} + C _ {1} \\ = \frac {- 3 - x}{2} \sqrt {2 x - x ^ {2}} - \frac {3}{2} \arcsin (1 - x) + C _ {1}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\int x\arcsin (1 - x)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{2x^2 - 3}{4}\arcsin (1 - x) - \frac{3 + x}{4}\sqrt{2x - x^2} +C.$
