【1767】 $\int x^{3}(1 - 5x^{2})^{10}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $1 - 5x^{2} = t$ ，则 $x^{2} = \frac{1}{5} (1 - t)$ ，从而，

$$
x ^ {3} \mathrm {d} x = \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} (x ^ {2}) = \frac {1}{1 0} (1 - t) \left(- \frac {1}{5}\right) \mathrm {d} t = - \frac {1}{5 0} (1 - t) \mathrm {d} t,
$$

故得 $\int x^{3}(1 - 5x^{2})^{10}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{50}\int (t^{10} - t^{11})\mathrm{d}t = -\frac{1}{550} t^{11} + \frac{1}{600} t^{12} + C = -\frac{1 + 55x^{2}}{6600} (1 - 5x^{2})^{11} + C.$
