19.8 Find $\int (x^2 - 1)\sqrt{x} dx$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int (x ^ {2} - 1) \sqrt {x} d x = \int (x ^ {2} - 1) x ^ {1 / 2} d x = \int \left(x ^ {5 / 2} - x ^ {1 / 2}\right) d x = \frac {2}{7} x ^ {7 / 2} - \frac {2}{3} x ^ {3 / 2} + C = \frac {2}{7} (\sqrt {x}) ^ {7} - \frac {2}{3} (\sqrt {x}) ^ {3} + C \\ = 2 (\sqrt {x}) ^ {3} \left(\frac {1}{7} x ^ {2} - \frac {1}{3}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
