42.88 Find $du / dt$ given that $u = x^{2}y^{3},\quad x = 2t^{3},\quad y = 3t^{2}.$

$$
\frac {d u}{d t} = u _ {x} \frac {d x}{d t} + u _ {y} \frac {d y}{d t} = 2 x y ^ {3} (6 t ^ {2}) + 3 x ^ {2} y ^ {2} (6 t) = 6 x y ^ {2} t (2 y t + 3 x) = 6 (2 t ^ {3}) (9 t ^ {4}) (t) (6 t ^ {3} + 6 t ^ {3}) = 1 2 9 6 t ^ {1 1}.
$$
