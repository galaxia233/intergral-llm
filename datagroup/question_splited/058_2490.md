【2490】 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1$ （ $0 <   b\leqslant a)$ ：（1）绕 $Ox$ 轴； (2）绕 $Oy$ 轴.

解（1） $y^{2} = b^{2} - \frac{b^{2}}{a^{2}} x^{2},\qquad y y^{\prime} = -\frac{b^{2}}{a^{2}} x,$

$$
y \sqrt {1 + y ^ {\prime 2}} = \sqrt {y ^ {2} + (y y ^ {\prime}) ^ {2}} = \frac {b}{a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - \frac {a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} x ^ {2}} = \frac {b}{a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - \varepsilon^ {2} x ^ {2}}.
$$

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P _ {x} = 2 \pi \frac {b}{a} \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - \varepsilon^ {2} x ^ {2}} d x = \frac {2 \pi b}{a} \left(a \sqrt {a ^ {2} - \varepsilon ^ {2} a ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{\varepsilon} \arcsin \varepsilon\right) = 2 \pi b \left(b + \frac {a}{\varepsilon} \arcsin \varepsilon\right),
$$

其中 $\varepsilon = \frac{\sqrt{a^2 - b^2}}{a}$ 是椭圆的离心率.
