【2494】 $\pm x = a\ln{\frac{a + \sqrt{a^2 - y^2}}{y}} - \sqrt{a^2 - y^2}$ 绕 $Ox$ 轴.

解 $x_{y}^{\prime} = \mp \frac{a + \sqrt{a^{2} - y^{2}}}{y},\quad \sqrt{1 + x_{y}^{\prime 2}} = \frac{a}{y}\quad (0\leqslant y\leqslant a).$

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P _ {x} = 2 \cdot 2 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {a} y \frac {a}{y} d y = 4 \pi a ^ {2}.
$$
