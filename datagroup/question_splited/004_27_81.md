27.81 Find the area under the curve $y = 1 / (x\sqrt{x^2 - 1})$ and above the segment $[\sqrt{2}, 2]$ on the $x$ -axis.

$$
A = \int_ {\sqrt {2}} ^ {2} \frac {1}{x \sqrt {x ^ {2} - 1}} d x = \sec^ {- 1} x ] _ {\sqrt {2}} ^ {2} = \sec^ {- 1} 2 - \sec^ {- 1} \sqrt {2} = \frac {\pi}{3} - \frac {\pi}{4} = \frac {\pi}{1 2}.
$$
