33.3 Given $\mathbf{A} = 3\mathbf{i} + 4\mathbf{j}$ and $\mathbf{C} = 2\mathbf{i} - \mathbf{j}$ , find the magnitude and direction of $\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{C}$ .

$\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{C} = 5\mathbf{i} + 3\mathbf{j}$ . Therefore, $|\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{C}| = \sqrt{(5)^2 + (3)^2} = \sqrt{34}$ . If $\theta$ is the angle made by $\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{C}$ with the positive $x$ -axis, $\tan \theta = \frac{3}{5}$ . From a table of tangents, $\theta \approx 30^{\circ} 58'$ .
