34.73 Derive the following quotient rule: $\frac{d}{dt}\left[\frac{\mathbf{G}(u)}{f(u)}\right] = \frac{f(u)\mathbf{G}'(u) - f'(u)\mathbf{G}(u)}{\left[f(u)\right]^2}.$

By the product rule, $\frac{d}{dt}\left[\frac{\mathbf{G}(u)}{f(u)}\right] = \frac{d}{dt}\left[\frac{1}{f(u)}\mathbf{G}(u)\right] = \frac{1}{f(u)}\mathbf{G}'(u) - \frac{f'(u)}{[f(u)]^2}\mathbf{G}(u) = \frac{f(u)\mathbf{G}'(u) - f'(u)\mathbf{G}(u)}{[f(u)]^2}.$
