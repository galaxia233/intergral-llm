【4301】 $\oint_{x^2 + y^2 = R^2} e^{-(x^2 + y^2)} (\cos 2xy \, dx + \sin 2xy \, dy)$ .

解 由于 $\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} = \mathrm{e}^{-(x^2 - y^2)}[(-2x\sin 2xy + 2y\cos 2xy) - (2y\cos 2xy - 2x\sin 2xy)] = 0,$

故有 $\oint_{x^2 + y^2 = R^2} \mathrm{e}^{-(\tau^2 + y^2)} (\cos 2xy \, \mathrm{d}x + \sin 2xy \, \mathrm{d}y) = \iint_{x^2 + y^2 \leqslant R^2} 0 \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y = 0.$
