41.22 Find the volume of the ellipsoid $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2} = 1.$

The plane $z = k$ cuts the ellipsoid in an ellipse

$$
\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} = 1 - \frac {k ^ {2}}{c ^ {2}} = \frac {c ^ {2} - k ^ {2}}{c ^ {2}} \quad \frac {x ^ {2}}{\left(a \sqrt {c ^ {2} - k ^ {2}} / c\right) ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{\left(b \sqrt {c ^ {2} - k ^ {2}} / c\right) ^ {2}} = 1
$$

By Problem 20.72, the area of this ellipse is $\pi (a\sqrt{c^2 - k^2 / c})(b\sqrt{c^2 - k^2 / c}) = \pi ab(c^2 -k^2) / c^2$ . Hence, by the cross-section formula for volume,

$$
V = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {c} \frac {\pi a b}{c ^ {2}} \left(c ^ {2} - k ^ {2}\right) d k = \frac {2 \pi a b}{c ^ {2}} \left(c ^ {2} k - \frac {1}{3} k ^ {3}\right) \Bigg ] _ {0} ^ {c} = \frac {2 \pi a b}{c ^ {2}} \left(c ^ {3} - \frac {1}{3} c ^ {3}\right) = \frac {4}{3} \pi a b c
$$
