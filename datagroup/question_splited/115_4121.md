【4121】 $(x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2})^{3} = \frac{a^{6}z^{2}}{x^{2} + y^{2}}.$

解采用球坐标 $x = r\cos \varphi \cos \psi$ ， $y = r\sin \varphi \cos \psi$ ， $z = r\sin \psi$ ，则区域 $V$ 的 $\frac{1}{8}$ 部分（第一卦限内）为

$$
0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant r \leqslant a \tan^ {\frac {1}{3}} \psi .
$$

于是，体积为 $V = 8\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\mathrm{d}\varphi \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\mathrm{d}\psi \int_{0}^{a\tan \frac{1}{3}\psi}r^{2}\cos \psi \mathrm{d}r = \frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin \psi \mathrm{d}\psi = \frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3}.$
