【2861】 $\frac{1}{1 - x - x^2}$

解 $\frac{1}{1 - x - x^2} = \frac{1}{\frac{5}{4} - \left(x + \frac{1}{2}\right)^2} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{5}}\left[\frac{1}{\frac{\sqrt{5}}{2} - \left(x + \frac{1}{2}\right)} + \frac{1}{\frac{\sqrt{5}}{2} + \left(x + \frac{1}{2}\right)}\right]$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {5}} \left[ \frac {2}{\sqrt {5} - 1} \left(1 - \frac {2}{\sqrt {5} - 1} x\right) ^ {- 1} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {5} + 1} \left(1 + \frac {2}{\sqrt {5} + 1} x\right) ^ {- 1} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{\sqrt {5}} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \left[ \left(\frac {2}{\sqrt {5} - 1}\right) ^ {n + 1} + (- 1) ^ {n} \left(\frac {2}{\sqrt {5} + 1}\right) ^ {n + 1} \right] x ^ {n} \\ = \frac {1}{\sqrt {5}} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \left[ \left(\frac {\sqrt {5} + 1}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} + (- 1) ^ {n} \left(\frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} \right] x ^ {n} \quad (| x | <   \frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2}). \\ \end{array}
$$
