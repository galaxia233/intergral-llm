39.37 Find the Taylor series for $f(x) = 2x^{2} + 4x - 3$ about 1.

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {I I M e t h o d 1 .} f ^ {\prime} (x) = 4 x + 4, \quad f ^ {\prime \prime} (x) = 4, \quad \text {a n d} \quad f ^ {(n)} (x) = 0 \quad \text {f o r} \quad n > 2. \quad \text {T h u s}, \quad f (1) = 3, \quad f ^ {\prime} (1) = 8, \\ f ^ {\prime \prime} (1) = 4, \quad \text {a n d} \quad f (x) = 3 + 8 (x - 1) + 2 (x - 1) ^ {2}. \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {M e t h o d 2 .} f (x) = 2 [ (x - 1) + 1 ] ^ {2} + 4 [ (x - 1) + 1 ] - 3 = 2 (x - 1) ^ {2} + 4 (x - 1) + 2 + 4 (x - 1) + 4 - 3 = 3 + 8 (x - 1) + 2 (x - 1) ^ {2}. \end{array}
$$
