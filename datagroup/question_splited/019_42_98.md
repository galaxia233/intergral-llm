42.98 Assume $\partial f / \partial x = 0$ for all $(x,y)$ . Show that $f(x,y) = h(y)$ for some function $h$ .

For each $y_0$ , $\frac{d}{dx} f(x, y_0) = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x}(x, y_0) = 0$ . Hence, $f(x, y_0)$ is a constant, $c$ . Let $h(y_0) = c$ . Then $f(x, y) = h(y)$ for all $x$ and $y$ .
