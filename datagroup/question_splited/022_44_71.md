44.70 Describe the solid whose volume is given by the integral $\int_0^5\int_0^{\sqrt{25 - x^2}}\int_0^3 dzdydx,$ and compute the volume.   
The solid is the part of the solid right circular cylinder $x^{2} + y^{2} \leq 25$ lying in the first octant between $z = 0$ and $z = 3$ (see Fig. 44-39). Its volume is $\frac{1}{4} (\pi r^2 h) = \frac{1}{4} [\pi (5)^2 \cdot 3] = \frac{75}{4} \pi$ .
