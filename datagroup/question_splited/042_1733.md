【1733】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)(x + 3)}.$

提示 注意 $1 = \frac{1}{4} [(x + 3) - (x - 1)]$ . 1734 题，1735 题及 1736 题均可仿本题的解法.

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)(x + 3)} = \frac{1}{4}\int \left(\frac{1}{x - 1} -\frac{1}{x + 3}\right)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4}\ln \left|\frac{x - 1}{x + 3}\right| + C.$
