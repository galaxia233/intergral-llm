【4018】 $z = \mathrm{e}^{-(x^2 -y^2)}$ ， $z = 0$ ， $x^{2} + y^{2} = R^{2}$

解利用对称性，得所求的体积为

$$
V = 4 \iint_ {\substack {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2} \\ x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0}} \mathrm {e} ^ {- (x ^ {2} - y ^ {2})} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \mathrm {d} \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {R} \mathrm {e} ^ {- r ^ {2}} r \mathrm {d} r = \pi (1 - \mathrm {e} ^ {R ^ {2}}).
$$
