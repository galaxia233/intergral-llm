【4117】 $\left(\frac{x}{a} +\frac{y}{b} +\frac{z}{c}\right)^4 = \frac{xyz}{abc}$ （ $x > 0,y > 0,z > 0)$

提示 仿 4116 题的解法.

解令 $x = \arccos^2\varphi \cos^2\psi ,y = br\sin^2\varphi \cos^2\psi ,z = cr\sin^2\psi$ ，则有 $|I| = 4abcr^{2}\cos \varphi \sin \varphi \cos^{3}\psi \sin \psi$ ，且区域 V 为

$$
0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, 0 \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, 0 \leqslant r \leqslant \cos^ {2} \varphi \sin^ {2} \varphi \cos^ {4} \psi \sin^ {2} \psi
$$

于是，体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 4 a b c \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {\cos^ {2} \varphi \sin^ {2} \varphi \cos^ {4} \psi \sin^ {2} \psi} r ^ {2} \cos \varphi \sin \varphi \cos^ {3} \psi \sin \psi d r = \frac {4}{3} a b c \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {7} \varphi \sin^ {7} \varphi d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {1 5} \psi \sin^ {7} \psi d \psi \\ = \frac {4}{3} a b c \cdot \frac {1}{2} \frac {\Gamma (4) \Gamma (4)}{\Gamma (8)} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \frac {\Gamma (8) \Gamma (4) ^ {*}}{\Gamma (1 2)} = \frac {1}{3} a b c \frac {3 ! 3 !}{7 !} \cdot \frac {7 ! 3 !}{1 1 !} = \frac {a b c}{5 5 4 4 0 0}. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用 3856 题的结果.
