【4216】 证明狄利克雷公式：

$$
\iint_ {x _ {1} \cdot x _ {2} \dots \dots c _ {n} \geq 0} ^ {\dots} x _ {1} ^ {p _ {1}} ^ {- 1} x _ {2} ^ {p _ {2}} ^ {- 1} \dots x _ {n} ^ {p _ {n}} ^ {- 1} d x _ {1} d x _ {2} \dots d x _ {n} = \frac {\Gamma (p _ {1}) \Gamma (p _ {2}) \cdots \Gamma (p _ {n})}{\Gamma (p _ {1} + p _ {2} + \cdots + p _ {n} + 1)} \quad (p _ {1}, p _ {2}, \dots , p _ {n} > 0).
$$

提示 用数学归纳法证明.

证 我们应用数学归纳法证明之.

当 $n = 1$ 时，公式显然成立，即 $\int_{0\leqslant x_1\leqslant 1}x_1^{p_1}\mathrm{d}x_1 = \frac{1}{p_1} = \frac{\Gamma(p_1)}{\Gamma(p_1 + 1)}.$

其次，设公式对 $n - 1$ 成立，今证公式对 $\pmb{n}$ 也成立.为此，将公式左端写为

$$
\int_ {1} ^ {1} x _ {n} ^ {p _ {n}} ^ {- 1} \mathrm {d} x _ {n} \iint_ {\substack {x _ {1}, x _ {2}, \dots , x _ {n} \geqslant 0 \\ x _ {1} + x _ {2} + \dots + x _ {n - 1} \leqslant 1 - r _ {n}}} x _ {1} ^ {p _ {1}} ^ {- 1} x _ {2} ^ {p _ {2}} ^ {- 1} \dots x _ {n} ^ {p _ {n}} ^ {- 1} ^ {- 1} \mathrm {d} x _ {1} \mathrm {d} x _ {2} \dots \mathrm {d} x _ {n - 1}.
$$

在里面的 $n - 1$ 重积分中作代换

$$
x _ {1} = \left(1 - x _ {n}\right) \xi_ {1}, x _ {2} = \left(1 - x _ {n}\right) \xi_ {2}, \dots , x _ {n - 1} = \left(1 - x _ {n}\right) \xi_ {n - 1},
$$

即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\Gamma \left(p _ {1}\right) \Gamma \left(p _ {2}\right) \cdots \Gamma \left(p _ {n - 1}\right)}{\Gamma \left(p _ {1} + p _ {2} + \cdots + p _ {n - 1} + 1\right)} \int_ {0} ^ {1} x _ {n} ^ {p _ {n} - 1} \left(1 - x _ {n}\right) x ^ {p _ {1} + p _ {2} + \dots + p _ {n - 1}} d x _ {n} \\ = \frac {\Gamma (p _ {1}) \cdots \Gamma (p _ {n - 1})}{\Gamma (p _ {1} + \cdots + p _ {n - 1} + 1)} B (p _ {n}, p _ {1} + \dots + p _ {n - 1} + 1) \\ = \frac {\Gamma \left(p _ {1}\right) \cdots \Gamma \left(p _ {n - 1}\right)}{\Gamma \left(p _ {1} + \cdots + p _ {n - 1} + 1\right)} \cdot \frac {\Gamma \left(p _ {n}\right) \Gamma \left(p _ {1} + \dots + p _ {n - 1} + 1\right)}{\Gamma \left(p _ {1} + \dots + p _ {n} + 1\right)} \\ = \frac {\Gamma \left(p _ {1}\right) \Gamma \left(p _ {2}\right) \cdots \Gamma \left(p _ {n}\right)}{\Gamma \left(p _ {1} + p _ {2} + \cdots + p _ {n} + 1\right)}. \\ \end{array}
$$

这样一来，我们得知公式对 $n$ 重积分也正确。从而，对 $n$ 为任意的正整数时，狄利克雷公式均成立
