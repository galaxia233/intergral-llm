19.28 Find $\int (x^4 + 1)^{1/3} x^7 dx$ .

Let $u = x^4 + 1$ , $du = 4x^3 dx$ . Note that $x^7 dx = x^4 \cdot x^3 dx = (u - 1) \cdot \frac{1}{4} du$ . Hence, $\int (x^4 + 1)^{1/3} x^7 dx = \int u^{1/3}(u - 1) \cdot \frac{1}{4} du = \frac{1}{4} \int (u^{4/3} - u^{1/3}) du = \frac{1}{4} (\frac{3}{7} u^{7/3} - \frac{3}{4} u^{4/3}) + C = \frac {3}{4} u ^ {4 / 3} \left(\frac {1}{7} u - \frac {1}{4}\right) = \frac {3}{4} (x^4 + 1)^ {4 / 3} [\frac {1}{7} (x^4 + 1) - \frac {1}{4}] + C = \frac {3}{112} (x^4 + 1)^ {4 / 3} (4 x ^ {4} - 3) + C.$
