33.33 Find the cosine of the angle between $\mathbf{A} = (1,2)$ and $\mathbf{B} = (3, - 4)$

$\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} = |\mathbf{A}| |\mathbf{B}| \cos \theta$ . So, $(1,2) \cdot (3, -4) = \sqrt{5} \sqrt{25} \cos \theta$ , $3 - 8 = 5 \sqrt{5} \cos \theta$ , $-1 = \sqrt{5} \cos \theta$ , $\cos \theta = -1 / \sqrt{5} = -\sqrt{5} / 5$ . Since $\cos \theta < 0$ , $\theta$ is an obtuse angle.
