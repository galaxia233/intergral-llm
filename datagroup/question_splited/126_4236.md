【4236】 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2},\sqrt{x^{2} + y^{2}}\mathrm{ch}\left(\arctan \frac{y}{x}\right) = a$ ，从点 $(a,0,0)$ 到点 $B(x,y,z)$

解令 $x = \sqrt{a^2 - z^2}\cos \varphi ,y = \sqrt{a^2 - z^2}\sin \varphi$ ，不妨设 $z > 0$ ，则有

$$
z = \sqrt {a ^ {2} - (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} = \sqrt {a ^ {2} \left(1 - \frac {1}{\operatorname {c h} ^ {2} \varphi}\right)} = a \operatorname {t h} \varphi .
$$

而 $\sqrt{a^2 - z^2} = \sqrt{a^2(1 - \mathrm{th}^2\varphi)} = \frac{a}{\mathrm{ch}\varphi}$ 故 $x = \frac{a\cos\varphi}{\mathrm{ch}\varphi},y = \frac{a\sin\varphi}{\mathrm{ch}\varphi},z = a\mathrm{th}\varphi$ 为曲线的参数方程，弧长的微分为

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\mathrm {d} \varphi}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\mathrm {d} y}{\mathrm {d} \varphi}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\mathrm {d} z}{\mathrm {d} \varphi}\right) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} \varphi = a \sqrt {\frac {\mathrm {c h} ^ {2} \varphi + \mathrm {s h} ^ {2} \varphi + 1}{\mathrm {c h} ^ {4} \varphi}} \mathrm {d} \varphi = \sqrt {2} a \frac {\mathrm {d} \varphi}{\mathrm {c h} \varphi}.
$$

于是，弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = \int_ {0} ^ {\varphi} \sqrt {2} a \frac {\mathrm {d} \varphi}{\mathrm {c h} \varphi} = \sqrt {2} a \int_ {0} ^ {\varphi} \frac {2}{\mathrm {e} ^ {\varphi} + \mathrm {e} ^ {- \varphi}} \mathrm {d} \varphi = 2 \sqrt {2} a \int_ {0} ^ {\varphi} \frac {1}{1 + (\mathrm {e} ^ {\varphi}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} (\mathrm {e} ^ {\varphi}) \\ = 2 \sqrt {2} a \arctan^ {\varphi} \left| _ {0} ^ {\varphi} = 2 \sqrt {2} a \left(\arctan \frac {a + z}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} - \frac {\pi}{4}\right) ^ {*}\right) = \sqrt {2} a \arctan \frac {z}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}} ^ {* *}) \\ \end{array}
$$

容易推证，当 $z < 0$ 时，弧长为 $s = \sqrt{2} a\arctan \frac{-z}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}.$

总之，最后得 $s = \sqrt{2} a\arctan{\frac{|z|}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}}$

*) 由 $z = a\tan \varphi$ 知： $z(\mathrm{e}^{\varphi} + \mathrm{e}^{-\varphi}) = a(\mathrm{e}^{\varphi} - \mathrm{e}^{-\varphi}),\quad z(\mathrm{e}^{2\varphi} + 1) = a(\mathrm{e}^{2\varphi} - 1),$

从而， $\mathrm{e}^{2\varphi} = \frac{a + z}{a - z}$ 或 $\mathrm{e}^{\varphi} = \frac{a + z}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}.$

$\text{串} \quad \text{串}$ 由于 $\tan \left(\arctan {\frac{a + z}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}} - \frac{\pi}{4}\right) = \frac{a - \sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}{z}, \tan \frac{1}{2}\left(\arctan {\frac{z}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}}\right) = \frac{a - \sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}{z},$

故在主值范围内有 $\arctan \frac{a + z}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}} - \frac{\pi}{4} = \frac {1}{2}\arctan \frac{z}{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}.$
