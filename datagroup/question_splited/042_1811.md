【1811】 $\int x^{5} \mathrm{e}^{\pi^{3}} \mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x^{5} \mathrm{e}^{x^{3}} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{3} \int x^{3} \mathrm{~d}\left(\mathrm{e}^{x^{3}}\right) = \frac{1}{3} x^{3} \mathrm{e}^{x^{3}} - \frac{1}{3} \int \mathrm{e}^{x^{3}} \mathrm{~d}\left(x^{3}\right) = \frac{1}{3}\left(x^{3}-1\right) \mathrm{e}^{x^{3}}+C.$
