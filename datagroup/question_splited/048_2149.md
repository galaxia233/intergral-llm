【2149】 $\int \frac{ax^2 + b}{x^2 + 1}\arctan x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{ax^2 + b}{x^2 + 1}\arctan x\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(a - \frac{a - b}{x^2 + 1}\right)\arctan x\mathrm{d}x = ax\arctan x - a\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{1 + x^2} -\frac{a - b}{2} (\arctan x)^2$

$$
= a \left[ x \arctan x - \frac {1}{2} \ln \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) \right] - \frac {a - b}{2} (\arctan x) ^ {2} + C.
$$
