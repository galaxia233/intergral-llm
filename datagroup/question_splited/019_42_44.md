42.45 If $f(x, y) = e^{x}y^{2} - x^{3}\ln y$ , verify that $f_{xxx}, f_{xyx},$ and $f_{yxx}$ all are equal.

$$
\begin{array}{l} f _ {x} = e ^ {x} y ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2} \ln y, \quad f _ {y} = 2 e ^ {x} y - x ^ {3} / y. \quad f _ {x x} = e ^ {x} y ^ {2} - 6 x \ln y, \quad f _ {x y} = 2 e ^ {x} y - 3 x ^ {2} / y, \quad f _ {y x} = 2 e ^ {x} y - 3 x ^ {2} / y, \\ f _ {x x y} = 2 e ^ {x} y - 6 x / y, \quad f _ {x y x} = 2 e ^ {x} y - 6 x / y, \quad f _ {y x x} = 2 e ^ {x} y - 6 x / y. \end{array}
