【2004】 $\int \tan^5 x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \tan^5 x\mathrm{d}x = \int \tan x(\sec^2 x - 1)^2\mathrm{d}x = \int \sec^4 x\tan x\mathrm{d}x - 2\int \sec^2 x\tan x\mathrm{d}x + \int \tan x\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int \sec^ {3} x d (\sec x) - 2 \int \sec x d (\sec x) - \int \frac {d (\cos x)}{\cos x} = \frac {1}{4} \sec^ {4} x - \sec^ {2} x - \ln | \cos x | + C _ {1} \\ = \frac {1}{4} \tan^ {4} x - \frac {1}{2} \tan^ {2} x - \ln | \cos x | + C. \\ \end{array}
