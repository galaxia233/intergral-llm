42.106 Find an equation of the tangent plane to $z = x^{2} + y^{2}$ at $(1,2,5)$ .

$\partial z / \partial x = 2x = 2, \partial z / \partial y = 2y = 4.$ Hence, by Problem 42.105, a normal vector to the tangent plane is $(2,4,-1)$ . Therefore, an equation of that plane is $2(x - 1) + 4(y - 2) - (z - 5) = 0$ , or, equivalently, $2x + 4y - z = 5$ .
