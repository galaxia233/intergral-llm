【4017】 $x^{2} + y^{2} - az = 0, (x^{2} + y^{2})^{2} = a^{2}(x^{2} - y^{2}), z = 0 (a > 0)$

解 若引用极坐标，则有 $z = \frac{r^2}{a},\qquad r^2 = a^2\cos 2\varphi \quad (a > 0).$

于是，利用对称性知，所求的体积为

$$
V = 4 \iint_ {\Omega} \frac {1}{a} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) d x d y = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a \sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi}} \frac {r ^ {2}}{a} r d r = a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \cos^ {2} 2 \varphi d \varphi = \frac {\pi a ^ {3}}{8}.
$$
