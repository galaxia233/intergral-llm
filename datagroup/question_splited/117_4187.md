【4187】 $\iint_{x^2 + y^2 \leq 1} \ln \frac{1}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}} \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y.$

解 采用极坐标，由于被积函数非负，故有

$$
\iint_ {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2} \leqslant 1} \ln \frac {1}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {1} r \ln \frac {1}{r} d r = - 2 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {1} r \ln r d r = - 2 \pi \left(\frac {r ^ {2}}{2} \ln r \Big | _ {0} ^ {1} - \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {r}{2} d r\right) = \frac {\pi}{2}.
$$
