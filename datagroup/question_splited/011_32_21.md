32.21 Find $\int_0^\infty x^3 e^{-x}dx$

$$
\begin{array}{l}\text {B y}\\3 \int x ^ {2} e ^ {- x} d x. \quad \text {S o}, \quad \int_ {0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {3} e ^ {- x} d x = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} (- x ^ {3} e ^ {- x}) ] _ {0} ^ {v} + \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} 3 \int_ {0} ^ {v} x ^ {2} e ^ {- x} d x = 0 + 3 \cdot 2 = 3!.\end{array}
$$
