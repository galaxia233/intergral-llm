【4412】求 $\operatorname{grad}\left\{\left|\boldsymbol{c} \times \boldsymbol{r}\right|^2\right\}$ （ $c$ 为常向量）.
解 $|c \times r|^2 = (c_yz - c_zy)^2 + (c_zx - c_xz)^2 + (c_xy - c_yx)^2.$ 于是，
$$
\begin{array}{l} \operatorname {g r a d} \left\{\left| c \times r \right| ^ {2} \right\} = \left[ 2 c _ {z} \left(c _ {z} x - c _ {x} z\right) - 2 c _ {y} \left(c _ {x} y - c _ {y} x\right) \right] i + \left[ - 2 c _ {z} \left(c _ {y} z - c _ {z} y\right) + 2 c _ {r} \left(c _ {x} y - c _ {y} x\right) \right] j \\ + \left[ 2 c _ {y} \left(c _ {y} z - c _ {z} y\right) - 2 c _ {x} \left(c _ {z} x - c _ {x} z\right) \right] k \\ = 2 \left[ x \left(c _ {x} ^ {2} + c _ {y} ^ {2} + c _ {z} ^ {2}\right) - c _ {x} \left(c _ {x} x + c _ {y} y + c _ {z} z\right) \right] i + 2 \left[ y \left(c _ {x} ^ {2} + c _ {y} ^ {2} + c _ {z} ^ {2}\right) - c _ {y} \left(c _ {x} x + c _ {y} y + c _ {z} z\right) \right] j \\ + 2 \left[ z \left(c _ {x} ^ {2} + c _ {y} ^ {2} + c _ {z} ^ {2}\right) - c _ {z} \left(c _ {x} x + c _ {y} y + c _ {z} z\right) \right] k \\ = 2 r (c \cdot c) - 2 c (r \cdot c). \\ \end{array}
$$
