【1844】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{3\sin^2x - 8\sin x\cos x + 5\cos^2x}$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{3\sin^2x - 8\sin x\cos x + 5\cos^2x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\tan x)}{3\tan^2x - 8\tan x + 5} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(\tan x - \frac{4}{3}\right)}{\left(\tan x - \frac{4}{3}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{1}{3}\right)^2}$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} \ln \left| \frac {\frac {1}{3} - \left(\tan x - \frac {4}{3}\right)}{\frac {1}{3} + \left(\tan x - \frac {4}{3}\right)} \right| + C _ {1} = \frac {1}{2} \ln \left| \frac {3 \sin x - 5 \cos x}{\sin x - \cos x} \right| + C.
$$
