【4088】 $\int_{1}^{1}\mathrm{d}x\int_{1}^{\sqrt{1 - r^2}}\mathrm{d}y\int_{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}}^{\sqrt{2r^2 + y^2}}z^2\mathrm{d}z.$

提示 注意积分域 $V$ 为 $0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2}, \frac{\pi}{4} \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2}, 0 \leqslant r \leqslant \sqrt{2}$ .

解变换为球坐标，积分域 $V$ 为

$$
0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \frac {\pi}{4} \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, 0 \leqslant r \leqslant \sqrt {2}.
$$

于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}} d y \int_ {\sqrt {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {2 - r ^ {2}} z ^ {2}} z ^ {2} d z = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2}} r ^ {2} \cos \psi \cdot r ^ {2} \sin^ {2} \psi d r = \frac {1}{5} 4 \sqrt {2} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} \int_ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos \psi \sin^ {2} \psi d \psi \\ = \frac {\pi}{5} 2 \sqrt {2} \cdot \frac {1}{3} \sin^ {3} \psi \left| _ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {\pi}{1 5} (2 \sqrt {2} - 1) \right. \\ \end{array}
$$
