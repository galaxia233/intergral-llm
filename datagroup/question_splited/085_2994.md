【2994】 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n(2n + 1)}.$

提示 利用146题的结果.

解 由 $\frac{1}{n(2n + 1)} = \frac{1}{n} -\frac{2}{2n + 1}$ 得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{n (2 n + 1)} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{n} - 2 \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{2 n + 1} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{n} - 2 \left(\sum_ {n _ {1} = 1} ^ {2 N + 1} \frac {1}{n _ {1}} - \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {n _ {2} = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{n _ {2}} - 1\right) \\ = (C + \ln N + \varepsilon_ {1}) - 2 \left[ (C + \ln (2 N + 1) + \varepsilon_ {2}) - \frac {1}{2} (C + \ln N + \varepsilon_ {3}) - 1 \right] ^ {\cdot}) \\ = 2 \ln \frac {N}{2 N + 1} + \alpha + 2, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $\varepsilon_1\rightarrow 0,\varepsilon_2\rightarrow 0,\varepsilon_3\rightarrow 0,\alpha = \varepsilon_1 - 2\varepsilon_2 + \varepsilon_3\rightarrow 0$ （当 $N\to \infty$ ).于是，

$$
\lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{n (2 n + 1)} = 2 \ln \frac {1}{2} + 2 = 2 (1 - \ln 2),
$$

即 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n(2n + 1)} = 2(1 - \ln 2).$

\*) 利用146题的结果.
