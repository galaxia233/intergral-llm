【2254】证明：若函数 $f(x)$ 在闭区间 $[a,b]$ 内连续，则

$$
\int_ {a} ^ {b} f (x) \mathrm {d} x = (b - a) \int_ {0} ^ {1} f [ a + (b - a) x ] \mathrm {d} x.
$$

证设 $x = a + (b - a)t$ ，则 $\mathrm{d}x = (b - a)\mathrm{d}t.$ 代人得

$$
\int_ {a} ^ {b} f (x) \mathrm {d} x = (b - a) \int_ {0} ^ {1} f [ a + (b - a) t ] \mathrm {d} t, \quad \text {即} \quad \int_ {a} ^ {b} f (x) \mathrm {d} x = (b - a) \int_ {0} ^ {1} f [ a + (b - a) x ] \mathrm {d} x.
$$
