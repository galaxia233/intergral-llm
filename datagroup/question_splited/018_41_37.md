41.37 Describe the level curves of $f(x, y) = 4x^{2} + 9y^{2}$ .

See Fig. 41-21. The level curves form a family of ellipses $4x^{2} + 9y^{2} = k^{2}$ , or $\frac{x^2}{(k/2)^2} + \frac{y^2}{(k/3)^2} = 1$ . There is a level curve through every point (considering the origin to be a level curve consisting of a single point).
