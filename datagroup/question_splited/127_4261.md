【4261】 $\int_{(1, - 1)}^{(1,1)}(x - y)(dx - dy).$

解 显然， $(x - y)(\mathrm{d}x - \mathrm{d}y) = \mathrm{d}\frac{(x - y)^2}{2}$ 是全微分.于是，

$$
\int_ {(1, - 1)} ^ {(1, 1)} (x - y) (\mathrm {d} x - \mathrm {d} y) = \int_ {(1, 1)} ^ {(1, 1)} \mathrm {d} \frac {(x - y) ^ {2}}{2} = \frac {(x - y) ^ {2}}{2} \left| _ {(1, - 1)} ^ {(1, 1)} = - 2. \right.
$$
