20.8 For the function $f$ graphed in Fig. 20-1, express $\int_0^5 f(x) \, dx$ in terms of the areas $A_1, A_2$ , and $A_3$ .

![](images/738a4262468e6cb6f809a527e2a6440b473be8e877c8bbe0f0fd4f3486625ab8.jpg)  
Fig. 20-1

The integral is equal to the sum of the areas above the $x$ -axis and under the graph, minus the sum of the areas under the $x$ -axis and above the graph. Hence, $\int_0^5 f(x)dx = A_2 - A_1 - A_3$
