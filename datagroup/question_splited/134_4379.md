【4379】 $\iint_{S}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos \alpha +\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cos \beta +\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}\cos \gamma\right)\mathrm{d}S.$

解 由于 $\frac{\partial P}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z} = \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial z^2} = \Delta u$ ，故得

$$
\iint_ {S} \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial x} \cos \alpha + \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} \cos \beta + \frac {\partial u}{\partial z} \cos \gamma\right) d S = \iiint_ {V} \Delta u d x d y d z.
$$
