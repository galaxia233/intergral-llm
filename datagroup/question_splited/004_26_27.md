26.27 A bacterial culture, growing exponentially, increases from 100 to 400 grams in 10 hours. How much was present after 3 hours?

$y = 100e^{Kt}$ . Hence, $400 = 100e^{10K}$ , $4 = e^{10K}$ , $2 = e^{5K}$ , $5K = \ln 2 \approx 0.6931$ , $K \approx 0.13862$ . After 3 hours, $y = 100e^{3K} = 100e^{0.41586} \approx 100(1.5156) = 151.56$ .
