【3125】取点 $x_{i} = 0, \pm \frac{1}{2}, \pm 1$ 为拉格朗日多项式的插值节点，对函数 $f(x) = |x|$ 作出在闭区间 $[-1,1]$ 上的拉格朗日插值多项式.

解 以 $x_{i} = 0, \pm \frac{1}{2}, \pm 1, y_{i} = 0, \frac{1}{2}, 1$ 代入拉格朗日插值公式，即得
$$
\begin{array}{l} P (x) = \frac {x \left(x - \frac {1}{2}\right) \left(x ^ {2} - 1\right)}{\left(- \frac {1}{2}\right) (- 1) \cdot \frac {1}{2} \left(- \frac {3}{2}\right)} \cdot \frac {1}{2} + \frac {x \left(x + \frac {1}{2}\right) \left(x ^ {2} - 1\right)}{\frac {1}{2} \cdot 1 \cdot \frac {3}{2} \left(- \frac {1}{2}\right)} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \\ + \frac {x (x - 1) \left(x ^ {2} - \frac {1}{4}\right)}{(- 1) \left(- \frac {1}{2}\right) \left(- \frac {3}{2}\right) (- 2)} \cdot 1 + \frac {x (x + 1) \left(x ^ {2} - \frac {1}{4}\right)}{1 \cdot \frac {3}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \cdot 2} \cdot 1 \\ = \frac {x ^ {2}}{3} (7 - 4 x ^ {2}) \quad (| x | \leqslant 1), \\ \end{array}
$$
此即所求的多项式.
