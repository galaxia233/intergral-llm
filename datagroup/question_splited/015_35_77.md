35.77 Find the arc length of the spiral $r = e^{\theta}$ from $\theta = 0$ to $\theta = \ln 2$ .

\(
dr / d\theta = e^{\theta}. \quad \text{Hence,} \quad L = \int_{0}^{\ln 2} \sqrt{e^{2\theta} + e^{2\theta}} d\theta = \int_{0}^{\ln 2} \sqrt{2} e^{\theta} d\theta = \sqrt{2} e^{\theta}]_{0}^{\ln 2} = \sqrt{2} (2 - 1) = \sqrt{2}.
\)
