【3072】 $\prod_{n = n_0}^{\infty}\frac{(n - a_1)(n - a_2)\cdots(n - a_p)}{(n - b_1)(b - b_2)\cdots(n - b_p)},$ 其中 $n_0 > b_i (i = 1, 2, \dots, p)$

解 $p_{n} = \frac{(n - a_{1})(n - a_{2})\cdots(n - a_{p})}{(n - b_{1})(b - b_{2})\cdots(n - b_{p})} = 1 + \frac{(\sum_{i = 1}^{p}b_{i} - \sum_{i = 1}^{p}a_{i})n^{p - 1} + \cdots + (-1)^{p}\left(\prod_{i = 1}^{p}a_{i} - \prod_{i = 1}^{p}b_{i}\right)}{\prod_{i = 1}^{p}(n - b_{i})}.$

令

$$
a _ {n} = \frac {\left(\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {p} b _ {i} - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {p} a _ {i}\right) n ^ {p - 1} + \cdots + (- 1) ^ {p} \left(\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {p} a _ {i} - \prod_ {i = 1} ^ {p} b _ {i}\right)}{\prod_ {i = 1} ^ {p} (n - b _ {i})},
$$

当 $\sum_{i=1}^{p} a_{i} = \sum_{i=1}^{p} b_{i}$ 时， $a_{n} = O\left(\frac{1}{n^{2}}\right)$ ，故 $\sum_{n=n_{0}}^{\infty} a_{n}$ 收敛，从而，原无穷乘积收敛。当 $\sum_{i=1}^{p} a_{i} \neq \sum_{i=1}^{p} b_{i}$ 时，由于当 $n > n_{0}$ 时， $\prod_{i=1}^{p} (n - b_{i}) > 0$ ，且 $a_{n} \sim \frac{1}{n}$ （ $\sum_{i=1}^{p} b_{i} - \sum_{i=1}^{p} a_{i}$ ），故级数 $\sum_{n=n_{0}}^{\infty} a_{n}$ 发散，从而，原无穷乘积也发散。
