【1716】+ $\int \frac{1}{1 - x^2}\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $\frac{1}{1 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\mathrm{d}\left(\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\right).$

解 $\int \frac{1}{1 - x^2}\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{d}\left(\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\right) = \frac{1}{4}\ln^2\frac{1 + x}{1 - x} +C.$
