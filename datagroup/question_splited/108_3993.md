【3993】 $\left(\frac{x}{a} +\frac{y}{b}\right)^4 = \frac{x^2}{h^2} +\frac{y^2}{k^2}\quad (x > 0,y > 0).$

解解法 1：

令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = brs\sin \varphi$ ，则方程化为

$$
r ^ {2} = \frac {\left(\frac {a}{h}\right) ^ {2} \cos^ {2} \varphi + \left(\frac {b}{k}\right) ^ {2} \sin^ {2} \varphi}{(\cos \varphi + \sin \varphi) ^ {4}} \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}).
$$

于是，曲线所围的面积为 $S = \iint_{S} ab r \, \mathrm{d}r \, \mathrm{d}\varphi = \frac{ab}{2} \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \frac{\left(\frac{a}{h}\right)^{2} \cos^{2}\varphi + \left(\frac{b}{k}\right)^{2} \sin^{2}\varphi}{(\cos\varphi + \sin\varphi)^{4}} \, \mathrm{d}\varphi.$

注意到

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\cos^ {2} \varphi}{(\cos \varphi + \sin \varphi) ^ {4}} d \varphi = \int \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {4}} d (\tan \varphi) = - \frac {1}{3 (1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {3}} + C, \\ \int \frac {\sin^ {2} \varphi}{(\cos \varphi + \sin \varphi) ^ {4}} d \varphi = \int \frac {\tan^ {2} \varphi}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {4}} d (\tan \varphi) = \int \frac {(\tan \varphi - 1) (\tan \varphi + 1) + 1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {4}} d (\tan \varphi) \\ = \int \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {2}} d (\tan \varphi) - 2 \int \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {3}} d (\tan \varphi) + \int \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {4}} d (\tan \varphi) \\ = - \frac {1}{1 + \tan \varphi} + \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{3} \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {3}} + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \frac {a b}{2} \left(\frac {a}{h}\right) ^ {2} \left[ - \frac {1}{3 (1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {3}} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2} - 0} + \frac {a b}{2} \left(\frac {b}{k}\right) ^ {2} \left[ - \frac {1}{1 + \tan \varphi} + \frac {1}{(1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{3 (1 + \tan \varphi) ^ {3}} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2} - 0} \\ = \frac {a b}{6} \left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{h ^ {2}} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{k ^ {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

解法 2：

令 $x = h r\cos \varphi, y = k r\sin \varphi$ ，则方程化为

$$
r ^ {2} = \frac {1}{\left(\frac {h}{a} \cos \varphi + \frac {k}{b} \sin \varphi\right) ^ {4}} = \left[ \frac {a ^ {2} b ^ {2}}{(h b) ^ {2} + (k a) ^ {2}} \right] ^ {2} \frac {1}{\sin^ {4} (\varphi + \alpha)} \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}),
$$

其中 $\tan \alpha = \frac{hb}{ka}$ 于是，曲线所围的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \iint_ {S} h k r d r d \varphi = \frac {h k a ^ {4} b ^ {4}}{\left[ (h b) ^ {2} + (k a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {d \varphi}{\sin^ {4} (\varphi + \alpha)} \\ = \frac {h k a ^ {4} b ^ {4}}{\left[ (h b) ^ {2} + (k a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {2}} \left[ - \frac {1}{6} \frac {\cos (\varphi + \alpha)}{\sin (\varphi + \alpha)} - \frac {1}{3} \frac {\cos (\varphi + \alpha)}{\sin (\varphi + \alpha)} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2} *} \\ = \frac {h k a ^ {4} b ^ {4}}{\left[ (h b) ^ {2} + (k a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {2}} \left[ \frac {1}{6} \left(\frac {\sin \alpha}{\cos^ {3} \alpha} + \frac {\cos \alpha}{\sin^ {3} \alpha}\right) + \frac {1}{3} (\tan \alpha + \cot \alpha) \right] \\ = \frac {h k a ^ {4} b ^ {4}}{\left[ (h b) ^ {2} + (k a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{6} \frac {\left[ (h b) ^ {2} + (k a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {3}}{(h b k a) ^ {3}} = \frac {a b}{6} \left(\frac {a ^ {2}}{h ^ {2}} + \frac {b ^ {2}}{k ^ {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

\*）利用 2012 题的结果。

$\text{串}$ ）由 $\tan \alpha = \frac{hb}{ka}$ 知： $\cot \alpha = \frac{ka}{hb},$ $\sin \alpha = \frac{hb}{\sqrt{(hb)^2 + (ka)^2}},\quad \cos \alpha = \frac{ka}{\sqrt{(hb)^2 + (ka)^2}}.$
