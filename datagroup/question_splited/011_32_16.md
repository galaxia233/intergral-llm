32.16 Evaluate $\int_{2}^{\infty}\frac{1}{x\ln^{k}x} dx.$

$$
\begin{array}{l}\int_ {2} ^ {\infty}; \frac {1}{x \ln^ {k} x} d x = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \int_ {2} ^ {v} \frac {1}{x \ln^ {k} x} d x = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \left. \frac {(\ln x) ^ {- k + 1}}{- k + 1} \right] _ {2} ^ {v} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \frac {1}{k - 1} \left[ \frac {- 1}{(\ln v) ^ {k - 1}} + \frac {1}{(\ln 2) ^ {k - 1}} \right] =\\\frac {1}{k - 1} \frac {1}{(\ln 2) ^ {k - 1}}.\end{array}
$$
