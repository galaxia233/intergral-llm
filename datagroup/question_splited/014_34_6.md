34.6 Sketch the curve with the parametric equations $x = \sec t, y = \tan t$ .

$x^{2} = y^{2} + 1$ . Hence, $x^{2} - y^{2} = 1$ . Thus, the curve is a rectangular hyperbola with the perpendicular asymptotes $y = \pm x$ . See Fig. 34-6.   
34.7 Sketch the curve with the parametric equations $x = \sin t, y = \cos 2t$ .   
$y = \cos 2t = 1 - 2\sin^2 t = 1 - 2x^2,$ defined for $|x|\leq 1$ . Thus, the curve is an arc of a parabola, with vertex at $(0,1)$ , opening downward, and with the $y$ -axis as axis of symmetry (Fig. 34-7).
