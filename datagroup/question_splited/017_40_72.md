40.72 Write equations for the line through the point $(1, 2, -6)$ and parallel to the vector $(4, 1, 3)$ .

The line is generated by the vector function $(1,2,-6) + t(4,1,3)$ . Parametric equations are $x = 1 + 4t$ , $y = 2 + t$ , $z = -6 + 3t$ . A set of rectangular equations is $\frac{x - 1}{4} = \frac{y - 2}{1} = \frac{z + 6}{3}$ .
