【4300】 $\oint_{C} e^{x}[(1 - \cos y) \mathrm{d}x - (y - \sin y) \mathrm{d}y]$ ，其中 $C$ 为区域 $0 < x < \pi, 0 < y < \sin x$ 的边界，并且积分时的围绕方向为正.

解 由于 $\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y} = \mathrm{e}^{x} (\sin y - y) - \mathrm{e}^{x} \sin y = -y \mathrm{e}^{x},$

故有 $\oint_{C} \mathrm{e}^{x}[(1 - \cos y) \mathrm{d}x - (y - \sin y) \mathrm{d}y] = -\iint_{\substack{0 \leqslant x \leqslant \pi \\ 0 \leqslant y \leqslant \sin x}} y \mathrm{e}^{x} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y = -\int_{0}^{\pi} \mathrm{e}^{x} \mathrm{d}x \int_{0}^{\sin x} y \mathrm{d}y$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \sin^ {2} x \mathrm {d} x = - \frac {1}{4} \left(\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \mathrm {d} x - \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \cos 2 x \mathrm {d} x\right) \\ = - \frac {1}{4} \left[ \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {\pi} - 1\right) - \frac {\cos 2 x + 2 \sin 2 x}{5} \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \right] _ {0} ^ {\pi} = - \frac {1}{5} \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {\pi} - 1\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
