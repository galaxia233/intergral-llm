【1985】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[3]{1 + x^3}}.$

提示 令 $x^{-3} + 1 = z^3$

解 $\frac{1}{\sqrt[3]{1 + x^3}} = x^0 (1 + x^3)^{-\frac{1}{3}}, m = 0, n = 3, p = -\frac{1}{3}; \frac{m + 1}{n} + p = 0$ 这是二项微分式的第三种情形.

设 $x^{-3} + 1 = z^3$ ，则 $x = (z^{3} - 1)^{-\frac{1}{3}}$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = -z^2 (z^3 -1)^{-\frac{4}{3}}\mathrm{dz}$ 代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt [ 3 ]{1 + x ^ {3}}} = - \int \frac {z}{z ^ {3} - 1} \mathrm {d} z = - \frac {1}{3} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} z}{z - 1} + \frac {1}{3} \int \frac {z - 1}{z ^ {2} + z + 1} \mathrm {d} z \\ = - \frac {1}{3} \ln | z - 1 | + \frac {1}{6} \ln (z ^ {2} + z + 1) - \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 z + 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + C \\ = \frac {1}{6} \ln \frac {z ^ {2} + z + 1}{(z - 1) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 z + 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $z = \frac{\sqrt[3]{1 + x^3}}{x}$
