【1769】 $\int \frac{x^5}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $1 - x^{2} = t$ ，则 $x^{2} = 1 - t$ ，从而，

$$
x ^ {5} \mathrm {d} x = \frac {1}{2} (x ^ {2}) ^ {2} \mathrm {d} (x ^ {2}) = - \frac {1}{2} (1 - t) ^ {2} \mathrm {d} t,
$$

故得 $\int \frac{x^5}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{2}\int t^{-\frac{1}{2}}(1 - t)^2\mathrm{d}t = -\frac{1}{2}\int (t^{-\frac{1}{2}} - 2t^{\frac{1}{2}} + t^{\frac{3}{2}})\mathrm{d}t = -t^{\frac{1}{2}} + \frac{2}{3} t^{\frac{3}{2}} - \frac{1}{5} t^{\frac{5}{2}} + C$

$$
= - \frac {1}{1 5} \left(8 + 4 x ^ {2} + 3 x ^ {4}\right) \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} + C.
$$
