30.9 $\int \frac{2x dx}{(x - 2)^2(x + 2)}.$

$\frac{2x}{(x - 2)^2(x + 2)} = \frac{A}{x - 2} + \frac{B}{(x - 2)^2} + \frac{C}{x + 2}$ . Then $2x = A(x - 2)(x + 2) + B(x + 2) + C(x - 2)^2$ . Let $x = 2$ . Then $4 = 4B$ , $B = 1$ . Let $x = -2$ . Then $-4 = 16C$ , $C = -\frac{1}{4}$ . To find $A$ , equate coefficients of $x^2$ : $0 = A + C$ , $A = -C = \frac{1}{4}$ . Thus, $\frac{2x}{(x - 2)^2(x + 2)} = \frac{1}{4}\frac{1}{x - 2} + \frac{1}{(x - 2)^2} - \frac{1}{4}\frac{1}{x + 2}$ , and $\int \frac{2xdx}{(x - 2)^2(x + 2)} = \frac{1}{4}\ln |x - 2| - \frac{1}{x - 2} - \frac{1}{4}\ln |x + 2| + C_1 = \frac{1}{4}\ln \left|\frac{x - 2}{x + 2}\right| - \frac{1}{x - 2} + C_1$ .
