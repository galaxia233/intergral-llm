【4427】计算：（1）divr； (2)div $\frac{r}{r}$
提示 利用散度定义，易得结果：(1)3；(2) $\frac{2}{r}$ .
解 (1) $\mathrm{div}r = \frac{\partial x}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial y}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial z} = 3.$
(2) $\operatorname{div} \frac{r}{r} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \frac{x}{r} \right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \left( \frac{y}{r} \right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial z} \left( \frac{z}{r} \right) = \left( \frac {1}{r} - \frac {x^2}{r^3} \right) + \left( \frac {1}{r} - \frac {y^2}{r^3} \right) + \left( \frac {1}{r} - \frac {z^2}{r^3} \right)$

$$
= \frac {3}{r} - \frac {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}{r ^ {3}} = \frac {3}{r} - \frac {1}{r} = \frac {2}{r}.
$$
