【1906】 $\int \frac{x^2 + x}{x^6 + 1}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $\frac{x^2 + x}{x^6 + 1} dx = \frac{1}{3}\frac{d(x^3)}{(x^3)^2 + 1} +\frac{1}{2}\frac{d(x^2)}{(x^2)^3 + 1}$ 并利用1881题的结果.

解 $\int \frac{x^2 + x}{x^6 + 1}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(x^3)}{(x^3)^2 + 1} +\frac{1}{2}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(x^2)}{(x^2)^3 + 1}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{3} \arctan (x ^ {3}) + \frac {1}{2} \left[ \frac {1}{6} \ln \frac {(x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}}{x ^ {4} - x ^ {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 x ^ {2} - 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) \right] ^ {*}) + C \\ = \frac {1}{3} \arctan (x ^ {3}) + \frac {1}{1 2} \ln \frac {(x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}}{x ^ {4} - x ^ {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 x ^ {2} - 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用1881题的结果.
