【2316】确定下列定积分的符号：
(2) $\int_{0}^{2\pi}\frac{\sin x}{x}\mathrm{d}x;$

提示 (2) 及 (3) 使用第一中值定理.

解（2）由第一中值定理知

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {\sin x}{x} d x = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin x}{x} d x + \int_ {\pi} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {\sin x}{x} d x = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin x}{x} d x - \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin t}{t + \pi} d t = \pi \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin x}{x (x + \pi)} d x \\ = \frac {\pi^ {2} \mathrm {s i n c}}{c (c + \pi)} > 0, \quad (\text {其 中} 0 <   c <   \pi). \\ \end{array}
$$
