【2536】 $\int_0^\pi \sqrt{3 + \cos x}\mathrm{d}x\quad (n = 6).$

解 $h = \frac{\pi}{6}$

$$
x _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 2;
$$

$$
x _ {1} = \frac {\pi}{6}, \quad y _ {1} = \sqrt {3 + \cos \frac {\pi}{6}} = \sqrt {3 . 8 6 6} = 1. 9 6 6;
$$

$$
x _ {2} = \frac {\pi}{3}, \quad y _ {2} = \sqrt {3 + \cos \frac {\pi}{3}} = \sqrt {3 . 5} = 1. 8 7 1;
$$

$$
x _ {3} = \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad y _ {3} = \sqrt {3 + \cos \frac {\pi}{2}} = \sqrt {3} = 1. 7 3 2;
$$

$$
x _ {4} = \frac {2 \pi}{3}, \quad y _ {4} = \sqrt {3 + \cos \frac {2 \pi}{3}} = \sqrt {2 . 5} = 1. 5 8 1;
$$

$$
x _ {5} = \frac {5 \pi}{6}, \quad y _ {5} = \sqrt {3 + \cos \frac {5 \pi}{6}} = \sqrt {2 . 1 3 4} = 1. 4 6 1;
$$

$$
x _ {6} = \pi , \quad y _ {6} = \sqrt {3 + \cos \pi} = \sqrt {2} = 1. 4 1 4.
$$

按辛普森公式，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \sqrt {3 + \cos x} d x \approx \frac {\pi}{1 8} \left[ (2 + 1. 4 1 4) + 4 (1. 9 6 6 + 1. 7 3 2 + 1. 4 6 1) + 2 (1. 8 7 1 + 1. 5 8 1) \right] \\ \approx 5. 4 0 2 5. \\ \end{array}
$$
