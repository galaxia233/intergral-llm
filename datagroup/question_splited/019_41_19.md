41.85 Write down the equations connecting spherical coordinates $(\rho, \phi, \theta)$ with rectangular and cylindrical coordinates.

See Fig. 41-28. $x = r \cos \theta = \rho \sin \phi \cos \theta, \quad y = r \sin \theta = \rho \sin \phi \sin \theta, \quad z = \rho \cos \phi, \quad \rho^2 = r^2 + z^2 = x^2 + y^2 + z^2$ .
