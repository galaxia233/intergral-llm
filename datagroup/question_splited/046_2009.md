【2009】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{\tan x}}.$

解设 $t = \sqrt{\tan x}$ ，则 $x = \arctan t^2$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = \frac{2t}{1 + t^4}\mathrm{dt}$ 代人得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {\tan x}} = 2 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{1 + t ^ {4}} = 2 \left(\frac {1}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {2}} \arctan \frac {t \sqrt {2}}{1 - t ^ {2}}\right) ^ {*}) + C \\ = \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \arctan \frac {t \sqrt {2}}{1 - t ^ {2}} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $t = \sqrt{\tan x}$

\*) 利用1884题的结果.
