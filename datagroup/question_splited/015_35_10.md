35.10 Describe the graph of the polar equation $\theta = \pi /4$

The graph is the line through the pole making an angle of $\pi /4$ radian with the polar axis (Fig. 35-2). Note that we obtain the points on that line below the $x$ -axis because $r$ can assume negative values.
