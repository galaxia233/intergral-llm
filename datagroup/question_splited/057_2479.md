【2479】 $y = \mathrm{e}^{-x}\sqrt{\sin x}$ （ $0 \leqslant x < +\infty$ ）绕 $Ox$ 轴.

解函数定义域为 $[2n\pi ,(2n + 1)\pi ],(n = 0,1,2,\dots)$ .于是，所求的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V _ {x} = \pi \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \int_ {2 n \pi} ^ {(2 n + 1) \pi} e ^ {- 2 x} \sin x d x = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {\pi}{5} e ^ {- 2 x} (- 2 \sin x - \cos x) \left| \begin{array}{l} (2 n + 1) \pi \\ 2 n \pi \end{array} \right. \\ = \frac {\pi}{5} \left(e ^ {- 2 \pi} + 1\right) \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} e ^ {- 4 n \pi} = \frac {\pi}{5} \cdot \frac {e ^ {- 2 \pi} + 1}{1 - e ^ {- 4 \pi}} = \frac {\pi}{5 \left(1 - e ^ {- 2 \pi}\right)}. \\ \end{array}
$$
