【4419】设 $u = \arctan \frac{z}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}}, c = i + j + k$ ，写出 $a = c \times \operatorname{grad} u$ 通过单位向量 $i, j, k$ 的表达式.
解 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{1}{1 + \frac{z^2}{x^2 + y^2}}\left(-\frac{xz}{(x^2 + y^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\right) = -\frac{rz}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)(x^2 + y^2)^{\frac{1}{2}}},$
$$
\frac {\partial u}{\partial y} = - \frac {y z}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}\right) \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{2}}}, \quad \frac {\partial u}{\partial z} = \frac {\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{2}}}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}.
$$

于是，
$$
\begin{array}{l} a = c \times \operatorname {g r a d} u = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} i & j & k \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \\ \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} & \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} & \frac {\partial u}{\partial z} \end{array} \right| \\ = \frac {1}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}\right) \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{2}}} \left[ \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + y z\right) i - \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + x z\right) j + (x - y) z k \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
