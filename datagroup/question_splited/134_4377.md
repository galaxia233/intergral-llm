【4377】 $\iint_{S} xy \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y + xz \, \mathrm{d}z \, \mathrm{d}x + yz \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z.$

解 由于 $\frac{\partial P}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R}{\partial z} = 0$ ，故得

$$
\iint_ {S} x y \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y + x z \mathrm {d} z \mathrm {d} x + y z \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z = \iiint_ {V} 0 \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z = 0.
$$
