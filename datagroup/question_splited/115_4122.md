【4122】 $\left(\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2}\right)^2 = \frac{z}{h}\cdot \mathrm{e}^{-\frac{\frac{z^2}{c^2}}{\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} + \frac{z^2}{c^2}}}}.$

解令 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos \psi ,y = br\sin \varphi \cos \psi ,z = cr\sin \psi$ ，则区域 $V$ 的 $\frac{1}{4}$ 部分（第一卦限内）为

$$
0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant r \leqslant \left(\frac {c}{h} \sin \psi e ^ {- \sin^ {2} \psi}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{3}}.
$$

这是由于 $z \geqslant 0$ ，故区域 $V$ 在 $Oxy$ 平面的上方。

于是，体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {\left(\frac {c}{h} \sin \psi e ^ {- \sin^ {2} \psi}\right) \frac {1}{3}} a b c r ^ {2} \cos \psi d r = \frac {4 c ^ {2} a b}{3 h} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} \cdot \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin \psi \cos \psi e ^ {- \sin^ {2} \psi} d \psi \\ = - \frac {\pi a b c ^ {2}}{3 h} e ^ {- \sin^ {2} \phi} \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {\pi a b c ^ {2}}{3 h} (1 - e ^ {- 1}) \right.. \\ \end{array}
$$
