【1824】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}}{(1 + x^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解记 $I_{1} = \int \frac{x\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}}{(1 + x^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}\mathrm{d}x,I_{2} = \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}}{(1 + x^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}\mathrm{d}x.$

则 $\left\{ \begin{array}{l} I_{1} = \int \mathrm{e}^{\arctan x} \mathrm{d}(-\left(1 + x^{2}\right)^{-\frac{1}{2}}) = -\frac{\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}}{\sqrt{1 + x^{2}}} + I_{2}, \\ I_{1} = \int \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 + x^{2}}} \mathrm{d}\left(\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}\right) = \frac{x \mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}}{\sqrt{1 + x^{2}}} - I_{2}. \end{array} \right.$ ①

由 $① + ②$ 即得 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x}}{(1 + x^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{x - 1}{2\sqrt{1 + x^2}}\mathrm{e}^{\arctan x} + C.$
