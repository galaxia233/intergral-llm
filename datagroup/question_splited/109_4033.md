【4033】 $z = \arctan{\frac{y}{x}}, z = 0, \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} = a\arctan{\frac{y}{x}} (y \geqslant 0).$

解 令 $x = r\cos \varphi, y = r\sin \varphi$ ，则方程化为 $z = c\varphi, r = a\varphi (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2})$ ，于是，曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
V = \iint_ {\Omega} z \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \int_ {0} ^ {a _ {\varphi}} c \varphi r \mathrm {d} r \mathrm {d} \varphi = \frac {a ^ {2} c}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \varphi^ {3} \mathrm {d} \varphi = \frac {a ^ {2} c}{8} \varphi^ {4} \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {\pi^ {4} a ^ {2} c}{1 2 8}. \right.
$$
