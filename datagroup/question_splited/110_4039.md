【4039】求曲面 $z^2 = 2xy$ 被平面 $x + y = 1, x = 0, y = 0$ 所截部分的面积.

解因为

$$
\sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2}} = \sqrt {1 + \frac {y ^ {2}}{z ^ {2}} + \frac {x ^ {2}}{z ^ {2}}} = \sqrt {\frac {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}{z ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \sqrt {\frac {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + 2 x y}{x y}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \frac {x + y}{\sqrt {x y}},
$$

于是，所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \frac {2}{\sqrt {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x \int_ {0} ^ {1 - x} \frac {x + y}{\sqrt {x y}} d y = \frac {2}{\sqrt {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {1} [ 2 \sqrt {x (1 - x)} + \frac {2}{3 \sqrt {x}} (1 - x) \sqrt {1 - x} ] d x \\ = \sqrt {2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {2 \sqrt {1 - x} (1 + 2 x)}{3 \sqrt {x}} d x = \frac {4 \sqrt {2}}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 - x} (1 + 2 x) d (\sqrt {x}) \\ = \frac {4 \sqrt {2}}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} (1 + 2 t ^ {2}) d t = \frac {4 \sqrt {2}}{3} \left(\frac {\pi}{4} + \frac {\pi}{8}\right) = \frac {\pi}{\sqrt {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$
