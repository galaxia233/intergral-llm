【1704】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos x}$

提示 注意 $\cos x = \sin \left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)$ , 并利用 1703 题的结果.

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)}{\sin\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)} = \ln \left|\tan \left(\frac{x}{2} + \frac{\pi}{4}\right)\right| + C.$
