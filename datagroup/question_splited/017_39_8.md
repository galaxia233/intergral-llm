39.8 Write the first nonzero terms of the Maclaurin series for sec $x$ .

Let $f(x) = \sec x$ . Then, $f'(x) = \sec x \tan x$ , $f''(x) = (\sec x)(1 + 2 \tan^2 x)$ , $f'''(x) = (\sec x \tan x)(5 + 6 \tan^2 x)$ , $f^{(4)}(x) = 12 \sec^3 x \tan^2 x + (5 + 6 \tan^2 x)(\sec^3 x + \tan^2 x \sec x)$ . Thus, $f(0) = 1$ , $f'(0) = 0$ , $f''(0) = 1$ , $f'''(0) = 0$ , $f^{(4)} = 5$ . The Maclaurin series is $1 + \frac{1}{2} x^2 + \frac{5}{24} x^4 + \dots$ .
