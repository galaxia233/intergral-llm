41.65 Find the domain of definition of the function $f(x, y) = \ln (16 - x^2 - y^2) + \ln (x^2 + y^2 - 1)$ .   
This is defined when the arguments of the ln function are positive, that is, when $16 - x^{2} - y^{2} > 0$ and $x^{2} + y^{2} - 1 > 0$ , or, equivalently, $1 < x^{2} + y^{2} < 16$ . Thus, the domain consists of all points between the concentric circles around the origin of radii 1 and 4.
