【2937】三角多项式 $p_n(x) = \sum_{i=0}^{n} (\alpha_i \cos i x + \beta_i \sin i x)$ 的傅里叶级数是怎样的？

解 $p_n(x)$ 是以 $2\pi$ 为周期的函数，不妨在 $[- \pi, \pi]$ 上展开成傅里叶级数.由于

$$
a _ {0} = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {- \pi} ^ {\pi} p _ {n} (x) d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {- \pi} ^ {\pi} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {n} \left(\alpha_ {i} \cos i x + \beta_ {i} \sin i x\right) d x = 2 \alpha_ {0},
$$

$$
a _ {n} = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {- \pi} ^ {\pi} p _ {n} (x) \cos n x d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {- \pi} ^ {\pi} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {n} \left(\alpha_ {i} \cos i x + \beta_ {i} \sin i x\right) \cos n x d x = a _ {n};
$$

$$
b _ {n} = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {- \pi} ^ {\pi} p _ {n} (x) \sin n x d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {- \pi} ^ {\pi} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {n} (\alpha_ {i} \cos i x + \beta_ {i} \sin i x) \sin n x d x = \beta_ {n}.
$$

于是，在 $[-\pi, \pi]$ 上，有

$$
p _ {n} (x) = \frac {a _ {0}}{2} + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left(a _ {n} \cos n x + b _ {n} \sin n x\right) = \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {n} \left(\alpha_ {i} \cos i x + \beta_ {i} \sin i x\right),
$$

即 $p_n(x)$ 的傅里叶级数就是它本身.
