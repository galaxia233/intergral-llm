【3106】 设函数 $f(x)$ 在闭区间 $[a,b]$ 上可以积分，且

$$
\delta_ {n} = \frac {b - a}{n}, \quad f _ {i n} = f (a + i \delta_ {n}) \quad (i = 1, 2, \dots , n),
$$

证明： $\lim_{n\to \infty}\prod_{i = 1}^{n}(1 + \delta_{n}f_{in}) = e^{\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx}.$

证 令 $y_{n} = \prod_{i=1}^{n}(1 + \delta_{n}f_{in})$ ，则

$$
\ln y _ {n} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \ln (1 + \delta_ {n} f _ {i n}) = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \left[ f _ {i n} \delta_ {n} + O \left(\delta_ {n} ^ {2}\right) \right] = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} f _ {i n} \delta_ {n} + O \left(\frac {1}{n}\right),
$$

于是，

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \ln y _ {n} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \left\{\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} f _ {i n} \delta_ {n} + O \left(\frac {1}{n}\right)\right\} = \int_ {a} ^ {b} f (x) d x,
$$

即 $\lim_{n\to \infty}y_n = \lim_{n\to \infty}\prod_{i = 1}^{n}(1 + \delta_nf_{in}) = e^{\int_a^b f(x)\mathrm{d}x}$ 证毕.
