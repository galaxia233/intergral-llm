【2549】 $\frac{1}{1\cdot 2} +\frac{1}{2\cdot 3} +\frac{1}{3\cdot 4} +\dots +\frac{1}{n(n + 1)} +\dots .$

提示 注意 $\frac{1}{n(n + 1)} = \frac{1}{n} -\frac{1}{n + 1}$ 即知 $S_{n} = 1 - \frac{1}{n + 1}$

解由于

$$
\begin{array}{l} S _ {n} = \frac {1}{1 \cdot 2} + \frac {1}{2 \cdot 3} + \frac {1}{3 \cdot 4} + \dots + \frac {1}{n (n + 1)} = \left(1 - \frac {1}{2}\right) + \left(\frac {1}{2} - \frac {1}{3}\right) + \dots + \left(\frac {1}{n} - \frac {1}{n + 1}\right) \\ = 1 - \frac {1}{n + 1}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故得 $S = \lim_{n\to +\infty}S_n = 1.$
