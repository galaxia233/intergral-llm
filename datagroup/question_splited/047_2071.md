【2071】 $\int (1 + x^2)^2\cos x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int (1 + x^2)^2\cos x\mathrm{d}x = \int (1 + 2x^2 +x^4)\cos x\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \sin x \left[ \left(1 + 2 x ^ {2} + x ^ {4}\right) - (4 + 1 2 x ^ {2}) + 2 4 \right] + \cos x \left[ \left(4 x + 4 x ^ {3}\right) - 2 4 x \right] ^ {*}) + C \\ = (2 1 - 1 0 x ^ {2} + x ^ {4}) \sin x - (2 0 x - 4 x ^ {3}) \cos x + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*）利用 2067 题的结果.
