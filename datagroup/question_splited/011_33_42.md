33.41 Derive the law of cosines by vector methods.

In the triangle of Fig. 33-11, let $|\mathbf{A}| = a$ , $|\mathbf{B}| = b$ , $|\mathbf{C}| = c$ . Then $a^2 = \mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{A} = (\mathbf{B} - \mathbf{C}) \cdot (\mathbf{B} - \mathbf{C}) = \mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B} + \mathbf{C} \cdot \mathbf{C} - 2\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{C} = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc\cos \theta$.
