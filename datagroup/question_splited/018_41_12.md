41.12 From what curve in the $xy$ -plane is the surface $9x^{2} + 25y^{2} + 9z^{2} = 225$ obtained by rotation about the $y$ -axis?

It is equivalent to $9(\sqrt{x^2 + z^2})^2 + 25y^2 = 225$ , which is obtained from $9x^2 + 25y^2 = 225$ by rotation about the $y$ -axis. The latter curve is the ellipse $\frac{x^2}{25} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1$ .
