【1909】 $\int \frac{x^{11} \mathrm{d}x}{x^8 + 3x^4 + 2}$

解 $\int \frac{x^{11}}{x^8 + 3x^4 + 2} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4} \int \frac{x^8 \mathrm{d}(x^4)}{(x^4 + 1)(x^4 + 2)} = \frac{1}{4} \int \left[1 - \frac{3x^4 + 2}{(x^4 + 1)(x^4 + 2)}\right] \mathrm{d}(x^4)$

$$
= \frac {1}{4} \int \left[ 1 + \frac {1}{x ^ {4} + 1} - \frac {4}{x ^ {4} + 2} \right] d (x ^ {4}) = \frac {x ^ {4}}{4} + \frac {1}{4} \ln \frac {x ^ {4} + 1}{(x ^ {4} + 2) ^ {4}} + C.
$$
