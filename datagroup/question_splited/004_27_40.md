27.40 $\int \frac{dx}{4 + 9x^2}.$   
$\int \frac{dx}{4 + 9x^2} = \int \frac{dx}{9\left(\frac{4}{9} + x^2\right)} = \frac{1}{9}\int \frac{dx}{\left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^2 + x^2}.$ By the formula given in Problem 27.39, this is equal to $\frac{1}{9}\cdot \frac{1}{\frac{2}{3}}\cdot \tan^{-1}\frac{x}{\frac{2}{3}} +C = \frac{1}{6}\tan^{-1}\frac{3x}{2} +C.$
