33.28 Prove Cauchy's inequality: $|\mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B}|\leq |\mathbf{A}||\mathbf{B}|.$

Case 1. $\mathbf{A} = \mathbf{0}$ . Then $|\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}| = |\mathbf{0} \cdot \mathbf{B}| = 0 = 0 \cdot |\mathbf{B}| = |\mathbf{0}| |\mathbf{B}|$ . Case 2. $\mathbf{A} \neq \mathbf{0}$ . Let $u = \mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{A}$ and $v = \mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}$ , and let $\mathbf{C} = u\mathbf{B} - v\mathbf{A}$ . Then, $\mathbf{C} \cdot \mathbf{C} = u^2 (\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}) - 2uv(\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}) + v^2 (\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{A}) = u^2 (\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}) - uv^2 = u[u(\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}) - v^2]$ . Since $\mathbf{A} \neq \mathbf{0}$ , $u > 0$ . In addition, $\mathbf{C} \cdot \mathbf{C} \geq 0$ . Thus, $u(\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}) - v^2 \geq 0$ , $u(\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}) \geq v^2$ , $\sqrt{u}\sqrt{\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}} \geq |v|$ , $|\mathbf{A}| |\mathbf{B}| \geq |\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}|$ .
