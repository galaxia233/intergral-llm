【1793】 $\int \left(\frac{\ln x}{x}\right)^2\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \left(\frac{\ln x}{x}\right)^2\mathrm{d}x = -\int \ln^2 x\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) = -\frac{1}{x}\ln^2 x + \int \frac{1}{x} 2\ln x\frac{1}{x}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{x}\ln^2 x - 2\int \ln x\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)$

$$
= - \frac {1}{x} \ln^ {2} x - \frac {2}{x} \ln x + 2 \int \frac {1}{x} \cdot \frac {1}{x} d x = - \frac {1}{x} (\ln^ {2} x + 2 \ln x + 2) + C.
$$
