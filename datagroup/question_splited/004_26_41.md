26.41 Show that if interest is compounded continuously at an annual percentage $r$ the effective annual percentage rate of interest is $100(e^{0.01r} - 1)$ .

Let $s$ be the effective annual percentage rate. Then $y_0 e^{0.01r} = y_0 (1 + 0.01s)$ . So $e^{0.01r} = 1 + 0.01s$ , $0.01s = e^{0.01r} - 1$ , $s = 100(e^{0.01r} - 1)$ .
