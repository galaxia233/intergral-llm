【3048】$$ \left[ 3 0 4 8 \right] ^ {+} \quad \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {x \sin x}{1 - 2 \alpha \cos x + \alpha^ {2}} d x.$$

提示 利用 2864 题的结果.

解利用 2864 题的结果，即得

$$
\frac {x \sin x}{1 - 2 \alpha \cos x + \alpha^ {2}} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \alpha^ {n} x \sin n x \quad (| \alpha | <   1).
$$

由于 $\int_0^\pi x\sin nx\mathrm{d}x = (-1)^{n - 1}\frac{\pi}{n}$ 所以，当 $|\alpha | <   1$ 时，就有

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {x \sin x \mathrm {d} x}{1 - 2 \alpha \cos x + \alpha^ {2}} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \alpha^ {n} \frac {\pi}{n} = \pi \ln (1 + \alpha).
$$

当 $|\alpha| > 1$ 时， $\left|\frac{1}{\alpha}\right| < 1$

$$
\frac {x \sin x}{1 - 2 \alpha \cos x + \alpha^ {2}} = \frac {1}{\alpha^ {2}} \cdot \frac {x \sin x}{1 - 2 \left(\frac {1}{\alpha}\right) \cos x + \left(\frac {1}{\alpha}\right) ^ {2}}.
$$

利用以上结果，即得：当 $|\alpha| > 1$ 时， $\int_{0}^{\pi} \frac{x \sin x}{1 - 2\alpha \cos x + \alpha^{2}} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi}{\alpha^{2}} \ln \left(1 + \frac{1}{\alpha}\right)$ .
