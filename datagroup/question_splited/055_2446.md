【2446】 $r = a\varphi$ （阿基米德螺线） $(0\leqslant \varphi \leqslant 2\pi)$

解 所求的弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \sqrt {a ^ {2} \varphi^ {2} + a ^ {2}} d \varphi = a \left\{\frac {\varphi}{2} \sqrt {\varphi^ {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{2} \ln (\varphi + \sqrt {\varphi^ {2} + 1}) \right\} \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \\ = a \left\{\pi \sqrt {1 + 4 \pi^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} \ln (2 \pi + \sqrt {1 + 4 \pi^ {2}}) \right\}. \\ \end{array}
$$
