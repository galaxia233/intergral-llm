20.31 $\int_{1}^{2}\frac{x^7 - 2x + 1}{4x^3} dx.$

$\int_{1}^{2}\frac{x^7 - 2x + 1}{4x^3} dx = \frac{1}{4}\int_{1}^{2}\left(x^4 -2x^{-2} + x^{-3}\right)dx = \frac{1}{4}\left(\frac{1}{5} x^5 +2x^{-1} - \frac{1}{2} x^{-2}\right)\big]_1^2 = \frac{1}{4} [(32\frac{32}{5} +1 - \frac{1}{8}) - (\frac{1}{5} +2 - \frac{1}{2})]$ $= \frac{1}{4} (\frac{31}{5} -1 + \frac{3}{8}) = \frac{223}{160}.$
