27.21 Find the domain and range of the function $f(x) = \cos (\tan^{-1}x)$ .

Since the domain of $\tan^{-1}x$ is the whole set $\mathcal{R}$ of real numbers and $\cos u$ is defined for all $u$ , the domain of $f$ is $\mathcal{R}$ . The values of $\tan^{-1}x$ form the interval $(- \pi / 2, \pi / 2)$ , and the cosines of angles in that interval form the interval $(0, 1]$ , which is, therefore, the range of $f$ .
