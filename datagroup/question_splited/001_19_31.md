19.31 Evaluate $\int \frac{\cos 3x}{\sin^2 3x} dx.$

Let $u = \sin 3x$ . By the chain rule, $du = 3\cos 3xdx$ . So,

$$
\int \frac {\cos 3 x}{\sin^ {2} 3 x} d x = \int \frac {1}{u ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {1}{3} d u = \frac {1}{3} \int u ^ {- 2} d u = \frac {1}{3} (- u ^ {- 1}) + C = - \frac {1}{3 u} + C = - \frac {1}{3 \sin 3 x} + C = - \frac {1}{3} \csc 3 x + C.
$$
