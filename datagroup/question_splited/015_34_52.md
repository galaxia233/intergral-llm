34.94 Define the curvature $\kappa$ and radius of curvature $\rho$ of a curve $\mathbf{R}(t)$ .

As in Problem 34.89, let $\phi$ denote the angle between the velocity vector $\mathbf{R}'(t)$ and the positive $x$ -axis. The curvature $\kappa$ is defined as $d\phi / ds$ , where $s$ is the arc length. The curvature measures how fast the tangent vector turns as a point moves along the curve. The radius of curvature is defined as $\rho = |1 / \kappa|$ .
