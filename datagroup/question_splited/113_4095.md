【4095】求函数 $f(x, y, z) = \mathrm{e}^{\sqrt{\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} + \frac{z^2}{c^2}}}$ 在区域 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} + \frac{z^2}{c^2} \leq 1$ 内的平均值.

解 由于区域 $V: \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} + \frac{z^2}{c^2} \leqslant 1$ 为椭球，其体积等于 $\frac{4}{3}\pi abc$ ，故平均值为

$$
f _ {\text {平 均}} = \frac {3}{4 \pi a b c} \iiint_ {V} \mathrm {e} \sqrt {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} + \frac {z ^ {2}}{c ^ {2}}} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z.
$$

若作变换 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos \psi, y = brs\sin \varphi \cos \psi, z = cr\sin \psi$ ，并利用对称性，则有

$$
f _ {\text {平 均}} = \frac {3}{4 \pi a b c} \cdot 8 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \mathrm {d} \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \mathrm {d} \psi \int_ {0} ^ {1} a b c e ^ {r} r ^ {2} \cos \psi \mathrm {d} r = 3 \left(\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos \psi \mathrm {d} \psi\right) \left(\int_ {0} ^ {1} r ^ {2} e ^ {r} \mathrm {d} r\right) = 3 (\mathrm {e} - 2).
$$
