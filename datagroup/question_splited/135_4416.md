【4416】求场 $u = \frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2}$ 在已知点 $M(x,y,z)$ 处沿此点的径向量 $\pmb{r}$ 之方向的导数.
在怎样的情况下，此导数等于梯度的大小？
解 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial r} = \frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\cos \alpha +\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\cos \beta +\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}\cos \gamma$ 其中 $\cos \alpha = \frac{x}{r},\cos \beta = \frac{y}{r},\cos \gamma = \frac{z}{r},r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}.$ 于是，
$$
\frac {\partial u}{\partial r} = \frac {2 x}{a ^ {2}} \frac {x}{r} + \frac {2 y}{b ^ {2}} \frac {y}{r} + \frac {2 z}{c ^ {2}} \frac {z}{r} = \frac {2 u}{r}.
$$

又 $|\operatorname{grad} u| = 2\sqrt{\frac{x^2}{a^4} + \frac{y^2}{b^4} + \frac{z^2}{c^4}}$ 。要 $|\operatorname{grad} u| = \frac{\partial u}{\partial r}$ ，只要 $\frac{u}{r} = \sqrt{\frac{x^2}{a^4} + \frac{y^2}{b^4} + \frac{z^2}{c^4}}$ ，即只要 $a = b = c$ ，此即所求的解。
