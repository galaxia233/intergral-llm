【4207】 $\iint_{\substack{x_1\geqslant 0, x_2\geqslant 0, \dots, x_n\geqslant 0 \\ x_1 + x_2 + \dots + x_n\leqslant 1}} \sqrt{x_1 + x_2 + \cdots + x_n} \mathrm{d}x_1 \mathrm{d}x_2 \dots \mathrm{d}x_n.$

解 作代换 $x_{1} = u_{1}(1 - u_{2})$

![](images/0bcad79c6faa7c27ea8a5039220f44306abb637185508871448dd3d39a0960b1.jpg)

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {2} = u _ {1} u _ {2} (1 - u _ {3}) \\ x _ {n - 1} = u _ {1} u _ {2} \dots u _ {n - 1} (1 - u _ {n}), \\ x _ {n} = u _ {1} u _ {2} \dots u _ {n}, \\ \end{array}
$$

则由 $x_{1} \geqslant 0, x_{2} \geqslant 0, \cdots, x_{n} \geqslant 0, x_{1} + x_{2} + \cdots + x_{n} \leqslant 1$ 知 $0 \leqslant u_{i} \leqslant 1 (i = 1, 2, \cdots, n)$ ，且有

$$
I = \left| \begin{array}{c c c c} 1 - u _ {2} & u _ {2} (1 - u _ {3}) & \dots u _ {2} u _ {3} \dots u _ {n - 1} (1 - u _ {n}) & u _ {2} u _ {3} \dots u _ {n} \\ - u _ {1} & u _ {1} (1 - u _ {3}) & \dots u _ {1} u _ {3} \dots u _ {n - 1} (1 - u _ {n}) & u _ {1} u _ {3} \dots u _ {n} \\ 0 & - u _ {1} u _ {2} & \dots & \dots \\ \vdots & \vdots & \vdots & \vdots \\ 0 & 0 & \dots u _ {1} u _ {2} \dots u _ {n - 2} (1 - u _ {n}) & u _ {1} \dots u _ {n - 2} u _ {n} \\ 0 & 0 & \dots - u _ {1} \dots u _ {n - 1} & u _ {1} \dots u _ {n - 1} \end{array} \right|
$$

如在每一列的元素上加上所有以后各列相应的元素，则在对角线下面的全部元素都等于零，而在对角线上的元素就等于 $1, u_{1}, u_{1}u_{2}, \dots, u_{1}\dots u_{n-1}$ 。因此，得

$$
I = u _ {1} ^ {n - 1} u _ {2} ^ {n - 2} \dots u _ {n - 1}.
$$

于是，最后得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {x _ {1} \geqslant 0, x _ {2} \geqslant 0, \dots , x _ {n} \geqslant 0} \sqrt {x _ {1} + x _ {2} + \cdots + x _ {n}} d x _ {1} d x _ {2} \dots d x _ {n} = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \dots \int_ {0} ^ {1} u _ {1} ^ {n - \frac {1}{2}} u _ {2} ^ {n - 2} \dots u _ {n - 1} d u _ {1} d u _ {2} \dots d u _ {n - 1} d u _ {n} \\ = \frac {2}{(n - 1) ! (2 n + 1)}. \\ \end{array}
$$
