【2087】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{\mathrm{e}^x - 1}}.$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{\mathrm{e}^x - 1}} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}}\sqrt{1 - (\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{2}})^2}} = -2\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{2}})}{\sqrt{1 - (\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{2}})^2}} = -2\arcsin (\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{2}}) + C.$
