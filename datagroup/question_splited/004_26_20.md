26.20 The number of bacteria in a certain culture is growing exponentially. If 100 bacteria are present initially and 400 are present after 1 hour, how many bacteria are present after $3\frac{1}{2}$ hours?

The growth equation is $y = 100e^{Kt}$ . The given information tells us that $400 = 100e^{K}, \quad 4 = e^{K}$ . At $t = 3.5$ , $y = 100e^{3.5K} = 100(e^{K})^{3.5} = 100(4)^{7/2} = 100(2)^{7} = 100(128) = 12,800$ .
