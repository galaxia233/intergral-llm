【1920】 $\int \frac{x^4 + 1}{x^6 + 1} dx.$

解 $\int \frac{x^4 + 1}{x^6 + 1}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{(x^4 - x^2 + 1) + x^2}{x^6 + 1}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{x^4 - x^2 + 1}{(x^2 + 1)(x^4 - x^2 + 1)}\mathrm{d}x + \int \frac{x^2\mathrm{d}x}{x^6 + 1}$

$$
= \int \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 1} d x + \frac {1}{3} \int \frac {d \left(x ^ {3}\right)}{\left(x ^ {3}\right) ^ {2} + 1} = \arctan x + \frac {1}{3} \arctan \left(x ^ {3}\right) + C.
$$
