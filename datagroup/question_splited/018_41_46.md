41.46 Describe the level surfaces of $f(x, y, z) = 3x^{2} + 5y^{2} - z^{2}$ .

The level surfaces $3x^{2} + 5y^{2} - z^{2} = k > 0$ are hyperboloids of one sheet around the $z$ -axis. The level surfaces $3x^{2} + 5y^{2} - z^{2} = k \leq 0$ are hyperboloids of two sheets around the $z$ -axis. There is a level surface through every point.
