【4092】 计算积分 $\iiint_{V} x^{2} \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z$

其中 $V$ 是曲面 $z = ay^{2}, z = by^{2}, y > 0 (0 < a < b), z = \alpha x, z = \beta x (0 < \alpha < \beta), x = h (h > 0)$ 所围的区域.

解题思路 作变量代换 $\frac{z}{y^2} = u, \frac{z}{x} = v, z = w$ ，则有 $x = \frac{w}{v}, y = \sqrt{\frac{w}{u}}, z = w$ 及 $|I| = \frac{w\sqrt{w}}{2u\sqrt{uv^2}}$ ，且区域 $V$ 为 $a \leqslant u \leqslant b, \alpha \leqslant v \leqslant \beta, 0 \leqslant w \leqslant h$ ，且 $|I| = \frac{\omega\sqrt{\omega}}{2u\sqrt{uv^2}}$ .

解 作变换 $\frac{z}{y^2} = u, \frac{z}{x} = v, z = w$ ，则 $x = \frac{w}{v}, y = \sqrt{\frac{w}{u}}, z = w.$ 从而，积分域变为

$$
V: a \leqslant u \leqslant b, \alpha \leqslant v \leqslant \beta , 0 \leqslant w \leqslant h,
$$

且雅可比行列式

$$
I = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} 0 & - \frac {w}{v ^ {2}} & \frac {1}{v} \\ \frac {- \sqrt {w}}{2 u \sqrt {u}} & 0 & \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {u w}} \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{array} \right| = \frac {- w \sqrt {w}}{2 u \sqrt {u} v ^ {2}}.
$$

于是， $\iiint_{V} x^{2} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y \mathrm{d}z = \int_{0}^{h} w^{\frac{7}{2}} \mathrm{d}w \int_{a}^{\beta} \frac{1}{v^{4}} \mathrm{d}v \int_{a}^{b} \frac{1}{2u\sqrt{u}} \mathrm{d}u = \frac{2}{27}\left(\frac{1}{\alpha^{3}} - \frac{1}{\beta^{3}}\right)\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{a}} - \frac{1}{\sqrt{b}}\right)h^{4}\sqrt{h}.$
