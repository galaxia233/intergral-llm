【2043】 $\int \frac{\sin x - \cos x}{\sin x + 2\cos x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解此为2042题的特例，这里 $a_1 = 1$ ， $b_{1} = -1$ ， $a = 1$ ， $b = 2$

$$
A = \frac {a a _ {1} + b b _ {1}}{a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} = \frac {1 - 2}{1 + 4} = - \frac {1}{5}, B = \frac {a b _ {1} - a _ {1} b}{a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} = \frac {- 1 - 2}{1 + 4} = - \frac {3}{5}.
$$

代入得 $\int \frac{\sin x - \cos x}{\sin x + 2\cos x}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{x}{5} -\frac{3}{5}\ln |\sin x + 2\cos x| + C.$
