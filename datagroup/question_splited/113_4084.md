【4084】 $\int_{0}^{x}\mathrm{d}\xi \int_{0}^{\xi}\mathrm{d}\eta \int_{0}^{\eta}f(\zeta)\mathrm{d}\zeta$

解 $\int_{0}^{x}\mathrm{d}\xi \int_{0}^{\xi}\mathrm{d}\eta \int_{0}^{\eta}f(\zeta)\mathrm{d}\zeta = \int_{0}^{x}\mathrm{d}\xi \int_{0}^{\xi}\mathrm{d}\zeta \int_{\zeta}^{\xi}f(\zeta)\mathrm{d}\eta = \int_{0}^{x}\mathrm{d}\xi \int_{0}^{\xi}f(\zeta)(\xi -\zeta)\mathrm{d}\zeta = \int_{0}^{x}\mathrm{d}\zeta \int_{\zeta}^{x}f(\zeta)(\xi -\zeta)\mathrm{d}\xi$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {x} f (\zeta) (x - \zeta) ^ {2} d \zeta .
$$
