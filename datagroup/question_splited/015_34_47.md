34.89 Let $\phi$ be the angle between the velocity vector and the positive $x$ -axis. Show that $|d\mathbf{T} / d\phi| = 1$ .

Since $\mathbf{T}$ is a unit vector in the same direction as the velocity vector, $\mathbf{T} = (\cos \phi, \sin \phi)$ . Hence, $d\mathbf{T} / d\phi = (-\sin \phi, \cos \phi)$ and $|d\mathbf{T} / d\phi| = 1$ .
