【2213】 $\int_0^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + \varepsilon\cos x}$ （ $0\leqslant \varepsilon <  1)$

解 令 $\tan \frac{x}{2} = t$ ，并记 $a = \sqrt{\frac{1 - \epsilon}{1 + \epsilon}}$ ，则有

$$
\int \frac {d x}{1 + \varepsilon \cos x} = \frac {2}{(1 + \varepsilon) a} \int \frac {d (a t)}{1 + (a t) ^ {2}} = \frac {2}{\sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2}}} \arctan (a \tan \frac {x}{2}) + C,
$$

故 $\int_{0}^{2\pi}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + \epsilon\cos x} = \frac{2}{\sqrt{1 - \epsilon^2}}\left[\arctan (a\tan \frac{x}{2})\bigg|_{0}^{\pi -0} + \arctan (a\tan \frac{x}{2})\bigg|_{\pi +0}^{2\pi -0}\right] = \frac{2\pi}{\sqrt{1 - \epsilon^2}}.$
