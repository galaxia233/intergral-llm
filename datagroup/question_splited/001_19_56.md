19.56 Find $\int \cos^3 5x\sin^2 5xdx$

Let $u = \sin 5x$ , $du = 5\cos 5xdx$ . Note that $\cos^2 5x = 1 - u^2$ . So, $\int \cos^3 5x\sin^2 5xdx = \int (1 - u^2)u^2$ . $\frac{1}{5} du = \frac{1}{5}\int (u^2 - u^4)du = \frac{1}{5} (\frac{1}{5} u^3 - \frac{1}{5} u^5) + C = \frac{1}{15}\sin^3 5x - \frac{1}{25}\sin^5 5x + C.$
