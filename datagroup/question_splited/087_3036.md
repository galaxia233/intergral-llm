【3036】 $\int_0^1\frac{\ln(1 + x)}{x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int_{0}^{1}\frac{\ln(1 + x)}{x}\mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{1}\frac{x - \frac{x^2}{2} + \frac{x^3}{3} - \cdots}{x}\mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{1}\left(1 - \frac{x}{2} +\frac{x^2}{3} -\dots\right)\mathrm{d}x = 1 - \frac{1}{2^2} +\frac{1}{3^2} -\dots = \frac{\pi^2}{12}$

\*) 利用 2961 题的结果.
