$$
\mathbf {A} \cdot (\mathbf {B} \times \mathbf {C}) = \left| \begin{array}{l l l} a _ {1} & a _ {2} & a _ {3} \\ b _ {1} & b _ {2} & b _ {3} \\ c _ {1} & c _ {2} & c _ {3} \end{array} \right|
$$

where $\mathbf{A} = (a_{1}, a_{2}, a_{3}), \mathbf{B} = (b_{1}, b_{2}, b_{3}), \mathbf{C} = (c_{1}, c_{2}, c_{3})$ .

By Problem 40.40, the cofactors of the first row are the respective components of $\mathbf{B} \times \mathbf{C}$ .
