39.35 Exhibit the nth nonzero term of the Maclaurin series for $e^{-x^2}$

$$
e ^ {x} = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {x ^ {n}}{n !}. \quad \text {T h e n}, \quad e ^ {- x ^ {2}} = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {x ^ {2 n}}{n !}. \quad \text {T h e n t h n o n z e r o t e r m i s} \quad (- 1) ^ {n - 1} x ^ {2 n - 2} / (n - 1)!
$$
