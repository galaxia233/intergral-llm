29.7 $\int \tan^2{\frac{x}{2}}dx.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {L e t} \quad x = 2 u, \quad d x = 2 d u. \quad \text {T h e n} \quad \int \tan^ {2} \frac {x}{2} d x = 2 \int \tan^ {2} u d u = 2 \int (\sec^ {2} u - 1) d u = 2 (\tan u - u) + C = \\ 2 \left(\tan \frac {x}{2} - \frac {x}{2}\right) + C = 2 \tan \frac {x}{2} - x + C. \end{array}
$$
