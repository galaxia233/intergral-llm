【2146】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(2 + \sin x)^2}$

解设 $\tan \frac{x}{2} = t$ ，不妨限制 $-\pi < x < \pi$ ，则 $\sin x = \frac{2t}{1 + t^2}$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = \frac{2\mathrm{dt}}{1 + t^2}$ 代人得

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(2 + \sin x) ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {1 + t ^ {2}}{(1 + t + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} t = \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {(1 + t + t ^ {2}) - \frac {1}{2} (2 t + 1) + \frac {1}{2}}{(1 + t + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} t
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{1 + t + t ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{4} \int \frac {(2 t + 1) \mathrm {d} t}{(1 + t + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{4} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{(1 + t + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 t + 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + \frac {1}{4 (1 + t + t ^ {2})} + \frac {1}{4} \left[ \frac {2 t + 1}{3 (1 + t + t ^ {2})} + \frac {4}{3 \sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 t + 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) \right] ^ {\prime \prime} + C _ {1} \\ = \frac {4}{3 \sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {1 + 2 \tan \frac {x}{2}}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + \frac {\cos x}{3 (2 + \sin x)} ^ {* *}) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用 1921 题的递推公式.

$$
\begin{array}{l} \begin{array}{r l} * *) & \frac {1}{4 (1 + t + t ^ {2})} + \frac {2 t + 1}{1 2 (1 + t + t ^ {2})} = \frac {t + 2}{6 (1 + t + t ^ {2})} = \frac {1}{6} \cdot \frac {\frac {\sin \frac {x}{2} + 2 \cos \frac {x}{2}}{\cos \frac {x}{2}}}{\frac {1 + \sin \frac {x}{2} \cos \frac {x}{2}}{\cos^ {2} \frac {x}{2}}} \end{array} \\ = \frac {1}{6} \cdot \frac {\frac {1}{2} \sin x + 1 + \cos x}{\frac {1}{2} \sin x + 1} = \frac {1}{6} + \frac {\cos x}{3 (2 + \sin x)}. \\ \end{array}
$$
