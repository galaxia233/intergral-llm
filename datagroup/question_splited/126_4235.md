【4235】 $x^{2} + y^{2} = cz, \frac{y}{x} = \tan \frac{z}{c}$ 从 $O(0,0,0)$ 到 $A(x_{0},y_{0},z_{0})$

提示 取曲线的参数方程为 $x = \sqrt{cz}\cos \frac{z}{c}, y = \sqrt{cz}\sin \frac{z}{c}, z = z.$

解取曲线的参数方程为 $x = \sqrt{cz}\cos \frac{z}{c},y = \sqrt{cz}\sin \frac{z}{c},z = z$ ，则弧长的微分为

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathrm {d} s = \left[ \left(\frac {\sqrt {c}}{2 \sqrt {z}} \cos \frac {z}{c} - \sqrt {\frac {z}{c}} \sin \frac {z}{c}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\sqrt {c}}{2 \sqrt {z}} \sin \frac {z}{c} - \sqrt {\frac {z}{c}} \cos \frac {z}{c}\right) ^ {2} + 1 \right] ^ {\frac {1}{2}} \mathrm {d} z = \sqrt {\frac {c}{4 z} + \frac {z}{c} + 1} \mathrm {d} z \\ = \frac {2 z + c}{\sqrt {4 c z}} d z. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，弧长为

$$
s = \int_ {0} ^ {z _ {0}} \frac {2 z + c}{\sqrt {4 c z}} d z = \int_ {0} ^ {z _ {0}} \sqrt {\frac {z}{c}} d z + \int_ {0} ^ {z _ {0}} \frac {\sqrt {c}}{2 \sqrt {z}} d z = \sqrt {c z _ {0}} \left(1 + \frac {2 z _ {0}}{3 c}\right).
$$
