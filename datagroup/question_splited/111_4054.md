【4054】 $x^{\frac{2}{3}} + y^{\frac{2}{3}} = a^{\frac{2}{3}} \quad (x > 0, y > 0)$ .

解 质量和对 $Oy$ 轴的一次矩分别为

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {a} d x \int_ {0} ^ {\left(a ^ {\frac {2}{3}} - x ^ {2/3}\right) ^ {3/2}} d y = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left(a ^ {\frac {2}{3}} - x ^ {\frac {2}{3}}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} d x = 3 a ^ {2} \rho \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {4} t \cos^ {2} t d t ^ {*}) = 3 a ^ {2} \rho \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (\sin^ {4} t - \sin^ {6} t) d t \\ = 3 a ^ {2} \rho \left(\frac {3}{4} \cdot \frac {1}{2} - \frac {5}{6} \cdot \frac {3}{4} \cdot \frac {1}{2}\right) \frac {\pi}{2} = \frac {3 \pi a ^ {2} \rho}{3 2}, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
M _ {y} = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {a} x \mathrm {d} x \int_ {0} ^ {\left(a ^ {\frac {2}{3}} - x ^ {2/3}\right) \frac {3}{2}} \mathrm {d} y = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {a} x \left(a ^ {\frac {2}{3}} - x ^ {\frac {2}{3}}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \mathrm {d} x = 3 a ^ {3} \rho \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {4} t \cos^ {5} t \mathrm {d} t = \frac {8 a ^ {3} \rho}{1 0 5}.
$$

于是，质心的横坐标为 $x_0 = \frac{M_y}{M} = \frac{256a}{315\pi}.$

由关于直线 $y = x$ 的对称性知， $x_0 = y_0 = \frac{256a}{315\pi}$

\*）作代换 $x = a\cos^3 t$
