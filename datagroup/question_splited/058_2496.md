【2496】 $x = a\cos^3 t$ ， $y = a\sin^3 t$ 绕直线 $y = x$

解 先求 $\mathbf{ds}$

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {x _ {t} ^ {\prime 2} + y _ {t} ^ {\prime 2}} \mathrm {d} t = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 3 a \sin t \cos t \mathrm {d} t, & \frac {\pi}{4} \leqslant t \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \\ - 3 a \sin t \cos t \mathrm {d} t, & \frac {\pi}{2} \leqslant t \leqslant \frac {3 \pi}{4}. \end{array} \right.
$$

利用对称性，并作旋转，即得所求的表面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} P = 2 \left[ 2 \pi \int_ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {y - x}{\sqrt {2}} \sqrt {x _ {t} ^ {\prime 2} + y _ {t} ^ {\prime 2}} d t + \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {3 \pi}{4}} \frac {y - x}{\sqrt {2}} \sqrt {x _ {t} ^ {\prime 2} + y _ {t} ^ {\prime 2}} d t \right] \\ = \frac {4 \pi}{\sqrt {2}} \left[ \int_ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (a \sin^ {3} t - a \cos^ {3} t) 3 a \sin t \cos t d t - \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {3 \pi}{4}} (a \sin^ {3} t - a \cos^ {3} t) 3 a \sin t \cos t d t \right] \\ = \frac {1 2 \pi a ^ {2}}{\sqrt {2}} \left[ \left(\frac {1}{5} \sin^ {5} t + \frac {1}{5} \cos^ {5} t\right) \left| \frac {\pi}{4} - \left(\frac {1}{5} \sin^ {5} t + \frac {1}{5} \cos^ {5} t\right) \right| \frac {3 \pi}{2} \right] \\ = \frac {3}{5} \pi a ^ {2} (4 \sqrt {2} - 1). \\ \end{array}
$$
