46.40 Find a procedure for solving a Bernoulli equation

$$
y ^ {\prime} + p (x) y = q (x) y ^ {n} \quad (n \neq 1) \tag {1}
$$

Let $v = y^{1 - n}$ . Then $v' = (1 - n)y^{-n}y'$ . From (1), $(1 - n)y^{-n}y' + (1 - n)p(x)y^{1 - n} = (1 - n)q(x)$ . Hence,

$$
v ^ {\prime} + (1 - n) p (x) v = (1 - n) q (x) \tag {2}
$$

Now we can use an integrating factor. Let $\rho = \exp \int (1 - n)p(x)dx$ . Then $v = \frac{1}{p} [\int (1 - n)\rho q(x)dx + C]$ , and $y = v^{1 / (1 - n)}$ .
