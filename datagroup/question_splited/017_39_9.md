39.9 Find the first three nonzero terms of the Maclaurin series for $\tan x$ .

Let $f(x) = \tan x$ . Then $f'(x) = \sec^2 x$ , $f''(x) = 2 \tan x \sec^2 x$ , $f'''(x) = 2 (\sec^4 x + 2 \tan^2 x + 2 \tan^4 x)$ , $f^{(4)}(x) = 8 (\tan x \sec^2 x)(2 + 3 \tan^2 x)$ , $f^{(5)}(x) = 48 \tan^2 x \sec^4 x + 8(2 + 3 \tan^2 x) (\sec^4 x + 2 \tan^2 x + 2 \tan^4 x)$ . So $f(0) = 0$ , $f'(0) = 1$ , $f''(0) = 0$ , $f'''(0) = 2$ , $f^{(4)}(0) = 0$ , $f^{(5)}(0) = 16$ . Thus, the Maclaurin series is $x + \frac{1}{3} x^3 + \frac{2}{15} x^3 + \dots$ .
