【1718】 $\int \frac{\sin x\cos x}{\sin^4x + \cos^4x}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $\frac{\sin x\cos x}{\sin^4x + \cos^4x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\cdot \frac{\sin 2x\mathrm{d}x}{1 - \frac{1}{2}\sin^22x} = -\frac{1}{2}\cdot \frac{\mathrm{d}(\cos 2x)}{1 + \cos^22x}.$

解 $\int \frac{\sin x\cos x}{\sin^4x + \cos^4x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \frac{\sin 2x\mathrm{d}x}{1 - \frac{1}{2}\sin^22x} = -\frac{1}{2}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\cos 2x)}{1 + \cos^22x} = -\frac{1}{2}\arctan (\cos 2x) + C.$
