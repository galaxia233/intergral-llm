【3018】 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{\sin nx}{n}.$

解 注意到 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{z^n}{n} = \ln \frac{1}{1-z}$ , 其中 $z = e^{iz}$ , 以及

$$
\begin{array}{l} \ln \frac {1}{1 - z} = - \ln (1 - \cos x - i \sin x) = - \frac {1}{2} \ln (2 - 2 \cos x) + i \arctan \frac {\sin x}{1 - \cos x} ^ {*} \\ = - \ln \left| 2 \sin \frac {x}{2} \right| + i \arctan \frac {\sin x}{1 - \cos x} \tag {1} \\ \end{array}
$$

及

$$
\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {z ^ {n}}{n} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\cos n x}{n} + i \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\sin n x}{n}, \tag {2}
$$

比较 (1)，（2)两式实数部分及虚数部分，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\cos n x}{n} = - \ln \left| 2 \sin \frac {x}{2} \right| \quad (0 <   x <   2 \pi), \tag {3} \\ \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\sin n x}{n} = \arctan \frac {\sin x}{1 - \cos x} = \arctan \left(\cot \frac {x}{2}\right) = \arctan \left(\tan \frac {\pi - x}{2}\right) = \frac {\pi - x}{2} \quad (0 <   x <   2 \pi). \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 其中用到 $\ln z = \ln (|z| \mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i} \arg z}) = \ln |z| + \mathrm{i} \arg z.$ 若 $z = x + \mathrm{i} y$ ，则 $\ln |z| = \frac{1}{2} \ln (x^{2} + y^{2})$ ，而 $\arg z = \arctan \frac{y}{x}$ .
