【4104】 $a z = x^{2} + y^{2},z = \sqrt{x^{2} + y^{2}} (a > 0).$

解 对区域 $V$ 在 $Oxy$ 平面上的投影作极坐标变换

$$
x = r \cos \varphi , \quad y = r \sin \varphi ,
$$

则区域 $V$ 为

且有 $|I| = r.$ 于是，体积为

$$
V = \iint_ {r ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2}} d x d y \int_ {\frac {r ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}{a}} ^ {\sqrt {r ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} d z = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a} r d r \int_ {\frac {r ^ {2}}{a}} ^ {r} d z = 2 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left(r ^ {2} - \frac {r ^ {3}}{a}\right) d r = \frac {\pi a ^ {3}}{6}.
$$
