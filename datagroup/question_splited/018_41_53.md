41.53 Find $\lim_{(x,y)\to (0,0)}\frac{xy}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}}$ if it exists.

$|x| \leq \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$ ; hence,

$$
\left| \frac {x y}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} \right| \leq | y | \quad \text {a n d} \quad \frac {x y}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} \rightarrow 0 \quad \text {a s} (x, y) \rightarrow (0, 0)
$$
