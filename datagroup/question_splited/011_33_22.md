33.21 Show that $\mathbf{A} = (3, -6)$ , $\mathbf{B} = (4, 2)$ , and $\mathbf{C} = (-7, 4)$ are the sides of a right triangle.

$\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B} + \mathbf{C} = \mathbf{0}$ . Hence, $\mathbf{A}, \mathbf{B}, \mathbf{C}$ form a triangle. In addition, $\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} = 3 \cdot 4 + (-6) \cdot 2 = 0$ . Hence, $\mathbf{A} \perp \mathbf{B}$ .
