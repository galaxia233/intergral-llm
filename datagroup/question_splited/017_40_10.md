40.10 Show that every sphere has an equation of the form $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} + Ax + By + Cz + D = 0$ .

The sphere with center $(a, b, c)$ and radius $r$ has the equation $(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 + (z - c)^2 = r^2$ . Expanding, we obtain $x^2 - 2ax + a^2 + y^2 - 2by + b^2 + z^2 - 2cz + c^2 = r^2$ , which is equivalent to $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 - 2ax - 2by - 2cz + (a^2 + b^2 + c^2 - r^2) = 0$ .
