20.26 $\int_2^5\sqrt{x^3 - 4} x^5 dx.$

Let $u = x^3 - 4$ , $x^3 = u + 4$ , $du = 3x^2 dx$ . When $x = 2$ , $u = 4$ , and, when $x = 5$ , $u = 121$ . Then $\int_{2}^{5}\sqrt{x^3 - 4} x^5 dx = \frac{1}{3}\int_{4}^{121}\sqrt{u} (u + 4)du = \frac{1}{3}\int_{4}^{121}(u^{3 / 2} + 4u^{1 / 2})du = \frac{1}{3} (\frac{7}{5} u^{5 / 2} + \frac{8}{3} u^{3 / 2})]_4^{121} = \frac{2}{45} u^{3 / 2}(3u + 20)]_4^{121}$ $= \frac{2}{45} [(11)^3 (383) - 8(32)] = \frac{113,226}{5}$ .
