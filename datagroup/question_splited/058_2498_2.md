【2498】 $r^2 = a^2\cos 2\varphi$ ：（1）绕极轴；（2）绕轴 φ = \frac{\pi}{2}（3）绕轴 φ = \frac{\pi}{4}

(2) $x = a\sqrt{\cos 2\varphi}\cos \varphi \left(-\frac{\pi}{4} \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{4}\right)$ . 于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P = 2 \pi \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} a \sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi} \cos \varphi \frac {a}{\sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi}} d \varphi = 2 \pi a ^ {2} \sqrt {2}.
$$
