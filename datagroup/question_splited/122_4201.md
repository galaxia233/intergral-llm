【4201】设 $K(x,y)$ 为区域 $R[a \leqslant x \leqslant b, a \leqslant y \leqslant b]$ 内的连续函数，且

$$
K _ {n} (x, y) = \int_ {a} ^ {b} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \dots \int_ {a} ^ {b} K (x, t _ {1}) K (t _ {1}, t _ {2}) \dots K (t _ {n}, y) d t _ {1} d t _ {2} \dots d t _ {n},
$$

证明： $K_{n + m + 1}(x,y) = \int_a^b K_n(x,t)K_m(t,y)\mathrm{d}t.$

证

$$
\begin{array}{l} K _ {n + m + 1} (x, y) \\ = \int_ {a} ^ {b} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \dots \int_ {a} ^ {b} K (x, t _ {1}) K (t _ {1}, t _ {2}) \dots K (t _ {n}, t) K (t, z _ {1}) K (z _ {1}, z _ {2}) \dots K (z _ {m}, y) d t _ {1} d t _ {2} \dots d t _ {n} d t d z _ {1} d z _ {2} \dots d z _ {m} \\ = \int_ {a} ^ {b} \left\{\left[ \int_ {a} ^ {b} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \dots \int_ {a} ^ {b} K (x, t _ {1}) K (t _ {1}, t _ {2}) \dots K (t _ {n}, t) d t _ {1} d t _ {2} \dots d t _ {n} \right] \right. \\ \left. \cdot \left[ \int_ {a} ^ {b} \int_ {a} ^ {b} \dots \int_ {a} ^ {b} K (t, z _ {1}) K (z _ {1}, z _ {2}) \dots K (z _ {m}, y) d z _ {1} d z _ {2} \dots d z _ {m} \right] \right\} d t \\ = \int_ {u} ^ {b} K _ {n} (x, t) K _ {m} (t, y) d t. \\ \end{array}
$$
