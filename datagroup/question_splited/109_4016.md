【4016】 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}, x^{2} + y^{2} \geqslant a|x| (a > 0)$

解只需计算由下列曲面所围区域的体积：

$$
x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2} = a ^ {2}, \quad x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a | x |.
$$

若引用极坐标，则 $r^2 + z^2 = a^2$ ， $r^2 \leqslant a |r \cos \varphi|$ ，其体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V _ {1} = 8 \iint_ {\substack {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a r \\ x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0}} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = 8 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \mathrm {d} \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a \cos \varphi} r \sqrt {a ^ {2} - r ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} r = - \frac {8}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (a ^ {2} - r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \left| \begin{array}{c} a \cos \varphi \\ 0 \end{array} \right. \mathrm {d} \varphi \\ = \frac {8 a ^ {3}}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (1 - \sin^ {3} \varphi) d \varphi = \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3} - \frac {1 6 a ^ {3}}{9}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，所求的体积为 $V = \frac{4\pi a^3}{3} -\left(\frac{4\pi a^3}{3} -\frac{16a^3}{9}\right) = \frac{16a^3}{9}.$
