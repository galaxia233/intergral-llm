27.53 $\int \frac{x dx}{x^2 + 8x + 20}.$

By completing the square, $x^{2} + 8x + 20 = (x + 4)^{2} + 4$ . Let $u = x + 4$ , $du = dx$ . Then

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x d x}{x ^ {2} + 8 x + 2 0} = \int \frac {(u - 4) d u}{u ^ {2} + 4} = \int \frac {u d u}{u ^ {2} + 4} - 4 \int \frac {d u}{u ^ {2} + 4} = \frac {1}{2} \ln (u ^ {2} + 4) - 4 \cdot \frac {1}{2} \tan^ {- 1} \frac {u}{2} + C \\ = \frac {1}{2} \ln (x ^ {2} + 8 x + 2 0) - 2 \tan^ {- 1} \frac {x + 4}{2} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
