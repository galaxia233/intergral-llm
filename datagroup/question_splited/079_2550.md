【2550】 $\frac{1}{1\cdot 4} +\frac{1}{4\cdot 7} +\dots +\frac{1}{(3n - 2)(3n + 1)} +\dots .$

提示 注意 $\frac{1}{(3n - 2)(3n + 1)} = \frac{1}{3}\left(\frac{1}{3n - 2} -\frac{1}{3n + 1}\right)$ 即知 $S_{n} = \frac{1}{3}\left(1 - \frac{1}{3n + 1}\right).$

解 由于

$$
\begin{array}{l} S _ {n} = \frac {1}{1 \cdot 4} + \frac {1}{4 \cdot 7} + \dots + \frac {1}{(3 n - 2) (3 n + 1)} = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {1}{(3 k - 2) (3 k + 1)} = \frac {1}{3} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \left(\frac {1}{3 k - 2} - \frac {1}{3 k + 1}\right) \\ = \frac {1}{3} \left(1 - \frac {1}{3 n + 1}\right), \\ \end{array}
$$

故得 $S = \lim_{n\to \infty}S_n = \frac{1}{3}$
