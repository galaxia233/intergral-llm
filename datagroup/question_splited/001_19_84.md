19.84 Find the family of curves for which the slope of the tangent line at $(x,y)$ is $(1 + x) / (1 + y)$ .

$dy / dx = (1 + x) / (1 + y)$ . Then $\int (1 + y) dy = \int (1 + x) dx, \quad y + \frac{1}{2} y^2 = x + \frac{1}{2} x^2 + C, \quad x^2 - y^2 + 2x - 2y + C = 0, \quad (x + 1)^2 - (y + 1)^2 + C_1 = 0$ . This consists of two families of hyperbolas with center at $(-1, -1)$ .
