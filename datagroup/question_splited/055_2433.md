【2433】 $y = a\mathrm{ch}\frac{x}{a}$ 从点 $A(0,a)$ 至点 $B(b,h)$

解 所求的弧长为

$$
s = \int_ {0} ^ {b} \sqrt {1 + \mathrm {s h} ^ {2} \frac {x}{a}} \mathrm {d} x = \int_ {0} ^ {b} \operatorname {c h} \frac {x}{a} \mathrm {d} x = a \mathrm {s h} \frac {x}{a} \left| _ {0} ^ {b} = a \mathrm {s h} \frac {b}{a} = \sqrt {h ^ {2} - a ^ {2}}\right).
$$

$\ast)$ 由于 $h = a \operatorname{ch} \frac{b}{a}$ , 故 $\operatorname{sh} \frac{b}{a} = \sqrt{\operatorname{ch}^2 \frac{b}{a} - 1} = \frac{1}{a} \sqrt{h^2 - a^2}$ .
