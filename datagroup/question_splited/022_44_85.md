44.84 Find the center of mass of the first octant of the ball of radius $a$ , $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \leq a^2$ , if the density is numerically equal to $z$ .

The mass is one-eighth of that of the ball in Problem 44.77, or $\pi a^4 /16$ . The moment about the $xz$ -plane is

$$
\begin{array}{l} M _ { x z } = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \int_ {0} ^ {a} \underbrace {\rho \sin \phi \sin \theta} _ {y} \cdot \underbrace {\rho \cos \phi} _ {z} \cdot \rho^ {2} \sin \phi d \rho d \phi d \theta = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \rho^ {5} \sin^ {2} \phi \cos \phi \sin \theta ] _ {0} ^ {a} d \phi d \theta \\ = \frac {a ^ {5}}{5} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \frac {1}{3} \sin^ {3} \phi \sin \theta \bigg ] _ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} d \theta = \frac {a ^ {5}}{5} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \frac {1}{3} \sin \theta d \theta = \frac {a ^ {5}}{1 5} (- \cos \theta) \bigg ] _ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} = \frac {a ^ {5}}{1 5}. \\ \end{array}
$$
