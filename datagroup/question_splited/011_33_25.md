33.24 Find the two unit vectors that are parallel to the vector $7\mathbf{i} - \mathbf{j}$

$|7i - j| = \sqrt{(7)^2 + (-1)^2} = \sqrt{50} = 5\sqrt{2}$ . Hence, $A = \frac{1}{5\sqrt{2}} (7i - j) = \frac{7}{5\sqrt{2}} i - \frac{1}{5\sqrt{2}} j$ is a unit vector in the same direction as $7i - j$ , and $-A = -\frac{7}{5\sqrt{2}} i + \frac{1}{5\sqrt{2}} j$ is the unit vector in the opposite direction.
