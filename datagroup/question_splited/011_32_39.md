32.40 Let $\mathcal{R}$ be the region in the first quadrant under $xy = 9$ and to the right of $x = 1$ . Find the volume generated by revolving $\mathcal{R}$ about the $x$ -axis.

By the disk formula, $V = \pi \int_{1}^{\infty}\left(\frac{9}{x}\right)^{2}dx = 81\pi \int_{1}^{\infty}\frac{dx}{x^{2}} = 81\pi \lim_{v\to +\infty}\left(-\frac{1}{v} +1\right) = 81\pi .$
