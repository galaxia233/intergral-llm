【4265】 $\int_{(x_1,y_1)}^{(x_2,y_2)}\varphi (x)\mathrm{d}x + \psi (y)\mathrm{d}y$ ，其中 $\varphi$ 和 $\psi$ 为连续函数.

解 由于 $\varphi, \psi$ 是连续函数，故显然有

$$
\varphi (x) \mathrm {d} x + \psi (y) \mathrm {d} y = \mathrm {d} F (x) + \mathrm {d} G (y) = \mathrm {d} [ F (x) + G (y) ],
$$

其中 $F(x) = \int_{x_1}^x\varphi (u)\mathrm{d}u,G(y) = \int_{y_1}^y\psi (v)\mathrm{d}v.$ 从而， $\varphi (x)\mathrm{d}x + \psi (y)\mathrm{d}y$ 是函数 $F(x) + G(y)$ 的全微分，于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {(x _ {1}, y _ {1})} ^ {(x _ {2}, y _ {2})} \varphi (x) d x + \psi (y) d y = [ F (x) + G (y) ] \left| _ {(x _ {1}, y _ {1})} ^ {(x _ {2}, y _ {2})} = [ F (x _ {2}) + G (y _ {2}) ] - [ F (x _ {1}) + G (y _ {1}) ] \right. \\ = \int_ {x _ {1}} ^ {x _ {2}} \varphi (u) d u + \int_ {y _ {1}} ^ {y _ {2}} \psi (v) d v. \\ \end{array}
$$
