【2122】 $\int \sqrt{\mathrm{th}x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \sqrt{\mathrm{th}x}\mathrm{d}x = \int \sqrt{\frac{\mathrm{e}^x - \mathrm{e}^{-x}}{\mathrm{e}^x + \mathrm{e}^{-x}}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^x - \mathrm{e}^{-x}}{\sqrt{\mathrm{e}^{2x} - \mathrm{e}^{-2x}}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{2x}\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{\mathrm{e}^{4x} - 1}} -\int \frac{\mathrm{e}^{-2x}\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{1 - \mathrm{e}^{-4x}}}$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {2 x}\right)}{\sqrt {\left(\mathrm {e} ^ {2 x}\right) ^ {2} - 1}} + \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {- 2 x}\right)}{\sqrt {1 - \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {- 2 x}\right) ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{2} \ln \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {2 x} + \sqrt {\mathrm {e} ^ {4 x} - 1}\right) + \frac {1}{2} \arcsin \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {- 2 x}\right) + C.
$$
