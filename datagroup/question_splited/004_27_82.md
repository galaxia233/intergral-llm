27.82 Find the area under $y = 1 / (1 + 3x^2)$ and above the segment [0, 1].

$$
A = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {d x}{1 + 3 x ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{3} \int^ {1} \frac {d x}{\frac {1}{3} + x ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{3} \cdot \sqrt {3} \tan^ {- 1} \sqrt {3} x J _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {\sqrt {3}}{3} (\tan^ {- 1} \sqrt {3} - \tan^ {- 1} 0) = \frac {\sqrt {3}}{3} \left(\frac {\pi}{3} - 0\right) = \frac {\pi \sqrt {3}}{9}.
$$
