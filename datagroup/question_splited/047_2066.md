【2066】证明：若 $P(x)$ 为 $\pmb{n}$ 次多项式，则

$$
\int P (x) \mathrm {e} ^ {a x} \mathrm {d} x = \mathrm {e} ^ {a x} \left[ \frac {P (x)}{a} - \frac {P ^ {\prime} (x)}{a ^ {2}} + \dots + (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {P ^ {(n)} (x)}{a ^ {n + 1}} \right] + C.
$$

证 $\int P(x)\mathrm{e}^{ax}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{a}\int P(x)\mathrm{d}(\mathrm{e}^{ax}) = \frac{1}{a} P(x)\mathrm{e}^{ax} - \frac{1}{a}\int \mathrm{e}^{ax}P'(x)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{a} P (x) e ^ {a x} - \frac {1}{a ^ {2}} \int P ^ {\prime} (x) d \left(e ^ {a x}\right) = \frac {1}{a} P (x) e ^ {a x} - \frac {1}{a ^ {2}} P ^ {\prime} (x) e ^ {a x} + \frac {1}{a ^ {2}} \int e ^ {a x} P ^ {\prime \prime} (x) d x \\ = \dots = e ^ {a x} \left[ \frac {P (x)}{a} - \frac {P ^ {\prime} (x)}{a ^ {2}} + \dots + (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {P ^ {(n)} (x)}{a ^ {n + 1}} \right] + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

因为 $P(x)$ 为 $n$ 次多项式，所以， $P^{(n+1)}(x) \equiv 0.$ 从而，上述等式括号中的导数到 $P^{(n)}(x)$ 为止.
