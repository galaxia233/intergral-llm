40.6 Find the equation of a sphere $\mathcal{S}$ of radius $r$ and center $(a,b,c)$ .

A point $(x,y,z)$ is on $\mathcal{S}$ if and only if its distance from $(a,b,c)$ is $r$ , that is, if and only if $\sqrt{(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 + (z - c)^2} = r$ , or, equivalently, $(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 + (z - c)^2 = r^2$ .
