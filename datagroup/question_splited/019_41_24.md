41.90 Find the rectangular coordinates of the point with spherical coordinates $(3, \pi/2, \pi/2)$ .

1 Geometrically, it is easy to see that the point is $(0,3,0)$ . By calculation,

$$
x = 3 \sin \frac {\pi}{2} \cos \frac {\pi}{2} = 3 (1) (0) = 0 \quad y = 3 \sin \frac {\pi}{2} \sin \frac {\pi}{2} = 3 (1) (1) = 3 \quad z = 3 \cos \frac {\pi}{2} = 3 (0) = 0
$$
