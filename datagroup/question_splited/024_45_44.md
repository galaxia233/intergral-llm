45.44 Let $\mathcal{C}$ be any curve in the $xy$ -plane. Show that $\int_{\mathcal{C}} y \, dx + x \, dy$ depends only on the endpoints of $\mathcal{C}$ .

$$
\int_ {\mathcal {C}} y d x + x d y = \int_ {a} ^ {b} \left(y \frac {d x}{d t} + x \frac {d y}{d t}\right) d t = \int_ {a} ^ {b} \frac {d}{d t} (x y) d t = x y \Bigg ] _ {a} ^ {b} = x (b) y (b) - x (a) y (a).
$$
