40.28 Find the direction cosines of $\mathbf{A} = 3\mathbf{i} + 12\mathbf{j} + 4\mathbf{k}$ . [Recall that $\mathbf{i} = (1,0,0)$ , $\mathbf{j} = (0,1,0)$ , and $\mathbf{k} = (0,0,1)$ .]

$|\mathbf{A}| = \sqrt{9 + 144 + 16} = \sqrt{169} = 13$ . Hence, $\cos \alpha = \frac{3}{13}$ , $\cos \beta = \frac{12}{13}$ , $\cos \gamma = \frac{4}{13}$ .
