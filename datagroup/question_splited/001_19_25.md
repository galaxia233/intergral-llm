19.25 Find $\int x^{2}\sqrt[3]{x^{3} + 5} dx.$

Let $u = x^3 + 5, \quad du = 3x^2 dx$ . Then $\int x^2 \sqrt[3]{x^3 + 5} dx = \int \sqrt[3]{u} \cdot \frac{1}{3} du = \frac{1}{3} \int u^{1/3} du = \frac{1}{3} \left( \frac {3}{4} \right) u^{4/3} + C = \frac {1}{4} (x^3 + 5)^{4/3} + C = \frac {1}{4} (\sqrt[3]{x^3 + 5})^4 + C.$
