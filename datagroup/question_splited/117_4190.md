【4190】 $\iint_{x^2 - y^2 \leqslant r} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2}}.$

提示 由关于 $Ox$ 轴的对称性与被积函数的非负性，采用极坐标即可获解.

解 由关于 $(\mathcal{X})$ 轴的对称性与被积函数的非负性，采用极坐标，有

$$
\iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant r} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} = 2 \iint_ {r ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant r} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \mathrm {d} \theta \int_ {0} ^ {\cos \theta} \mathrm {d} r = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos \theta \mathrm {d} \theta = 2.
$$
