33.16 Write the vector of length 2 and direction $150^{\circ}$ in the form $a\mathbf{i} + b\mathbf{j}$ .

In general, the vector of length $r$ obtained by a counterclockwise rotation $\theta$ from the positive axis is given by $r(\cos \theta \mathbf{i} + \sin \theta \mathbf{j})$ . In this case, we have $2\left(-\frac{\sqrt{3}}{2}\mathbf{i} + \frac{1}{2}\mathbf{j}\right) = -\sqrt{3}\mathbf{i} + \mathbf{j}$ .
