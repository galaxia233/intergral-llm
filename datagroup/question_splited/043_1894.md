【1894】 $\int \frac{x^2\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 + 2x + 2)^2}$

提示 本题如不用奥斯特罗格拉茨基方法，可有更简单的方法。事实上，有

$$
\frac {x ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}} = \frac {(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) - (2 x + 2)}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}}.
$$

解 $Q = (x^{2} + 2x + 2)^{2}, Q_{1} = Q_{2} = x^{2} + 2x + 2.$

设 $\frac{x^2}{(x^2 + 2x + 2)^2} = \left(\frac{Ax + B}{x^2 + 2x + 2}\right)' + \frac{Cx + D}{x^2 + 2x + 2}$ ，从而，

$$
x ^ {2} \equiv A (x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) - 2 (x + 1) (A x + B) + (C x + D) (x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2).
$$

比较等式两端 $x$ 的同次幂系数，得

$$
\begin{array}{l l} x ^ {3} & C = 0, \\ x ^ {2} & - A + 2 C + D = 1, \\ x ^ {1} & - 2 B + 2 C + 2 D = 0, \\ x ^ {0} & 2 A - 2 B + 2 D = 0. \end{array}
$$

由此， $A = 0$ ， $B = 1$ ， $C = 0$ ， $D = 1$ .于是，

$$
\int \frac {x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} x}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2} + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2} = \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2} + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (x + 1)}{(x + 1) ^ {2} + 1} = \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2} + \arctan (x + 1) + C.
$$

本题如不用奥斯特罗格拉茨基方法，则更容易得出上述结果。事实上，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} x}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}} = \int \frac {(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) - (2 x + 2)}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} x = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2} - \int \frac {(2 x + 2) \mathrm {d} x}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}} \\ = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (x + 1)}{(x + 1) ^ {2} + 1} - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2)}{(x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2) ^ {2}} = \arctan (x + 1) + \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 2 x + 2} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
