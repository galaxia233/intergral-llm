【3905】把积分域 $S: \{x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1\}$ 分为有限个直径小于 $\delta$ 的可求积的子区域 $\Delta S_i (i = 1, 2, \dots , n)$ . 怎样的值 $\delta$ 能保证不等式

$$
\left| \iint_ {S} \sin (x + y) d S - \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \sin (x _ {i} + y _ {i}) \Delta S _ {i} \right| <   0. 0 0 1
$$

成立？其中 $(x_{i},y_{i})\in \Delta S_{i}$

解 记函数 $\sin (x + y)$ 在 $\Delta S_{i}$ 中的振幅为 $\omega_{i}$ ，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \iint_ {S} \sin (x + y) d S - \sum_ {t = 1} ^ {n} \sin (x _ {t} + y _ {t}) \Delta S _ {t} \right| = \left| \sum_ {t = 1} ^ {n} \iint_ {S _ {t}} [ \sin (x + y) - \sin (x _ {t} + y _ {t}) ] d S \right| \\ \leqslant \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \iint_ {\Delta S _ {i}} | \sin (x + y) - \sin (x _ {i} + y _ {i}) | d S \leqslant \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \iint_ {\Delta S _ {i}} \omega_ {i} d S = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \omega_ {i} \Delta S _ {i}. \\ \end{array}
$$

由于积分域 $S: \{x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 1\}$ 的面积等于 $\pi$ ，故只要 $\omega_t < \frac{0.001}{\pi}$ ，便能满足原不等式的要求。但因为

$$
\begin{array}{l} \omega_ {i} = \sup  _ {\substack {(x _ {i}, y _ {i}) \in \Delta S _ {i} \\ (x _ {i} ^ {\prime}, y _ {i} ^ {\prime}) \in \Delta S _ {i}}} | \sin (x _ {i} ^ {\prime} + y _ {i} ^ {\prime}) - \sin (x _ {i} + y _ {i}) | \leqslant \sup  _ {\substack {(x _ {i}, y _ {i}) \in \Delta S _ {i} \\ (x _ {i} ^ {\prime}, y _ {i} ^ {\prime}) \in \Delta S _ {i}}} | (x _ {i} ^ {\prime} + y _ {i} ^ {\prime}) - (x _ {i} + y _ {i}) | \\ \leqslant \sup  _ {\substack {(x _ {i}, y _ {i}) \in \Delta S _ {i} \\ (x _ {i} ^ {\prime}, y _ {i} ^ {\prime}) \in \Delta S _ {i}}} [ | x _ {i} ^ {\prime} - x _ {i} | + | y _ {i} ^ {\prime} - y _ {i} | ] \leqslant \sup  _ {\substack {(x _ {i}, y _ {i}) \in \Delta S _ {i} \\ (x _ {i} ^ {\prime}, y _ {i} ^ {\prime}) \in \Delta S _ {i}}} \sqrt {2 [ (x _ {i} ^ {\prime} - x _ {i}) ^ {2} + (y _ {i} ^ {\prime} - y _ {i}) ^ {2} ]} = \sqrt {2} \delta_ {i}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故只要取 $\delta < \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}\pi} \times 0.001 \approx 0.00022,$

即有 $\left|\iint_{S}\sin (x + y)\mathrm{d}S - \sum_{i = 1}^{n}\sin (x_i + y_i)\Delta S_i\right| <   0.001.$
