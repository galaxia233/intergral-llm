【4099】求 $\iiint_{x^2 + y^2 + z^2 \leqslant 1} x^m y^n z^p \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z$ ，其中 $m, n, p$ 为非负整数.

解 分两种情况：

（1）设 $m, n, p$ 中至少有一个是奇数。例如，设 $p$ 为奇数。于是，

$$
I = \iiint_ {r ^ {2} \cdot y ^ {2} \cdot z ^ {2} \leqslant 1} x ^ {m} y ^ {n} z ^ {p} d x d y d z = \iiint_ {r ^ {2} \cdot y ^ {2} + z ^ {2} \leqslant 1} x ^ {m} y ^ {n} z ^ {p} d x d y d z + \iiint_ {r ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2} \leqslant 1} x ^ {m} y ^ {n} z ^ {p} d x d y d z = I _ {1} + I _ {2}.
$$

今在积分 $I_{2}$ 中作变量代换 $x = u, y = v, z = -w$ ，则 $\frac{D(x, y, z)}{D(u, v, w)} = -1$ ，从而，注意到 $p$ 为奇数，可知

$$
I _ {2} = - \iiint_ {\substack {u ^ {2} + v ^ {2} + w ^ {2} \leqslant 1 \\ w \geqslant 0}} u ^ {m} v ^ {n} w ^ {p} \mathrm {d} u \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w = - I _ {1},
$$

于是， $I = I_{1} - I_{1} = 0$

（2）设 $m, n, p$ 均为偶数. 此时被积函数 $x^{m}y^{n}z^{p}$ 关于三个坐标平面皆对称. 于是，

$$
I = \iiint_ {r ^ {2} - y ^ {2} - z ^ {2} \leqslant 1} x ^ {m} y ^ {n} z ^ {p} d x d y d z = 8 \iiint_ {\substack {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} - z ^ {2} \leqslant 1 \\ x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0, z \geqslant 0}} x ^ {m} y ^ {n} z ^ {p} d x d y d z.
$$

引用球坐标， $x = r\cos \varphi \cos \psi ,y = r\sin \varphi \cos \psi ,z = r\sin \psi$ ，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iiint_ { \begin{array}{l} r ^ {2} - y ^ {2} + z ^ {2} \leq 1 \\ r \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0, z \geqslant 0 \end{array} } x ^ {m} y ^ {n} z ^ {p} d x d y d z = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {m} \varphi \sin^ {n} \varphi d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {m + n + 1} \psi \sin^ {p} \psi d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {1} r ^ {m + n + p + 2} d r \\ = \frac {1}{m + n + p + 3} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {\Gamma (\frac {m + 1}{2}) \Gamma (\frac {n + 1}{2})}{\Gamma (\frac {m + n + 2}{2})} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {\Gamma (\frac {m + n + 2}{2}) \Gamma (\frac {p + 1}{2}) ^ {*}}{\Gamma (\frac {m + n + p + 3}{2})} \\ = \frac {1}{4 (m + n + p + 3)} \cdot \frac {\Gamma (\frac {m + 1}{2}) \Gamma (\frac {n + 1}{2}) \Gamma (\frac {p + 1}{2})}{\Gamma (\frac {m + n + p + 3}{2})} \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{4 (m + n + p + 3)} \cdot \frac {\frac {(m - 1) ! !}{2 ^ {\frac {m}{2}}} \cdot \frac {(n - 1) ! !}{2 ^ {\frac {n}{2}}} \cdot \frac {(p - 1) ! !}{2 ^ {\frac {p}{2}}} \cdot \pi \sqrt {\pi}}{\frac {(m + n + p + 1) ! !}{2 ^ {\frac {m - n - p - 2}{2}}} \cdot \sqrt {\pi}} \\ = \frac {\pi}{2 (m + n + p + 3)} \cdot \frac {(m - 1) ! ! (n - 1) ! ! (p - 1) ! !}{(m + n + p + 1) ! !}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故 $I = \iiint_{x^2 + y^2 - z^2 \leqslant 1} x^m y^n z^p \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z = \frac{4\pi}{m + n + p + 3} \cdot \frac{(m - 1)!! (n - 1)!! (p - 1)!!}{(m + n + p + 1)!!}$ .

\*) 利用3856题的结果.
