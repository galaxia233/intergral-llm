27.63 The region $\mathcal{R}$ under the curve $y = 1 / x^2\sqrt{x^2 - 1}$ , above the $x$ -axis and between the lines $x = 2 / \sqrt{3}$ and $x = 2$ , is revolved around the $y$ -axis. Find the volume of the resulting solid.

By the cylindrical shell formula, $V = 2\pi \int_{2/\sqrt{3}}^{2} xy \, dx = 2\pi \int_{2/\sqrt{3}}^{2} \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{x^2 - 1}} = 2\pi \sec^{-1} x \bigg]_{2/\sqrt{3}}^{2} = 2\pi (\sec^{-1} 2 - \sec^{-1} 3) = 2\pi (\pi / 3 - \pi / 6) = 2\pi (\pi / 6) = \frac{1}{3}\pi^2.$
