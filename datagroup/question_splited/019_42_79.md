42.79 If $f(x, y)$ is homogeneous of degree $n$ , show that $f_x$ is homogeneous of degree $n - 1$ .

$f(tx,ty) = t^{n}f(x,y)$ . Differentiate with respect to $x$ : $f_{x}(tx,ty)(t) + f_{y}(tx,ty)(0) = t^{n}[f_{x}(x,y)(1) + f_{y}(x,y)(0)]$ , $f_{x}(tx,ty)(t) = t^{n}f_{x}(x,y)$ , $f_{x}(tx,ty) = t^{n - 1}f_{x}(x,y)$ .
