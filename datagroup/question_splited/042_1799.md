【1799】 $\int x^{2}\sin 2x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x^{2}\sin 2x\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{2}\int x^{2}\mathrm{d}(\cos 2x) = -\frac{1}{2} x^{2}\cos 2x + \frac{1}{2}\int x\mathrm{d}(\sin 2x)$

$$
= - \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} \cos 2 x + \frac {1}{2} x \sin 2 x - \frac {1}{2} \int \sin 2 x d x = - \frac {2 x ^ {2} - 1}{4} \cos 2 x + \frac {1}{2} x \sin 2 x + C.
$$
