32.52 Evaluate $\int_0^9\frac{dx}{(x - 1)^{2 / 3}}.$

There is a discontinuity at $x = 1$ . Then

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {9} \frac {d x}{(x - 1) ^ {2 / 3}} = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {d x}{(x - 1) ^ {2 / 3}} + \int_ {1} ^ {9} \frac {d x}{(x - 1) ^ {2 / 3}} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow 1 ^ {-}} \int_ {0} ^ {v} \frac {d x}{(x - 1) ^ {2 / 3}} + \lim  _ {w \rightarrow 1 ^ {+}} \int_ {w} ^ {9} \frac {d x}{(x - 1) ^ {2 / 3}} \\ = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow 1 ^ {-}} 3 (x - 1) ^ {1 / 3} \Bigg ] _ {0} ^ {v} + \lim  _ {w \rightarrow 1 ^ {+}} 3 (x - 1) ^ {1 / 3} \Bigg ] _ {w} ^ {9} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow 1 ^ {-}} 3 [ (v - 1) ^ {1 / 3} - (- 1) ] + \lim  _ {w \rightarrow 1 ^ {+}} 3 [ 2 - (w - 1) ^ {1 / 3} ] \\ = 3 + 6 = 9 \\ \end{array}
$$
