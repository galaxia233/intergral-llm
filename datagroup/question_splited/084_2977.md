【2977】在区间 $(0, \frac{\pi}{2})$ 内把函数 $f(x) = x\left(\frac{\pi}{2} - x\right)$ 展开：(1) 依角的奇倍数的余弦展开；(2) 依角的奇倍数的正弦展开.

绘出情形(1)与(2)的傅里叶级数之和的图像.

提示 (1) 利用 2975 题的结果，延拓函数，求出 $a_{2k+1}(k=0,1,2,\cdots)$ .

(2)利用2976题的结果，延拓函数，求出 $b_{2k+1}(k=0,1,2,\dots)$ .

解（1）利用2975题的结果，延拓函数，使有 $f(-x) = f(x),f(\pi -x) = -f(x).$ 于是，有

$$
a _ {2 k - 1} = \frac {2}{\pi} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} f (x) \cos (2 k + 1) x d x + \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\pi} \left[ - f (\pi - x) \right] \cos (2 k + 1) x d x \right\}.
$$

若在上式右端第二个积分中令 $\pi - x = y$ ，则得到与第一个积分同样的结果，即

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {2 k + 1} = \frac {4}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} f (x) \cos (2 k + 1) x d x \\ = \frac {4}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} x \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \cos (2 k + 1) x d x \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) \pi} x \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \sin (2 k + 1) x \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} - \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - 2 x\right) \sin (2 k + 1) x d x \right. \\ = \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2} \pi} \left[ \frac {\pi}{2} \cos (2 k + 1) x - 2 x \cos (2 k + 1) x \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} + \frac {8}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2} \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos (2 k + 1) x d x \\ = - \frac {2}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {8}{(2 k + 1) ^ {3} \pi} \sin (2 k + 1) \frac {\pi}{2} \\ = - \frac {2}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {8 (- 1) ^ {k}}{(2 k + 1) ^ {3} \pi} \quad (k = 0, 1, 2, \dots). \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $f(x)$ 的展开式为

$$
\begin{array}{l} - 2 \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \left\{\left[ \frac {1}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} - \frac {4}{\pi} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k}}{(2 k + 1) ^ {3}} \right] \cos (2 k + 1) x \right\} \\ = x \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \quad (0 \leqslant x \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}), \\ \end{array}
$$

（2）利用2976题的结果，延拓函数，使有

$$
f (- x) = - f (x), \quad f (\pi - x) = f (x).
$$

于是，有

$$
b _ {2 k + 1} = \frac {2}{\pi} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} f (x) \sin (2 k + 1) x d x + \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\pi} f (\pi - x) \sin (2 k + 1) x d x \right].
$$

若在上式右端第二个积分中令 $\pi - x = y$ ，则得到与第一个积分同样的结果，即

$$
\begin{array}{l} b _ {2 k + 1} = \frac {4}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} f (x) \sin (2 k + 1) x d x \\ = \frac {4}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} x \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \sin (2 k + 1) x d x \\ = - \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) \pi} x \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \cos (2 k + 1) x \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} + \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - 2 x\right) \cos (2 k + 1) x d x \right. \\ = \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2} \pi} \left[ \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - 2 x\right) \sin (2 k + 1) x \right] \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} + \frac {8}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2} \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin (2 k + 1) x d x \right. \\ = \frac {2 (- 1) ^ {k + 1}}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {8}{(2 k + 1) ^ {3} \pi} \quad (k = 0, 1, 2, \dots). \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $f(x)$ 的展开式为

$$
2 \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \left\{\left[ \frac {(- 1) ^ {k + 1}}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) ^ {3} \pi} \right] \sin (2 k + 1) x \right\} = x \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \quad (0 \leqslant x \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}).
$$
