【4282】 $\int_{C}y^{2}\mathrm{d}x + z^{2}\mathrm{d}y + x^{2}\mathrm{d}z$ ，式中 $C$ 为维维亚尼曲线 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2},x^{2} + y^{2} = ax(z\geqslant 0,a > 0)$

![](images/08d40af0e10bc27551aeda1040bdf1d3b5b9d46307d6455140a12350bd52c6be.jpg)  
图 8.63

若从 $Ox$ 轴的正向 $(x > 0)$ 看去，沿逆时针方向为正

解 柱面 $x^{2} + y^{2} = ax$ 可变为 $\left(x - \frac{a}{2}\right)^{2} + y^{2} = \left(\frac{a}{2}\right)^{2},$

故若令 $x - \frac{a}{2} = \frac{a}{2}\cos t, y = \frac{a}{2}\sin t (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2\pi)$ ，则

$$
z = \sqrt {a ^ {2} - (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} = \sqrt {a ^ {2} - \left[ \frac {a ^ {2} (1 + \cos t) ^ {2}}{4} + \frac {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} t}{4} \right]} = a \sin \frac {t}{2}.
$$

从而，曲线的参数方程为 $x = \frac{a(1 + \cos t)}{2}, y = \frac{a\sin t}{2}, z = a\sin \frac{t}{2} (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2\pi).$

于是， $\int_{C}y^{2}\mathrm{d}x + z^{2}\mathrm{d}y + x^{2}\mathrm{d}z = \int_{0}^{2\pi}\left(-\frac{a^{3}\sin^{3}t}{8} +\frac{a^{3}\sin^{2}\frac{t}{2}\cos t}{2} +\frac{a^{3}\cos^{3}\frac{t}{2}}{2}\right)\mathrm{d}t$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {a ^ {3}}{8} (1 - \cos^ {2} t) d (\cos t) + \frac {a ^ {3}}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {1 - \cos t}{2} \cos t d t + a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \left(1 - \sin^ {2} \frac {t}{2}\right) d \left(\sin \frac {t}{2}\right) \\ = \frac {a ^ {3}}{8} \left(\cos t - \frac {1}{3} \cos^ {3} t\right) \left| _ {0} ^ {2 \pi} + \frac {a ^ {3}}{4} \left[ \sin t - \left(\frac {t}{2} + \frac {1}{4} \sin 2 t\right) \right] \right| _ {0} ^ {2 \pi} + a ^ {3} \left(\sin \frac {t}{2} - \frac {1}{3} \sin^ {3} \frac {t}{2}\right) \left| _ {0} ^ {2 \pi} = - \frac {\pi a ^ {3}}{4}. \right. \\ \end{array}
$$
