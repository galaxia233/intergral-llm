【4085】 $\int_{0}^{1}\mathrm{d}x\int_{0}^{1}\mathrm{d}y\int_{0}^{x - y}f(z)\mathrm{d}z.$

解化为先对 $y$ 积分，再对 $x, z$ 积分，可将原积分表示成如下两部分：

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} z \left[ \int_ {\tau} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {0} ^ {1} f (z) \mathrm {d} y + \int_ {0} ^ {\tau} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {z - \tau} ^ {1} f (z) \mathrm {d} y \right] = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} z \int_ {z} ^ {1} f (z) \mathrm {d} x + \int_ {0} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} z \int_ {0} ^ {z} f (z) (1 - z + x) \mathrm {d} x \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {1} f (z) (1 - z) d z + \int_ {0} ^ {1} f (z) (1 - z) z d z + \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} f (z) z ^ {2} d z \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {1} f (z) \left(1 - \frac {z ^ {2}}{2}\right) d z = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} f (z) \left(2 - z ^ {2}\right) d z; \\ \int_ {1} ^ {2} \mathrm {d} z \int_ {z - 1} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {z - x} ^ {1} f (z) \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {1} ^ {2} \mathrm {d} z \int_ {z - 1} ^ {1} f (z) (1 - z + x) \mathrm {d} x = \int_ {1} ^ {2} \left[ f (z) (1 - z) x + \frac {1}{2} f (z) x ^ {2} \right] \Bigg | _ {z - 1} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} z \\ = \int_ {1} ^ {2} f (z) \left[ 1 - z + (z - 1) ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} - \frac {1}{2} (z - 1) ^ {2} \right] d z = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {1} ^ {2} f (z) (z - 2) ^ {2} d z, \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\int_{0}^{1}\mathrm{d}x\int_{0}^{1}\mathrm{d}y\int_{0}^{1 + y}f(z)\mathrm{d}z = \frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{1}f(z)(2 - z^2)\mathrm{d}z + \frac{1}{2}\int_{1}^{2}f(z)(2 - z)^2\mathrm{d}z.$
