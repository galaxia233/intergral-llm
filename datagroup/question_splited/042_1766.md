【1766】 $\int x^{2}\sqrt[3]{1 - x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $1 - x = t$ ，则 $x = 1 - t, \mathrm{d}x = -\mathrm{d}t,$

故得 $\int x^{2}\sqrt[3]{1 - x}\mathrm{d}x = -\int (1 - t)^{2}t^{\frac{1}{3}}\mathrm{d}t = -\int (t^{\frac{1}{3}} - 2t^{\frac{4}{3}} + t^{\frac{7}{3}})\mathrm{d}t = -\frac{3}{4} t^{\frac{4}{3}} + \frac{6}{7} t^{\frac{7}{3}} - \frac{3}{10} t^{\frac{10}{3}} + C$

$$
= - \frac {3}{1 4 0} (9 + 1 2 x + 1 4 x ^ {2}) (1 - x) ^ {\frac {4}{3}} + C.
$$
