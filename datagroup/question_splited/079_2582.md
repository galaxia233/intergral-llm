【2582】 $\frac{(1!)^2}{2} +\frac{(2!)^2}{2^4} +\frac{(3!)^2}{2^9} +\dots +\frac{(n!)^2}{2^{n^2}} +\dots .$

解 由于

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {a _ {n + 1}}{a _ {n}} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {\frac {[ (n + 1) ! ] ^ {2}}{2 ^ {(n + 1) ^ {2}}}}{\frac {(n !) ^ {2}}{2 ^ {n ^ {2}}}} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {(n + 1) ^ {2}}{2 ^ {2 n + 1}} = 0 <   1,
$$

故级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(n!)^2}{2^n}$ 收敛.
