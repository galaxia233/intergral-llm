33.2 Given vectors $\mathbf{A} = (2,4)$ and $\mathbf{C} = (-3,8)$ , find $\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{C}$ , $\mathbf{A} - \mathbf{C}$ , and 3A.

By componentwise addition, subtraction, and scalar multiplication, $\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{C} = (2 + (-3), 4 + 8) = (-1, 12)$ , $\mathbf{A} - \mathbf{C} = (2 - (-3), 4 - 8) = (5, -4)$ , and $3\mathbf{A} = (3 \cdot 2, 3 \cdot 4) = (6, 12)$ .
