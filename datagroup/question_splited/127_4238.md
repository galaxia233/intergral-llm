【4238】 $\int_{C}x^{2}\mathrm{d}s$ ，其中 $C$ 为圆周 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2},x + y + z = 0.$

提示 由对称性知： $\int_{C}x^{2}\mathrm{d}s = \int_{C}y^{2}\mathrm{d}s = \int_{C}z^{2}\mathrm{d}s.$

解 解法1：

由对称性知： $\int_{C}x^{2}\mathrm{d}s = \int_{C}y^{2}\mathrm{d}s = \int_{C}z^{2}\mathrm{d}s.$ 于是，

$$
\int_ {C} x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} s = \frac {1}{3} \int_ {C} \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}\right) \mathrm {d} s = \frac {a ^ {2}}{3} \int_ {C} \mathrm {d} s = \frac {2 \pi a ^ {3}}{3}.
$$

解法2：

作代换 $u = \frac{x - y}{\sqrt{2}}$ ， $v = \frac{x + y - 2z}{\sqrt{6}}$ ， $w = \frac{x + y + z}{\sqrt{3}}$ ，则圆周 $C$ 化为 $u^2 + v^2 + w^2 = a^2$ ， $w = 0$ 。于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {C} x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} s = \int_ {C} \left(\frac {u}{\sqrt {2}} + \frac {v}{\sqrt {6}} + \frac {w}{\sqrt {3}}\right) ^ {2} \mathrm {d} s = \int_ {C} \left(\frac {u}{\sqrt {2}} + \frac {v}{\sqrt {6}}\right) ^ {2} \mathrm {d} s = \frac {1}{6} \int_ {C} \left(3 u ^ {2} + v ^ {2}\right) \mathrm {d} s + \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \int_ {C} u v \mathrm {d} s \\ = \frac {1}{6} \int_ {C} a ^ {2} d s + \frac {1}{3} \int_ {C} u ^ {2} d s + \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \int_ {C} u v d s = \frac {1}{3} \pi a ^ {3} + \frac {1}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} a ^ {3} \cos^ {2} \varphi d \varphi + \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} a ^ {3} \cos \varphi \sin \varphi d \varphi \\ = \frac {1}{3} \pi a ^ {3} + \frac {1}{3} \pi a ^ {3} = \frac {2}{3} \pi a ^ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$
