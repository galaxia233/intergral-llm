【4112】 $\left(\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2}\right)^2 = \frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2}.$

解令 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos \psi$ ， $y = br\sin \varphi \cos \psi$ ， $z = cr\sin \psi$ ，并利用对称性，即得体积

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 8 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {\cos \psi} a b c r ^ {2} \cos \psi d r = 8 \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} \cdot \frac {a b c}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {4} \psi d \psi = \frac {4 \pi a b c}{3} \cdot \frac {3}{4} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} = \frac {\pi^ {2}}{4} a b c. \\ \text {【 4 1 1 3 】} \quad \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} + \frac {z ^ {2}}{c ^ {2}} = 1, \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} = \frac {z}{c}. \\ \end{array}
$$
