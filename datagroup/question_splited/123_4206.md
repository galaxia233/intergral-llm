【4206】 $\int_0^1\mathrm{d}x_1\int_0^{x_1}\mathrm{d}x_2\dots \int_0^{x_{n - 1}}x_1x_2\dots x_n\mathrm{d}x_n.$

提示 化成累次积分或利用 4203 题的结果，均可获解。

解 $\int_{0}^{1}\mathrm{d}x_{1}\int_{0}^{x_{1}}\mathrm{d}x_{2}\dots \int_{0}^{x_{n - 1}}x_{1}x_{2}\dots x_{n}\mathrm{d}x_{n} = \int_{0}^{1}\mathrm{d}x_{1}\int_{0}^{x_{1}}\mathrm{d}x_{2}\dots \int_{0}^{x_{n - 2}}\frac{1}{2} x_{1}x_{2}\dots x_{n - 1}^{3}\mathrm{d}x_{n - 1}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x _ {1} \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {1}} d x _ {2} \dots \int_ {0} ^ {r _ {n - 3}} \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{4} x _ {1} x _ {2} \dots x _ {n - 2} ^ {5} d x _ {n - 2} \\ = \dots = \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{4} \dots \frac {1}{2 (n - 1)} \int_ {0} ^ {1} x _ {1} ^ {2 n - 1} d x _ {1} = \frac {1}{2 \cdot 4 \cdots 2 n} = \frac {1}{2 ^ {n} n !}. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*）也可利用 4203 题的结果直接得

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} x _ {1} \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {1}} \mathrm {d} x _ {2} \dots \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {n - 1}} x _ {1} x _ {2} \dots x _ {n} \mathrm {d} x _ {n} = \frac {1}{n !} \left(\int_ {0} ^ {1} \tau \mathrm {d} \tau\right) ^ {n} = \frac {1}{2 ^ {n} n !}.
$$
