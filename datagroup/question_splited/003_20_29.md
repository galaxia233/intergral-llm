20.29 $\int_0^x\frac{x}{(x + 1)^{3 / 2}} dx.$

Let $u = x + 1, \quad x = u - 1, \quad du = dx$ . Then $\int_{0}^{8} \frac{x}{(x + 1)^{3/2}} \, dx = \int_{1}^{9} \frac {u - 1}{u^{3/2}} \, du = \int_{1}^{9} (u^{-1/2} - u^{-3/2}) \, du = (2u^{1/2} + 2u^{-1/2})]_{1}^{9} = 2[(3 + \frac {1}{3}) - (1 + 1)] = \frac {8}{3}$ .
