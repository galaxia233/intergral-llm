【4432】求包含多个引力中心的有限系统所产生的引力场之散度.
解 引力 $F = \frac{k r}{r^{3}}$ （ $k$ 为常数）。于是，
$$
\operatorname {d i v} \boldsymbol {F} = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} \left(\frac {k x}{r ^ {3}}\right) + \frac {\partial}{\partial y} \left(\frac {k y}{r ^ {3}}\right) + \frac {\partial}{\partial z} \left(\frac {k z}{r ^ {3}}\right) = k \left[ \left(\frac {1}{r ^ {3}} - \frac {3 x ^ {2}}{r ^ {5}}\right) + \left(\frac {1}{r ^ {3}} - \frac {3 y ^ {2}}{r ^ {5}}\right) + \left(\frac {1}{r ^ {3}} - \frac {3 z ^ {2}}{r ^ {5}}\right) \right]
$$
$$
= k \left[ \frac {3}{r ^ {3}} - \frac {3 \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}\right)}{r ^ {5}} \right] = k \left(\frac {3}{r ^ {3}} - \frac {3}{r ^ {3}}\right) = 0.
$$
