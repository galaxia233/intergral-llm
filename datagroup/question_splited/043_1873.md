【1873】 $\int \left(\frac{x}{x^2 - 3x + 2}\right)^2\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\left(\frac{x}{x^2 - 3x + 2}\right)^2 = \frac{x^2}{(x - 1)^2(x - 2)^2} = \frac{A}{x - 1} +\frac{B}{(x - 1)^2} +\frac{C}{x - 2} +\frac{D}{(x - 2)^2},$

通分后应有 $x^{2} \equiv A(x - 1)(x - 2)^{2} + B(x - 2)^{2} + C(x - 2)(x - 1)^{2} + D(x - 1)^{2}$ . 在这恒等式中，

令 $x = 1$ ，得 $B = 1$ ；令 $x = 2$ ，得 $D = 4$ ；比较 $x^3$ 及 $x^2$ 的系数，得

$$
A + C = 0 \quad \text {及} \quad - 5 A + B - 4 C + D = 1;
$$

由此， $A = 4$ ， $C = -4.$ 于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \left(\frac {x}{x ^ {2} - 3 x + 2}\right) ^ {2} d x = \int \left[ \frac {4}{x - 1} + \frac {1}{(x - 1) ^ {2}} - \frac {4}{x - 2} + \frac {4}{(x - 2) ^ {2}} \right] d x \\ = 4 \ln | x - 1 | - \frac {1}{x - 1} - 4 \ln | x - 2 | - \frac {4}{x - 2} + C = 4 \ln \left| \frac {x - 1}{x - 2} \right| - \frac {5 x - 6}{x ^ {2} - 3 x + 2} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
