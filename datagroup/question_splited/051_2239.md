【2239】 $\int_0^{\ln 2}xe^{-x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int_0^{\ln 2}x\mathrm{e}^{-x}\mathrm{d}x = -\int_0^{\ln 2}x\mathrm{d}(\mathrm{e}^{-x}) = -x\mathrm{e}^{-x}\left|_{0}^{\ln 2} + \int_0^{\ln 2}\mathrm{e}^{-x}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{2}\ln 2 - \mathrm{e}^{-x}\right|_{0}^{\ln 2} = -\frac{1}{2}\ln 2 + \frac{1}{2} = \frac{1}{2}\ln \frac{\mathrm{e}}{2}.$
