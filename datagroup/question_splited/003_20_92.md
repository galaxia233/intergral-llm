20.92 Find the volume of the solid generated when the region between the semicircle $y = 1 - \sqrt{1 - x^2}$ and the line $y = 1$ is rotated around the $x$ -axis (see Fig. 20-8).

![](images/bd0c9053f1e5237a9220ef16c8536395d85e7420c4637040e421d812a8414dea.jpg)  
Fig. 20-8

By the disk formula, $V = \pi \int_{-1}^{1} \left[ 1^2 - (1 - \sqrt{1 - x^2})^2 \right] dx = \pi \int_{-1}^{1} \left[ 1 - (1 - 2\sqrt{1 - x^2} + 1 - x^2) \right] dx = \pi \int_{-1}^{1} (2\sqrt{1 - x^2} + x^2 - 1) dx = 2\pi \int_{-1}^{1} \sqrt{1 - x^2} dx + \pi \left( \frac{1}{3} x^3 - x \right) \bigg|_{-1}^{1} = 2\pi \left( \frac{1}{2}\pi \right) + \pi \left[ \left( \frac{1}{3} - 1 \right) - (-\frac{1}{3} + 1) \right] = \pi^2 - \frac{4}{3}\pi.$ (The integral $\int_{-1}^{1} \sqrt{1 - x^2} dx = \frac{1}{2}\pi$ by Problem 20.71.)
