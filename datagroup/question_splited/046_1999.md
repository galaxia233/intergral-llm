【1999】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^3 x}$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^3 x} = -\int \frac{1}{\sin x} \mathrm{d}(\cot x) = -\frac{\cot x}{\sin x} - \int \cot x \frac{\cos x}{\sin^2 x} \mathrm{d}x = -\frac{\cos x}{\sin^2 x} - \int \frac{1 - \sin^2 x}{\sin^3 x} \mathrm{d}x$

$$
= - \frac {\cos x}{\sin^ {2} x} - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sin^ {3} x} + \ln \left| \tan \frac {x}{2} \right|,
$$

于是， $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^3 x} = -\frac{\cos x}{2 \sin^2 x} + \frac{1}{2} \ln \left| \tan \frac{x}{2} \right| + C.$
