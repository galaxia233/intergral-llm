33.29 Prove the triangle inequality: $|\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}| \leq |\mathbf{A}| + |\mathbf{B}|$ .

By the Cauchy inequality, $|\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}|^2 = (\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B})\cdot (\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}) = \mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{A} + 2\mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B} + \mathbf{B}\cdot \mathbf{B}\leq |\mathbf{A}|^2 +2|\mathbf{A}||\mathbf{B}| + |\mathbf{B}|^2 = (|\mathbf{A}| + |\mathbf{B}|)^2.$ Therefore, $|\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}|\leq |\mathbf{A}| + |\mathbf{B}|$
