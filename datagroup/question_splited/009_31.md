31.1 If the region under a curve $y = f(x)$ , above the $x$ -axis, and between $x = a$ and $x = b$ , is revolved about the $x$ -axis, state a formula for the surface area $S$ of the resulting solid.

$$
S = 2 \pi \int y d s = 2 \pi \int_ {a} ^ {b} y \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {d y}{d x}\right) ^ {2}} d x = 2 \pi \int_ {f (a)} ^ {f (b)} y \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {d x}{d y}\right) ^ {2}} d y.
$$

[For revolution about the $y$ -axis, change the factor $y$ to $x$ in either integrand.]
