【2280】 $\int_0^{\ln 2}\mathrm{sh}^4 x\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 利用 1761 题的结果.

解 $\int_0^{\ln 2}\mathrm{sh}^4 x\mathrm{d}x = \int_0^{\ln 2}\mathrm{sh}^2 x(\mathrm{ch}^2 x - 1)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4}\int_0^{\ln 2}\mathrm{sh}^2 2x\mathrm{d}x - \int_0^{\ln 2}\mathrm{sh}^2 x\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{4} \left(- \frac {x}{2} + \frac {1}{8} \operatorname {s h} 4 x\right) ^ {*}) \left| _ {0} ^ {\ln 2} - \left(- \frac {x}{2} + \frac {1}{4} \operatorname {s h} 2 x\right) ^ {*}) \right| _ {0} ^ {\ln 2} = \frac {3}{8} \ln 2 - \frac {2 2 5}{1 0 4 2}.
$$

\*) 利用 1761 题的结果.
