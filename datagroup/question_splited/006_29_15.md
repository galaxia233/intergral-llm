29.15 $\int \sin 5x\sin 7xdx.$

Recall $\sin Ax\sin Bx = \frac{1}{2} [\cos (A - B)x - \cos (A + B)x]$ . So $\int \sin 5x\sin 7x dx = \frac{1}{2}\int [\cos (-2x) - \cos 12x] dx = \frac{1}{2}\int (\cos 2x - \cos 12x) dx = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{\sin 2x}{2} - \frac{\sin 12x}{12}\right) + C = \frac{1}{24} (6\sin 2x - \sin 12x) + C.$
