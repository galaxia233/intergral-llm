32.56 Find the area in the first quadrant under the curve $y = 1 / (x^2 + 6x + 10)$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} A = \int_ {0} ^ {\infty} \frac {d x}{x ^ {2} + 6 x + 1 0} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \int_ {0} ^ {v} \frac {d x}{(x + 3) ^ {2} + 1} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \tan^ {- 1} (x + 3) ] _ {0} ^ {v} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} [ \tan^ {- 1} (v + 3) - \tan^ {- 1} 3 ] \\ = \pi / 2 - \tan^ {- 1} 3 \\ \end{array}
$$
