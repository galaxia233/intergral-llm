【3922】 $\Omega$ 一圆环 $1\leqslant x^{2} + y^{2}\leqslant 4.$

解 如图8.4所示. 若先对 $y$ 后对 $x$ 积分，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} I = \int_ {- 2} ^ {- 1} d x \int_ {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d y + \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d x \left\{\int_ {- \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} ^ {- \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d y \right. \\ \left. + \int_ {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d y \right\} + \int_ {1} ^ {2} d x \int_ {- \sqrt {4 - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d y. \\ \end{array}
$$

若先对 $x$ 后对 $y$ 积分，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} I = \int_ {- 2} ^ {- 1} d y \int_ {\sqrt {4 - y ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {1 - y ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d x + \int_ {1} ^ {1} d y \left\{\int_ {- \sqrt {4 - y ^ {2}}} ^ {- \sqrt {1 - y ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d x \right. \\ + \int_ {\sqrt {1 - y ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {4 - y ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d x \bigg \} + \int_ {1} ^ {2} d y \int_ {- \sqrt {1 - y ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {4 - y ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d x. \\ \end{array}
$$
