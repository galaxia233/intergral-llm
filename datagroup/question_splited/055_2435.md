【2435】 $x = \frac{1}{4} y^2 -\frac{1}{2}\ln y$ （ $1\leqslant y\leqslant e)$

解 所求的弧长为

$$
s = \int_ {1} ^ {\mathrm {e}} \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {y}{2} - \frac {1}{2 y}\right) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {1} ^ {\mathrm {e}} \frac {1 + y ^ {2}}{2 y} \mathrm {d} y = \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {2} + 1}{4}.
$$
