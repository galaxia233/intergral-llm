40.22 Write an equation of the sphere with radius 2 and center on the positive $x$ -axis, and tangent to the $yz$ -plane.

The radius from the center to the point of tangency on the $yz$ -plane must be perpendicular to that plane. Hence, the radius must lie on the $x$ -axis and the point of tangency must be the origin. So, the center must be $(2,0,0)$ and the equation of the sphere is $(x - 2)^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 4$ .
