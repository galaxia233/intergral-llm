【2421】 $r = \frac{p}{1 - \cos\varphi}$ （抛物线）， $\varphi = \frac{\pi}{4},\quad \varphi = \frac{\pi}{2}.$

解 所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {p ^ {2}}{(1 - \cos \varphi) ^ {2}} d \varphi = \frac {p ^ {2}}{4} \int_ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \csc^ {4} \frac {\varphi}{2} d \left(\frac {\varphi}{2}\right) = - \frac {p ^ {2}}{4} \left(\cot \frac {\varphi}{2} + \frac {1}{3} \cot^ {3} \frac {\varphi}{2}\right) \Bigg | _ {\frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \\ = \frac {p ^ {2}}{6} (4 \sqrt {2} + 3) ^ {*}). \\ \end{array}
$$
