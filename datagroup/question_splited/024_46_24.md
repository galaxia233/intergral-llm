46.24 Find the orthogonal trajectories of the family of all straight lines through the origin $y = mx$ .

$\frac{dy}{dx} = m = \frac{y}{x}$ . Replace $\frac{dy}{dx}$ by its negative reciprocal: $-\frac{1}{dy / dx} = \frac{y}{x}$ , $\frac{dy}{dx} = -\frac{x}{y}$ , $\int y dy = -\int x dx$ , $\frac{1}{2} y^2 = -\frac{1}{2} x^2 + C_1$ , $y^2 = -x^2 + C$ , $x^2 + y^2 = C$ . Thus, we have obtained the family of all circles with center at the origin.
