【4435】证明：
(1) $\operatorname{rot}(a + b) = \operatorname{rot}a + \operatorname{rot}b$ ; (2) $\operatorname{rot}(ua) = u\operatorname{rot}a + \operatorname{grad}u \times a$ .

提示 利用旋度的定义易证.
证（1）设 $a = a_{x}i + a_{y}j + a_{z}k, b = b_{x}i + b_{y}j + b_{z}k$ ，则有
$$
\operatorname {r o t} (\boldsymbol {a} + \boldsymbol {b}) = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} i & j & k \\ \frac {\partial}{\partial x} & \frac {\partial}{\partial y} & \frac {\partial}{\partial z} \\ a _ {x} + b _ {x} & a _ {y} + b _ {y} & a _ {z} + y _ {z} \end{array} \right| = \left| \begin{array}{c c c} i & j & k \\ \frac {\partial}{\partial x} & \frac {\partial}{\partial y} & \frac {\partial}{\partial z} \\ a _ {x} & a _ {y} & a _ {z} \end{array} \right| + \left| \begin{array}{c c c} i & j & k \\ \frac {\partial}{\partial x} & \frac {\partial}{\partial y} & \frac {\partial}{\partial z} \\ b _ {x} & b _ {y} & b _ {z} \end{array} \right| = \operatorname {r o t} \boldsymbol {a} + \operatorname {r o t} \boldsymbol {b}.
$$

(2) $\operatorname{rot}_x(ua) = \frac{\partial}{\partial y}(ua_x) - \frac{\partial}{\partial z}(ua_y) = u\left(\frac{\partial a_x}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial a_y}{\partial z}\right) + \left(a_x\frac{\partial u}{\partial y} - a_y\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}\right) = u\operatorname{rot}_x\boldsymbol{a} + (\operatorname{grad}u \times \boldsymbol{a})_x,$

同法可得
$$
\operatorname {r o t} _ {y} (u a) = u \operatorname {r o t} _ {y} a + (\operatorname {g r a d} u \times a) _ {y}, \quad \operatorname {r o t} _ {z} (u a) = u \operatorname {r o t} _ {z} a + (\operatorname {g r a d} u \times a) _ {z}
$$

于是， $\operatorname{rot}(u\mathbf{a}) = u\operatorname{rot}a + \operatorname{grad}u \times \mathbf{a}$
