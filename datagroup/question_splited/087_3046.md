【3046】 $\int_0^{2\pi}\mathrm{e}^{\cos x}\cos (\sin x)\cos nx\mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi}{n!}\quad (n = 0,1,2,\dots).$

解 若复数 $w = u + \mathrm{i}v$ ，记 $\operatorname{Re}\{w\} = u$ 为其实部，则有 $\operatorname{Re}\{\mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i}x}\} = \mathrm{e}^{\cos x}\cos (\sin x)$ 。因此，原定积分为

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {n} = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} e ^ {\cos x} \cos (\sin x) \cos n x d x \\ = \operatorname {R e} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} x} \cos n x \mathrm {d} x \right\} = \operatorname {R e} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} x} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} n x} + \mathrm {e} ^ {- \mathrm {i} n x}\right) \mathrm {d} x \right\} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \operatorname {R e} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \sum_ {m = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {\left(\mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} r}\right) ^ {m}}{m !} \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} n r} + \mathrm {e} ^ {- \mathrm {i} n r}\right) \mathrm {d} x \right\} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left[ \sum_ {m _ {1} = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{m _ {1} !} \operatorname {R e} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {2 n} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} (m _ {1} + n) x} \mathrm {d} x \right\} + \sum_ {m _ {2} = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{m _ {2} !} \operatorname {R e} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} (m _ {2} - n) x} \mathrm {d} x \right\} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$

注意，对任意整数 $k$ ，有积分关系：

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} k x} \mathrm {d} x = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 2 \pi , & k = 0, \\ 0, & k \neq 0. \end{array} \right.
$$

从而，当 $n\geqslant 0$ ， $m\geqslant 0$ 时，有：

（1）当 $n = 0$ 时，

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} e ^ {i (m + n) x} d x = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 2 \pi , & m = 0, \\ 0, & m \neq 0. \end{array} \right. \quad \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} e ^ {i (m - n) x} d x = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 2 \pi , & m = n, \\ 0, & m \neq n. \end{array} \right.
$$

此时相应地得 $I_{0} = \frac{1}{2} (2\pi + 2\pi) = 2\pi.$

(2) 当 $n = 1, 2, 3, \cdots$ 时，

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} (m + n) x} \mathrm {d} x = 0; \quad \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} (m - n) x} \mathrm {d} x = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 2 \pi , & m = n, \\ 0, & m \neq n. \end{array} \right.
$$

此时相应地得 $I_{n} = \frac{1}{2}\cdot \left(\frac{1}{n!} 2\pi\right) = \frac{\pi}{n!}.$
