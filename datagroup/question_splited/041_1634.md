【1634】 $\int \frac{\sqrt{x} - 2\sqrt[3]{x^2} + 1}{\sqrt[4]{x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{\sqrt{x} - 2\sqrt[3]{x^2} + 1}{\sqrt[4]{x}}\mathrm{d}x = \int (x^{\frac{1}{4}} - 2x^{\frac{5}{12}} + x^{-\frac{1}{4}})\mathrm{d}x = \frac{4}{5} x\sqrt[4]{x} -\frac{24}{17} x\sqrt[12]{x^5} +\frac{4}{3}\sqrt[4]{x^3} +C.$
