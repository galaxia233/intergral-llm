26.5 If the world population in 1980 was 4.5 billion and if it is growing exponentially with a growth constant $K = 0.04 \ln 2$ , find the population in the year 2030.

Let $y$ be the population in billions in year $t$ , with $t = 0$ in 1980. Then $y = 4.5e^{0.04(\ln 2)t}$ . In 2030, when $t = 50$ , $y = 4.5e^{0.04(\ln 2)\cdot 50} = 4.5(e^{\ln 2})^2 = 4.5(2)^2 = 18$ billion people.
