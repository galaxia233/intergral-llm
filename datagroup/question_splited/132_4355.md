【4355】求均质曲面 $z = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}$ 被曲面 $x^{2} + y^{2} = ax$ 所割下部分的质心坐标.

解 质量为 $M = \iint_{S} \rho_0 \mathrm{d}S = \sqrt{2} \rho_0 \iint_{\substack{x^2 + y^2 \leqslant a x}} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y = \sqrt{2} \rho_0 \left(\frac{a}{2}\right)^2 \pi = \frac{\sqrt{2} \pi a^2 \rho_0}{4}.$

从而，质心坐标为

$$
x _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \cdot \sqrt {2} \rho_ {0} \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a x} x d x d y = \frac {4}{\pi a ^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {a} x d x \int_ {- \sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}}} d y = \frac {8}{\pi a ^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {a} x \sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}} d x
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {8}{\pi a ^ {2}} \int_ {- \frac {a}{2}} ^ {\frac {a}{2}} \left(\frac {a}{2} + t\right) \sqrt {\left(\frac {a}{2}\right) ^ {2} - t ^ {2}} d t ^ {* *} = \frac {8}{\pi a ^ {2}} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {a}{2}} \sqrt {\left(\frac {a}{2}\right) ^ {2} - t ^ {2}} d t = \frac {a}{2}, \\ y _ {1 0} = \frac {1}{M} \cdot \sqrt {2} \rho_ {0} \int_ {0} ^ {a} d x \int_ {\sqrt {a r - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {a r - x ^ {2}}} y d y = 0, \\ z _ {n} = \frac {1}{M} \cdot \sqrt {2} \rho_ {0} \iint_ {r ^ {2} \cdot y ^ {2} \cdot z _ {a r}} z d x d y = \frac {4}{\pi a ^ {2}} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a \cos \varphi} r ^ {2} d r = \frac {8 a}{3 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {3} \varphi d \varphi = \frac {1 6 a}{9 \pi}, \\ \end{array}
$$

即质心为 $(\frac{a}{2},0,\frac{16a}{9\pi})$

\*）作变换 $t = x - \frac{a}{2}$
