40.81 Find an equation of the plane containing the point $P_{1}(4,3, - 2)$ and perpendicular to the vector (5,-4,6).

We know (from Problem 40.80) that the plane has an equation of the form $5x - 4y + 6z = d$ . Since $P_{1}$ lies in the plane, $5(4) - 4(3) + 6(-2) = d$ . So, $d = -4$ and the plane has the equation $5x - 4y + 6z = -4$ .
