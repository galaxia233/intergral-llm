【2003】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x\cos^4x}$

提示：与2002题相同.

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x\cos^4x} = \int \frac{\sin^2x + \cos^2x}{\sin x\cos^4x}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\sin x}{\cos^4x}\mathrm{d}x + \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x\cos^2x}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (\cos x)}{\cos^ {4} x} + \int \frac {\sin x}{\cos^ {2} x} \mathrm {d} x + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sin x} = \frac {1}{3 \cos^ {3} x} - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (\cos x)}{\cos^ {2} x} + \ln \left| \tan \frac {x}{2} \right| \\ = \frac {1}{3 \cos^ {3} x} + \frac {1}{\cos x} + \ln \left| \tan \frac {x}{2} \right| + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
