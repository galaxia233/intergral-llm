28.44 Find a reduction formula for $\int x^n\sin axdx$ for $n\geq 1$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {L e t} u = x ^ {n}, d v = \sin a x d x, d u = n x ^ {n - 1} d x, v = - (1 / a) \cos a x. \text {T h e n} \int x ^ {n} \sin a x d x = - \frac {1}{a} x ^ {n} \cos a x + \\ \frac {n}{a} \int x ^ {n - 1} \cos a x d x. \end{array}
$$
