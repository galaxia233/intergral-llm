42.13 If $z$ is implicitly defined as a function of $x$ and $y$ by $x^{2} + y^{2} - z^{2} = 3$ , find $\partial z / \partial x$ and $\partial z / \partial y$ .

By implicit differentiation with respect to $x$ , $2x - 2z(\partial z / \partial x) = 0$ , $x = z(\partial z / \partial x)$ , $\partial z / \partial x = x / z$ . By implicit differentiation with respect to $y$ , $2y - 2z(\partial z / \partial y) = 0$ , $y = z(\partial z / \partial y)$ , $\partial z / \partial y = y / z$ .
