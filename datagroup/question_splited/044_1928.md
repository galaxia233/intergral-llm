【1928】 $\int \frac{x\sqrt[3]{2 + x}}{x + \sqrt[3]{2 + x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $\sqrt[3]{2 + x} = t$ ，则 $x = t^3 - 2, \mathrm{d}x = 3t^2\mathrm{d}t.$ 代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x \sqrt [ 3 ]{2 + x}}{x + \sqrt [ 3 ]{2 + x}} d x = 3 \int \frac {t ^ {6} - 2 t ^ {3}}{t ^ {3} + t - 2} d t = 3 \int \left(t ^ {3} - t + \frac {t ^ {2} - 2 t}{t ^ {3} + t - 2}\right) d t \\ = \frac {3}{4} t ^ {4} - \frac {3}{2} t ^ {2} + 3 \int \left[ - \frac {1}{4 (t - 1)} + \frac {\frac {5}{4} t - \frac {1}{2}}{t ^ {2} + t + 2} \right] d t \\ = \frac {3}{4} t ^ {4} - \frac {3}{2} t ^ {2} - \frac {3}{4} \ln | t - 1 | + \frac {1 5}{8} \int \frac {2 t + 1}{t ^ {2} + t + 2} d t - \frac {2 7}{8} \int \frac {d (t + \frac {1}{2})}{(t + \frac {1}{2}) ^ {2} + \frac {7}{4}} \\ = \frac {3}{4} t ^ {4} - \frac {3}{2} t ^ {2} - \frac {3}{4} \ln | t - 1 | + \frac {1 5}{8} \ln (t ^ {2} + t + 2) - \frac {2 7}{4 \sqrt {7}} \arctan \left(\frac {2 t + 1}{\sqrt {7}}\right) + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $t = \sqrt[3]{2 + x}$
