40.23 Find an equation of the sphere with center at $(1,2,3)$ and tangent to the $yz$ -plane.

The radius from the center to the $yz$ -plane is perpendicular to the $yz$ -plane and, therefore, cuts the $yz$ -plane at $(0,2,3)$ . So, the radius is 1, and an equation of the sphere is $(x - 1)^2 + (y - 2)^2 + (z - 3)^2 = 1$ .
