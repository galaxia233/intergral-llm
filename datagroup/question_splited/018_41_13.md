41.13 Describe and sketch the graph of $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2} = 1,$ where $a,b,c > 0$

See Fig. 41-8. Each section parallel to one of the coordinate planes is an ellipse (or a point or nothing). The surface is bounded, $|x| \leq a$ , $|y| \leq b$ , $|z| \leq c$ . This surface is called an ellipsoid. When $a = b = c$ , the surface is a sphere.
