【 4 1 1 3 】 $\quad \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} + \frac {z ^ {2}}{c ^ {2}} = 1, \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} = \frac {z}{c}.$

解 令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi, z = z$ ，则 $r$ 满足方程 $r^4 + r^2 - 1 = 0$ . 解得 $r = \sqrt{\frac{\sqrt{5} - 1}{2}}$ . 于是，体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {\frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2}}} a b r d r \int_ {c r ^ {2}} ^ {c \sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}} d z = 2 \pi a b c \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {\frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2}}} r (\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}} - r ^ {2}) d r \\ = 2 \pi a b c \left[ - \frac {1}{3} (1 - r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} - \frac {1}{4} r ^ {4} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\sqrt {\frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2}}} = \frac {5 \pi a b c (3 - \sqrt {5})}{1 2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
