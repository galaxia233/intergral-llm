41.40 Describe the level curves of $f(x, y) = 1 / xy$ .

These are the rectangular hyperbolas $xy = k$ ( $k > 0$ ) and $xy = k$ ( $k < 0$ ); see Fig. 41-24. There is a level curve through every point not on a coordinate axis.
