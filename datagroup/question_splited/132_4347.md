【4347】 $\iint_{S} \frac{\mathrm{d}S}{\rho}$ , 式中 $S$ 为椭球面, $\rho$ 为椭球中心到与椭球面微元 $\mathrm{d}S$ 相切的平面的距离.

解 设椭球面方程为 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} + \frac{z^2}{c^2} = 1,$

则曲面上任一点 $(x,y,z)$ 的法矢向为 $\left\{\frac{x}{a^2},\frac{y}{b^2},\frac{z}{c^2}\right\}$ . 从而，由题设知： $\rho = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \cos (n,r)$ , 其中 $n,r$ 分别表示点 $(x,y,z)$ 处的法向量和径向量，即 $\rho = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\frac{x^2}{a^4} + \frac{y^2}{b^4} + \frac{z^2}{c^4}}}$ ,

而法线与 $Oz$ 轴夹角的余弦为 $\frac{\frac{z}{c^2}}{\sqrt{\frac{x^2}{a^4} + \frac{y^2}{b^4} + \frac{z^2}{c^4}}}$ .

于是， $\iint_{S} \frac{\mathrm{d}S}{\rho} = \iint_{\substack{\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} \leqslant 1 \\ \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} \leqslant 1}} \frac{c^2\left(\frac{x^2}{a^4} + \frac{y^2}{b^4} + \frac{z^2}{c^4}\right)}{|z|} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y = 2 \iint_{\substack{\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} \leqslant 1 \\ \frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} \leqslant 1}} \frac{c\left[\left(\frac{x^2}{a^4} + \frac{y^2}{b^4}\right) + \frac{1}{c^2}\left(1 - \frac{x^2}{a^2} - \frac{y^2}{b^2}\right)\right]}{\sqrt{1 - \left(\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2}\right)}} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {1} \mathrm {d} r \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {c}{\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}} \left(\frac {r ^ {2} \cos^ {2} \theta}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {r ^ {2} \sin^ {2} \theta}{b ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{c ^ {2}} - \frac {r ^ {2}}{c ^ {2}}\right) a b r \mathrm {d} \theta^ {*}) \\ = 2 \pi a b c \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left[ \frac {1}{\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}} \left(\frac {1}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{b ^ {2}}\right) - \sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}} \left(\frac {1}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{b ^ {2}}\right) + 2 \frac {\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}}{c ^ {2}} \right] r d r ^ {\dots} \\ = - \pi a b c \left[ 2 \sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}} \left(\frac {1}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{b ^ {2}}\right) - \frac {2}{3} (1 - r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \left(\frac {1}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{b ^ {2}}\right) + \frac {4}{3 c ^ {2}} (1 - r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {1} \\ = \frac {4 \pi}{3} a b c \left(\frac {1}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{b ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{c ^ {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

*）作广义极坐标变换 $x = a r \cos \theta ,y = b r \sin \theta .$

$\star \star)$ 利用关系式： $\frac{r^2}{\sqrt{1 - r^2}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - r^2}} -\sqrt{1 - r^2}.$
