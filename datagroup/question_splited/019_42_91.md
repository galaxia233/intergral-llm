42.91 If $u = f(x, y)$ and $x = r \cosh s$ , $y = \sinh s$ , show that $\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\right)^2 = \left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial r}\right)^2 - \frac{1}{r^2} \left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial s}\right)^2$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial u}{\partial r} = \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} \frac {\partial x}{\partial r} + \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} \frac {\partial y}{\partial r} = \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} (\cosh s) + \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} (\sinh s). \quad \frac {\partial u}{\partial s} = \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} \frac {\partial x}{\partial s} + \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} \frac {\partial y}{\partial s} = \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} (r \sinh s) + \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} (r \cosh s). \\ \text {H e n c e ,} \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial r}\right) ^ {2} - \frac {1}{r ^ {2}} \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial s}\right) ^ {2} = \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} \cosh^ {2} s + 2 \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} \cosh s \sinh s + \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} \sinh^ {2} s \\ - \left[ \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} \sinh^ {2} s + 2 \frac {\partial u}{\partial x} \frac {\partial u}{\partial y} \sinh s \cosh s + \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} \cosh^ {2} s \right] \\ = \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} \left(\cosh^ {2} s - \sinh^ {2} s\right) + \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} \left(\sinh^ {2} s - \cosh^ {2} s\right) = \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} - \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} \\ \end{array}
