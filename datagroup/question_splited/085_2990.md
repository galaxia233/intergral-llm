【2990】 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{1}{n(n + m)}$ （ $m$ 为正整数）.

解 由 $\frac{1}{n(n + m)} = \frac{1}{m} (\frac{1}{n} -\frac{1}{m + n})$ ，考虑适当大的正整数 $N$ ，并令 $N\to \infty$ ，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n (n + m)} = \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{n (n + m)} = \frac {1}{m} \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \left(\frac {1}{n} - \frac {1}{n + m}\right) \\ = \frac {1}{m} \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \left(1 + \frac {1}{2} + \dots + \frac {1}{m} - \frac {1}{N + 1} - \frac {1}{N + 2} - \dots - \frac {1}{N + m}\right) \\ = \frac {1}{m} \left(1 + \frac {1}{2} + \dots + \frac {1}{m}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
