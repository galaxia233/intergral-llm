【4242】求曲线 $x = at, y = \frac{a}{2} t^2, z = \frac{a}{3} t^3$ （ $0 \leqslant t \leqslant 1$ ）的弧的质量，其密度按规律 $\rho = \sqrt{\frac{2y}{a}}$ 而变化.

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{a^2 + a^2t^2 + a^2t^4}\mathrm{dt} = a\sqrt{1 + t^2 + t^4}\mathrm{dt},$

而密度 $\rho = \sqrt{\frac{2y}{a}} = t.$ 于是，质量为（作代换 $u = t^2$ ）

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \int_ {C} \sqrt {\frac {2 y}{a}} d s = a \int_ {0} ^ {1} t \sqrt {1 + t ^ {2} + t ^ {4}} d t = \frac {a}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 + u + u ^ {2}} d u \\ = \frac {a}{2} \left[ \frac {u + \frac {1}{2}}{2} \sqrt {1 + u + u ^ {2}} + \frac {3}{8} \ln \left(u + \frac {1}{2} + \sqrt {1 + u + u ^ {2}}\right) \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {1} \\ = \frac {a}{8} \left[ (3 \sqrt {3} - 1) + \frac {3}{2} \ln \frac {3 + 2 \sqrt {3}}{3} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
