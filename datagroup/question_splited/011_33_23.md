33.22 In Fig. 33-5, the ratio of segment $PQ$ to segment $PR$ is a certain number $t$ , with $0 < t < 1$ . Express $C$ in terms of $A$ , $B$ , and $t$ .

$\overrightarrow{PQ} = t\overrightarrow{PR}$ . But, $\overrightarrow{PR} = \mathbf{B} - \mathbf{A}$ . So, $\mathbf{C} = \mathbf{A} + \overrightarrow{PQ} = \mathbf{A} + t\overrightarrow{PR} = \mathbf{A} + t(\mathbf{B} - \mathbf{A}) = (1 - t)\mathbf{A} + t\mathbf{B}$ .
