【3023】 $\sum_{n = 2}^{\infty}(-1)^{n}\frac{\cos nx}{n^{2} - 1}.$

解首先仿照3018题的解法，只要在公式 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{z^n}{n} = \ln \frac{1}{1 - z}$ 中令 $z = -\mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i}\tau}$ ，并注意幅角主值的取法，就有

$$
\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\sin n x}{n} = - \frac {x}{2} \quad (| x | <   \pi) \tag {1}
$$

及

$$
\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\cos n x}{n} = - \ln \left(2 \cos \frac {x}{2}\right). \tag {2}
$$

由于 $\frac{1}{n^2 - 1} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{n - 1} -\frac{1}{n + 1}\right)$ ，故有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 2} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\cos n x}{n ^ {2} - 1} = \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {n = 2} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\cos n x}{n - 1} - \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {n = 2} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\cos n x}{n + 1} \\ = - \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {\cos (m + 1) x}{m} + \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {m = 3} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {\cos (m - 1) x}{m} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {1}{m} [ \cos (m - 1) x - \cos (m + 1) x ] - \left(- \frac {1}{2} + \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \cos x\right) \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left(1 - \frac {\cos x}{2}\right) + \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {1}{m} \sin m x \sin x = \frac {1}{2} \left(1 - \frac {\cos x}{2}\right) - \frac {x}{2} \sin x (| x | <   \pi). \\ \end{array}
$$
