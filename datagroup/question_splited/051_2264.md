【2264】设 $f(x) = \frac{(x + 1)^2(x - 1)}{x^3(x - 2)}$ ，求积分 $\int_{-1}^{3}\frac{f'(x)}{1 + f^2(x)}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 参看 2217 题后面所加的注意.

解 $\int_{-1}^{3}\frac{f'(x)}{1 + f^2(x)}\mathrm{d}x = \int_{-1}^{0}\frac{f'(x)}{1 + f^2(x)}\mathrm{d}x + \int_{0}^{2}\frac{f'(x)}{1 + f^2(x)}\mathrm{d}x + \int_{2}^{3}\frac{f'(x)}{1 + f^2(x)}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \arctan f (x) \left| \begin{array}{l} 0 \\ - 1 \end{array} + \arctan f (x) \right| \left| \begin{array}{l} 2 \\ 0 \end{array} + \arctan f (x) \right| _ {2} ^ {3} \\ = \left(- \frac {\pi}{2} - 0\right) ^ {*}) + \left(- \frac {\pi}{2} - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) + \left(\arctan \frac {4 ^ {2} \cdot 2}{3 ^ {3} \cdot 1} - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) \\ = \arctan \frac {3 2}{2 7} - 2 \pi . \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 参看 2217 题后的注意.
