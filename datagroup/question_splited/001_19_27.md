19.27 Find $\int \sqrt[3]{x^2 - 2x + 1} dx$ .

Note that $x^{2} - 2x + 1 = (x - 1)^{2}$ . So, let $u = x - 1$ , $du = dx$ . Then $\int \sqrt[3]{x^2 - 2x + 1} dx = \int \sqrt[3]{u^2} du = \int u^{2/3} du = \frac {3}{5} u^{5 / 3} + C = \frac {3}{5} (\sqrt[3]{u})^5 + C = \frac {3}{5} (\sqrt[3]{x - 1})^5 + C$ .
