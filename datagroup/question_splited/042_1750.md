【1750】 $\int \cos^4 x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \cos^4 x\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(\frac{1 + \cos 2x}{2}\right)^2\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4}\int \left(1 + 2\cos 2x + \frac{1 + \cos 4x}{2}\right)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{8} \int (3 + 4 \cos 2 x + \cos 4 x) d x = \frac {3}{8} x + \frac {1}{4} \sin 2 x + \frac {1}{3 2} \sin 4 x + C.
$$
