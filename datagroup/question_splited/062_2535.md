【2535】 $\int_{1}^{9}\sqrt{x}\mathrm{d}x\quad (n = 4).$

解 $h = 2$

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {0} = 1, \quad y _ {0} = 1; \quad x _ {1} = 3, \quad y _ {1} = \sqrt {3} = 1. 7 3 2; \\ x _ {2} = 5, \quad y _ {2} = \sqrt {5} = 2. 2 3 6; \quad x _ {3} = 7, \quad y _ {3} = \sqrt {7} = 2. 6 4 6; \\ x _ {4} = 9, \quad y _ {4} = 3. \\ \end{array}
$$

按辛普森公式，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {1} ^ {9} \sqrt {x} d x \approx \frac {h}{3} \left[ \left(y _ {0} + y _ {4}\right) + 4 \left(y _ {1} + y _ {3}\right) + 2 y _ {2} \right] \\ = \frac {2}{3} [ 4 + 4 (1. 7 3 2 + 2. 6 4 6) + 2 (2. 2 3 6) ] \approx 1 7. 3 2 3. \\ \end{array}
$$

实际上， $\int_{1}^{9}\sqrt{x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{2}{3} x^{\frac{3}{2}}\Bigg|_{1}^{9} = \frac{52}{3}\approx 17.333.$
