28.8 $\int x\cos (5x - 1)dx.$

Let \( u = x \), \( dv = \cos (5x - 1)dx \), \( du = dx \), \( v = \frac{1}{3}\sin (5x - 1) \). Then \( \int x\cos (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx = \frac{1}{3} x\sin (5x - 1)dx \).
