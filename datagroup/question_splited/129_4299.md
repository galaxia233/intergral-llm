【4299】 $\oint_{C} (x + y) \, \mathrm{d}x - (x - y) \, \mathrm{d}y$ ，式中 $C$ 为椭圆 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1$ .

解 由于 $P = x + y, Q = -(x - y)$ ，故有

$$
\oint_ {C} (x + y) \mathrm {d} x - (x - y) \mathrm {d} y = \iint_ {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leqslant 1} (- 1 - 1) \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = - 2 \pi a b.
$$

如果直接计算，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \oint_ {C} (x + y) d x - (x - y) d y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \left[ \left(a \cos t + b \sin t\right) (- a \sin t) - \left(a \cos t - b \sin t\right) (b \cos t) \right] d t \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \left[ \left(b ^ {2} - a ^ {2}\right) \cos t \sin t - a b \right] d t = - 2 \pi a b. \\ \end{array}
$$
