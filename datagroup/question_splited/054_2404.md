【2404】 $y^{2} = x^{2}(a^{2} - x^{2}).$

解如图 4.22 所示，图形对称于原点.所求的面积为

$$
S = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {a} x \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = - \frac {4}{3} (a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \left| _ {0} ^ {a} = \frac {4}{3} a ^ {3}. \right.
$$
