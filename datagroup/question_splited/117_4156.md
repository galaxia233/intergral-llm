【4156】求球壳层 $R_1^2 \leqslant \xi^2 + \eta^2 + \zeta^2 \leqslant R_2^2$ 在点 $P(x, y, z)$ 的牛顿引力势. 设密度 $\rho = f(R)$，其中 $f$ 为已知函数，而 $R = \sqrt{\xi^2 + \eta^2 + \zeta^2}$.

提示 仿 4155 题。

解取 $O\zeta$ 轴通过点 $P(x,y,z)$，即得牛顿引力势

$$
u (x, y, z) = \iiint_ {R _ {1} ^ {2} \leqslant \xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} + \zeta^ {2} \leqslant R _ {2} ^ {2}} f (\sqrt {\xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} + \zeta^ {2}}) \frac {\mathrm {d} \xi \mathrm {d} \eta \mathrm {d} \zeta}{\sqrt {\xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} + (\zeta - r) ^ {2}}},
$$

其中 $r = x^2 + y^2 + z^2$.

若引入球坐标，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} u (x, y, z) = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {R _ {1}} ^ {R _ {2}} \rho^ {2} f (\rho) \cos \psi \frac {d \rho}{\sqrt {\rho^ {2} + r ^ {2} - 2 \rho r \sin \psi}} = 2 \pi \int_ {R _ {1}} ^ {R _ {2}} d \rho \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \rho^ {2} f (\rho) \frac {\cos \psi d \psi}{\sqrt {\rho^ {2} + r ^ {2} - 2 \rho r \sin \psi}} \\ = 2 \pi \int_ {R _ {1}} ^ {R _ {2}} \rho^ {2} f (\rho) \left(- \frac {1}{\rho r} \sqrt {\rho^ {2} + r ^ {2} - 2 \rho r \sin \psi}\right) \Bigg | _ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \rho = 2 \pi \int_ {R _ {1}} ^ {R _ {2}} \rho^ {2} f (\rho) \left\{- \frac {1}{\rho r} [ | \rho - r | - (\rho + r) ] \right\} d \rho \\ = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 4 \pi \int_ {R _ {1}} ^ {R _ {2}} \rho f (\rho) d \rho , & \rho > r, \\ 4 \pi \int_ {R _ {1}} ^ {R _ {2}} \frac {\rho^ {2}}{r} f (\rho) d \rho , & \rho \leqslant r. \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

合并之，最后得 $u(x,y,z) = 4\pi \int_{R_1}^{R_2}f(\rho)\min \left(\frac{\rho^2}{r},\rho\right)\mathrm{d}\rho .$
