44.52 Find the average distance $\bar{\rho}$ from the center of a ball $\mathcal{B}$ of radius $a$ to all other points of the ball.

$$
\begin{array}{l} \bar {\rho} = \frac {1}{\text {v o l u m e} V} \cdot \iint \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {a} \rho \cdot \rho^ {2} \sin \phi d \rho d \phi d \theta = \frac {3}{4 \pi a ^ {3}} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {1}{4} \rho^ {4} \sin \phi \Bigg ] _ {0} ^ {a} d \phi d \theta = \\ \frac {3}{4 \pi a ^ {3}} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {1}{4} a ^ {4} \sin^ {\mathcal {B}} \phi d \phi d \theta = \frac {3 a}{1 6 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} - \cos \phi \Bigg ] _ {0} ^ {\pi} d \theta = \frac {3 a}{1 6 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} - (- 1 - 1) d \theta = \frac {3 a}{8 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta = \frac {3 a}{8 \pi} \cdot 2 \pi = \frac {3}{4} a. \end{array}
