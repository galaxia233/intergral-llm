【2065】推出积分 $I_{n} = \int \left(\frac{\sin\frac{x - a}{2}}{\sin\frac{x + a}{2}}\right)^{n}\mathrm{d}x$ （ $n$ 为正整数）的递推公式.

解 解法 1：

设 $t = \frac{\sin\frac{x - a}{2}}{\sin\frac{x + a}{2}}$ 则 $x = 2\arctan \left(\frac{1 + t}{1 - t}\tan \frac{a}{2}\right)$ $\mathrm{dx} = \frac{4\tan\frac{a}{2}}{t^2\sec^2\frac{a}{2} + 2t(\tan^2\frac{a}{2} - 1) + \sec^2\frac{a}{2}}\mathrm{dt}.$

由于 $\frac{4t^n\tan\frac{a}{2}}{t^2\sec^2\frac{a}{2} + 2t\left(\tan^2\frac{a}{2} - 1\right) + \sec^2\frac{a}{2}}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {4 \tan \frac {a}{2}}{\sec^ {2} \frac {a}{2}} t ^ {n - 2} + \frac {- 4 \tan \frac {a}{2}}{t ^ {2} \sec^ {2} \frac {a}{2} + 2 t \left(\tan^ {2} \frac {a}{2} - 1\right) + \sec^ {2} \frac {a}{2}} \cdot \frac {2 \left(\tan^ {2} \frac {a}{2} - 1\right)}{\sec^ {2} \frac {a}{2}} t ^ {n - 1} \\ + \frac {- 4 \tan \frac {a}{2}}{t ^ {2} \sec^ {2} \frac {a}{2} + 2 t \left(\tan^ {2} \frac {a}{2} - 1\right) + \sec^ {2} \frac {a}{2}} t ^ {n - 2} \quad (n > 2), \\ \end{array}
$$

两端对 $t$ 积分，即得递推公式 $I_{n} = \frac{2\sin a}{n - 1} t^{n - 1} + 2\cos a I_{n - 1} - I_{n - 2}.$

解法 2：

设 $y = \frac{x + a}{2}$ 则 $\frac{x - a}{2} = y - a$ ，从而，

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {n} = 2 \int \left[ \frac {\sin (y - a)}{\sin y} \right] ^ {n} d y = 2 \int \frac {\sin (y - a)}{\sin y} \left[ \frac {\sin (y - a)}{\sin y} \right] ^ {n - 1} d y = 2 \int \frac {\sin y \cos a - \cos y \sin a}{\sin y} \left[ \frac {\sin (y - a)}{\sin y} \right] ^ {n - 1} d y \\ = \cos a I _ {n - 1} - 2 \sin a \int \frac {\cos y}{\sin y} \left[ \frac {\sin (y - a)}{\sin y} \right] ^ {n - 1} d y. \\ \end{array}
$$

再设 $\frac{\sin(y - a)}{\sin y} = t = \frac{\sin\frac{x - a}{2}}{\sin\frac{x + a}{2}}, J_n = 2\int \frac{\cos y}{\sin y} t^n\mathrm{d}y,$

则 $I_{n} = \cos a I_{n - 1} - \sin a J_{n - 1},\quad J_{n - 1} = \frac{\cos a I_{n - 1} - I_{n}}{\sin a}.$ (1)

又 $J_{n} = 2\int \frac{\cos y}{\sin y}\left[\frac{\sin(y - a)}{\sin y}\right]^{n}\mathrm{d}y = -\frac{2}{n}\int \sin^{n}(y - a)\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{\sin^{n}y}\right)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {2}{n} \left[ \frac {\sin (y - a)}{\sin y} \right] ^ {n} + 2 \int \frac {\sin^ {n - 1} (y - a)}{\sin^ {n} y} \cos (y - a) d y = - \frac {2}{n} t ^ {n} + 2 \int t ^ {n - 1} \frac {\cos y \cos a + \sin y \sin a}{\sin y} d y \\ = - \frac {2}{n} t ^ {n} + \cos a J _ {n - 1} + \sin a I _ {n - 1}. \tag {2} \\ \end{array}
$$

由（1）式和（2）式解得

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {n} = \cos a I _ {n - 1} - \sin a J _ {n - 1} = \cos a I _ {n - 1} - \sin a \left(- \frac {2}{n - 1} t ^ {n - 1} + \cos a J _ {n - 2} + \sin a I _ {n - 2}\right) \\ = \cos a I _ {n - 1} + \frac {2 \sin a}{n - 1} t ^ {n - 1} - \sin a \cos a \left(\frac {\cos a I _ {n - 2} - I _ {n - 1}}{\sin a}\right) - \sin^ {2} a I _ {n - 2} = 2 \cos a I _ {n - 1} - I _ {n - 2} + \frac {2 \sin a}{n - 1} t ^ {n - 1}. \\ \end{array}
$$
