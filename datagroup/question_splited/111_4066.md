【4066】求曲线 $(x^{2} + y^{2})^{2} = a^{2}(x^{2} - y^{2})$ 所围区域 $S$ 的极转动惯量

$$
I _ {0} = \iint_ {S} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y.
$$

解引用极坐标，则图形 $S$ 的界线的极坐标方程为

$$
r ^ {2} = a ^ {2} \cos 2 \varphi ,
$$

这是双纽线。利用对称性，得 $I_0 = \iint \limits_{S}(x^2 +y^2)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y = 4\int \limits_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}\mathrm{d}\varphi \int \limits_0^{a\sqrt{\cos 2\varphi}}r^3\mathrm{d}r = \int \limits_0^{\frac{\pi}{4}}a^4\cos^2 2\varphi \mathrm{d}\varphi = \frac{\pi a^4}{8}.$
