【4223】 $\int_{C} (x^{2} + y^{2}) \mathrm{d}s$ 其中 $C$ 为曲线 $x = a(\cos t + t \sin t), y = a(\sin t - t \cos t) (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2\pi)$ .

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{a^2t^2\cos^2t + a^2t^2\sin^2t}\mathrm{dt} = at\mathrm{dt}.$

于是， $\int_{C} (x^2 + y^2) \, \mathrm{d}s = \int_{0}^{2\pi} [a^2 (\cos t + t \sin t)^2 + a^2 (\sin t - t \cos t)^2] at \, \mathrm{d}t = \int_{0}^{2\pi} a^3 t (1 + t^2) \, \mathrm{d}t = 2\pi^2 a^3 (1 + 2\pi^2).$
