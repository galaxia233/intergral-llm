34.92 Show that $\mathbf{T}'(t) = \frac{v\mathbf{a} - (dv / dt)\mathbf{v}}{v^2}$ .

$\mathbf{T}(t) = (1 / v)\mathbf{R}'(t)$ . By the product rule,

$$
\mathbf {T} ^ {\prime} (t) = \frac {1}{v} \mathbf {R} ^ {\prime \prime} (t) - \frac {1}{v ^ {2}} \frac {d v}{d t} \mathbf {R} ^ {\prime} (t) = \frac {1}{v ^ {2}} \left[ v \mathbf {R} ^ {\prime \prime} (t) - \frac {d v}{d t} \mathbf {R} ^ {\prime} (t) \right] = \frac {v \mathbf {a} - (d v / d t) \mathbf {v}}{v ^ {2}}
$$
