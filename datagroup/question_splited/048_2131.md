【2131】 $\int \frac{x + 2}{x^2\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $x = \sin t$ ，并限制 $-\frac{\pi}{2} < t < \frac{\pi}{2}$ ，则 $\mathrm{dx} = \cos t\mathrm{dt}$ 代人得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x + 2}{x ^ {2} \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} d x = \int \frac {\sin t + 2}{\sin^ {2} t} d t = \int \frac {d t}{\sin t} + 2 \int \frac {d t}{\sin^ {2} t} = \ln | \csc t - \cot t | - 2 \cot t + C \\ = - \ln \frac {1 + \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}{| x |} - \frac {2 \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}{x} + C (0 <   | x | <   1). \\ \end{array}
$$
