【4425】求 $\operatorname {div}(\operatorname {grad}u)$
提示利用梯度及散度的结果，易得结果为 $\Delta u$ （或记成 $\nabla^2 u$ ）
解 $\operatorname{div}(\operatorname{grad} u) = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial x}\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial y}\right) + \frac{\partial}{\partial z}\left(\frac{\partial u}{\partial z}\right) = \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial y^2} + \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial z^2} = \Delta u$ （或记成 $\nabla^2 u$ ）.
