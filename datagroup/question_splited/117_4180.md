【4180】 $\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}\int_{-\infty}^{+\infty}xy\mathrm{e}^{-\left(\frac{x^2}{a^2} +2\varepsilon \frac{x}{a}\cdot \frac{y}{b} +\frac{y^2}{b^2}\right)}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y(0 <   |\varepsilon | <   1).$

解 作广义极坐标变换 $x = \arccos \theta, y = b\sin \theta$ ，则有

$$
I = \int_ {- \infty} ^ {+ \infty} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {+ \infty} x y e ^ {- \left(\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + 2 \epsilon \frac {x}{a} \cdot \frac {y}{b} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}\right)} d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {1}{2} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} r ^ {3} \sin 2 \theta e ^ {- r ^ {2} (1 + \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)} d r d \theta . \tag {1}
$$

由于 $\left|r^{3} \sin 2 \theta e^{-r^{2}(1 + \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)}\right| \leqslant r^{3} e^{-r^{2}(1 - |\epsilon|)}$ ，而积分

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} r ^ {3} e ^ {- r ^ {2} (1 - | \epsilon |)} d r d \theta = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} r ^ {3} e ^ {- r ^ {2} (1 - | \epsilon |)} d r = 2 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} r ^ {3} e ^ {- r ^ {2} (1 - | \epsilon |)} d r <   + \infty ,
$$

故 (1) 式中的二重广义积分收敛。于是，

$$
I = \frac {1}{2} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \sin 2 \theta d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} r ^ {3} e ^ {- r ^ {2} (1 + \varepsilon \sin 2 \theta)} d r. \tag {2}
$$

但是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} r ^ {3} e ^ {- r ^ {2} (1 + \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)} d r = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} t e ^ {- t (1 + \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)} d t = - \frac {1}{2 (1 + \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)} \left[ t e ^ {- t (1 + \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)} \left| _ {r = 0} ^ {t = + \infty} - \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} e ^ {- t (1 - \epsilon \sin 2 \theta)} d t \right. \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 (1 + \varepsilon \sin 2 \theta)} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} e ^ {- t (1 + \varepsilon \sin 2 \theta)} d t = \frac {1}{2 (1 + \varepsilon \sin 2 \theta) ^ {2}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故

$$
\begin{array}{l} I = \frac {1}{4} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {\sin 2 \theta}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin 2 \theta) ^ {2}} d \theta = \frac {1}{2} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin 2 \theta}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin 2 \theta) ^ {2}} d \theta = \frac {1}{4} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {\sin u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} d u \\ = \frac {1}{4} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} d u + \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\pi} \frac {\sin u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} d u + \int_ {\pi} ^ {\frac {3 \pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} d u + \int_ {\frac {3 \pi}{2}} ^ {2 \pi} \frac {\sin u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} d u \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2} a ^ {2} b ^ {2} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u \mathrm {d} u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} - \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u \mathrm {d} u}{(1 - \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} \right]. \tag {3} \\ \end{array}
$$

但是（作代换 $u = \frac{\pi}{2} - v$ ），

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u \mathrm {d} u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{\varepsilon} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \left[ \frac {1}{1 + \varepsilon \sin u} - \frac {1}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} \right] \mathrm {d} u = \frac {1}{\varepsilon} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \left[ \frac {1}{1 + \varepsilon \cos v} - \frac {1}{(1 + \varepsilon \cos v) ^ {2}} \right] \mathrm {d} v,
$$

同理，有 $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\frac{\sin u\mathrm{d}u}{(1 - \varepsilon\sin u)^{2}} = -\frac{1}{\varepsilon}\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\left[\frac{1}{1 - \varepsilon\cos v} -\frac{1}{(1 - \varepsilon\cos v)^{2}}\right]\mathrm{d}v.$

根据 2028 题 (1) 和 2063 题的结果，可知（当 $0 < |\varepsilon| < 1$ 时）

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{1 + \varepsilon \cos x} = \frac {2}{\sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2}}} \arctan \left(\sqrt {\frac {1 - \varepsilon}{1 + \varepsilon}} \tan \frac {x}{2}\right) + C, \tag {4}
$$

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(1 + \varepsilon \cos x) ^ {2}} = - \frac {\varepsilon \sin x}{(1 - \varepsilon^ {2}) (1 + \varepsilon \cos x)} + \frac {2}{(1 - \varepsilon^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} \arctan \left(\sqrt {\frac {1 - \varepsilon}{1 + \varepsilon}} \tan \frac {x}{2}\right) + C. \tag {5}
$$

（注意，2028 题 (1) 和 2063 题中假定 $0 < \varepsilon < 1$ ，但从其推导过程可以看出公式 (4)、(5) 当 $-1 < \varepsilon < 0$ 时也成立）于是，

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u \mathrm {d} u}{(1 + \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{\varepsilon} \left[ \frac {2}{\sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2}}} \arctan \sqrt {\frac {1 - \varepsilon}{1 + \varepsilon}} + \frac {\varepsilon}{1 - \varepsilon^ {2}} - \frac {2}{(1 - \varepsilon^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} \arctan \sqrt {\frac {1 - \varepsilon}{1 + \varepsilon}} \right],
$$

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\sin u \mathrm {d} u}{(1 - \varepsilon \sin u) ^ {2}} = - \frac {1}{\varepsilon} \left[ \frac {2}{\sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2}}} \arctan \sqrt {\frac {1 + \varepsilon}{1 - \varepsilon}} - \frac {\varepsilon}{1 - \varepsilon^ {2}} - \frac {2}{(1 - \varepsilon^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} \arctan \sqrt {\frac {1 + \varepsilon}{1 - \varepsilon}} \right].
$$

从而，由 (3) 式得 $I = \frac{1}{\varepsilon} a^{2}b^{2}\left[\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \varepsilon^{2}}} -\frac{1}{(1 - \varepsilon^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}\right]\left[\arctan \sqrt{\frac{1 - \varepsilon}{1 + \varepsilon}} +\arctan \sqrt{\frac{1 + \varepsilon}{1 - \varepsilon}}\right].$

但对任何的 $x > 0$ ，有 $\arctan x + \arctan \frac{1}{x} = \frac{\pi}{2}$

故最后得 $I = \frac{1}{\varepsilon} a^{2}b^{2}\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - \varepsilon^{2}}} -\frac{1}{(1 - \varepsilon^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}\right)\frac{\pi}{2} = -\frac{\pi\varepsilon a^{2}b^{2}}{2(1 - \varepsilon^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}}$
