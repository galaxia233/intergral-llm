【2085】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 + \mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}} + \mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{3}} + \mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{6}}}$

解设 $\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{6}} = t$ ，则 $x = 6\ln t$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = \frac{6}{t}\mathrm{dt}$ .代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{1 + \mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {x}{2}} + \mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {t}{3}} + \mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {t}{6}}} = 6 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{t (1 + t ^ {3} + t ^ {2} + t)} \\ = 6 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{t (t + 1) \left(t ^ {2} + 1\right)} = 6 \int \left[ \frac {1}{t} - \frac {1}{2 (t + 1)} - \frac {t + 1}{2 \left(t ^ {2} + 1\right)} \right] \mathrm {d} t \\ = 6 \ln t - 3 \ln (t + 1) - \frac {3}{2} \ln (1 + t ^ {2}) - 3 \arctan t + C \\ = x - 3 \ln \left[ \left(1 + e ^ {\frac {x}{6}}\right) \sqrt {1 + e ^ {\frac {x}{3}}} \right] - 3 \arctan \left(e ^ {\frac {x}{6}}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
