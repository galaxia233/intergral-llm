【2466】 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2},\quad x^{2} + y^{2} = ax.$

解如图4.44所示，过点 $M(x,0,0)$ 垂直于 $Ox$ 轴作一平面，在所给立体上截出一曲边梯形，其曲边由方程

$$
z = \sqrt {\left(a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}\right) - y ^ {2}}
$$

给出（上半面），其变化范围为：

$-\sqrt{ax - x^2} \leqslant y \leqslant \sqrt{ax - x^2}$ （如图中ABCD）.

从而，其截面积为

![](images/5c8e8dbe51c369b78a0f0fdef871f66cb6298b0632a32553d1d92e4cb8a12b23.jpg)  
图4.43

$$
\begin{array}{l} S (x) = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}}} \sqrt {(a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}) - y ^ {2}} d y \\ = a ^ {\frac {3}{2}} x ^ {\frac {1}{2}} - a ^ {\frac {1}{2}} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} + \left(a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}\right) \arcsin \sqrt {\frac {x}{a + x}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，所求的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {a} S (x) d x \\ = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left[ a ^ {\frac {3}{2}} x ^ {\frac {1}{2}} - a ^ {\frac {1}{2}} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} + \left(a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}\right) \arcsin \sqrt {\frac {x}{a + x}} \right] d x \\ = 4 \left\{\frac {1}{3} a ^ {3} - \frac {1}{5} a ^ {3} + \left[ \left(\frac {\pi a ^ {3}}{4} - \frac {1}{2} a ^ {3}\right) - \left(\frac {1}{1 2} \pi a ^ {3} - \frac {1 3}{9 0} a ^ {3}\right) \right] \right\} \\ = \frac {2}{3} a ^ {3} \left(\pi - \frac {4}{3}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
