【2939】在区间 $(0, 2l)$ 内展开 $f(x) = \begin{cases} A, & 0 < x < l, \\ 0, & l < x < 2l, \end{cases}$ 其中 $A$ 为常数.

解由于

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {0} = \frac {1}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {2 l} f (x) d x = \frac {1}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {l} A d x = A, \\ a _ {n} = \frac {1}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {2 l} f (x) \cos \frac {n \pi x}{l} d x = \frac {1}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {l} A \cos \frac {n \pi x}{l} d x = 0; \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
b _ {n} = \frac {1}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {2 l} f (x) \sin \frac {n \pi x}{l} d x = \frac {1}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {l} A \sin \frac {n \pi x}{l} d x = \frac {A}{n \pi} [ 1 - (- 1) ^ {n} ],
$$

故按展开定理， $f(x)$ 可展开为

$$
\frac {A}{2} + \frac {2 A}{\pi} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{2 k + 1} \sin (2 k + 1) \frac {\pi x}{l} = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} A, & 0 < x < l \\ \frac {A}{2}, & x = l, \\ 0, & l < x < 2 l. \end{array} \right.
$$
