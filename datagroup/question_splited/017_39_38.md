39.38 Find the Taylor series for $x^4$ about $-3$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbf {I} x ^ {4} = [ - 3 + (x + 3) ] ^ {4} \\ = (- 3) ^ {4} + 4 (- 3) ^ {3} (x + 3) + \frac {4 \cdot 3}{1 \cdot 2} (- 3) ^ {2} (x + 3) ^ {2} + \frac {4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2}{1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3} (- 3) (x + 3) ^ {3} + \frac {4 \cdot 3 \cdot 2 \cdot 1}{1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3 \cdot 4} (x + 3) ^ {4} \\ = 8 1 - 1 0 8 (x + 3) + 5 4 (x + 3) ^ {2} - 1 2 (x + 3) ^ {3} + (x + 3) ^ {4}. \\ \end{array}
$$
