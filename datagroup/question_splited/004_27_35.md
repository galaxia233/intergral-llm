27.35 $y = \tan^{-1}\left(\frac{a + x}{1 - ax}\right).$

$$
\begin{array}{l} y ^ {\prime} = \frac {1}{1 + \left[ (a + x) / (1 - a x) \right] ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {(1 - a x) - (a + x) (- a)}{(1 - a x) ^ {2}} = \frac {1 - a x + a ^ {2} + a x}{(1 - a x) ^ {2} + (a + x) ^ {2}} = \frac {1 + a ^ {2}}{1 + a ^ {2} x ^ {2} + a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {1 + a ^ {2}}{(1 + a ^ {2}) (1 + x ^ {2})} = \frac {1}{1 + x ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$
