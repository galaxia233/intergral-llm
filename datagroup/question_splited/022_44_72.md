$I = \int_{z_1}^{z_2}\int_{y_1}^{y_2}\int_{x_1}^{x_2}f(x)g(y)h(z)dxdydz = \int_{z_1}^{z_2}\int_{y_1}^{y_2}g(y)h(z)\left[\int_{x_1}^{x_2}f(x)dx\right]dydz = a\int_{z_1}^{z_2}h(z)\left[\int_{y_1}^{y_2}g(y)dy\right]dz =$ $ab\int_{z_1}^{z_2}h(z)dz = abc.$
