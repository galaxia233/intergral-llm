【4103】 $x^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}, x + y = \pm a, x - y = \pm a.$

解 $V = 8\int_{0}^{a}\mathrm{d}x\int_{0}^{a - x}\mathrm{d}y\int_{0}^{\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}}\mathrm{d}z = 8\int_{0}^{a}(a - x)\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= 8 a \left[ \frac {x}{2} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{2} \arcsin \frac {x}{a} \right] \left| _ {0} ^ {a} + \frac {8}{3} (a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \right| _ {0} ^ {a} = \frac {2 a ^ {3}}{3} (3 \pi - 4).
$$
