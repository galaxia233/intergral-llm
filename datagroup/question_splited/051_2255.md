【2255】证明等式： $\int_0^a x^3 f(x^2)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int_0^{a^2}xf(x)\mathrm{d}x\quad (a > 0).$

证设 $x = \sqrt{t}$ ，则

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {a} x ^ {3} f (x ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x = \int_ {0} ^ {a ^ {2}} t ^ {\frac {3}{2}} f (t) \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{2 \sqrt {t}} = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {a ^ {2}} t f (t) \mathrm {d} t, \quad \text {即} \quad \int_ {0} ^ {a} x ^ {3} f (x ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {a ^ {2}} x f (x) \mathrm {d} x.
$$
