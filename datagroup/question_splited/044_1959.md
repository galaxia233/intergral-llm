【1959】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x^4)\sqrt{1 + x^2}}.$

解设 $x = \tan t$ ，并限制 $-\frac{\pi}{2} < t < \frac{\pi}{2}$ 且 $|t| \neq \frac{\pi}{4}$ ，则 $\mathrm{d}x = \sec^2 t \, \mathrm{d}t$ ， $\sqrt{1 + x^2} = \sec t.$

代入得 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x^4)\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} = \int \frac{\sec^2 t \, \mathrm{d}t}{(1 - \tan^4 t) \sec t} = \int \frac{\cos^3 t \, \mathrm{d}t}{1 - 2\sin^2 t} = \int \frac{1 - \sin^2 t}{1 - 2\sin^2 t} \, \mathrm{d}(\sin t)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {1 - 2 \sin^ {2} t}{1 - 2 \sin^ {2} t} d (\sin t) + \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {d (\sin t)}{1 - 2 \sin^ {2} t} = \frac {1}{2} \sin t + \frac {1}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \left| \frac {1 + \sqrt {2} \sin t}{1 - \sqrt {2} \sin t} \right| + C \\ = \frac {x}{2 \sqrt {1 + x ^ {2}}} + \frac {1}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \left| \frac {\sqrt {1 + x ^ {2}} + x \sqrt {2}}{\sqrt {1 + x ^ {2}} - x \sqrt {2}} \right| + C \quad (| x | \neq 1). \\ \end{array}
