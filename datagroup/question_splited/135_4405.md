【4405】求场 $u = \frac{x}{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$ 在点 $A(1,2,2)$ 及 $B(-3,1,0)$ 的梯度之间的夹角 $\varphi$
解 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = \frac{y^2 + z^2 - x^2}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^2}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = -\frac{2xy}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^2}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = -\frac{2xz}{(x^2 + y^2 + z^2)^2}$ .
在点 $A, B$ 的梯度分别为
$$
\operatorname {g r a d} u (A) = \frac {7}{8 1} i - \frac {4}{8 1} j - \frac {4}{8 1} k, \quad \operatorname {g r a d} u (B) = - \frac {2}{2 5} i + \frac {3}{5 0} j.
$$
于是，
$$
\cos \theta = \frac {\operatorname {g r a d} u (A) \cdot \operatorname {g r a d} u (B)}{| \operatorname {g r a d} u (A) | \cdot | \operatorname {g r a d} u (B) |} = \frac {- \frac {1}{4 0 5}}{\frac {1}{9} \cdot \frac {1}{1 0}} = - \frac {8}{9}.
$$
