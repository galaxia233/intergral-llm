【4246】求均匀的弧 $x = \mathrm{e}^{t}\cos t, y = \mathrm{e}^{t}\sin t, z = \mathrm{e}^{t}(-\infty < t \leqslant 0)$ 的质心的坐标.

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{\mathrm{e}^{2t}(\cos t - \sin t)^2 + \mathrm{e}^{2t}(\sin t + \cos t)^2 + \mathrm{e}^{2t}}\mathrm{dt} = \sqrt{3}\mathrm{e}^t\mathrm{dt}$ 质量为 $M = \int_{\infty}^{0}\sqrt{3}\mathrm{e}^{t}\mathrm{d}t = \sqrt{3}.$

于是，质心的坐标为

$$
\begin{array}{l} x _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {t} \cos t \cdot \sqrt {3} e ^ {t} d t = \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {2 t} \cos t d t = \frac {2 \cos t + \sin t}{5} e ^ {2 t} \left| _ {- \infty} ^ {0} = \frac {2}{5}. \right. \\ y _ {1 0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {t} \sin t \cdot \sqrt {3} e ^ {t} d t = \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {2 t} \sin t d t = \frac {2 \sin t - \cos t}{5} e ^ {2 t} \left| _ {- \infty} ^ {0} = - \frac {1}{5}. \right. \\ z _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {t} \cdot \sqrt {3} e ^ {t} d t = \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {2 t} d t = \frac {1}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
