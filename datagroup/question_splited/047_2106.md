【2106】 $\int \sqrt{x}\arctan \sqrt{x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \sqrt{x} \arctan \sqrt{x} \, \mathrm{d}x = \frac{2}{3} \int \arctan \sqrt{x} \, \mathrm{d}\left(x^{\frac{3}{2}}\right) = \frac{2}{3} x^{\frac{3}{2}} \arctan \sqrt{x} - \frac{1}{3} \int \frac{x}{1 + x} \, \mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {2}{3} x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \arctan \sqrt {x} - \frac {1}{3} \int \left(1 - \frac {1}{1 + x}\right) d x = \frac {2}{3} x \sqrt {x} \arctan \sqrt {x} - \frac {x}{3} + \frac {1}{3} \ln (1 + x) + C.
$$
