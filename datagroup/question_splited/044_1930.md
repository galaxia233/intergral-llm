【1930】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x}(1 + \sqrt[4]{x})^3}$

解设 $\sqrt[4]{x} = t$ ，则 $x = t^4$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = 4t^{3}\mathrm{dt}$ 代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {x} (1 + \sqrt [ 4 ]{x}) ^ {3}} = 4 \int \frac {t \mathrm {d} t}{(1 + t) ^ {3}} = 4 \int \left[ \frac {1}{(1 + t) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{(1 + t) ^ {3}} \right] \mathrm {d} t = - \frac {4}{1 + t} + \frac {2}{(1 + t) ^ {2}} + C \\ = \frac {2}{(1 + \sqrt [ 4 ]{x}) ^ {2}} - \frac {4}{1 + \sqrt [ 4 ]{x}} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
