【4438】证明： $\operatorname {div}(a\times b) = b\cdot \operatorname {rot}a - a\cdot \operatorname {rot}b.$
提示 利用散度及旋度的定义易证.
证 $\operatorname{div}(\pmb{a} \times \pmb{b}) = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} (a_y b_z - a_z b_y) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y} (a_z b_x - a_x b_z) + \frac{\partial}{\partial z} (a_x b_y - a_y b_z)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = b _ {x} \left(\frac {\partial a _ {z}}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial a _ {y}}{\partial z}\right) + b _ {y} \left(\frac {\partial a _ {x}}{\partial z} - \frac {\partial a _ {z}}{\partial x}\right) + b _ {z} \left(\frac {\partial a _ {y}}{\partial x} - \frac {\partial a _ {x}}{\partial y}\right) \\ - a _ {x} \left(\frac {\partial b _ {z}}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial b _ {y}}{\partial z}\right) - a _ {y} \left(\frac {\partial b _ {x}}{\partial z} - \frac {\partial b _ {z}}{\partial x}\right) - a _ {z} \left(\frac {\partial b _ {y}}{\partial x} - \frac {\partial b _ {x}}{\partial y}\right) \\ = \boldsymbol {b} \cdot \operatorname {r o t} \boldsymbol {a} - \boldsymbol {a} \cdot \operatorname {r o t} \boldsymbol {b}. \\ \end{array}
$$
