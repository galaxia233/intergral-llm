【2416】 $x = a(2\cos t - \cos 2t),y = a(2\sin t - \sin 2t).$

解 所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \left(x y _ {t} ^ {\prime} - y x _ {t} ^ {\prime}\right) d t \\ = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} [ a (2 \cos t - \cos 2 t) a (2 \cos t - 2 \cos 2 t) - a (2 \sin t - \sin 2 t) a (- 2 \sin t + 2 \sin 2 t) ] d t \\ = 3 a ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (1 - \cos t \cos 2 t - \sin t \sin 2 t) d t = 3 a ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (1 - \cos t) d t = 6 \pi a ^ {2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
