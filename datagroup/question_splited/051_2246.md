【2246】 $\int_0^a x^2\sqrt{a^2 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x(a > 0).$

解设 $x = a\sin t$ ，则

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {a} x ^ {2} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = a ^ {4} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2} t \cos^ {2} t d t = \frac {a ^ {2}}{4} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2} 2 t d t = \frac {a ^ {4}}{8} \left(t - \frac {1}{4} \sin 4 t\right) \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {\pi a ^ {4}}{1 6}.
$$
