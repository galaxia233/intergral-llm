【4295】当单位质量从点 $M_{1}(x_{1},y_{1},z_{1})$ 移动到点 $M_2(x_2,y_2,z_2)$ 时，求引力 $F = \frac{G}{r^2}$ （其中 $r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$ ）对它所作的功，其中 $G$ 是引力常量.

解引力指向坐标原点，故它的方向余弦为

$$
\cos a = - \frac {x}{r}, \quad \cos \beta = - \frac {y}{r}, \quad \cos \gamma = - \frac {z}{r},
$$

而引力的投影为 $X = -\frac{Gx}{r^3}, \quad Y = -\frac{Gy}{r^3}, \quad Z = -\frac{Gz}{r^3}.$

于是，引力所作的功为

$$
\begin{array}{l} A = - G \int_ {\left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right)} ^ {\left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right)} \frac {x d x + y d y + z d z}{r ^ {3}} = - \frac {G}{2} \int_ {\left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right)} ^ {\left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right)} \frac {d \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}\right)}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} = \frac {G}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}} \Bigg | _ {\left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right)} ^ {\left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right)} \\ = k \left(\frac {1}{x _ {2} ^ {2} + y _ {2} ^ {2} + z _ {2} ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{x _ {1} ^ {2} + y _ {1} ^ {2} + z _ {1} ^ {2}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

当然，这里假设从 $M_{1}$ 点到 $M_{2}$ 点的路径是不经过原点的，上式表明功与路径无关，仅决定于起始点的坐标.
