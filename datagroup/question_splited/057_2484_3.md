【2484】 $(x^{2} + y^{2})^{2} = a^{2}(x^{2} - y^{2})$
(3) 绕直线 $y = x$ .

解（3）利用对称性知，所求的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \frac {4 \pi}{3} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} r ^ {3} \sin \left(\frac {\pi}{4} - \varphi\right) d \varphi = \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \sqrt {\cos^ {3} 2 \varphi} \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \cos \varphi - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \sin \varphi\right) d \varphi \\ = \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3 \sqrt {2}} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{4}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \sqrt {\cos^ {3} 2 \varphi} \cos \varphi d \varphi . \\ \end{array}
$$

若用本题（2）的变换，即得

$$
V = \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3 \sqrt {2}} 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \cos^ {4} x d x = \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {4} x d x = \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3} \cdot \frac {3 \cdot 1}{4 \cdot 2} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} = \frac {\pi^ {2} a ^ {3}}{4}.
$$
