41.92 Find a spherical equation for the surface whose rectangular equation is $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} + 6z = 0$ .

$x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = \rho^{2}$ and $z = \rho \cos \phi$ . Thus, we get $\rho^2 + 6\rho \cos \phi = 0$ . Then $\rho = 0$ or $\rho + 6\cos \phi = 0$ . Since the origin is the only solution of $\rho = 0$ and the origin also lies on $\rho + 6\cos \phi = 0$ , then $\rho + 6\cos \phi = 0$ is the desired equation.
