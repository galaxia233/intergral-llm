【4171】 $\iint_{x^2 + y^2 \leq 1} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{1 - x^2 - y^2}}.$

解 采用极坐标，由于被积函数非负，故有

$$
\iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant 1} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {d} \theta \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {r}{\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}} \mathrm {d} r = 2 \pi (- \sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}) \Big | _ {r = 0} ^ {r = 1} = 2 \pi .
$$
