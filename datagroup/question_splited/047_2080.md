【2080】 $\int \cos^2\sqrt{x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $\sqrt{x} = t$ ，则 $x = t^2, \mathrm{d}x = 2t\mathrm{d}t.$ 于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \cos^ {2} \sqrt {x} d x = 2 \int t \cos^ {2} t d t = \int t (1 + \cos 2 t) d t \\ = \frac {t ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {1}{2} \int t d (\sin 2 t) = \frac {t ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {1}{2} t \sin 2 t - \frac {1}{2} \int \sin 2 t d t \\ = \frac {t ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {1}{2} t \sin 2 t + \frac {1}{4} \cos 2 t + C = \frac {x}{2} + \frac {1}{2} \sqrt {x} \sin (2 \sqrt {x}) + \frac {1}{4} \cos (2 \sqrt {x}) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
