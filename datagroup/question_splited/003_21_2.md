21.2 Sketch and find the area of the region above the line $y = 3x - 2$ , in the first quadrant, and below the line $y = 4$ .

See Fig. 21-2. The region has a base on the $y$ -axis. We must solve $y = 3x - 2$ for $x: x = \frac{1}{3}(y + 2)$ . Then the area is $\int_{0}^{4} \frac{1}{3}(y + 2) dy = \frac{1}{3}\left(\frac{1}{2} y^2 + 2y\right)\int_{0}^{4} = \frac{1}{3}(8 + 8) = \frac{16}{3}$ .
