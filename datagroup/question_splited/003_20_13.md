20.13 $\int_4^5\left(\frac{2}{\sqrt{x}} -x\right)dx.$

$\int \left(\frac{2}{\sqrt{x}} - x\right) dx = \int (2x^{-1/2} - x) dx = 4x^{1/2} - \frac{1}{2}x^2.$ Hence, $\int_4^5 \left(\frac{2}{\sqrt{x}} - x\right) dx = (4x^{1/2} - \frac{1}{2}x^2)\bigg]_4^5 = [4\sqrt{5} - \frac{1}{2}(5)^2] - [4\sqrt{4} - \frac{1}{2}(4)^2] = (4\sqrt{5} - \frac{25}{2}) - (8 - 8) = 4\sqrt{5} - \frac{25}{2}.$
