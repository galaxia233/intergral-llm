【4061】 求由下列曲线所围的面积 $(\rho = 1)$ 对坐标轴 $Ox$ 和 $Oy$ 的转动惯量 $I_{x}$ 和 $I_{y}$ :$\frac{x}{b_1} +\frac{y}{h} = 1,\frac{x}{b_2} +\frac{y}{h} = 1,y = 0(b_1 > 0,b_2 > 0,h > 0).$

解 若设 $b_{2} > b_{1}$ ，则

$$
I _ {x} = \int_ {0} ^ {h} y ^ {2} d y \int_ {(1 - \frac {y}{h}) b _ {1}} ^ {(1 - \frac {y}{h}) h _ {0}} d x = (b _ {2} - b _ {1}) \int_ {0} ^ {h} y ^ {2} \left(1 - \frac {y}{h}\right) d y = \frac {\left(b _ {2} - b _ {1}\right) h ^ {3}}{1 2},
$$

$$
I _ {y} = \int_ {0} ^ {h} d y \int_ {(1 - \frac {x}{h}) b _ {1}} ^ {(1 - \frac {x}{h}) b _ {2}} x ^ {2} d x = \frac {b _ {2} ^ {3} - b _ {1} ^ {3}}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {h} \left(1 - \frac {y}{h}\right) ^ {3} d y = \frac {h \left(b _ {2} ^ {3} - b _ {1} ^ {3}\right)}{1 2};
$$

若设 $b_{1} > b_{2}$ ，则 $I_{x} = \frac{(b_{1} - b_{2})h^{3}}{12},\qquad I_{y} = \frac{h(b_{1}^{3} - b_{2}^{3})}{12}.$
