【4269】 $\int_{(0,0)}^{(a,b)}\mathrm{e}^x (\cos y\mathrm{d}x - \sin y\mathrm{d}y).$

解 显然，有 $\mathrm{e}^r (\cos y\mathrm{d}x - \sin y\mathrm{d}y) = \mathrm{d}(\mathrm{e}^x\cos y)$ ，于是，

$$
\int_ {(0, 0)} ^ {(a, b)} \mathrm {e} ^ {x} (\cos y \mathrm {d} x - \sin y \mathrm {d} y) = \int_ {(0, 0)} ^ {(a, b)} \mathrm {d} (\mathrm {e} ^ {x} \cos y) = (\mathrm {e} ^ {x} \cos y) \left| _ {(0, 0)} ^ {(a, b)} = \mathrm {e} ^ {a} \cos b - 1. \right.
$$
