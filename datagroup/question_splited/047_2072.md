【2072】 $\int x^{7}\mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x^{7} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2} \int (x^{2})^{3} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} \mathrm{d}(x^{2}) = \frac{1}{2} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} \left( \frac{x^{6}}{-1} - \frac{3x^{4}}{1} + \frac{6x^{2}}{-1} - \frac{6}{1} \right)^{+1} + C$

$$
= - \frac {1}{2} e ^ {- x ^ {2}} (x ^ {6} + 3 x ^ {4} + 6 x ^ {2} + 6) + C.
$$

\*) 利用 2066 题的结果.
