【1768】 $\int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{2 - x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $2 - x = t$ ，则 $x = 2 - t, \mathrm{d}x = -\mathrm{d}t,$

故得 $\int \frac{x^2}{\sqrt{2 - x}}\mathrm{d}x = -\int t^{-\frac{1}{2}}(2 - t)^2\mathrm{d}t = -\int (4t^{-\frac{1}{2}} - 4t^{\frac{1}{2}} + t^{\frac{3}{2}})\mathrm{d}t = -8t^{\frac{1}{2}} + \frac{8}{3} t^{\frac{3}{2}} - \frac{2}{5} t^{\frac{5}{2}} + C$

$$
= - \frac {2}{1 5} (3 2 + 8 x + 3 x ^ {2}) \sqrt {2 - x} + C.
$$
