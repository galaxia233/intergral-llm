45.12 Prove that $\frac{d}{dt}[\mathbf{R}(t) \times \mathbf{R}'(t)] = \mathbf{R}(t) \times \mathbf{R}''(t)$ .

By Problem 45.11, $\frac{d}{dt}[\mathbf{R}(t) \times \mathbf{R}'(t)] = [\mathbf{R}(t) \times \mathbf{R}''(t)] + [\mathbf{R}'(t) \times \mathbf{R}'(t)]$ . But $\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{A} = \mathbf{0}$ for all $\mathbf{A}$ . Hence, the term $\mathbf{R}'(t) \times \mathbf{R}'(t)$ vanishes.
