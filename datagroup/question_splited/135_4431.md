【4431】设流体充满空间并以恒定的角速度 $\pmb{\varpi}$ 依逆时针方向绕 $Oz$ 轴旋转。求速度向量 $\pmb{\nu}$ 和加速度向量 $\pmb{w}$ 在已知时刻在空间点 $M(x, y, z)$ 处的散度.
解 $\nu = \nu_{0} + w \times r$ . 微分之，即得
$$
\begin{array}{l} \boldsymbol {w} = \boldsymbol {w} _ {0} + \dot {\boldsymbol {w}} \times \boldsymbol {r} + \boldsymbol {w} \times \dot {\boldsymbol {r}} = \boldsymbol {w} _ {0} + \dot {\boldsymbol {w}} \times \boldsymbol {r} + \boldsymbol {w} \times \boldsymbol {v} = \boldsymbol {w} _ {0} + \dot {\boldsymbol {w}} \times \boldsymbol {r} + \boldsymbol {w} \times (\boldsymbol {v} _ {0} + \boldsymbol {w} \times \boldsymbol {r}) \\ = w _ {0} + w \times r + w \times v _ {0} + (w \cdot r) w - (w \cdot w) r ^ {*}). \\ \end{array}
$$

为了计算 $\mathrm{div}\pmb{\nu}$ 和 $\mathrm{div}\pmb{w}$ ，先计算 $\mathrm{div}(a\times r)$ ，此处 $a$ 为常向量。由于
$$
(a \times r) _ {x} = a _ {y} z - a _ {z} y, \quad (a \times r) _ {y} = a _ {z} x - a _ {x} z, \quad (a \times r) _ {z} = a _ {x} y - a _ {y} x,
$$
故得 $\operatorname{div}(a \times r) = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} (a_y z - a_z y) + \frac{\partial}{\partial y} (a_x x - a_x z) + \frac{\partial}{\partial z} (a_x y - a_y x) = 0.$
于是，即得
$$
\operatorname {d i v} \boldsymbol {v} = \operatorname {d i v} \boldsymbol {v} _ {0} + \operatorname {d i v} (\boldsymbol {w} \times \boldsymbol {r}) = 0.
$$
$$
\operatorname {d i v} w = \operatorname {d i v} w _ {0} + \operatorname {d i v} (w \times r) + \operatorname {d i v} (w \times v _ {0}) + \operatorname {d i v} [ (w \cdot r) w ] - \operatorname {d i v} [ (w \cdot w) r ],
$$
而 $\operatorname{div}[(\boldsymbol{w} \cdot \boldsymbol{r})\boldsymbol{w}] = \boldsymbol{w} \cdot r\operatorname{div}\boldsymbol{w} + \boldsymbol{w} \cdot \operatorname{grad}(\boldsymbol{w} \cdot \boldsymbol{r})^{**} = \boldsymbol{w} \cdot \boldsymbol{w}^{***} = \boldsymbol{w}^2$
及 $\operatorname{div}[(\boldsymbol{w} \cdot \boldsymbol{w})\boldsymbol{r}] = \boldsymbol{w} \cdot \boldsymbol{w}\operatorname{div}\boldsymbol{r} + \boldsymbol{r} \cdot \operatorname{grad}(\boldsymbol{w} \cdot \boldsymbol{w}) = 3\boldsymbol{w}^2,$
从而，最后得 $\mathrm{div}w = w^{2} - 3w^{2} = -2w^{2}$
