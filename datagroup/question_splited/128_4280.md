【4280】 $\int_{C} y \mathrm{~d} x + z \mathrm{~d} y + x \mathrm{~d} z$ ，式中 $C$ 为纽形螺线 $x = a \cos t, y = a \sin t, z = b t (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2 \pi)$ ，以参数增加方向为正.

解 $\int_{C} y \mathrm{d}x + z \mathrm{d}y + x \mathrm{d}z = \int_{0}^{2\pi} (-a^{2} \sin t \sin t + abt \cos t + abc \cos t) \mathrm{d}t$

$$
= \left. \left(- \frac {a t ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {a ^ {2} \sin 2 t}{4} + a b t \sin t + a b \cos t + a b \sin t\right) \right| _ {0} ^ {2 \pi} = - \pi a ^ {2}.
$$
