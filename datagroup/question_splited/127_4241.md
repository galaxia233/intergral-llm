【4241】+ 设曲线 $x = a\cos t, y = b\sin t$ （ $0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2\pi$ ）在点 $(x, y)$ 的线密度等于 $\rho = |y|$ ，求其质量.

解 质量 $M = \int_{C} |y| \, \mathrm{d}s$ ，其中 $C$ 为椭圆 $x = a\cos t, y = b\sin t (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2\pi)$ .

先设 $a > b$ 这时

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} t + b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} \mathrm {d} t = a \sqrt {1 - \epsilon^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} \mathrm {d} t
$$

其中 $\varepsilon = \frac{\sqrt{a^2 - b^2}}{a}$ 于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} a b \sin t \sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d t + \int_ {\pi} ^ {2 \pi} a (- b \sin t) \sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2} \cos ^ {2} t} d t \\ = - a b \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d (\cos t) + a b \int_ {\pi} ^ {2 \pi} \sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d (\cos t) \\ = a b \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 - \epsilon^ {2} u ^ {2}} d u + a b \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 - \epsilon^ {2} u ^ {2}} d u = 1 a b \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 - \epsilon^ {2} u ^ {2}} d u \\ = \frac {4 a b}{\varepsilon} \left[ \frac {1}{2} \varepsilon u \sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2} u ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} \arcsin (\varepsilon u) \right] \Bigg | _ {u = 0} ^ {u = 1} = 2 b ^ {2} + 2 a b \frac {\arcsin \varepsilon}{\varepsilon}. \\ \end{array}
$$

次设 $a < b$ 。这时

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} t + b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} \mathrm {d} t = a \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} \mathrm {d} t,
$$

其中 $\varepsilon_{1} = \frac{\sqrt{b^{2} - a^{2}}}{a}$ 仿前，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} a b \sin t \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d t + \int_ {\pi} ^ {2 \pi} a (- b \sin t) \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d t = 4 a b \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2} u ^ {2}} d u \\ = \frac {4 a b}{\varepsilon_ {1}} \left[ \frac {1}{2} \varepsilon_ {1} u \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2} u ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} \ln \left(\varepsilon_ {1} u + \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2} u ^ {2}}\right) \right] \Bigg | _ {u = 0} ^ {u - 1} = 2 b ^ {2} + 2 a b \frac {\ln \left(\varepsilon_ {1} + \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2}}\right)}{\varepsilon_ {1}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

最后，若 $a = b$ ，则椭圆退化成圆，这时 $\mathrm{ds} = a\mathrm{dt}$ ，故

$$
M = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} a ^ {2} \sin t \mathrm {d} t + \int_ {\pi} ^ {2 \pi} (- a \sin t) a \mathrm {d} t = 4 a ^ {2}.
$$

综上所述，可知：

$$
M = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 2 b ^ {2} + 2 a b \frac {\arcsin \varepsilon}{\varepsilon}, & a > b, \\ 2 b ^ {2} + 2 a b \frac {\ln \left(\varepsilon_ {1} + \sqrt {1 + \varepsilon_ {1} ^ {2}}\right)}{\varepsilon_ {1}}, & a <   b, \\ 4 a ^ {2}, & a = b, \end{array} \right.
$$

其中 $\varepsilon = \frac{\sqrt{a^2 - b^2}}{a} (a > b),\quad \varepsilon_1 = \frac{\sqrt{b^2 - a^2}}{a} (a <   b),$
