32.55 Find the area between the curves $y = 1 / x$ and $y = 1 / (x + 1)$ to the right of the line $x = 1$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {T h e a r e a} A = \int_ {1} ^ {\infty} \left(\frac {1}{x} - \frac {1}{x + 1}\right) d x = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \int_ {1} ^ {v} \left(\frac {1}{x} - \frac {1}{x + 1}\right) d x = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} (\ln | x | - \ln | x + 1 |) ] _ {1} ^ {v} \\ = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \ln \left| \frac {v}{v + 1} \right| - (- \ln 2) = \ln 2 \\ \end{array}
