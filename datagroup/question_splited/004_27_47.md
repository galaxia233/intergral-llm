27.47 $\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{9x^2 - 16}}.$

$\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{9x^2 - 16}} = \int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{9(x^2 - \frac{16}{9})}} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{x^2 - (\frac{4}{3})^2}}.$ From the formula in Problem 27.46, we get $\frac{1}{3} \cdot \frac{1}{(\frac{4}{3})} \sec^{-1}\left(\frac{x}{\frac{4}{3}}\right) + C = \frac{1}{4} \sec^{-1} \frac{3x}{4} + C.$
