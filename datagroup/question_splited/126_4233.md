【4233】 $y = a\arcsin \frac{x}{a}, z = \frac{a}{4}\ln \frac{a - x}{a + x}$ 从 $O(0,0,0)$ 到 $A(x_0, y_0, z_0)$ .

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{1 - \frac{a^2}{a^4 - x^2} + \frac{a^4}{4(a^2 - x^2)^2}} = \frac{3a^2 - 2x^2}{2(a^2 - x^2)}\mathrm{d}x$ （ $|x_0| < a)$

于是，当 $x_0 \geq 0$ 时，有

$$
s = \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {0}} \frac {3 a ^ {2} - 2 x ^ {2}}{2 \left(a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}\right)} d x = \frac {a}{4} \ln \frac {a + x _ {0}}{a - x _ {0}} + x _ {0} = | z _ {0} | + | x _ {0} |;
$$

当 $x_0 < 0$ 时，有

$$
s = \int_ {x _ {0}} ^ {0} \frac {3 a ^ {2} - 2 x ^ {2}}{2 \left(a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}\right)} d x = - \frac {a}{4} \ln \frac {a + x _ {0}}{a - x _ {0}} - x _ {0} = | z _ {0} | + | x _ {0} |.
$$

总之，当 $|x_0| < a$ ，有 $s = |z_0| + |x_0|$
