【4029】 $z = xy, x^2 = y, x^2 = 2y, y^2 = x, y^2 = 2x, z = 0.$

提示仿 4028 题，令 $x = uy^2$ ， $y = vx^2$ ，则积分域 $\Omega$ 变为正方形域 $\frac{1}{2} \leq u \leq 1, \frac{1}{2} \leq v \leq 1$ ，且 $|I| = \frac{1}{3} u^{2}v^{2}$

解曲面所围区域的立体在 $Oxy$ 平面上的投影域 $\Omega$ 由曲线 $x^{2} = y, x^{2} = 2y, y^{2} = x$ 及 $y^{2} = 2x$ 围成.我们有

$$
V = \iint_ {\Omega} z d x d y = \iint_ {\Omega} x y d x d y.
$$

作变量代换

$$
x = u y ^ {2}, \quad y = v x ^ {2}, \quad \text {或} x = u ^ {- \frac {1}{3}} v ^ {- \frac {2}{3}}, \quad y = u ^ {- \frac {2}{3}} v ^ {- \frac {1}{3}},
$$

则积分域 $\Omega$ 变为正方形域 $\frac{1}{2} \leq u \leq 1, \frac{1}{2} \leq v \leq 1$ ，且 $|I| = \frac{1}{3} u^{2}v^{-2}$ 。于是，曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
V = \iint_ {\Omega} x y \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = \frac {1}{3} \int_ {\frac {1}{2}} ^ {1} \int_ {\frac {1}{2}} ^ {1} u ^ {- 3} v ^ {- 3} \mathrm {d} u \mathrm {d} v = \frac {1}{3} \left(\int_ {\frac {1}{2}} ^ {1} u ^ {- 3} \mathrm {d} u\right) ^ {2} = \frac {1}{3} \cdot \frac {9}{4} = \frac {3}{4}.
$$
