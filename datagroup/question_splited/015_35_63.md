35.63 Find the area swept out by $r = \tan \theta$ from $\theta = 0$ to $\theta = \pi /4$

See Fig. 35-12. The area is $\frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{\pi /4}\tan^{2}\theta d\theta = \frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{\pi /4}(\sec^{2}\theta -1)d\theta = \frac{1}{2} (\tan \theta -\theta)]_{0}^{\pi /4} = \frac{1}{2} [1 - (\pi /4)].$
