【4173】 $\iint_{y\geqslant x^2 +1}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y}{x^4 + y^2}.$

解 由于被积函数非负，故

$$
I = \iint_ {y \geq x ^ {2} + 1} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{x ^ {4} + y ^ {2}} = \int_ {- \infty} ^ {+ \infty} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {x ^ {2} + 1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {\mathrm {d} y}{x ^ {4} + y ^ {2}} = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {x ^ {2} + 1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {\mathrm {d} y}{x ^ {4} + y ^ {2}}.
$$

由于 $\int_{x^2 + 1}^{+\infty}\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{x^4 + y^2} = \frac{1}{x^2}\arctan \frac{y}{x^2}\Bigg|_{y = x^2 + 1}^{y = +\infty} = \frac{1}{x^2}\left[\frac{\pi}{2} - \arctan \left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)\right],$

故

$$
\begin{array}{l} I = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {1}{x ^ {2}} \left[ \frac {\pi}{2} - \arctan \left(1 + \frac {1}{x ^ {2}}\right) \right] d x \\ = - \frac {2}{x} \left[ \frac {\pi}{2} - \arctan \left(1 + \frac {1}{x ^ {2}}\right) \right] \Bigg | _ {x = 0} ^ {x = + \infty} + 2 \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {\frac {1}{x} \cdot \frac {2}{x ^ {3}}}{1 + \left(1 + \frac {1}{x ^ {2}}\right) ^ {2}} d x = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {d x}{x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $\lim_{x\to +0}\frac{\frac{\pi}{2} - \arctan\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)}{x} = \lim_{x\to +0}\frac{\frac{-\frac{2}{x^3}}{1 + \left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)^2}}{1} = \lim_{x\to +0}\left(-\frac{x}{x^4 + x^2 + \frac{1}{2}}\right) = 0.$

下面计算积分 $\int_{0}^{+\infty}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^1 + x^2 + \frac{1}{2}}$ 为简单计，记 $a = \sqrt{\sqrt{2} - 1}, b = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}$ 则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2}} = \frac {1}{\left(x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}}\right) ^ {2} - (\sqrt {2} - 1) x ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{(x ^ {2} + b) ^ {2} - (a x) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {1}{(x ^ {2} + a x + b) (x ^ {2} - a x + b)} = \frac {1}{2 a b} \left[ \frac {x + a}{x ^ {2} + a x + b} - \frac {x - a}{x ^ {2} - a x + b} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{4 a b} \left[ \frac {2 x + a}{x ^ {2} + a x + b} + \frac {a}{x ^ {2} + a x + b} - \frac {2 x - a}{x ^ {2} - a x + b} + \frac {a}{x ^ {2} - a x + b} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2}} = \frac {1}{4 a b} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \left[ \frac {2 x + a}{x ^ {2} + a x + b} - \frac {2 x - a}{x ^ {2} - a x + b} \right] \mathrm {d} x + \frac {1}{4 b} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \left[ \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + a x + b} + \frac {1}{x ^ {2} - a x + b} \right] \mathrm {d} x \\ = \frac {1}{4 a b} \left(\ln \frac {x ^ {2} + a x + b}{x ^ {2} - a x + b}\right) \Bigg | _ {x = 0} ^ {x = - x} + \frac {1}{4 b} \left(\frac {2}{\sqrt {4 b - a ^ {2}}} \arctan \frac {2 x + a}{\sqrt {4 b - a ^ {2}}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {4 b - a ^ {2}}} \arctan \frac {2 x - a}{\sqrt {4 b - a ^ {2}}}\right) \Bigg | _ {x = 0} ^ {x = + x} \\ = 0 + \frac {1}{4 b} \frac {2 \pi}{\sqrt {4 b - a ^ {2}}} = \frac {\pi}{2 b \sqrt {4 b - a ^ {2}}} = \frac {\pi}{2 \cdot \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \sqrt {\frac {4}{\sqrt {2}} - (\sqrt {2} - 1)}} = \frac {\pi}{\sqrt {2} \cdot \sqrt {\sqrt {2} + 1}} \\ = \frac {\pi \sqrt {\sqrt {2} - 1}}{\sqrt {2} \sqrt {\sqrt {2} + 1} \sqrt {\sqrt {2} - 1}} = \frac {\pi \sqrt {\sqrt {2} - 1} \sqrt {2}}{2} = \frac {\pi \sqrt {2 (\sqrt {2} - 1)}}{2}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故 $I = 2\int_{0}^{+\infty}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^4 + x^2 + \frac{1}{2}} = \pi \sqrt{2(\sqrt{2} - 1)}.$
