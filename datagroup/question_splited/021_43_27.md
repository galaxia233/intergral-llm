43.27 Find all relative maxima and minima of $f(x, y) = x^2 + 2xy + 2y^2$ .

Since $f(x, y) = (x + y)^2 + y^2 \geq 0$ , there is an absolute minimum 0 at $(0, 0)$ . $f_x = 2x + 2y$ , $f_y = 2x + 4y$ . Setting $f_x = 0$ , $f_y = 0$ , we get $x + y = 0$ , $x + 2y = 0$ , and, therefore, $x = y = 0$ . Thus, $(0, 0)$ is the only critical point—the only possible site of an extremum.
