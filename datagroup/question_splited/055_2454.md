【2454】抛物线 $4ay = x^2$ 沿 $Ox$ 轴滚动。证明：抛物线的焦点的轨迹是悬链线.

解如图 4.38 所示，设抛物线切 $Ox$ 轴于点 $A(s,0), O'$ 为抛物线的顶点， $P'$ 为焦点，且 $O'Y'$ 为对称轴， $O'X' \perp O'Y'$ ，过 $A$ 作 $AB \perp O'X'$ .

引入参数 $O^{\prime}N = t$ ，则由抛物线的性质易知： $P^{\prime}N\bot Ox$

$O'B = 2O'N = 2t.$ 从而有

$$
A B = \frac {(2 t) ^ {2}}{4 a} = \frac {t ^ {2}}{a}, \quad A N = t \sqrt {1 + \frac {t ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}}},
$$

$$
s = \int_ {0} ^ {2 t} \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {x}{2 a}\right) ^ {2}} d x = t \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}} + a \ln \left[ \frac {t}{a} + \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}} \right],
$$

$$
P ^ {\prime} N = a \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}}.
$$

于是，焦点 $P^{\prime}$ 的坐标 $\pmb{x},\pmb{y}$ 由参数 $\pmb{t}$ 表出：

$$
\left\{ \begin{array}{l} x = s - A N = a \ln \left[ \frac {t}{a} + \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}} \right], \\ y = P ^ {\prime} N = a \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}}. \end{array} \right. \tag {1}
$$

由（1）式得

$$
\mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {x}{a}} = \frac {t}{a} + \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}}, \quad \mathrm {e} ^ {- \frac {x}{a}} = - \frac {t}{a} + \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {t}{a}\right) ^ {2}}.
$$

上面两式相加，得 $\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{a}} + \mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{a}} = 2\sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{t}{a}\right)^2}.$

再以 (2) 式代入上式，最后得 $y = \frac{a}{2} (\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{a}} + \mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{a}}) = a \operatorname{ch} \frac{x}{a}.$

这说明抛物线的焦点的轨迹是悬链线.
