【3082】 $\prod_{n = 1}^{\infty}\left(1 - \frac{x}{\sqrt{n}}\right)\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{\sqrt{n}} +\frac{x^2}{2n}}.$

解 对于任意 $x$ ，考虑通项

$$
\begin{array}{l} p _ {n} = \left(1 - \frac {x}{\sqrt {n}}\right) e ^ {\frac {x}{\sqrt {n}} + \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 n}} \\ = \left(1 - \frac {x}{\sqrt {n}}\right) \left[ 1 + \left(\frac {x}{\sqrt {n}} + \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 n}\right) + \frac {1}{2} \left(\frac {x}{\sqrt {n}} + \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 n}\right) ^ {2} + O \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {n ^ {3}}}\right) \right] \\ = \left[ 1 + \frac {x}{\sqrt {n}} + \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 n} + \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {x ^ {2}}{n} + O \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {n ^ {3}}}\right) \right] + \left[ - \frac {x}{\sqrt {n}} - \frac {x ^ {2}}{n} + O \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {n ^ {3}}}\right) \right] = 1 + O \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {n ^ {3}}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

因此，若记 $p_n = 1 + \alpha_n$ ，则有 $\alpha_{n} = O\left(\frac{1}{\sqrt{n^{3}}}\right)$ .于是，由级数 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\alpha_{n}$ 的绝对收敛，可知级数 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\ln (1 + \alpha_n)$ 绝对收敛，从而知，原无穷乘积收敛.
