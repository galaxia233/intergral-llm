【2432】 $y^{2} = 2px$ $(0\leqslant x\leqslant x_0)$

解 $y^\prime = \frac{p}{y},\sqrt{1 + y^{\prime 2}} = \sqrt{1 + \frac{p^2}{y^2}} = \sqrt{1 + \frac{p}{2x}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\frac{\sqrt{p + 2x}}{\sqrt{x}}.$ 所求的弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {0}} \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \frac {\sqrt {p + 2 x}}{\sqrt {x}} d x = 2 \sqrt {2} \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {0}} \sqrt {p + 2 x} d (\sqrt {x}) \\ = 2 \sqrt {2} \left\{\frac {1}{2} \sqrt {x (p + 2 x)} + \frac {p}{2 \sqrt {2}} \ln \left(\sqrt {x} + \sqrt {x + \frac {p}{2}}\right) \right\} \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {x _ {0}} \\ = 2 \sqrt {x _ {0} \left(x _ {0} + \frac {p}{2}\right)} + p \ln \left(\frac {\sqrt {x _ {0}} + \sqrt {x _ {0} + \frac {p}{2}}}{\sqrt {\frac {p}{2}}}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$
