46.64 Solve $y^{\prime \prime} + Ay = B$ ， where $A > 0$

The general solution of the corresponding homogeneous equation is $y_{g} = C_{1}\cos \sqrt{A} x + C_{2}\sin \sqrt{A} x$ , by Problem 46.55. A particular solution is $y_{p} = B / A$ . Hence, the general solution is $B / A + C_{1}\cos \sqrt{A} x + C_{2}\sin \sqrt{A} x$ .
