【2467】 $z^2 = b(a - x),\quad x^2 +y^2 = ax.$

解 先求体积的四分之一部分，截面积为

$$
S (x) = \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}}} \sqrt {b (a - x)} d y = \sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}} \sqrt {b (a - x)}.
$$

从而， $\frac{1}{4} V = \int_{0}^{a} S(x) \, \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{a} \sqrt{ax - x^2} \sqrt{b(a - x)} \, \mathrm{d}x = \sqrt{b} \int_{0}^{a} \sqrt{x}(a - x) \, \mathrm{d}x = \frac{4}{15} a^2 \sqrt{ab}$ .

于是，所求的体积为 $V = \frac{16}{15} a^2\sqrt{ab}$
