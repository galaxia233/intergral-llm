【2710】+ $\sum_{n = 0}^{\infty}x^{\left[\frac{n}{2}\right]}y^{\left[\frac{n + 1}{2}\right]}$ （|xy|<1).

解题思路 仿 2709 题，有

$$
\sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} y ^ {[ \frac {n + 1}{2} ]} = \sum_ {n \in A _ {1}} x ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} y ^ {[ \frac {n + 1}{2} ]} + \sum_ {n \in A _ {2}} x ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} y ^ {[ \frac {n + 1}{2} ]},
$$

其中： $A_{1} = \{n|n = 2k,k = 0,1,2,\dots \}$ ， $A_{2} = \{n|n = 2k + 1,k = 0,1,2,\dots \}$

解设将 $n = 0,1,2,\dots$ 分成二类：

$$
A _ {1} = \{n \mid n = 2 k, k = 0, 1, 2, \dots \}, A _ {2} = \{n \mid n = 2 k + 1, k = 0, 1, 2, \dots \},
$$

则

$$
\sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} y ^ {[ \frac {n + 1}{2} ]} = \sum_ {n \in A _ {1}} x ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} y ^ {[ \frac {n + 1}{2} ]} + \sum_ {n \in A _ {2}} x ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} y ^ {[ \frac {n + 1}{2} ]} = \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {k} y ^ {k} + \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {k} y ^ {k + 1}.
$$

显然上式右端两级数当 $|xy| < 1$ 时绝对收敛，故原级数收敛，且其和为

$$
\sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {\left[ \frac {n}{2} \right]} y ^ {\left[ \frac {n + 1}{2} \right]} = \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} (x y) ^ {k} + y \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} (x y) ^ {k} = (1 + y) \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} (x y) ^ {k} = \frac {1 + y}{1 - x y}.
$$
