34.64 At $t = 2$ $\mathbf{F}(t) = \mathbf{i} + \mathbf{j}$ and $\mathbf{F}'(t) = 2\mathbf{i} - 3\mathbf{j}$ . Find $\frac{d}{dt} [t^2\mathbf{F}(t)]$ at $t = 2$ .

By Problem 34.54, $\frac{d}{dt} [t^2\mathbf{F}(t)] = t^2\mathbf{F}'(t) + 2t\mathbf{F}(t)$ . At $t = 2$ , $\frac{d}{dt} [t^2\mathbf{F}(t)] = 4(2\mathbf{i} - 3\mathbf{j}) + 4(\mathbf{i} + \mathbf{j}) = 12\mathbf{i} - 8\mathbf{j}$ .
