40.71 Find the vector representation, parametric equations, and rectangular equations for the line through the points $P(3,2,1)$ and $Q(-1,2,4)$ .

$\overrightarrow{PQ} = (-4,0,3)$ . So, the line is generated by the vector function $(3,2,1) + t(-4,0,3)$ . The parametric equations are $x = 3 - 4t$ , $y = 2$ , $z = 1 + 3t$ . The rectangular equations are $\frac{x - 3}{-4} = \frac{z - 1}{3}$ , $y = 2$ .
