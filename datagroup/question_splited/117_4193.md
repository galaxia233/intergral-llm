【4193】 $\iiint_{|x| + |y| + |z| > 1} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \mathrm{~d}y \mathrm{~d}z}{|x|^p + |y|^q + |z|^r} (p > 0, q > 0, r > 0).$

解 由对称性及被积函数的非负性，有

$$
\iiint_ {| x |: | y | + | z | > 1} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z}{| x | ^ {p} + | y | ^ {q} + | z | ^ {r}} = 8 \iiint_ {\substack {x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0, z \geqslant 0 \\ r + y + z > 1}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z}{x ^ {p} + y ^ {q} + z ^ {r}} = 8 \iiint_ {\Omega_ {1}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z}{x ^ {p} + y ^ {q} + z ^ {r}} + 8 \iiint_ {\Omega_ {2}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z}{x ^ {p} + y ^ {q} + z ^ {r}}.
$$

其中，令

$$
\Omega_ {1} = \{(x, y, z) | x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0, z \geqslant 0, x + y + z > 1, x ^ {p} + y ^ {q} + z ^ {r} \leqslant 3 \},
$$

$$
\Omega_ {2} = \{(x, y, z) | x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0, z \geqslant 0, x + y + z > 1, x ^ {p} + y ^ {q} + z ^ {r} > 3 \}.
$$

令 $\Omega_{3} = \{(x,y,z)|x\geqslant 0,y\geqslant 0,z\geqslant 0,x^{p} + y^{q} + z^{r} > 3\}$ ，由于当 $x\geqslant 0,y\geqslant 0,z\geqslant 0,x^{p} + y^{q} + z^{r} > 3$ 时必有 $x + y$ $+z > 1$ （否则， $x + y + z\leqslant 1$ ，就有 $x\leqslant 1,y\leqslant 1,z\leqslant 1$ ，从而， $x^p\leqslant 1,y^q\leqslant 1,z^r\leqslant 1$ ，于是 $x^{p} + y^{q} + z^{r}\leqslant 3)$ ，故 $\Omega_{2} =$ $\Omega_{3}$ .显然， $\iiint_{\Omega_1}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\mathrm{d}z}{x^p + y^q + z^r}$ 为常义积分，故积分 $\iiint_{|x| + |y| - |z| > 1}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\mathrm{d}z}{|x|^p + |y|^q + |z|^r}$ 的敛散性取决于 $\iiint_{\Omega_3}\frac{\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y\mathrm{d}z}{x^p + y^q + z^r}$ 的敛散性。对此积分，作变量代换

$$
x = R ^ {\frac {2}{p}} \cos^ {\frac {2}{p}} \varphi \cos^ {\frac {2}{p}} \psi , \quad y = R ^ {\frac {2}{q}} \sin^ {\frac {2}{q}} \varphi \cos^ {\frac {2}{q}} \psi , \quad z = R ^ {\frac {2}{r}} \sin^ {\frac {2}{r}} \psi .
$$

则易知

$$
\frac {D (x , y , z)}{D (R , \varphi , \psi)} = \frac {8}{p q r} R ^ {\frac {2}{p} + \frac {2}{q} + \frac {2}{r} - 1} \cos^ {\frac {2}{p} - 1} \varphi \sin^ {\frac {2}{q} - 1} \varphi \sin^ {\frac {2}{r} - 1} \psi \cos^ {\frac {2}{p} + \frac {2}{q}} \psi .
$$

于是，由被积函数的非负性，并利用 3856 题的结果，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iiint_ {\bar {a} _ {3}} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \mathrm {d} z}{x ^ {p} + y ^ {q} + z ^ {r}} = \frac {8}{p q r} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {\frac {2}{r} - 1} \psi \cos^ {\frac {2}{p} + \frac {2}{q} - 1} \psi \mathrm {d} \psi \cdot \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {\frac {2}{q} - 1} \psi \cos^ {\frac {2}{p} - 1} \varphi \mathrm {d} \varphi \cdot \int_ {\sqrt {3}} ^ {+ \infty} R ^ {\frac {2}{p} + \frac {2}{q} + \frac {2}{r} - 3} \mathrm {d} R \\ = \frac {8}{p q r} \cdot \frac {1}{2} B \left(\frac {1}{r}, \frac {1}{p} + \frac {1}{q}\right) \cdot \frac {1}{2} B \left(\frac {1}{q}, \frac {1}{p}\right) \int_ {\sqrt {3}} ^ {+ \infty} R ^ {\frac {2}{p} + \frac {2}{q} + \frac {2}{r}} d R \\ = \frac {2}{p q r} B \left(\frac {1}{r}, \frac {1}{p} + \frac {1}{q}\right) B \left(\frac {1}{q}, \frac {1}{p}\right) \int_ {\sqrt {3}} ^ {+ \infty} R ^ {\frac {2}{p} + \frac {2}{q} + \frac {2}{r} - 3} d R. \\ \end{array}
$$

由于积分 $\int_{\sqrt{3}}^{+\infty} R^{\frac{2}{p} + \frac{2}{q} + \frac{2}{r} - 3} \mathrm{d}R$ 当 $\frac{2}{p} + \frac{2}{q} + \frac{2}{r} - 3 < -1$ （即 $\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{q} + \frac{1}{r} < 1$ ）时收敛，当 $\frac{2}{p} + \frac{2}{q} + \frac{2}{r} - 3 \geq -1$ 时发散，故积分 $\iiint_{\Omega_3} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z}{x^p + y^q + z^r}$ （从而，积分 $\iiint_{|x| + |y| + |z| > 1} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z}{|x|^p + |y|^q + |z|^r}$ ）当 $\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{q} + \frac{1}{r} < 1$ 时收敛，当 $\frac{1}{p} + \frac{1}{q} + \frac{1}{r} \geq 1$ 时发散.
