【3053】 $\prod_{n = 2}^{\infty}\left[1 - \frac{2}{n(n + 1)}\right] = \frac{1}{3}.$

证 记 $p_n = 1 - \frac{2}{n(n + 1)}$ . 由于部分乘积

$$
P _ {n} = \prod_ {i = 2} ^ {n} p _ {i} = \frac {4 \cdot 1}{2 \cdot 3} \cdot \frac {5 \cdot 2}{3 \cdot 4} \dots \frac {(n + 2) (n - 1)}{n (n + 1)} = \frac {1}{3} \cdot \frac {n + 2}{n} \rightarrow \frac {1}{3} \quad (\text {当} n \rightarrow \infty \text {时}),
$$

故 $\prod_{n = 2}^{\infty}\left[1 - \frac{2}{n(n + 1)}\right] = \frac{1}{3}.$
