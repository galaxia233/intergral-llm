【4298】 $\oint_{C} xy^{2} \mathrm{d}y - x^{2} y \mathrm{~d}x$ ，式中 $C$ 为圆周 $x^{2} + y^{2} = a^{2}$

解 由于 $P = -x^{2}y, Q = xy^{2}$ ，故有

$$
\oint_ {C} x y ^ {2} \mathrm {d} y - x ^ {2} y \mathrm {d} x = \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2}} \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {d} \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a} r ^ {3} \mathrm {d} r = \frac {\pi a ^ {4}}{2}.
$$

如果直接计算，可令 $x = a\cos t, y = a\sin t$ ，则

$$
\oint_ {C} x y ^ {2} \mathrm {d} y - x ^ {2} y \mathrm {d} x = a ^ {4} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (\cos^ {2} t \sin^ {2} t + \cos^ {2} t \sin^ {2} t) \mathrm {d} t = \frac {a ^ {4}}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \sin^ {2} 2 t \mathrm {d} t = \frac {\pi a ^ {4}}{2}.
$$
