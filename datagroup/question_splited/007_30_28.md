30.28 $\int \frac{dx}{1 + \sqrt[4]{x - 1}}.$   
Let $x - 1 = z^4$ , $dx = 4z^3 dz$ . Then $\int \frac{dx}{1 + \sqrt[4]{x - 1}} = \int \frac{4z^3dz}{1 + z} = 4\int \left(z^2 - z + 1 - \frac{1}{1 + z}\right)dz = 4(\frac{1}{3}z^3 - \frac{1}{2}z^2 + z - \ln |1 + z|) + C = \frac{4}{3}(\sqrt[4]{x - 1})^3 - 2\sqrt{x - 1} + \sqrt[4]{x - 1} - \ln (1 + \sqrt[4]{x - 1}) + C.$
