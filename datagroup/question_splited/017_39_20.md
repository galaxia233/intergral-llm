39.20 Show that $\ln (1 - x)$ is equal to its Maclaurin series for $|x| < 1$ .

As you will find, employment of the Lagrange remainder establishes the desired representation only on the subinterval $-1 < x \leqslant \frac{1}{2}$ . However, appeal to Problems 38.50 and 39.11 immediately leads to the full result.
