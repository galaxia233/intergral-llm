34.5 Sketch the curve with the parametric equations $x = \sin t, y = -3 + 2\cos t.$

$x^{2} + \left(\frac{y + 3}{2}\right)^{2} = \sin^{2} t + \cos^{2} t = 1$ . Thus, we have an ellipse with center $(0, -3)$ , semimajor axis of length 2 along the $y$ -axis, and semiminor axis of length 1 along the line $y = -3$ (Fig. 34-5).
