19.46 Find $\int (x^4 - 4x^3)^3 (x^3 - 3x^2) dx$ .

Note that, if we let $g(x) = x^4 - 4x^3$ , then $g'(x) = 4x^3 - 12x^2 = 4(x^3 - 3x^2)$ . Thus, our integral has the form $\int (g(x))^3 \cdot \frac{1}{4} g'(x) \, dx = \frac{1}{4} \int (g(x))^3 g'(x) \, dx = \frac{1}{4} \cdot \frac{1}{4} \cdot (g(x))^4 + C = \frac{1}{16} (x^4 - 4x^3)^4 + C$ , using the result of Problem 19.1.
