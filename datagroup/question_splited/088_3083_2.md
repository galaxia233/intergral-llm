【3083】 $\prod_{n = 1}^{\infty}\left(1 + \frac{x^n}{n^p}\right)\cos \frac{x^n}{n^q}.$

(2) 当 $x = 1$ 时，在 $p > 1, q > \frac{1}{2}$ 的情况下，由于通项

$$
\begin{array}{l} p _ {n} = \left(1 + \frac {1}{n ^ {p}}\right) \cos \frac {1}{n ^ {q}} = \left(1 + \frac {1}{n ^ {p}}\right) \left[ 1 - \frac {1}{2 n ^ {2 q}} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {4 q}}\right) \right] = 1 + \frac {1}{n ^ {p}} - \frac {1}{2 n ^ {2 q}} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {4 q}}\right) + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {p + 4 q}}\right) \\ = 1 + \frac {1}{n ^ {\rho}} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2 q}}\right), \\ \end{array}
$$

若记 $p_n = 1 + \alpha_n$ ， $\alpha_{n} = \frac{1}{n^{p}} +O\left(\frac{1}{n^{2q}}\right)$ ，则 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\alpha_{n}$ 绝对收敛，且由

$$
a _ {n} ^ {2} = \frac {1}{n ^ {2 p}} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {4 q}}\right) + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2 q + p}}\right) = \frac {1}{n ^ {2 p}} + O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {4 q}}\right),
$$

易知 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \alpha_n^2$ 也收敛，故此时无穷乘积 $\prod_{n=1}^{\infty} p_n$ 收敛.
