34.41 Find the arc length of $x = \ln \sin \theta, y = \theta, \pi / 6 \leq \theta \leq \pi / 2$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {I} d x / d \theta = \cot \theta , \quad d y / d \theta = 1. \quad \text {H e n c e}, \quad s = \int_ {\pi / 6} ^ {\pi / 2} \sqrt {\cot^ {2} \theta + 1}   d \theta = \int_ {\pi / 6} ^ {\pi / 2} \csc \theta   d \theta = \ln | \csc \theta - \cot \theta | ] _ {\pi / 6} ^ {\pi / 2} = \\ [ \ln 1 - \ln (2 - \sqrt {3}) ] = - \ln (2 - \sqrt {3}) = \ln (2 + \sqrt {3}). \end{array}
$$
