【2492】 $x^{\frac{2}{3}} + y^{\frac{2}{3}} = a^{\frac{2}{3}}$ 绕 $Ox$ 轴

解 $y^\prime = -\sqrt[3]{\frac{y}{x}}, \quad \sqrt{1 + y'^2} = \frac{a^{\frac{1}{3}}}{x^{\frac{1}{3}}}$ .

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P _ {x} = 2 \cdot 2 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left(a ^ {\frac {3}{2}} - x ^ {\frac {2}{3}}\right) ^ {\frac {2}{3}} \frac {a ^ {\frac {1}{3}}}{x ^ {\frac {1}{3}}} d x = - \frac {1 2 \pi a ^ {\frac {1}{3}}}{5} \left(a ^ {\frac {2}{3}} - x ^ {\frac {2}{3}}\right) ^ {\frac {5}{2}} \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {a} = \frac {1 2 \pi a ^ {2}}{5}.
$$
