【2092】若 $P\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) = a_0 + \frac{a_1}{x} + \dots + \frac{a_n}{x^n}, a_0, a_1, \dots, a_n$ 为常数，则在什么情形下，积分 $\int P\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) \mathrm{e}^x \mathrm{d}x$

为初等函数？

解 $\int \frac{a_k}{x^k}\mathrm{e}^x\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{a_k}{k - 1}\cdot \frac{\mathrm{e}^x}{x^{k - 1}} +\frac{a_k}{k - 1}\int \frac{\mathrm{e}^x}{x^{k - 1}}\mathrm{d}x = \dots$

$$
= - \frac {a _ {k}}{k - 1} \cdot \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x ^ {k - 1}} - \frac {a _ {k}}{(k - 1) (k - 2)} \cdot \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x ^ {k - 2}} - \dots - \frac {a _ {k}}{(k - 1) !} \cdot \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x} + \frac {a _ {k}}{(k - 1) !} \int \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x} d x.
$$

于是， $\int P\left(\frac{1}{x}\right)\mathrm{e}^{x}\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(\sum_{k = 0}^{n}\frac{a_k}{x^k}\right)\mathrm{e}^{x}\mathrm{d}x = \sum_{k = 0}^{n}\int \frac{a_k}{x^k}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= - \sum_ {k = 2} ^ {n} \sum_ {j = 1} ^ {k - 1} \frac {a _ {k}}{(k - 1) (k - 2) \cdots (k - j)} \cdot \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x ^ {k - j}} + \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {a _ {k}}{(k - 1) !} \int \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x} \mathrm {d} x + a _ {0} \mathrm {e} ^ {x}.
$$

因而，若 $\sum_{k=1}^{n} \frac{a_k}{(k-1)!} = 0$ ，即 $a_1 + \frac{a_2}{1!} + \frac{a_3}{2!} + \dots + \frac{a_n}{(n-1)!} = 0$ ，则积分 $\int P\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) \mathrm{e}^x \, \mathrm{d}x$ 是初等函数。
