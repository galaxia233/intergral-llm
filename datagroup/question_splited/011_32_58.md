32.59 Calculate $L\{\cos t\}$

1 $L\{\cos t\} = \int_0^\infty e^{-st}\cos tdt = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\int_0^v e^{-st}\cos tdt.$ By integration by parts (see Problem 28.9), we obtain

$$
\lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \frac {e ^ {- s t}}{s ^ {2} + 1} (- s \cos t + \sin t) \Bigg ] _ {0} ^ {v} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \frac {e ^ {- s v}}{s ^ {2} + 1} (\sin v - s \cos v) + \frac {s}{s ^ {2} + 1} = \frac {s}{s ^ {2} + 1}
$$

Thus, $L\{\cos t\} = s / (s^2 +1)$
