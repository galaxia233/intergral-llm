【2290】 $\int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^{2m}x\cos^{2n}x\mathrm{d}x.$

解方法 1：记 $I_0 = \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^{2m}x\cos^{2n}x\mathrm{d}x,$

易见 $I_0 = \frac{1}{4} I$ ，其中

利用欧拉公式，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sin^ {2 m} x \cos^ {2 n} x = \left(\frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} x} - \mathrm {e} ^ {- \mathrm {i} x}}{2 \mathrm {i}}\right) ^ {2 m} \left(\frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {\mathrm {i} x} + \mathrm {e} ^ {- \mathrm {i} x}}{2}\right) ^ {2 n} \\ = \frac {(- 1) ^ {m}}{2 ^ {2 n + 2 m}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {2 m} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} e ^ {2 (m - k) i x} \sum_ {l = 0} ^ {2 n} C _ {2 n} ^ {l} e ^ {2 (n - l) i x} \\ = \frac {(- 1) ^ {m}}{2 ^ {2 n + 2 m}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {2 m} \sum_ {l = 0} ^ {2 n} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} C _ {2 n} ^ {l} e ^ {2 (m + n - k - l) i x} \\ = \frac {(- 1) ^ {m}}{2 ^ {2 n + 2 m}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {2 m} \sum_ {l = 0} ^ {2 n} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} C _ {2 n} ^ {l} [ \cos 2 (m + n - k - l) x + i \sin 2 (m + n - k - l) x ], \\ \end{array}
$$

今不妨设 $m \leqslant n^{*}$ , 作积分计算，则有

$$
\begin{array}{l} I = \frac {(- 1) ^ {m}}{2 ^ {2 m + 2 n}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {2 m} \sum_ {l = 0} ^ {2 n} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} C _ {2 n} ^ {l} \left(\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \cos 2 (m + n - k - l) x d x + i \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \sin 2 (m + n - k - l) x d x\right) \\ = \frac {(- 1) ^ {m} \pi}{2 ^ {2 m + 2 n - 1}} \sum_ {\substack {k + l = m + n \\ 0 \leqslant k \leqslant 2 m \\ 0 \leqslant l \leqslant 2 n}} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} C _ {2 n} ^ {l} = \frac {(- 1) ^ {m} \pi}{2 ^ {2 m + 2 n - 1}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {2 m} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} C _ {2 n} ^ {m + n - k} \\ \end{array}
$$

经计算，可以验证有

$$
(- 1) ^ {m} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {2 m} (- 1) ^ {k} C _ {2 m} ^ {k} C _ {2 n} ^ {m + n - k} = \frac {(2 m) ! (2 n) !}{m ! n ! (m + n) !}.
$$

于是，得 $I_0 = \frac{1}{4} I = \frac{\pi(2m)!(2n)!}{2^{2m + 2n + 1}m!n!(m + n)!}$ .

方法 2：令 $I_{m,n} = \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^{2m}x\cos^{2n}x\mathrm{d}x.$ 显然有

$$
I _ {m, 0} = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2 m} x d x = \frac {(2 m - 1) ! !}{(2 m) ! !} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2},
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {m, n} = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2 m} x \cos^ {2 n - 1} x d (\sin x) = \frac {1}{2 m + 1} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {2 n - 1} x d (\sin^ {2 m + 1} x) \\ = \frac {1}{2 m + 1} \cos^ {2 n - 1} x \sin^ {2 m + 1} x \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} - \frac {1}{2 m + 1} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2 m + 1} x d (\cos^ {2 n - 1} x) \right. \\ = \frac {2 n - 1}{2 m + 1} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2 m + 2} x \cos^ {2 n - 2} x d x = \frac {2 n - 1}{2 m + 1} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {2 m} x (1 - \cos^ {2} x) \cos^ {2 (n - 1)} x d x \\ = \frac {2 n - 1}{2 m + 1} I _ {m, n - 1} - \frac {2 n - 1}{2 m + 1} I _ {m, n}, \\ \end{array}
$$

整理后得 $I_{m,n} = \frac{2n - 1}{2(m + n)} I_{m,n - 1}$

由此不难得到

$$
I _ {m, n} = \frac {(2 n - 1) ! !}{2 ^ {n} (m + n) (m + n - 1) \cdots (m + 1)} I _ {m, 0} = \frac {(2 n - 1) ! ! m !}{2 ^ {n} (m + n) !} \cdot \frac {(2 m - 1) ! !}{(2 m) ! !} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2}
$$
