【2873】(4) 利用不同方法，求下列函数的幂级数展开式：(4) $f(x) = \arctan \frac{2x}{2 - x^2}$ ;   
解 （4）由于 $f^{\prime}(x) = \left(\arctan \frac{2x}{2 - x^{2}}\right)^{\prime} = \frac{4 + 2x^{2}}{4 + x^{4}}$ 故

$$
\begin{array}{l} \arctan \frac {2 x}{2 - x ^ {2}} = \int_ {0} ^ {x} \frac {4 + 2 t ^ {2}}{4 + t ^ {4}} d t = \int_ {0} ^ {x} \left[ \left(1 + \frac {t ^ {2}}{2}\right) \cdot \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \left(\frac {t ^ {4}}{4}\right) ^ {n} \right] d t \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {x} \left[ \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} \frac {t ^ {2 n}}{2 ^ {n}} \right] d t = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} \frac {x ^ {2 n + 1}}{2 ^ {n} (2 n + 1)} \quad (| x | <   \sqrt {2}). \\ \end{array}
$$

当 $|x| = \sqrt{2}$ 时，级数为

$$
\sqrt {2} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} \frac {1}{2 n + 1} \quad \text {及} \quad - \sqrt {2} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {[ \frac {n}{2} ]} \frac {1}{2 n + 1},
$$

它们均由两收敛级数

$$
\sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {1}{4 n + 1} \quad \text {及} \quad \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {1}{4 n + 3}
$$

逐项相加并分别乘以常数 $\sqrt{2}$ 及 $-\sqrt{2}$ 而得，故它们收敛。因此，原级数的收敛域为 $|x|\leqslant \sqrt{2}$
