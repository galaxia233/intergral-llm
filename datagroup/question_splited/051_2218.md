【2218】求 $\int_{0}^{100\pi}\sqrt{1 - \cos 2x}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 在每一个区间 $[(k - 1)\pi , k\pi] (k = 1, 2, \dots, 100)$ 上积分，再相加.

解 $\int_{0}^{100\pi}\sqrt{1 - \cos 2x}\mathrm{d}x = \sum_{k = 1}^{100}\sqrt{2}\int_{(k - 1)\pi}^{k\pi}\sqrt{\sin^2x}\mathrm{d}x = \sum_{k = 1}^{100}\sqrt{2}\int_{0}^{\pi}\sqrt{\sin^2x}\mathrm{d}x = 100\sqrt{2}\int_{0}^{\pi}\sin x\mathrm{d}x = 200\sqrt{2}.$
