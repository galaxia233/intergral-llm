32.5 For $p \leq 1$ , is $\int_{1}^{\infty} \frac{\ln x}{x^{p}} dx$ convergent?

$\frac{\ln x}{x^p} \geq \frac{1}{x^p}$ for $x \geq e$ . Hence, $\int_{e}^{v} \frac{\ln x}{x^p} dx \geq \int_{e}^{v} \frac{1}{x^p} dx \to +\infty$ by Problem 32.3. Hence, $\int_{1}^{x} \frac{\ln x}{x^p} dx$ is divergent for $p \leq 1$ .
