29.37 Find $\int \frac{\cos^3 x}{1 - \sin x} dx$ .

$$
\frac {\cos^ {3} x}{1 - \sin x} = \frac {\cos^ {3} x}{1 - \sin x} \cdot \frac {1 + \sin x}{1 + \sin x} = \frac {\cos^ {3} x (1 + \sin x)}{\cos^ {2} x} = \cos x (1 + \sin x) = \cos x + \cos x \sin x. \quad \text {T h u s},
$$

$$
\int \frac {\cos^ {3} x}{1 - \sin x} d x = \int \cos x d x + \int \cos x \sin x d x = \sin x + \frac {1}{2} \sin^ {2} x + C.
