【1910】 $\int \frac{x^9\mathrm{d}x}{(x^{10} + 2x^5 + 2)^2}.$

解 $\int \frac{x^9\mathrm{d}x}{(x^{10} + 2x^5 + 2)^2} = \frac{1}{5}\int \frac{x^5\mathrm{d}(x^5)}{[(x^5 + 1)^2 + 1]^2} = \frac{1}{5}\int \frac{(x^5 + 1)\mathrm{d}(x^5 + 1)}{[(x^5 + 1)^2 + 1]^2} -\frac{1}{5}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(x^5 + 1)}{[(x^5 + 1)^2 + 1]^2}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{1 0} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} [ (x ^ {5} + 1) ^ {2} + 1 ]}{[ (x ^ {5} + 1) ^ {2} + 1 ] ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{5} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (x ^ {5} + 1)}{[ (x ^ {5} + 1) ^ {2} + 1 ] ^ {2}} = - \frac {1}{1 0 [ (x ^ {5} + 1) ^ {2} + 1 ]} - \frac {1}{5} \left\{\frac {x ^ {5} + 1}{2 [ (x ^ {5} + 1) ^ {2} + 1 ]} \right. \\ \left. + \frac {1}{2} \arctan \left(x ^ {5} + 1\right) \right\} ^ {*}) + C = - \frac {x ^ {5} + 2}{1 0 \left(x ^ {1 0} + 2 x ^ {5} + 2\right)} - \frac {1}{1 0} \arctan \left(x ^ {5} + 1\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用1817题的结果.
