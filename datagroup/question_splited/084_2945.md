【2945】在区间 $(-\pi, \pi)$ 内展开 $f(x) = \cos ax$ ( $a$ 不是整数).

解 因为 $f(x) = \cos ax$ 为偶函数，从而， $b_n = 0$ ，且

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {0} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos a x d x = \frac {2}{a \pi} \sin a \pi , \\ a _ {n} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos a x \cos n x d x = \frac {1}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} [ \cos (n + a) x + \cos (n - a) x ] d x = \frac {2 \sin a \pi}{\pi} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n + 1} a}{n ^ {2} - a ^ {2}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故按展开定理， $f(x)$ 可展开为 $\frac{2\sin a\pi}{\pi}\left[\frac{1}{2a} +\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}(-1)^{n + 1}\frac{a\cos nx}{n^2 - a^2}\right] = \cos ax\quad (-\pi < x < \pi).$
