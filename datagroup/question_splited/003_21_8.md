21.8 The region bounded by the curves $y = \sin x, \quad y = \cos x, \quad x = 0, \quad \text{and} \quad x = \pi / 4$ .

See Fig. 21-8. The upper boundary is $y = \cos x$ , the lower boundary is $y = \sin x$ , and the left side is the $y$ -axis. The area is given by $\int_0^{\pi /4} (\cos x - \sin x) dx = (\sin x + \cos x) \int_0^{\pi /4} = \left( \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \right) - (0 + 1) = \sqrt{2} - 1$ .
