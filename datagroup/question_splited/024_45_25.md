45.25 Compute the divergence, $\operatorname{div}\mathbf{F}$ , for the vector field $\mathbf{F} = (xy, yz, xz)$ .

By definition, if $\mathbf{F}(x,y,z) = (f(x,y,z),g(x,y,z),h(x,y,z))$ , then $\operatorname{div}\mathbf{F} = \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial g}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial h}{\partial z}$ . In this case, $\operatorname{div}\mathbf{F} = y + z + x$ .
