43.42 Find the volume $V$ of the largest rectangular box that may be inscribed in the ellipsoid $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{\dot{z}^2}{c^2} = 1.$

Let $(x, y, z)$ be the vertex in the first octant. Then the sides must have lengths $2x, 2y, 2z$ , and the volume $V = 8xyz$ . Think of $z$ as a function of $x$ and $y$ . Then $V$ is a function of $x$ and $y$ . $V_x = 8y\left(x \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + z\right)$ , $V_y = 8x\left(y \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} + z\right)$ . Differentiating the equation of the ellipsoid, we get $\frac{2x}{a^2} + \frac{2z}{c^2} \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = 0$ and $\frac{2y}{b^2} + \frac{2z}{c^2} \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = 0$ . Thus, $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = -\frac{c^2}{z} \frac{x}{a^2}$ and $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = -\frac{c^2}{z} \frac{y}{b^2}$ . Hence, $V_x = 8y\left(-\frac{c^2}{z} \frac{x^2}{a^2} + z\right)$ and $V_y = 8x\left(-\frac{c^2}{z} \frac{y^2}{b^2} + z\right)$ . Set $V_x = V_y = 0$ . Then $z = c^2 x^2 / za^2$ , $z = c^2 y^2 / zb^2$ . Therefore, $x^2 / a^2 = x^2 / c^2 = y^2 / b^2$ . The equation of the ellipsoid now gives: $x = a / \sqrt{3}$ , $z = c / \sqrt{3}$ , $y = b / \sqrt{3}$ , and $V = 8abc / 3\sqrt{3}$ .
