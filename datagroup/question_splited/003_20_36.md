20.36 Verify the Mean-Value Theorem for integrals, for the function $f(x) = x^3$ on [0, 1].

$\frac{1}{b - a}\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx = \int_{0}^{1}x^{3}dx = \frac{1}{4} x^{4}]_{0}^{1} = \frac{1}{4} (1 - 0) = \frac{1}{4}.$ But $\frac{1}{4} = x^3$ when $x = 1 / \sqrt[3]{4},$ and $0 <   1 / \sqrt[3]{4} <  1.$
