【1893】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 + 1)^3}$

解 $Q = (x^{2} + 1)^{3}, Q_{1} = (x^{2} + 1)^{2}, Q_{2} = x^{2} + 1.$

设 $\frac{1}{(x^2 + 1)^3} = \left[\frac{Ax^3 + Bx^2 + Cx + D}{(x^2 + 1)^2}\right]' + \frac{Ex + F}{x^2 + 1}$ ，从而，

$$
1 \equiv (3 A x ^ {2} + 2 B x + C) (x ^ {2} + 1) - 4 x (A x ^ {3} + B x ^ {2} + C x + D) + (E x + F) (x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}.
$$

比较等式两端 $x$ 的同次幂系数，得

$$
\begin{array}{l l} x ^ {5} & E = 0, \\ x ^ {4} & - A + F = 0, \\ x ^ {3} & - 2 B + 2 E = 0, \\ x ^ {2} & 3 A - 3 C + 2 F = 0, \\ x ^ {1} & 2 B - 4 D + E = 0, \\ x ^ {0} & C + F = 1. \end{array}
$$

由此， $A = \frac{3}{8}$ ， $B = 0$ ， $C = \frac{5}{8}$ ， $D = 0$ ， $E = 0$ ， $F = \frac{3}{8}$ .于是，

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {3}} = \frac {x (3 x ^ {2} + 5)}{8 (x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {3}{8} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {2} + 1} = \frac {x (3 x ^ {2} + 5)}{8 (x ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {3}{8} \arctan x + C.
$$
