【2136】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^4 - 2x^2 - 1}}.$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^4 - 2x^2 - 1}} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^3\sqrt{1 - 2x^{-2} - x^{-4}}} = -\frac{1}{2}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(x^{-2} + 1)}{\sqrt{2 - (x^{-2} + 1)^2}} = -\frac{1}{2}\arcsin \left(\frac{x^{-2} + 1}{\sqrt{2}}\right) + C.$

$$
= - \frac {1}{2} \arcsin \left(\frac {x ^ {2} + 1}{x ^ {2} \sqrt {2}}\right) + C _ {1} = \frac {1}{2} \arccos  \left(\frac {x ^ {2} + 1}{x ^ {2} \sqrt {2}}\right) + C.
$$
