42.14 If $z$ is implicitly defined as a function of $x$ and $y$ by $x\sin z - z^2 y = 1$ , find $\partial z / \partial x$ and $\partial z / \partial y$ .

By implicit differentiation with respect to $x$ , $x \cos z(\partial z / \partial x) + \sin z - 2yz(\partial z / \partial x) = 0$ , $(\partial z / \partial x)(x \cos z - 2yz) = -\sin z$ , $\partial z / \partial x = \sin z / (2yz - x \cos z)$ . By implicit differentiation with respect to $y$ , $x \cos z(\partial z / \partial y) - 2yz - 2yz(\partial z / \partial y) = 0$ , $(\partial z / \partial y)(x \cos z - 2yz) = z^2$ , $\partial z / \partial y = z^2 / (x \cos z - 2yz)$ .
