27.44 $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{3 - 2x^2}}.$   
$\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{3 - 2x^2}} = \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{2(\frac{3}{2} - x^2)}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{\frac{3}{2} - x^2}}.$ By the formula in Problem 27.41, we obtain $\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\sin^{-1}\frac{x}{\sqrt{\frac{3}{2}}} + C = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\sin^{-1}\frac{\sqrt{2}x}{\sqrt{3}} + C.$
