【4230】 $\int_{C} \frac{\mathrm{d}s}{y^2}$ ，其中 $C$ 为悬链线 $y = a \operatorname{ch} \frac{x}{a}$

解 弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{1 + \mathrm{sh}^2\frac{x}{a}}\mathrm{dx} = \mathrm{ch}\frac{x}{a}\mathrm{dx}.$

于是， $\int_{c} \frac{\mathrm{d}s}{y^2} = \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \frac{\operatorname{ch} \frac{x}{a}}{a^2 \operatorname{ch}^2 \frac{x}{a}} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{a} \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \frac{\operatorname{d}\left(\operatorname{sh} \frac{x}{a}\right)}{1 + \operatorname{sh}^2 \frac{x}{a}} = \frac{1}{a} \arctan \left(\operatorname{sh} \frac{x}{a}\right) \Bigg|_{-\infty}^{+\infty} = \frac{\pi}{a}.$
