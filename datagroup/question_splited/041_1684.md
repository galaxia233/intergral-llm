【1684】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 + 1)^{\frac{3}{2}}}$

提示 注意 $\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 + 1)^{\frac{3}{2}}} = \frac{\operatorname{sgn}x\mathrm{d}x}{x^3\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)^{\frac{3}{2}}} = -\frac{1}{2}\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)^{-\frac{3}{2}}\operatorname{sgn}x\mathrm{d}\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right).$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 + 1)^{\frac{3}{2}}} = \int \frac{\operatorname{sgn}x\mathrm{d}x}{x^3\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)^{\frac{3}{2}}} = -\frac{1}{2}\int \left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)^{-\frac{3}{2}}\operatorname{sgn}x\mathrm{d}\left(1 + \frac{1}{x^2}\right)$

$$
= \left(1 + \frac {1}{x ^ {2}}\right) ^ {- \frac {1}{2}} \operatorname {s g n} x + C = \frac {x}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} + 1}} + C.
$$
