【2148】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x\sqrt{1 + \cos x}}.$

解设 $1 + \cos x = t^2$ ，并限制 $t > 0$ ，则 $\sin x = t\sqrt{2 - t^2}$ ， $\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{2}{\sqrt{2 - t^2}}\mathrm{d}t.$ 代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sin x \sqrt {1 + \cos x}} = - \int \frac {2 \mathrm {d} t}{t ^ {2} (2 - t ^ {2})} = - \int \left(\frac {1}{t ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2 - t ^ {2}}\right) \mathrm {d} t = \frac {1}{t} - \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {\sqrt {2} + t}{\sqrt {2} - t} + C \\ = \frac {1}{\sqrt {1 + \cos x}} - \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {\sqrt {2} + \sqrt {1 + \cos x}}{\sqrt {2} - \sqrt {1 + \cos x}} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
