45.30 Compute div $\mathbf{P}$ and curl $\mathbf{P}$ , for $\mathbf{P} = (x, y, z)$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {I} \quad \operatorname {d i v} \mathbf {P} = \frac {\partial x}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial y}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial z} = 1 + 1 + 1 = 3. \quad \operatorname {c u r l} \mathbf {P} = \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial y}{\partial z}, \quad \frac {\partial x}{\partial z} - \frac {\partial z}{\partial x}, \quad \frac {\partial y}{\partial x} - \frac {\partial x}{\partial y}\right) = (0 - 0, 0 - 0, 0 - 0) = \\ 0. \end{array}
$$
