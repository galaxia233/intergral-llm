【4031】 $z = x^{\frac{3}{2}} + y^{\frac{3}{2}}, z = 0, x + y = 1, x = 0, y = 0.$

解曲面所界的立体在 $Oxy$ 平面上的投影域 $\Omega$ 由直线 $x + y = 1, x = 0, y = 0$ 围成.于是，曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \iint_ {\Omega} z d x d y = \iint_ {\Omega} \left(x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} + y ^ {\frac {3}{2}}\right) d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \int_ {0} ^ {1 - r} \left(x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} + y ^ {\frac {3}{2}}\right) d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left[ x ^ {\frac {3}{2}} (1 - x) + \frac {2}{5} (1 - x) ^ {\frac {5}{2}} \right] d x \\ = \frac {5}{2} x ^ {\frac {5}{2}} \left| _ {0} ^ {1} - \frac {7}{2} x ^ {\frac {7}{2}} \right| _ {0} ^ {1} - \frac {4}{3 5} (1 - x) ^ {\frac {7}{2}} \left| _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {2}{5} - \frac {2}{7} + \frac {4}{3 5} = \frac {8}{3 5}. \right. \\ \end{array}
$$
