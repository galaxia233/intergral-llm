40.67 Show that $\mathbf{K}_i\cdot \mathbf{V}_i = 1$ for $i = 1,2,3$ . (For the notation, see Problem 40.65.)

$\mathbf{K}_1 \cdot \mathbf{V}_1 = \frac{\mathbf{V}_2 \times \mathbf{V}_3}{c} \cdot \mathbf{V}_1 = \frac{\mathbf{V}_1 \cdot (\mathbf{V}_2 \times \mathbf{V}_3)}{c} = \frac{c}{c} = 1.$ The other cases are similar.
