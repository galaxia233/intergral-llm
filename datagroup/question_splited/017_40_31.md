40.31 Find the unit vector $\mathbf{u}$ in the same direction as $\mathbf{A} = (-2, 3, 6)$ .

$|\mathbf{A}| = \sqrt{4 + 9 + 36} = \sqrt{49} = 7$ . So, $\mathbf{u} = \frac{1}{2}\mathbf{A} = (-\frac{2}{7},\frac{3}{7},\frac{6}{7})$ . Note that $\mathbf{u} = (\cos \alpha, \cos \beta, \cos \gamma)$ , the vector composed of the direction cosines of $\mathbf{A}$ .
