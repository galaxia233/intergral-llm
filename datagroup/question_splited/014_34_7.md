34.8 Sketch the curve with the parametric equations $x = t + 1 / t, y = t - 1 / t$ .

$x^{2} = t^{2} + 2 + 1 / t^{2}, y^{2} = t^{2} - 2 + 1 / t^{2}$ . Subtracting the second equation from the first, we obtain the hyperbola $x^{2} - y^{2} = 4$ (Fig. 34-8).   
34.9 Sketch the curve with the parametric equations $x = 1 + t, y = 1 - t$ .   
$x + y = 2$ . Thus, we have a straight line, going through the point $(1,1)$ and parallel to the vector $(1, -1)$ ; see Fig. 34-9.
