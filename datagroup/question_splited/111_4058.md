【4058】 $x = a(t - \sin t),y = a(1 - \cos t)(0\leqslant t\leqslant 2\pi),y = 0.$

解 质量和对 $Ox$ 轴的一次矩分别为

$$
\begin{array}{l} M = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi a} d x \int_ {0} ^ {y} d y = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} a ^ {2} (1 - \cos t) ^ {2} d t = 3 \pi \rho a ^ {2}, \\ M _ {x} = \rho \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi a} d x \int_ {0} ^ {y} y d y = \frac {1}{2} \rho \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi a} y ^ {2} d x = \frac {1}{2} \rho a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (1 - \cos t) ^ {3} d t = \frac {5}{2} \pi \rho a ^ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，质心的纵坐标为 $y_0 = \frac{M_x}{M} = \frac{5}{6} a.$

由对称性知， $x_0 = \pi a$
