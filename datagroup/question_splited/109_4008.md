【4008】 $x + y + z = a, x^2 + y^2 = R^2, x = 0, y = 0, z = 0 (a \geqslant R\sqrt{2})$

解 $V = \int_{0}^{R}\mathrm{d}x\int_{0}^{\sqrt{R^2 - x^2}}(a - x - y)\mathrm{d}y = \int_{0}^{R}\left[(a - x)\sqrt{R^2 - x^2} -\frac{R^2 - x^2}{2}\right]\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \int_ {0} ^ {R} a \sqrt {R ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x - \int_ {0} ^ {R} \left(x \sqrt {R ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} + \frac {R ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}{2}\right) d x = \frac {\pi a R ^ {2}}{4} - \frac {2 R ^ {3}}{3}.
$$
