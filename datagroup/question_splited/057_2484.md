【2484】 $(x^{2} + y^{2})^{2} = a^{2}(x^{2} - y^{2})$
(1) 绕 $Ox$ 轴

解（1）曲线的极坐标方程为 $r^2 = a^2 (2\cos^2\varphi - 1)$

$$
V _ {x} = 2 \cdot \frac {2 \pi}{3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} [ a ^ {2} (2 \cos^ {2} \varphi - 1) ] ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \sin \varphi d \varphi .
$$

由于 $\int (2\cos^2\varphi -1)^{\frac{3}{2}}\sin \varphi \mathrm{d}\varphi$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \int [ (\sqrt {2} \cos \varphi) ^ {2} - 1 ] ^ {\frac {3}{2}} d (\sqrt {2} \cos \varphi) \\ = - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \left[ \frac {\sqrt {2} \cos \varphi}{8} (4 \cos^ {2} \varphi - 5) \sqrt {2 \cos^ {2} \varphi - 1} + \frac {3}{8} \ln (\sqrt {2} \cos \varphi + \sqrt {2 \cos^ {2} \varphi - 1}) \right] + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

所以， $V_{x} = -\frac{4\pi a^{3}}{3\sqrt{2}}\left[\frac{\sqrt{2}\cos\varphi}{8} (4\cos^{2}\varphi -5)\sqrt{2\cos^{2}\varphi - 1} +\frac{3}{8}\ln (\sqrt{2}\cos \varphi +\sqrt{2\cos^{2}\varphi - 1})\right]\Bigg{|}_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}$

$$
= \frac {4 \pi a ^ {3}}{3 \sqrt {2}} \left[ \frac {3}{8} \ln (\sqrt {2} + 1) - \frac {\sqrt {2}}{8} \right] = \frac {1}{4} \pi a ^ {3} \left[ \sqrt {2} \ln (\sqrt {2} + 1) - \frac {2}{3} \right].
$$
