【4266】 $\int_{(-2, - 1)}^{(3,0)}(x^4 +4xy^3)\mathrm{d}x + (6x^2 y^2 -5y^4)\mathrm{d}y.$

解 $P = x^4 + 4xy^3, Q = 6x^2y^2 - 5y^4$ . 显然， $P, Q$ 在全平面上具有连续偏导数，并且

$$
\frac {\partial Q}{\partial x} = 1 2 x y ^ {2} \quad \frac {\partial P}{\partial y} = 1 2 x y ^ {2},
$$

故 $\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial P}{\partial y}$ . 由于全平面是单连通区域，故在整个平面上表达式 $P \mathrm{~d}x + Q \mathrm{~d}y$ 是某函数 $u(x, y)$ 的全微分，并且线积分 $\int_{C} P \mathrm{~d}x + Q \mathrm{~d}y$ 与路径无关，因而，可按平行于坐标轴的直线段来计算所给积分，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {(- 2, - 1)} ^ {(3, 0)} \left(x ^ {4} + 4 x y ^ {3}\right) d x + \left(6 x ^ {2} y ^ {2} - 5 y ^ {4}\right) d y = \int_ {- 2} ^ {3} \left(x ^ {4} + 4 x \cdot 0 ^ {3}\right) d x + \int_ {- 1} ^ {0} \left[ 6 (- 2) ^ {2} y ^ {2} - 5 y ^ {4} \right] d y \\ = 5 5 + 7 = 6 2. \\ \end{array}
$$

注 也可利用简单的技巧求出函数 $u(x, y)$ 来。我们有

$$
(x ^ {4} + 4 x y ^ {3}) \mathrm {d} x + (6 x ^ {2} y ^ {2} - 5 y ^ {4}) \mathrm {d} y = \mathrm {d} \left(\frac {x ^ {5}}{5}\right) + 2 y ^ {3} \mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {2}\right) + 2 x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} \left(y ^ {3}\right) - \mathrm {d} \left(y ^ {5}\right) = \mathrm {d} \left(\frac {x ^ {5}}{5} + 2 x ^ {2} y ^ {3} - y ^ {5}\right),
$$

从而， $\int_{(-2, - 1)}^{(3,0)}(x^4 +4xy^3)\mathrm{d}x + (6x^2 y^2 -5y^4)\mathrm{d}y = \left(\frac{x^5}{5} +2x^2 y^3 -y^5\right)\bigg|_{(-2, - 1)}^{(3,0)} = 62.$
