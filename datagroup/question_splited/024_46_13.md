46.13 Solve $\frac{dy}{dx} = \sqrt{\frac{1 - y^2}{1 - x^2}}$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} I \int \frac {d y}{\sqrt {1 - y ^ {2}}} = \int \frac {d x}{\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}, \sin^ {- 1} y = \sin^ {- 1} x + C, \\ y = \sin (\sin^ {- 1} x + C) = \sin (\sin^ {- 1} x) \cos C + \cos (\sin^ {- 1} x) \sin C = x \cos C + \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \sin C \\ \end{array}
$$
