42.53 If $f(x, y) = \tan^{-1}(y / x)$ , verify that $f_{xx} + f_{yy} = 0$ .

$f_{x} = \frac{1}{1 + y^{2} / x^{2}}\left(\frac{-y}{x^{2}}\right) = -\frac{y}{x^{2} + y^{2}},$ $f_{xx} = \frac{y}{(x^2 + y^2)^2}$ (2x) = 2xy. $f_{y} = \frac{1}{1 + y^{2} / x^{2}}\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) = \frac{x}{x^{2} + y^{2}},$ $f_{yy} = -\frac{2yx}{(x^2 + y^2)^2}$ . Therefore, $f_{xx} + f_{yy} = 0$ .
