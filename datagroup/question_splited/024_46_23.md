46.23 Find the orthogonal trajectories of the family of hyperbolas $xy = c$ .

$xy' + y = 0$ . Replace $y'$ by $-1 / y'$ : $-x / y' + y = 0$ , $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{x}{y}$ , $\int y dy = \int x dx$ , $\frac{1}{2} y^2 = \frac{1}{2} x^2 + C_1$ , $y^2 = x^2 + C$ , $y^2 - x^2 = C$ . Hence, the orthogonal trajectories form another family of equilateral hyperbolas (and the pair of lines $y = \pm x$ ).
