42.25 If $f(x, y) = \cos 3x \sin 4y$ , find $f_{x}(\pi / 12, \pi / 6)$ and $f_{y}(\pi / 12, \pi / 6)$ .

$f_{x}(x,y) = -3\sin 3x\sin 4y.$ Therefore,

$$
f _ {x} \left(\frac {\pi}{1 2}, \frac {\pi}{6}\right) = - 3 \sin \frac {\pi}{4} \sin \frac {2 \pi}{3} = - 3 \left(\frac {\sqrt {2}}{2}\right) \left(\frac {\sqrt {3}}{2}\right) = - \frac {3 \sqrt {6}}{4} \quad f _ {y} (x, y) = 4 \cos 3 x \cos 4 y = 4 \left(\frac {\sqrt {2}}{2}\right) \left(- \frac {1}{2}\right) = - \sqrt {2}
$$
