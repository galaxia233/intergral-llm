【1933】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[4]{x^3(a - x)}} (a > 0).$

提示 令 $\sqrt[4]{\frac{a - x}{x}} = t$ ，并分别利用1921题的递推公式及1884题的结果.

解设 $\sqrt[4]{\frac{a - x}{x}} = t$ ，则 $x = \frac{a}{1 + t^4}$ $\mathrm{dx} = -\frac{4at^3}{(1 + t^4)^2}\mathrm{dt}.$ 代人得

$$
\int \frac {x \mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt [ 4 ]{x ^ {3} (a - x)}} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt [ 4 ]{\frac {a - x}{x}}} = - 4 a \int \frac {t ^ {2}}{(1 + t ^ {4}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} t = - 4 a \int \left[ \frac {t}{(t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1) (t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1)} \right] ^ {2} \mathrm {d} t
$$

$$
= - \frac {a}{2} \int \left(\frac {1}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} - \frac {1}{t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1}\right) ^ {2} d t = - \frac {a}{2} \int \frac {d t}{(t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1) ^ {2}} - \frac {a}{2} \int \frac {d t}{(t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1) ^ {2}} + a \int \frac {d t}{t ^ {4} + 1}.
$$

现在分别求上述积分，利用1921题的递推公式，即得

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{(t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1) ^ {2}} = \frac {2 t - \sqrt {2}}{2 (t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1)} + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} = \frac {2 t - \sqrt {2}}{2 (t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1)} + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(t - \frac {\sqrt {2}}{2}\right)}{\left(t - \frac {\sqrt {2}}{2}\right) ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2}}
$$

$$
= \frac {2 t - \sqrt {2}}{2 \left(t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1\right)} + \sqrt {2} \arctan (\sqrt {2} t - 1) + C _ {1}
$$

及 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{(t^2 + t\sqrt{2} + 1)^2} = \frac{2t + \sqrt{2}}{2(t^2 + t\sqrt{2} + 1)} +\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{t^2 + t\sqrt{2} + 1} = \frac{2t + \sqrt{2}}{2(t^2 + t\sqrt{2} + 1)} +\int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(t + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\right)}{\left(t + \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2}\right)^2 + \frac{1}{2}}.$

$$
= \frac {2 t + \sqrt {2}}{2 \left(t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1\right)} + \sqrt {2} \arctan (\sqrt {2} t + 1) + C _ {2}.
$$

利用1884题的结果，即得 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{t^4 + 1} = \frac{1}{4\sqrt{2}}\ln \frac{t^2 + t\sqrt{2} + 1}{t^2 - t\sqrt{2} + 1} +\frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\arctan \frac{t\sqrt{2}}{1 - t^2} +C_3.$

最后得到

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x \mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt [ 4 ]{x ^ {3} (a - x)}} = - \frac {a}{2} \left[ \frac {2 t - \sqrt {2}}{2 (t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1)} + \frac {2 t + \sqrt {2}}{2 (t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1)} \right] - \frac {a \sqrt {2}}{2} \left[ \arctan (\sqrt {2} t - 1) + \arctan (\sqrt {2} t + 1) \right] \\ + \frac {a}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} + \frac {a}{2 \sqrt {2}} \arctan \left(\frac {t \sqrt {2}}{1 - t ^ {2}}\right) + C _ {4} \\ = - \frac {a t ^ {3}}{1 + t ^ {4}} + \frac {a}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} - \frac {a}{2 \sqrt {2}} \arctan \left(\frac {t \sqrt {2}}{1 - t ^ {2}}\right) + C _ {4} \\ = - \frac {a t ^ {3}}{1 + t ^ {4}} + \frac {a}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {t ^ {2} + t \sqrt {2} + 1}{t ^ {2} - t \sqrt {2} + 1} + \frac {a}{2 \sqrt {2}} \arctan \left(\frac {1 - t ^ {2}}{t \sqrt {2}}\right) + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $t = \sqrt[4]{\frac{a - x}{x}} (0 <   x <   a).$
