变换成极坐标，求下列曲面所围区域的体积：
【4013】 $z^2 = xy, x^2 + y^2 = a^2.$

提示 注意对称性，令 $x = r\cos \varphi, y = r\sin \varphi$ ，并利用 3856 题的结果.

解 因为 $z = \sqrt{xy}$ ，故所求的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 4 \iint_ {\substack {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2} \\ x \geqslant 0, y \geqslant 0}} \sqrt {x y} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {a} \mathrm {d} r \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {\cos \varphi \sin \varphi} r ^ {2} \mathrm {d} \varphi = \frac {4}{3} a ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {\frac {1}{2}} \varphi \sin^ {\frac {1}{2}} \varphi \mathrm {d} \varphi \\ = \frac {4}{3} a ^ {3} \frac {1}{2} B \left(\frac {3}{4}, \frac {3}{4}\right) ^ {*}) = \frac {2}{3} a ^ {3} \frac {\Gamma^ {2} \left(\frac {3}{4}\right)}{\Gamma \left(\frac {3}{2}\right)} = \frac {2}{3} a ^ {3} \frac {\Gamma^ {2} \left(\frac {3}{4}\right)}{\frac {1}{2} \Gamma \left(\frac {1}{2}\right)} = \frac {4}{3} a ^ {3} \frac {\Gamma^ {2} \left(\frac {3}{4}\right)}{\sqrt {\pi}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

*) 利用 3856 题的结果.
