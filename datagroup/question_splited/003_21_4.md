21.4 The bounded region between the curves $y = x^2$ and $y = x^3$ .

See Fig. 21-4. The curves intersect at $(0,0)$ and $(1,1)$ . Between $x = 0$ and $x = 1$ , $y = x^2$ lies above $y = x^3$ . The area of the region between them is $\int_0^1 (x^2 - x^3) dx = \left(\frac{1}{3} x^3 - \frac{1}{4} x^4\right) \bigg]_0^1 = \frac{1}{3} - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{1}{12}$ .
