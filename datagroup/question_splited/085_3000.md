【3000】+ $\sum_{n = 2}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^n}{n^2 + n - 2}.$

解考虑部分和

$$
S _ {N} = \sum_ {n = 2} ^ {N} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n}}{n ^ {2} + n - 2} = \sum_ {n = 2} ^ {N} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n}}{3} \left(\frac {1}{n - 1} - \frac {1}{n + 2}\right) = \frac {1}{3} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N - 1} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k + 1}}{k} + \frac {1}{3} \sum_ {l = 4} ^ {N + 2} \frac {(- 1) ^ {l + 1}}{l}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {2}{3} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k - 1}}{k} + \frac {1}{3} \left(- 1 + \frac {1}{2} - \frac {1}{3}\right) + O \left(\frac {1}{N}\right) \\ = \frac {2}{3} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {N} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k - 1}}{k} - \frac {5}{1 8} + O \left(\frac {1}{N}\right) = \frac {2}{3} \ln 2 - \frac {5}{1 8} + O \left(\frac {1}{N}\right), \\ \end{array}
$$

故得 $\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^n}{n^2 + n - 2} = \lim_{N \to \infty} S_N = \frac{2}{3} \ln 2 - \frac{5}{18} = \frac{1}{18} (12 \ln 2 - 5)$ .
