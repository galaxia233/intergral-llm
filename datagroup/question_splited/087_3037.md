【3037】 $\int_0^1 x^{p - 1}\ln (1 - x^q)\mathrm{d}x\quad (p > 0,q > 0).$

解 $\int_0^1 x^p\ln (1 - x^q)\mathrm{d}x = \int_0^1 x^{p - 1}\left(-x^q -\frac{x^{2q}}{2} -\frac{x^{3q}}{3} -\dots\right)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left(x ^ {p - q - 1} + \frac {1}{2} x ^ {p - 2 q - 1} + \frac {1}{3} x ^ {p - 3 q - 1} + \dots\right) d x = - \left(\frac {1}{p + q} + \frac {1}{p + 2 q} \cdot \frac {1}{2} + \frac {1}{p + 3 q} \cdot \frac {1}{3} + \dots\right) \\ = - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{n (p + n q)}. \\ \end{array}
$$
