【4225】 $\int_{C} (x^{\frac{1}{3}} + y^{\frac{1}{3}}) \mathrm{d}s$ ，其中 $C$ 为星形线 $x^{\frac{2}{3}} + y^{\frac{2}{3}} = a^{\frac{2}{3}}$ 的弧.

解解法 1：

按直角坐标方程计算，弧长的微分为 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{1 + y'^2}\mathrm{dx} = \frac{a^{\frac{1}{3}}}{x^{\frac{1}{3}}}\mathrm{dx}.$

于是， $\int_{C} (x^{\frac{4}{3}} + y^{\frac{4}{3}}) \mathrm{d}s = 4\int_{0}^{a} [x^{\frac{4}{3}} + (a^{\frac{2}{3}} - x^{\frac{2}{3}})^{2}] \frac{a^{\frac{1}{3}}}{x^{\frac{1}{3}}} \mathrm{d}x = 4a^{\frac{1}{3}}\int_{0}^{a} (2x + a^{\frac{4}{3}}x^{-\frac{1}{3}} - 2a^{\frac{2}{3}}x^{\frac{1}{3}}) \mathrm{d}x = 4a^{\frac{7}{3}}.$

解法 2：

按参数方程计算。若令 $x = a\cos^3 t, y = a\sin^3 t$ ，则

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {9 a ^ {2} \cos^ {4} t \sin^ {2} t + 9 a ^ {2} \sin^ {4} t \cos^ {2} t} \mathrm {d} t = 3 a \cos t \sin t \mathrm {d} t \quad (0 \leqslant t \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}).
$$

于是， $\int_{C} (x^{\frac{4}{3}} + y^{\frac{4}{3}}) \mathrm{d}s = 4a^{\frac{4}{3}} \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} (\cos^{4} t + \sin^{4} t) 3a \cos t \sin t \mathrm{d}t = 24a^{\frac{7}{3}} \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin^{5} t \mathrm{d}(\sin t) = 4a^{\frac{7}{3}}.$
