【2974】函数 $x = x(s)$ ， $y = y(s)$ $(0\leqslant s\leqslant 4a)$ 是正方形； $0 < x < a$ ， $0 < y < a$ 的围线的参数方程，其中s

为依逆时针方向从点 $O(0,0)$ 起计算的弧长.试将这两个函数展开成傅里叶级数

解 根据定义， $x(s)$ 的表达式可写为

$$
x (s) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} s, & 0 \leqslant s \leqslant a, \\ a, & a \leqslant s \leqslant 2 a, \\ 3 a - s, & 2 a \leqslant s \leqslant 3 a, \\ 0, & 3 a \leqslant s \leqslant 4 a. \end{array} \right.
$$

于是， $x(s)$ 在 $[0, 4a]$ 上的傅里叶级数展开式为

$$
\frac {a _ {0}}{2} + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left(a _ {n} \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} + b _ {n} \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a}\right),
$$

其中

$$
a _ {0} = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {4 a} x (s) d s = \frac {1}{2 a} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {a} s d s + \int_ {a} ^ {2 a} a d s + \int_ {2 a} ^ {3 a} (3 a - s) d s \right] = a,
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {n} = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {4 a} x (s) \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {a} s \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {a} ^ {2 a} a \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {2 a} ^ {3 a} (3 a - s) \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left\{\left[ \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2} + \left(\frac {2 a}{n \pi}\right) ^ {2} \left(\cos \frac {n \pi}{2} - 1\right) \right] + \left[ - \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2} \right] \right. \\ \left. + \left[ \left(\frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) - \frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2} - \left(\frac {2 a}{n \pi}\right) ^ {2} \left(\cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2} - \cos n \pi\right) \right] \right\} \\ = \frac {2 a}{(n \pi) ^ {2}} \left(\cos \frac {n \pi}{2} - 1 - \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2} + \cos n \pi\right) \\ = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & n = 2 k, k = 1, 2, 3, \dots ; \\ - \frac {4 a}{\pi^ {2} (2 k + 1) ^ {2}}, & n = 2 k + 1, k = 0, 1, 2, 3, \dots . \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} b _ {n} = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {4 a} x (s) \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left[ \int_ {0} ^ {a} s \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {a} ^ {2 a} a \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {2 a} ^ {3 a} (3 a - s) \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left\{\left[ - \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {n \pi}{2} + \left(\frac {2 a}{n \pi}\right) ^ {2} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2} \right] + \left[ - \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos n \pi + \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {n \pi}{2} \right] \right. \\ \left. + \left[ \left(- \frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2} + \frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos n \pi\right) + \left(\frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2} - \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos n \pi\right) + \left(- \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) \right] \right\} \\ = \frac {2 a}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \left(\sin \frac {n \pi}{2} - \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) \\ = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & n = 2 k, k = 1, 2, 3, \dots , \\ \frac {4 a (- 1) ^ {k}}{\pi^ {2} (2 k + 1) ^ {2}}, & n = 2 k + 1, k = 0, 1, 2, 3, \dots . \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

因此，按展开定理，注意到 $x(0) = x(4a),x(s)$ 的傅里叶展开式为

$$
x (s) = \frac {a}{2} - \frac {4 a}{\pi^ {2}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} \cos \frac {(2 k + 1) \pi s}{2 a} + \frac {4 a}{\pi^ {2}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k}}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} \sin \frac {(2 k + 1) \pi s}{2 a} \quad (0 \leqslant s \leqslant 4 a).
$$

同样，根据定义， $y(s)$ 的表达式为

$$
y (s) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & 0 \leqslant s \leqslant a, \\ s - a, & a \leqslant s \leqslant 2 a, \\ a, & 2 a \leqslant s \leqslant 3 a, \\ 4 a - s, & 3 a \leqslant s \leqslant 4 a. \end{array} \right.
$$

于是， $y(s)$ 在 $[0,4a]$ 上的傅里叶级数展开式为

$$
\frac {A _ {0}}{2} + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left(A _ {n} \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} + B _ {n} \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a}\right),
$$

其中

$$
A _ {0} = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {a} ^ {4 a} y (s) d s = \frac {1}{2 a} \left[ \int_ {a} ^ {2 a} (s - a) d s + \int_ {2 a} ^ {3 a} a d s + \int_ {3 a} ^ {4 a} (4 a - s) d s \right] = a,
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} A _ {n} = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {4 a} y (s) \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left[ \int_ {a} ^ {2 a} (s - a) \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {2 a} ^ {3 a} a \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {3 a} ^ {4 a} (4 a - s) \cos \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left\{\left[ \left(- \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2} + \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} (\cos n \pi - \cos \frac {n \pi}{2})\right) - \left(- \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2}\right) \right] \right. \\ \left. + \left[ \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2} \right] + \left[ \left(- \frac {8 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) - \left(- \frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2} + \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} - \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) \right] \right\} \\ = \frac {2 a}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \left(\cos n \pi - \cos \frac {n \pi}{2} - 1 + \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) \\ = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & n = 2 k, k = 1, 2, 3, \dots , \\ - \frac {4 a}{\pi^ {2} (2 k + 1) ^ {2}}, & n = 2 k + 1, k = 0, 1, 2, 3, \dots . \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} B _ {n} = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {4 a} y (s) \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left[ \int_ {a} ^ {2 a} (s - a) \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {2 a} ^ {3 a} a \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s + \int_ {3 a} ^ {4 a} (4 a - s) \sin \frac {n \pi s}{2 a} d s \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \left\{ \right.\left[\left(- \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos n \pi + \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {n \pi}{2} - \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \sin \frac {n \pi}{2}\right) - \left(- \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos n \pi + \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {n \pi}{2}\right)\right] \\ + \left(- \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2} + \frac {2 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos n \pi\right) + \left[ \left(- \frac {8 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} + \frac {8 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) \right. \\ \left. \left. - \left(- \frac {8 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} - \frac {6 a ^ {2}}{n \pi} \cos \frac {3 n \pi}{2} - \frac {4 a ^ {2}}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2}\right) \right] \right\} \\ = \frac {2 a}{n ^ {2} \pi^ {2}} \left(\sin \frac {3 n \pi}{2} - \sin \frac {n \pi}{2}\right) \\ = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & n = 2 k, k = 1, 2, 3, \dots , \\ \frac {4 a (- 1) ^ {k + 1}}{\pi^ {2} (2 k + 1) ^ {2}}, & n = 2 k + 1, k = 0, 1, 2, 3, \dots . \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

因此，按展开定理，注意到 $y(0) = y(4a)$ ，得 $y(s)$ 的傅里叶级数展开式为

$$
y (s) = \frac {a}{2} - \frac {4 a}{\pi^ {2}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {1}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} \cos \frac {(2 k + 1) \pi s}{2 a} + \frac {4 a}{\pi^ {2}} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k + 1}}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2}} \sin \frac {(2 k + 1) \pi s}{2 a} \quad (0 \leqslant s \leqslant 4 a).
$$
