【4411】求 $\operatorname{grad}(\pmb{c} \cdot \pmb{r})$ ，其中 $\pmb{c}$ 为常向量， $\pmb{r}$ 为引自坐标原点的径向量.
解设 $c = c_{r}i + c_{y}j + c_{z}k$ ，其中 $c_{x},c_{y},c_{z}$ 为常数.由于
$$
\pmb {c} \cdot \pmb {r} = c _ {x} x + c _ {y} y + c _ {z} z \quad \text {及} \quad \frac {\partial (\pmb {c} \cdot \pmb {r})}{\partial x} = c _ {x}, \quad \frac {\partial (\pmb {c} \cdot \pmb {r})}{\partial y} = c _ {y}, \quad \frac {\partial (\pmb {c} \cdot \pmb {r})}{\partial z} = c _ {z},
$$
故 $\operatorname{grad}(\pmb{c} \cdot \pmb{r}) = \pmb{c}$
