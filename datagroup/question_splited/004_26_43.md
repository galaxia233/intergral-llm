26.43 A radioactive substance decays exponentially. What is the average quantity present over the first half-life?

$y = y_0e^{Kt}$ so $y_{\mathrm{avg}} = \frac{1}{T}\int_0^T y_0e^{Kt}dt = \frac{y_0}{KT} (e^{KT} - 1)$ . But by Problem 26.15, $KT = -\ln 2$ . Hence, $y_{\mathrm{avg}} = \frac{y_0}{-\ln 2}\left(\frac{1}{2} -1\right) = \frac{y_0}{2\ln 2} = \frac{y_0}{\ln 4}$ .
