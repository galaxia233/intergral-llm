【4140】 $z = x^{2} + y^{2}, z = \frac{1}{2} (x^{2} + y^{2}), x + y = \pm 1, x - y = \pm 1.$

解 作代换： $x - y = u$ ， $x + y = v$ ，则有

$$
x = \frac {u + v}{2}, \quad y = \frac {v - u}{2}, \quad z = \frac {u ^ {2} + v ^ {2}}{4} \quad \text {及} \quad z = \frac {u ^ {2} + v ^ {2}}{2},
$$

且 $|I| = \frac{1}{2}$ 及区域 $V$ 为： $-1\leq u\leq 1, - 1\leq v\leq 1,\frac{u^2 + v^2}{4}\leq z\leq \frac{u^2 + v^2}{2}.$ 于是，

$$
M = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d v \int_ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{3}} ^ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{2}} d z = \frac {1}{3}.
$$

$$
x _ {0} = \frac {1}{4 M} \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {1} ^ {1} d v \int_ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{3}} ^ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{2}} (u + v) d z = 0,
$$

$$
y _ {0} = \frac {1}{4 M} \int_ {1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {1} ^ {1} d v \int_ {\frac {u ^ {2} + v ^ {2}}{4}} ^ {\frac {u ^ {2} + v ^ {2}}{2}} (v - u) d z = 0,
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} z _ {n} = \frac {1}{2 M} \int_ {1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {1} ^ {1} d v \int_ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{4}} ^ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{2}} z d z = \frac {3}{6 4} \cdot \frac {1}{M} \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} (u ^ {4} + 2 u ^ {2} v ^ {2} + v ^ {4}) d v \\ = \frac {3}{6 4 M} \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} \left(2 u ^ {4} + \frac {4 u ^ {2}}{3} + \frac {2}{5}\right) d u = \frac {7}{2 0}, \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，质心的坐标为 $x_0 = y_0 = 0, z_0 = \frac{7}{20}$
