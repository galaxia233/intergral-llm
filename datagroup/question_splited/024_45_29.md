45.29 Find curl $\mathbf{F}$ when $\mathbf{F} = (yz, xz, xy)$ .

$$
\operatorname {c u r l} \mathbf {F} = \left(\frac {\partial (x y)}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial (x z)}{\partial z}, \frac {\partial (y z)}{\partial z} - \frac {\partial (x y)}{\partial x}, \frac {\partial (x z)}{\partial x} - \frac {\partial (y z)}{\partial y}\right) = (x - x, y - y, z - z) = (0, 0, 0) = \mathbf {0}.
$$
