46.14 Solve $\frac{dy}{dx} = 3x^2 y^2$ , given $y(0) = \frac{1}{2}$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {d y}{y ^ {2}} = \int 3 x ^ {2} d x, \quad - \frac {1}{y} = x ^ {3} + C, \quad y = - \frac {1}{x ^ {3} + C}. \quad \text {S i n c e} \quad y (0) = \frac {1}{2}, \quad \frac {1}{2} = - 1 / C, \quad C = - 2. \quad \text {H e n c e}, \\ y = - \hat {1} / (x ^ {3} - 2). \\ \end{array}
$$
