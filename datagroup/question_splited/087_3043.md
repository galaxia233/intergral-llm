【3043】利用按椭圆离心率的正整数次幂展开的级数表示椭圆 $x = a\cos t$ ， $y = b\sin t$ （ $0\leqslant t\leqslant 2\pi$ ) 的弧长.

解设 $a > b$ ，则 $\varepsilon = \frac{\sqrt{a^2 - b^2}}{a},\left(\frac{b}{a}\right)^2 = 1 - \varepsilon^2.$ 弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} t + b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d t = 4 a \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {1 - \varepsilon^ {2} \cos^ {2} t} d t \\ = 4 a \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \left\{1 - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(2 n - 3) ! !}{(2 n) ! !} \varepsilon^ {2 n} \cos^ {2 n} t \right\} d t = 4 a \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} \left\{1 - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left[ \frac {(2 n - 1) ! !}{(2 n) ! !} \right] ^ {2} \frac {\varepsilon^ {2 n}}{2 n - 1} \right\} \\ = 2 \pi a \left\{1 - \left(\frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2} \varepsilon^ {2} - \left(\frac {1 \cdot 3}{2 \cdot 4}\right) ^ {2} \cdot \frac {\varepsilon^ {4}}{3} - \dots \right\}. \\ \end{array}
$$
