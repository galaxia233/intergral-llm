【4365】 $\iint_{S} \frac{\mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z}{x} + \frac{\mathrm{d}z \, \mathrm{d}x}{y} + \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{z}$ , 式中 $S$ 为椭球面 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} + \frac{y^2}{b^2} + \frac{z^2}{c^2} = 1$ 的外侧.

提示 根据轮换对称知，只要计算 $\iint_{S} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{z}$ , 与 4362 题类似，注意曲面的侧，并利用广义极坐标 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi$ .

解 根据轮换对称知，只要计算一个积分。例如，计算 $\iint_{S} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{z}$ . 利用广义极坐标，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {S} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{z} = \iint_ {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leqslant 1} \frac {1}{c \sqrt {1 - \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} - \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}}} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y - \iint_ {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leqslant 1} \frac {- 1}{c \sqrt {1 - \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} - \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}}} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \\ = \frac {2}{c} \iint_ {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leqslant 1} \frac {1}{\sqrt {1 - \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} - \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}}} d x d y = \frac {2 a b}{c} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {r}{\sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}}} d r = \frac {4 \pi a b}{c} [ - \sqrt {1 - r ^ {2}} ] \Big | _ {0} ^ {1} = 4 \pi \frac {a b}{c}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，我们有 $\iint_{S} \frac{\mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z}{x} + \frac{\mathrm{d}z \, \mathrm{d}x}{y} + \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{z} = 4\pi \left( \frac{bc}{a} + \frac{ac}{b} + \frac{ab}{c} \right) = \frac{4\pi}{abc} (b^2 c^2 + a^2 c^2 + a^2 b^2).$
