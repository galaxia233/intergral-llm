41.58 Find $\lim_{(x,y)\to (0,0)}\frac{x^2y^2}{x^4 + y^4}$ if it exists.

Let $y = mx$ . Then

$$
\frac {x ^ {2} y ^ {2}}{x ^ {4} + y ^ {4}} = \frac {x ^ {2} \left(m ^ {2} x ^ {2}\right)}{x ^ {4} + m ^ {4} x ^ {4}} = \frac {m ^ {2}}{1 + m ^ {4}}
$$

Therefore, the limit does not exist. (For example, we get different limits for $m = 0$ and $m = 1$ .)
