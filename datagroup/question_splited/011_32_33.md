32.34 Find the area under $y = 1 / (x^{2} - a^{2})$ for $x\geq a + 1$

From Problem 32.27,

$$
\begin{array}{l} A = \int_ {a + 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {d x}{x ^ {2} - a ^ {2}} = \lim  _ {u \rightarrow + \infty} \int_ {a + 1} ^ {u} \frac {d x}{x ^ {2} - a ^ {2}} = \frac {1}{2 a} \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \ln \left| \frac {a - x}{a + x} \right| ] _ {a + 1} ^ {v} \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \left(\ln \frac {v - a}{v + a} - \ln \frac {1}{| 2 a + 1 |}\right) = \frac {1}{2 a} \ln | 2 a + 1 | \\ \end{array}
$$
