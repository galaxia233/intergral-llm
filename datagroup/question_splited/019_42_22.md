42.22 If $z = \int_{x}^{y}e^{\sin t}dt$ ， find $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$ and $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$

$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x}\left(-\int_{y}^{x}e^{\sin t}dt\right) = -e^{\sin x},$ since, in general, $\frac{d}{dx}\int_{a}^{x}f(t)dt = f(x).$ Similarly, $\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = e^{\sin y}.$
