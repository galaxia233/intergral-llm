【2353】 (1) $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln \sin x\mathrm{d}x;$

解题思路 首先，容易证明它们是收敛的。

其次，令 $t = \frac{\pi}{2} - x$ ，可得 $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \ln \sin x \, \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \ln \cos x \, \mathrm{d}x = A.$ 相加即得 $2A = A - \frac{\pi}{2} \ln 2$ .

解 先证明它们是收敛的。事实上，当 $x \to +0$ 时， $\sqrt{x} \ln \sin x \to 0$ , 所以，积分 $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \ln \sin x \mathrm{d}x$ 收敛.

同法可证积分 $\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\ln \cos x\mathrm{d}x$ 也收敛.

其次，求这两个积分的值.设 $t = \frac{\pi}{2} -x$ ，则有

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \ln \cos x d x = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \ln \sin x d x = A.
$$

相加得 $2A = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} (\ln \sin x + \ln \cos x) \, \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \ln \left( \frac{1}{2} \sin 2x \right) \, \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \ln \sin 2x \, \mathrm{d}x - \ln 2 \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \, \mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \ln \sin t d t - \frac {\pi}{2} \ln 2 = \frac {1}{2} \left(\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \ln \sin t d t + \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\pi} \ln \sin t d t\right) - \frac {\pi}{2} \ln 2 \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \ln \sin t d t - \frac {\pi}{2} \ln 2 = A - \frac {\pi}{2} \ln 2, \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $2A = A - \frac{\pi}{2}\ln 2$ ， $A = -\frac{\pi}{2}\ln 2$ ，即

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \ln \sin x d x = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \ln \cos x d x = - \frac {\pi}{2} \ln 2.
$$
