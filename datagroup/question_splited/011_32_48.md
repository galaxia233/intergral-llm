32.49 Evaluate $\int_0^\infty \frac{x dx}{x^4 + 1}$

Let $u = x^2$ , $du = 2x \, dx$ . Then $\int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{x \, dx}{x^4 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \lim_{v \to +\infty} \tan^{-1} u \big]_0^v = \frac{1}{2} \lim_{v \to +\infty} (\tan^{-1} v) = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{\infty} \frac{du}{u^2 + 1} = \frac{1}{2} u \, dx.$
