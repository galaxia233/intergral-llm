44.5 Evaluate the iterated integral $I = \int_0^1\int_0^z\int_0^y (x + y + z)dxdydz.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {y} (x + y + z) d x = \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} + (y + z) x ] _ {0} ^ {y} = \frac {1}{2} y ^ {2} + (y + z) z. \quad \text {H e n c e}, \quad \int_ {0} ^ {z} \int_ {0} ^ {y} (x + y + z) d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {z} (\frac {1}{2} y ^ {2} + y z + \\ z ^ {2}) d y = \frac {1}{6} y ^ {3} + \frac {1}{2} y ^ {2} z + z ^ {2} y ] _ {0} ^ {z} = \frac {1}{6} z ^ {3} + \frac {1}{2} z ^ {3} + z ^ {3} = \frac {5}{3} z ^ {3}. \quad \text {T h e r e f o r e ,} \quad I = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {5}{3} z ^ {3} d z = \frac {5}{1 2} z ^ {4} ] _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {5}{1 2}. \end{array}
$$
