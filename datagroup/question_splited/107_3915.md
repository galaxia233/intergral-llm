【3915】求圆 $(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 \leqslant R^2$ 上的点到原点的距离之平方的平均值.

解题思路 平均值 $I_0 = \frac{1}{\pi R^2} \iint_{(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 \leqslant R^2} (x^2 + y^2) \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y.$ 可以求得

$$
\frac {1}{\pi R ^ {2}} \iint_ {(x - a) ^ {2} \cdot (y b) ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} y ^ {2} d x d y = b ^ {2} + \frac {R ^ {2}}{4}, \quad \frac {1}{\pi R ^ {2}} \iint_ {(x - a) ^ {2} \cdot (y b) ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} x ^ {2} d x d y = a ^ {2} + \frac {R ^ {2}}{4}.
$$

从而， $I_{0} = a^{2} + b^{2} + \frac{R^{2}}{2}$

解 平均值 $I_0 = \frac{1}{\pi R^2} \iint_{(x - x)^2 + (y - b)^2 \leqslant R^2} (x^2 + y^2) \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y.$ 由于

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{\pi R ^ {2}} \iint_ {(r - a) ^ {2} + (y - b) ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} y ^ {2} d x d y = \frac {1}{\pi R ^ {2}} \int_ {a R} ^ {a - R} d x \int_ {b} ^ {b \cdot \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2}}} \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (r - a) ^ {2}} y ^ {2} d y \\ = \frac {1}{3 \pi R ^ {2}} \left\{6 b ^ {2} \int_ {a - K} ^ {a + R} \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2}} d x + 2 \int_ {a - K} ^ {a + R} \left[ R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {\frac {3}{2}} d x \right\} \\ = \frac {2 b ^ {2}}{\pi R ^ {2}} \left[ \frac {x - a}{2} \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2}} + \frac {R ^ {2}}{2} \arcsin \frac {x - a}{R} \right] \Bigg | _ {a - R} ^ {a - R} + \frac {2}{3 \pi R ^ {2}} \left\{\frac {x - a}{8} [ 5 R ^ {2} - 2 (x - a) ^ {2} ] \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2}} \right. \\ \left. + \frac {3 R ^ {4}}{8} \arcsin \frac {x - a}{R} \right\} \right| _ {a - R} ^ {a - R} \\ = \frac {2 b ^ {2}}{\pi R ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {\pi R ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {1}{3 \pi R ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {3 \pi R ^ {4}}{8} = b ^ {2} + \frac {R ^ {2}}{4}. \\ \end{array}
$$

同理，有 $\frac{1}{\pi R^2} \iint_{(r - a)^2 - (y - h)^2 \leqslant R^2} x^2 \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y = a^2 + \frac{R^2}{4}$ .
