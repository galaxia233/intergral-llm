40.33 Find the vector projection of $\mathbf{A} = (1,2,4)$ on $\mathbf{B} = (4, - 2,4)$

As in the planar case, the scalar projection of $\mathbf{A}$ on $\mathbf{B}$ is $\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} / |\mathbf{B}|$ (see Fig. 40-3). As the unit vector in the direction of $\mathbf{B}$ is $\mathbf{B} / |\mathbf{B}|$ , the vector projection is $\frac{\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}}{|\mathbf{B}|^2} \mathbf{B} = \frac{\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B}}{\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B}} \mathbf{B}$ . For the data, $\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} = 1(4) + 2(-2) + 4(4) = 16$ and $\mathbf{B} \cdot \mathbf{B} = 4^2 + (-2)^2 + 4^2 = 36$ . So, the vector projection of $\mathbf{A}$ on $\mathbf{B}$ is $\frac{16}{36} \mathbf{B} = \frac{1}{9} \mathbf{B} = \left(\frac{16}{9}, -\frac{8}{9}, \frac{16}{9}\right)$ .
