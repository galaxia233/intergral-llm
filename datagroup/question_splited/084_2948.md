【2948】+ 在区间 $(-h, h)$ 内展开 $f(x) = e^{ax}$ .

解由于

$$
a _ {0} = \frac {1}{h} \int_ {- h} ^ {h} e ^ {a x} d x = \frac {1}{a h} \left(e ^ {a h} - e ^ {- a h}\right) = \frac {2}{a h} \operatorname {s h} a h,
$$

$$
a _ {n} = \frac {1}{h} \int_ {- h} ^ {h} e ^ {a x} \cos \frac {n \pi x}{h} d x = \frac {1}{h} \cdot \left. \frac {a \cos \frac {n \pi x}{h} + \frac {n \pi}{h} \sin \frac {n \pi x}{h}}{a ^ {2} + \left(\frac {n \pi}{h}\right) ^ {2}} e ^ {a x} \right| _ {- h} ^ {h} = \frac {(- 1) ^ {n} 2 a h}{(a h) ^ {2} + (n \pi) ^ {2}} \text {s h a h};
$$

$$
b _ {n} = \frac {1}{h} \int_ {- h} ^ {h} e ^ {a x} \sin \frac {n \pi x}{h} d x = \frac {1}{h} \cdot \left. \frac {a \sin \frac {n \pi x}{h} - \frac {n \pi}{h} \cos \frac {n \pi x}{h}}{a ^ {2} + \left(\frac {n \pi}{h}\right) ^ {2}} e ^ {a x} \right| _ {- h} ^ {h} = \frac {(- 1) ^ {n + 1} 2 n \pi}{(a h) ^ {2} + (n \pi) ^ {2}} \text {s h a h},
$$

故按展开定理， $f(x)$ 可展开为

$$
2 \operatorname {s h a} h \left[ \frac {1}{2 a h} + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {a h \cos \frac {n \pi x}{h} - n \pi \sin \frac {n \pi x}{h}}{(a h) ^ {2} + (n \pi) ^ {2}} \right] = e ^ {a x} \quad (- h < x < h).
$$
