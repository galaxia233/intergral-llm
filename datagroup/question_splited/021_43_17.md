43.17 For the function $f$ of Problem 43.16, find the rate of change of $f$ at $(4,1,0)$ along the normal line to the plane $3(x - 4) - (y - 1) + 2z = 0$ in the direction of increasing $x$ .

A vector parallel to the normal line to the given plane is $(3, -1, 2)$ and it is pointing in the direction of increasing $x$ . The unit vector in this direction is $\mathbf{u} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{14}} (3, -1, 2)$ . Since $\nabla f = (8, 8, -8)$ , the rate of change is $\nabla f \cdot \mathbf{u} = 8(1, 1, -1) \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{14}} (3, -1, 2) = \frac{8}{\sqrt{14}} (0) = 0$ .
