45.31 Compute div $\mathbf{F}$ and curl $\mathbf{F}$ for $\mathbf{F} = (\cos 2x, \sin 2y, \tan z)$ .

$$
\operatorname {I} \quad \operatorname {d i v} \mathbf {F} = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} (\cos 2 x) + \frac {\partial}{\partial y} (\sin 2 y) + \frac {\partial}{\partial z} (\tan z) = - 2 \sin 2 x + 2 \cos 2 y + \sec^ {2} z
$$

$$
\operatorname {c u r l} \mathbf {F} = \left(\frac {\partial (\tan z)}{\partial y} - \frac {\partial (\sin 2 y)}{\partial z}, \frac {\partial (\cos 2 x)}{\partial z} - \frac {\partial (\tan z)}{\partial x}, \frac {\partial (\sin 2 y)}{\partial x} - \frac {\partial (\cos 2 x)}{\partial y}\right) = (0 - 0, 0 - 0, 0 - 0) = \mathbf {0}
$$
