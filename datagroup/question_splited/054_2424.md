【2424】+ 求由曲线 $\varphi = r\arctan r$ 及二射线 $\varphi = 0$ 及 $\varphi = \frac{\pi}{\sqrt{3}}$ 所围成之扇形的面积。

解 当 $\varphi$ 由 0 变到 $\frac{\pi}{\sqrt{3}}, r$ 从 0 变到 $\sqrt{3}$ ，而

$$
\mathrm {d} \varphi = \left(\frac {r}{1 + r ^ {2}} + \arctan r\right) \mathrm {d} r.
$$

所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{\sqrt {3}}} r ^ {2} d \varphi = \left. \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {3}} \left(\frac {r ^ {3}}{1 + r ^ {2}} + r ^ {2} \arctan r\right) d r = \left[ \frac {1}{6} r ^ {2} - \frac {1}{6} \ln (1 + r ^ {2}) + \frac {1}{6} r ^ {3} \arctan r \right] \right| _ {0} ^ {\sqrt {3}} \\ = \frac {1}{2} - \frac {1}{3} \ln 2 + \frac {\sqrt {3}}{6} \pi . \\ \end{array}
$$
