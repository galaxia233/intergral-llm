【4426】求 $\operatorname{div}[\operatorname{grad}f(r)]$ ，其中 $r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$ 。在怎样的情况下 $\operatorname{div}[\operatorname{grad}f(r)] = 0$ ?
提示 利用4410题的结果.
解 由4410题的结果知， $\operatorname{grad}f(r) = f'(r)\frac{r}{r}$ .
于是，
$$
\begin{array}{l} \operatorname {d i v} [ \operatorname {g r a d} f (r) ] = \frac {\partial}{\partial x} \left[ f ^ {\prime} (r) \frac {x}{r} \right] + \frac {\partial}{\partial y} \left[ f ^ {\prime} (r) \frac {y}{r} \right] + \frac {\partial}{\partial z} \left[ f ^ {\prime} (r) \frac {z}{r} \right] \\ = f ^ {\prime \prime} (r) \left(\frac {x ^ {2}}{r ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{r ^ {2}} + \frac {z ^ {2}}{r ^ {2}}\right) + f ^ {\prime} (r) \left(\frac {r ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}{r ^ {3}} + \frac {r ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{r ^ {3}} + \frac {r ^ {2} - z ^ {2}}{r ^ {3}}\right) = f ^ {\prime \prime} (r) + \frac {2}{r} f ^ {\prime} (r). \\ \end{array}
$$

要 $\operatorname{div}[\operatorname{grad}f(r)] = 0$ ，只要 $f''(r) + \frac{2}{r} f'(r) = 0$ . 将上述方程写成下述形式：
$$
r f ^ {\prime \prime} (r) + 2 f ^ {\prime} (r) = 0. \quad \text {或} [ r f ^ {\prime \prime} (r) + f ^ {\prime} (r) ] + f ^ {\prime} (r) = 0.
$$

积分之，即得 $rf^{\prime}(r) + f(r) = C$ （C为常数）.
再积分之，得 $rf(r) = Cr + C_1$ （ $C_1$ 为常数）.
于是，最后得 $f(r) = C + \frac{C_1}{r},$
此即所求的解.
