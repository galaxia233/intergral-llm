【4144】 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} +\frac{z^2}{c^2} = 1.$

提示 作代换 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos \psi, y = brsin\varphi \cos \psi, z = cr\sin \psi$ ，并利用对称性。

解 若令 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos \psi, y = b r \sin \varphi \cos \psi, z = c r \sin \psi$ ，则有

$$
I _ {r y} = a b c ^ {3} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {1} r ^ {3} \cos \psi \sin^ {2} \psi d r = \frac {a b c ^ {3}}{5} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos \psi \sin^ {2} \psi d \psi = \frac {a b c ^ {3}}{1 5} 2 \pi \sin^ {3} \psi \left| _ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {4}{1 5} \pi a b c ^ {3}. \right.
$$

利用对称性可得 $I_{yz} = \frac{4}{15}\pi a^3 bc,\qquad I_{zx} = \frac{4}{15}\pi ab^3 c.$
