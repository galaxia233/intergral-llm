【1814】 $\int x^{2}\ln \frac{1 - x}{1 + x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x^{2}\ln \frac{1 - x}{1 + x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{3}\int \ln \frac{1 - x}{1 + x}\mathrm{d}(x^{3}) = \frac{x^{3}}{3}\ln \frac{1 - x}{1 + x} +\frac{2}{3}\int \frac{x^{3}}{1 - x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

$$
= \frac {x ^ {3}}{3} \ln \frac {1 - x}{1 + x} + \frac {2}{3} \int \left(- x + \frac {x}{1 - x ^ {2}}\right) d x = \frac {x ^ {3}}{3} \ln \frac {1 - x}{1 + x} - \frac {1}{3} x ^ {2} - \frac {1}{3} \ln (1 - x ^ {2}) + C.
$$
