27.48 $\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{3x^2 - 2}}.$   
$\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{3x^2 - 2}} = \int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{3(x^2 - \frac{2}{3})}} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\int \frac{dx}{x\sqrt{x^2 - \frac{2}{3}}}$ . From the formula in Problem 27.46, we obtain $\frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}\cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}}} \sec^{-1} \frac{x}{\sqrt{\frac{2}{3}}} + C = \frac{\sqrt{2}}{2} \sec^{-1} \frac{\sqrt{6}x}{2} + C.$
