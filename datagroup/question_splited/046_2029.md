【2029】 $\int \frac{\sin^2x}{1 + \sin^2x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{\sin^2 x}{1 + \sin^2 x} \mathrm{d}x = \int \left(1 - \frac{1}{1 + \sin^2 x}\right) \mathrm{d}x = x - \int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\tan x)}{\sec^2 x + \tan^2 x} = x - \int \frac{\mathrm{d}(\tan x)}{1 + 2\tan^2 x}$

$$
= x - \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \arctan (\sqrt {2} \tan x) + C.
$$
