【4101】 $z = x^{2} + y^{2}, z = 2x^{2} + 2y^{2}, y = x, y = x^{2}$

解区域V为 $0\leqslant x\leqslant 1,x^{2}\leqslant y\leqslant x,x^{2} + y^{2}\leqslant z\leqslant 2x^{2} + 2y^{2},$

故体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x \int_ {x ^ {2}} ^ {x} d y \int_ {x ^ {2} \cdot y ^ {2}} ^ {2 x ^ {2} \cdot 2 y ^ {2}} d z = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x \int_ {x ^ {2}} ^ {x} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) d y = \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left(\frac {4}{3} x ^ {3} - x ^ {4} - \frac {1}{3} x ^ {6}\right) d x \\ = \left(\frac {1}{3} x ^ {4} - \frac {1}{5} x ^ {5} - \frac {1}{2 1} x ^ {7}\right) \Big | _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {3}{3 5}. \\ \end{array}
$$
