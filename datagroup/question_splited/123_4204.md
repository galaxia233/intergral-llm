【4204】 (1) $\int_{0}^{1}\int_{0}^{1}\dots \int_{0}^{1}(x_1^2 +x_2^2 +\dots +x_n^2)\mathrm{d}x_1\mathrm{d}x_2\dots \mathrm{d}x_n;$

提示 (1) 化成累次积分即可获解；

解（1） $\int_0^1\int_0^1\dots \int_0^1 (x_1^2 +x_2^2 +\dots +x_n^2)\mathrm{d}x_1\mathrm{d}x_2\dots \mathrm{d}x_n = \int_0^1\mathrm{d}x_1\int_0^1\mathrm{d}x_2\dots \int_0^1 (x_1^2 +x_2^2 +\dots +x_n^2)\mathrm{d}x_n$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x _ {1} \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x _ {2} \dots \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left(x _ {1} ^ {2} + x _ {2} ^ {2} + \dots + x _ {n - 1} ^ {2} + \frac {1}{3}\right) d x _ {n - 1} \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x _ {1} \int_ {0} ^ {1} d x _ {2} \dots \int_ {0} ^ {1} \left(x _ {1} ^ {2} + x _ {2} ^ {2} + \dots + x _ {n - 2} ^ {2} + \frac {1}{3} + \frac {1}{3}\right) d x _ {n - 2} \\ = \dots = \frac {n}{3}. \\ \end{array}
$$
