34.19 Find $dy / dx$ and $d^2 y / dx^2$ along the general curve $x = x(t),\quad y = y(t)$

Using dot notation for $t$ -derivatives, we have

$$
\frac {d y}{d x} = \frac {\dot {y}}{\dot {x}}
$$

$$
\frac {d ^ {2} y}{d x ^ {2}} = \frac {d}{d t} \left(\frac {\dot {y}}{\dot {x}}\right) / \dot {x} = \frac {\dot {x} \ddot {y} - \dot {y} \ddot {x}}{\dot {x} ^ {3}}
$$
