40.52 Verify that $\mathbf{B}\times \mathbf{A} = -(\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{B})$

Let $\mathbf{A} = (a_{1}, a_{2}, a_{3})$ and $\mathbf{B} = (b_{1}, b_{2}, b_{3})$ . Then $\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B} = (a_{2}b_{3} - a_{3}b_{2}, a_{3}b_{1} - a_{1}b_{3}, a_{1}b_{2} - a_{2}b_{1})$ and $\mathbf{B} \times \mathbf{A} = (b_{2}a_{3} - b_{3}a_{2}, b_{3}a_{1} - b_{1}a_{3}, b_{1}a_{2} - b_{2}a_{1}) = -(\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B})$ .
