【4074】物体对挤压面 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2}\leqslant 1$ 的压强分布由公式 $p = p_0\left(1 - \frac{x^2}{a^2} -\frac{y^2}{b^2}\right)$ 给出。求物体对此面的平均压强

解 物体在椭圆面上的平均压强为

$$
p _ {i p} = \frac {1}{\pi a b} \iint_ {\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leq 1} p _ {0} \left(1 - \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} - \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}\right) d x d y = \frac {4}{\pi a b} \int_ {a} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \theta \int_ {a} ^ {1} p _ {0} (1 - r ^ {2}) a b r d r = \frac {4}{\pi a b} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} \cdot \frac {p _ {0} a b}{4} = \frac {p _ {0}}{2}.
$$
