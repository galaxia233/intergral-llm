【2278】 $\int_0^\pi (x\sin x)^2\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int_0^\pi (x\sin x)^2\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int_0^\pi x^2 (1 - \cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{6} x^3\Bigg|_0^\pi -\frac{1}{2}\int_0^\pi x^2\cos 2x\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {\pi^ {3}}{6} - \frac {x ^ {2}}{4} \sin 2 x \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} + \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x \sin 2 x d x = \frac {\pi^ {3}}{6} - \frac {x}{4} \cos 2 x \right| _ {0} ^ {\pi} + \frac {1}{4} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos 2 x d x = \frac {\pi^ {3}}{6} - \frac {\pi}{4}.
$$
