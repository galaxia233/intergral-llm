【4078】 $\iiint_{V} xyz \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z$ ，其中 $V$ 是曲面 $x^2 + y^2 + z^2 = 1, x = 0, y = 0, z = 0$ 所围的区域.

解 $\iiint_{V} xyz \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y \, \mathrm{d}z = \int_{0}^{1} x \, \mathrm{d}x \int_{0}^{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} y \, \mathrm{d}y \int_{0}^{\sqrt{1 - x^2 - y^2}} z \, \mathrm{d}z = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{1} x \, \mathrm{d}x \int_{0}^{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} y (1 - x^2 - y^2) \, \mathrm{d}y$

$$
= \frac {1}{8} \int_ {0} ^ {1} x (1 - x ^ {2}) ^ {2} d x = \frac {1}{4 8}.
$$
