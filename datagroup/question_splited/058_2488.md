【2488】 $y = \tan x$ （ $0 \leqslant x \leqslant \frac{\pi}{4}$ ）绕 $Ox$ 轴.

解 $\sqrt{1 + y'^2} = \sqrt{1 + \sec^4 x} = \frac{\sqrt{\cos^4 x + 1}}{\cos^2 x}$ . 于是，所求的表面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} P _ {r} = 2 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \tan x \frac {\sqrt {\cos^ {4} x + 1}}{\cos^ {2} x} d x = \pi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \sqrt {\cos^ {4} x + 1} d \left(\frac {1}{\cos^ {2} x}\right) \\ = \pi \left[ \frac {\sqrt {\cos^ {4} x + 1}}{\cos^ {2} x} - \ln (\cos^ {2} x + \sqrt {\cos^ {4} x + 1}) \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} = \pi \left[ \sqrt {5} - \sqrt {2} + \ln \frac {(\sqrt {2} + 1) (\sqrt {5} - 1)}{2} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
