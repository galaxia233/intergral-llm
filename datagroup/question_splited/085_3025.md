【3025】 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}(-1)^{n - 1}\frac{\sin nx}{n(n + 1)}.$

解 由于 $\frac{1}{n(n + 1)} = \frac{1}{n} -\frac{1}{n + 1}$ 故得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {\sin n x}{n (n + 1)} = - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\sin n x}{n} + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\sin n x}{n + 1} \\ = - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\sin n x}{n} - \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {\sin (m - 1) x}{m} \\ = - \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\sin n x}{n} - \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {\sin m x}{m} \cos x + \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m} \frac {\cos m x}{m} \sin x \\ = - (1 + \cos x) \left(- \frac {x}{2}\right) - \sin x \ln \left(2 \cos \frac {x}{2}\right) ^ {*}) \\ = \frac {x}{2} (1 + \cos x) - \sin x \ln \left(2 \cos \frac {x}{2}\right) \quad (| x | <   \pi). \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用解 3023 题的 (1)、(2) 两式的结果.
