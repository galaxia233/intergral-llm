【2500】由直线 $x = \frac{p}{2}$ 与抛物线 $y^{2} = 2px$ 围成的图形绕直线 $y = p$ 旋转而构成一旋转体，求其体积和表面积.

解 所求的体积为

$$
V _ {y = p} = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {p}{2}} \pi (p + \sqrt {2 p x}) ^ {2} d x - \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {p}{2}} \pi (p - \sqrt {2 p x}) ^ {2} d x = 4 \pi p \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {p}{2}} \sqrt {2 p x} d x = \frac {4}{3} \pi p ^ {3}.
$$

旋转体的侧面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S _ {\text {侧}} = \int_ {(l)} 2 \pi (p + \sqrt {2 p x}) \mathrm {d} s + \int_ {(l)} 2 \pi (p - \sqrt {2 p x}) \mathrm {d} s = 4 \pi p \int_ {(l)} \mathrm {d} s = 4 \pi p \int_ {0} ^ {p} \sqrt {1 + \frac {y ^ {2}}{p ^ {2}}} \mathrm {d} y \\ = 4 \pi \int_ {0} ^ {p} \sqrt {y ^ {2} + p ^ {2}} d y = 4 \pi \left[ \frac {y}{2} \sqrt {y ^ {2} + p ^ {2}} + \frac {p ^ {2}}{2} \ln (y + \sqrt {y ^ {2} + p ^ {2}}) \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {p} \\ = 2 \pi p ^ {2} [ \sqrt {2} + \ln (1 + \sqrt {2}) ], \\ \end{array}
$$

而底面积为

$$
S _ {\text {底}} = \pi (2 p) ^ {2} = 4 \pi p ^ {2},
$$

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P = S _ {\text {侧}} + S _ {\text {底}} = 2 \pi p ^ {2} \left[ (2 + \sqrt {2}) + \ln (1 + \sqrt {2}) \right].
$$
