27.34 $y = x\sqrt{a^2 - x^2} + a^2\sin^{-1}(x / a), \quad \text{where} \quad a > 0.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} y ^ {\prime} = x \cdot \frac {- x}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 - (x / a) ^ {2}}} \cdot \frac {1}{a} = \frac {- x ^ {2}}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} = \frac {2 (a ^ {2} - x ^ {2})}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} \\ = 2 \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
