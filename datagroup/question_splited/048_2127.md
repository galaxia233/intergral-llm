【2127】 $\int \frac{x^2\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x^2)^3}$

解 $\int \frac{x^2\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x^2)^3} = \int \frac{(x^2 - 1) + 1}{(1 - x^2)^3}\mathrm{d}x = -\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 - 1)^2} -\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(x^2 - 1)^3}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\left(x ^ {2} - 1\right) ^ {2}} - \left[ \frac {2 x}{2 (- 4) \left(x ^ {2} - 1\right) ^ {2}} - \frac {3}{4} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\left(x ^ {2} - 1\right) ^ {2}} \right] ^ {*}) \\ = - \frac {1}{4} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\left(x ^ {2} - 1\right) ^ {2}} + \frac {x}{4 \left(1 - x ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} = - \frac {1}{4} \left\{- \frac {x}{2 \left(x ^ {2} - 1\right)} - \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {2} - 1} \right\} ^ {(*)} + \frac {x}{4 \left(1 - x ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {x + x ^ {3}}{8 (1 - x ^ {2}) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{1 6} \ln \left| \frac {1 + x}{1 - x} \right| + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用 1921 题的递推公式.
