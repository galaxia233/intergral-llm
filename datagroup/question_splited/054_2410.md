【2410】 $y = \mathrm{e}^{-x}\sin x\cdot y = 0(x\geqslant 0).$

提示 所求的面积为 $S = \lim_{n\to +\infty}\sum_{k = 0}^{n}(-1)^{k}\int_{k\pi}^{(k + 1)\pi}\mathrm{e}^{-x}\sin x\mathrm{d}x$ ，并利用 1829 题的结果。

解 令 $\sin x = 0$ ，得 $x = k\pi (k = 0, \pm 1, \pm 2, \dots)$ 。当 $x \geqslant 0$ 时，由于 $\sin x$ 在 $(\pi, 2\pi), (3\pi, 4\pi), \dots, ((2k - 1)\pi, 2k\pi), \dots$ 中的值为负，而在 $(0, \pi), (2\pi, 3\pi), \dots, (2k\pi, (2k + 1)\pi), \dots$ 中的值为正，故所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} e ^ {- x} \sin x d x - \int_ {\pi} ^ {2 \pi} e ^ {- x} \sin x d x + \int_ {2 \pi} ^ {3 \pi} e ^ {- x} \sin x d x - \dots + (- 1) ^ {k} \int_ {k \pi} ^ {(k + 1) \pi} e ^ {- x} \sin x d x + \dots \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow + \infty} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k} \int_ {k \pi} ^ {(k + 1) \pi} e ^ {- x} \sin x d x = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k} \frac {- e ^ {- x} (\sin x + \cos x)}{2} \Bigg | _ {k \pi} ^ {(k + 1) \pi} \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k + 1} \frac {1}{2} \left[ e ^ {- (k + 1) x} \cos (k + 1) \pi - e ^ {- k x} \cos k \pi \right] \\ = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} \frac {1}{2} (- 1) ^ {k + 1} [ (- 1) ^ {k + 1} e ^ {- (k + 1) n} - (- 1) ^ {k} e ^ {- k n} ] = \frac {1}{2} \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n} [ e ^ {- (k + 1) n} + e ^ {- k n} ] \\ = \frac {1}{2} \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \left[ 1 + 2 e ^ {- \pi} \sum_ {k = 0} ^ {n - 1} e ^ {- k \pi} + e ^ {- (n + 1) \pi} \right] = \frac {1}{2} \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \left[ 1 + 2 e ^ {- \pi} \frac {1 - e ^ {- n \pi}}{1 - e ^ {- \pi}} + e ^ {- (n + 1) \pi} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left(1 + \frac {2 e ^ {- \pi}}{1 - e ^ {- \pi}}\right) = \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {e ^ {\pi} + 1}{e ^ {\pi} - 1} = \frac {1}{2} \coth \frac {\pi}{2} \approx 0. 5 4 5. \\ \end{array}
$$
