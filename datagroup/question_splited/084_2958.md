【2958】 $f(x) = |\cos x|$

解 由于

$$
f (x) = | \cos x | = \left| \sin \left(x + \frac {\pi}{2}\right) \right|,
$$

故有 $|\cos x| = \frac{2}{\pi} - \frac{4}{\pi} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\cos 2n\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)^{n}}{4n^{2} - 1} = \frac{2}{\pi} - \frac{4}{\pi} \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n}\cos 2nx}{4n^{2} - 1}$

$$
= \frac {2}{\pi} + \frac {4}{\pi} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n + 1} \frac {\cos 2 n x}{4 n ^ {2} - 1} \quad (- \infty < x < + \infty).
$$

\*）利用2957题的结果.
