35.69 Find the area inside the circle $r = \sin \theta$ and outside the cardioid $r = 1 - \cos \theta$ .

For $0 \leq \theta \leq \pi / 2$ , $\sin^2 \theta = 1 - \cos^2 \theta \geq 1 - \cos \theta \geq (1 - \cos \theta)^2$ , so that $\sin \theta \geq 1 - \cos \theta$ and the desired area is as shown in Fig. 35-17.

$$
\begin{array}{l} A = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \left[ \sin^ {2} \theta - (1 - \cos \theta) ^ {2} \right] d \theta = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} (2 \cos \theta - 1 - \cos 2 \theta) d \theta = \frac {1}{2} (2 \sin \theta - \theta - \frac {1}{2} \sin 2 \theta) ] _ {0} ^ {\pi / 2} \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left(2 - \frac {\pi}{2}\right) = 1 - \frac {\pi}{4} \\ \end{array}
$$
