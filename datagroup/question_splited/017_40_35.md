40.35 Find the angle $\theta$ between the vectors $\mathbf{A} = (1,2,3)$ and $\mathbf{B} = (2, - 3, - 1)$

$\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} = |\mathbf{A}| |\mathbf{B}| \cos \theta, 1(2) + 2(-3) + 3(-1) = (\sqrt{1 + 4 + 9})(\sqrt{4 + 9 + 1})(\cos \theta), -7 = 14 \cos \theta, \cos \theta = -\frac{1}{2},$ $\theta = 120^{\circ} = 2\pi / 3.$
