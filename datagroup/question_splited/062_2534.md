$$
x _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 1;

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {1 - \frac {1}{4} \sin^ {2} x} d x \approx h \left(\frac {y _ {0} + y _ {6}}{2} + \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {5} y _ {i}\right) = 0. 2 6 1 8 (0. 9 3 3 0 + 4. 6 7 2 2) \approx 1. 4 6 7 4.

$$
\left| R _ {n} \right| \leqslant \frac {\pi^ {3}}{8 \times 1 2 \times 6} \cdot \frac {\sqrt {3}}{6} <   2. 5 9 \times 1 0 ^ {- 3}.
