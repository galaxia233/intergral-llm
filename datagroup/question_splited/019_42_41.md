42.42 If $f(x, y, z) = x^{2}y + y^{2}z - 2xz$ , find $f_{xy}, f_{yx}, f_{xz}, f_{zx}, f_{yz}, f_{zy}$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} f _ {x} = 2 x y - 2 z, \quad f _ {y} = x ^ {2} + 2 y z, \quad f _ {z} = y ^ {2} - 2 x. \quad f _ {x y} = 2 x, \quad f _ {y x} = 2 x, \quad f _ {x z} = - 2, \quad f _ {z x} = - 2, \quad f _ {y z} = 2 y, \\ f _ {z y} = 2 y. \end{array}
$$
