33.5 Resolve $\mathbf{A} = (4, 3)$ into components $\mathbf{A}_1$ and $\mathbf{A}_2$ that are, respectively, parallel and perpendicular to $\mathbf{B} = (3, 1)$ .

From Problem 33.4 $c = (\mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B}) / |\mathbf{B}|^2 = [(4\cdot 3) + (3\cdot 1) / 10] = \frac{3}{2}.$ So, $\mathbf{A}_1 = c\mathbf{B} = \frac{3}{2} (3,1) = (\frac{9}{2},\frac{3}{2})$ and $\mathbf{A}_2 = \mathbf{A} - \mathbf{A}_1 = (4,3) - (\frac{9}{2},\frac{3}{2}) = (-\frac{1}{2},\frac{3}{2}).$
