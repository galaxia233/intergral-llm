【1820】 $\int x^{2}\sqrt{a^{2} + x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解题思路 首先，有 $x^{2}\sqrt{a^{2} + x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2} x(a^{2} + x^{2})^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}(a^{2} + x^{2}) = \frac{1}{3} x\mathrm{d}\left[(a^{2} + x^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}\right]$ ，使用分部积分
法。其次，将积分 $\int (a^2 +x^2)^{\frac{3}{2}}\mathrm{d}x$ 分成 $a^2\int (a^2 +x^2)^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}x$ 与 $\int x^{2}(a^{2} + x^{2})^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}x$ 两项，并利用 1786 题的结果.

解 $\int x^{2}\sqrt{a^{2} + x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int x(a^{2} + x^{2})^{\frac{1}{2}}\mathrm{d}(a^{2} + x^{2}) = \frac{1}{3}\int x\mathrm{d}\left[(a^{2} + x^{2})^{\frac{3}{2}}\right]$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{3} x \left(a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} - \frac {1}{3} \int \left(a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} d x \\ = \frac {1}{3} x \left(a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}\right) \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} - \frac {a ^ {2}}{3} \int \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} d x - \frac {1}{3} \int x ^ {2} \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} d x. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，得 $\int x^{2}\sqrt{a^{2} + x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{3}{4}\left[\frac{1}{3} x(a^{2} + x^{2})\sqrt{a^{2} + x^{2}} -\frac{a^{2}}{3}\int \sqrt{a^{2} + x^{2}}\mathrm{d}x\right]$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{4} x \left(a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}\right) \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} - \frac {a ^ {2}}{4} \left[ \frac {x}{2} \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{2} \ln \left(x + \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}}\right) \right] ^ {*}) + C \\ = \frac {x \left(2 x ^ {2} + a ^ {2}\right)}{8} \sqrt {a ^ {2} + x ^ {2}} - \frac {a ^ {4}}{8} \ln (x + \sqrt {x ^ {2} + a ^ {2}}) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用 1786 题的结果.
