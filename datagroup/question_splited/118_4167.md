【4167】证明： $\lim_{n\to \infty}\iint \limits_{\substack{x\leqslant n\\ y\leqslant n}}\sin (x^2 +y^2)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y = \pi ,$

然而 $\lim_{n\to \infty}\iint \limits_{x^2 -y^2\leqslant 2n\pi}\sin (x^2 +y^2)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y = 0$ （ $n$ 为正整数）.

证 利用极坐标，我们有

$$
\iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant 2 n \pi} \sin (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \theta \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2 \pi n}} r \sin r ^ {2} d r = \pi (1 - \cos 2 n \pi) = 0 \quad (n = 1, 2, \dots),
$$

故 $\lim_{n\to \infty}\iint \limits_{x^2 -y^2\leqslant 2n\pi}\sin (x^2 +y^2)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y = 0.$

但由对称性，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {\substack {| x | \leqslant n \\ | y | \leqslant n}} \sin (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = 4 \iint_ {\substack {| x | \leqslant n \\ | y | \leqslant n}} \sin (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {n} \mathrm {d} y \int_ {0} ^ {n} (\sin x ^ {2} \cos y ^ {2} + \cos x ^ {2} \sin y ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x \\ = 4 \left(\int_ {0} ^ {n} \cos y ^ {2} d y\right) \left(\int_ {0} ^ {n} \sin x ^ {2} d x\right) + 4 \left(\int_ {0} ^ {n} \cos x ^ {2} d x\right) \left(\int_ {0} ^ {n} \sin y ^ {2} d y\right) = 8 \left(\int_ {0} ^ {n} \cos x ^ {2} d x\right) \left(\int_ {0} ^ {n} \sin x ^ {2} d x\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

根据 3830 题的结果，可知 $\int_{0}^{\infty}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{\infty}\cos x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}},$

故 $\lim_{n\to \infty}\int_{0}^{n}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \lim_{n\to \infty}\int_{0}^{n}\cos x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}}.$

从而，得 $\lim_{n\to \infty}\iint \limits_{\substack{|x|\leqslant n\\ |y|\leqslant n}}\sin (x^2 +y^2)\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y = 8\cdot \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}}\cdot \frac{1}{2}\sqrt{\frac{\pi}{2}} = \pi .$
