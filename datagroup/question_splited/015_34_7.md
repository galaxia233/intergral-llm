34.49 Find the unit tangent vector for the circle $\mathbf{R} = (a\cos \theta, a\sin \theta)$ .

$\mathbf{R}^{\prime}(\theta) = (-a\sin \theta ,a\cos \theta).$ $|\mathbf{R}'(\theta)| = \sqrt{a^2\sin^2\theta + a^2\cos^2\theta} = a.$ Hence, $\mathbf{T} = \mathbf{R}'(\theta) / |\mathbf{R}'(\theta)| = (-\sin \theta ,\cos \theta)$
