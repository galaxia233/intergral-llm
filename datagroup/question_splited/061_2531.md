【2531】利用矩形公式 $(n = 12)$ ，近似地计算 $\int_0^{2\pi}x\sin x\mathrm{d}x$ 并把结果同精确答案进行比较.

解 $h = \frac{\pi}{6}$

$$
x _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 0;
$$

$$
x _ {1} = \frac {\pi}{6},
$$

$$
y _ {1} = \frac {\pi}{6} \sin \frac {\pi}{6} = 0. 2 6 1 8;
$$

$$
x _ {2} = \frac {\pi}{3}, \quad y _ {2} = \frac {\pi}{3} \sin \frac {\pi}{3} = 0. 9 0 6 9;
$$

$$
x _ {3} = \frac {\pi}{2},
$$

$$
y _ {3} = \frac {\pi}{2} \sin \frac {\pi}{2} = 1. 5 7 0 8;
$$

$$
x _ {4} = \frac {2 \pi}{3}, \quad y _ {4} = \frac {2 \pi}{3} \sin \frac {2 \pi}{3} = 1. 8 1 3 8;
$$

$$
x _ {5} = \frac {5 \pi}{6},
$$

$$
y _ {5} = \frac {5 \pi}{6} \sin \frac {5 \pi}{6} = 1. 3 0 9 0;
$$

$$
x _ {6} = \pi , \quad y _ {6} = \pi \sin \pi = 0;
$$

$$
x _ {7} = \frac {7 \pi}{6},
$$

$$
y _ {7} = \frac {7 \pi}{6} \sin \frac {7 \pi}{6} = - 1. 8 3 2 6;
$$

$$
x _ {8} = \frac {4 \pi}{3}, \quad y _ {8} = \frac {4 \pi}{3} \sin \frac {4 \pi}{3} = - 3. 6 2 7 6;
$$

$$
x _ {9} = \frac {3 \pi}{2},
$$

$$
y _ {9} = \frac {3 \pi}{2} \sin \frac {3 \pi}{2} = - 4. 7 1 2 4;
$$

$$
x _ {1 0} = \frac {5 \pi}{3}, \quad y _ {1 0} = \frac {5 \pi}{3} \sin \frac {5 \pi}{3} = - 4. 5 3 4 5;
$$

$$
x _ {1 1} = \frac {1 1 \pi}{6},
$$

$$
y _ {1 1} = \frac {1 1 \pi}{6} \sin \frac {1 1 \pi}{6} = - 2. 8 7 9 8.
$$

按矩形公式，得

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} x \sin x d x \approx \frac {\pi}{6} \left(y _ {0} + y _ {1} + y _ {2} + y _ {3} + y _ {4} + y _ {5} + y _ {6} + y _ {7} + y _ {8} + y _ {9} + y _ {1 0} + y _ {1 1}\right) \approx - 6. 1 3 9 0.
$$

实际上， $\int_0^{2\pi}x\sin x\mathrm{d}x = -x\cos x\Bigg|_0^{2\pi} + \int_0^{2\pi}\cos x\mathrm{d}x\approx -6.2832.$
