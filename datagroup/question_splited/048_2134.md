【2134】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[3]{x^2(1 - x)}}.$

提示 令 $\sqrt[3]{\frac{1 - x}{x}} = t$

解设 $\sqrt[3]{\frac{1 - x}{x}} = t$ ，则 $x = \frac{1}{t^3 + 1},\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{3t^2}{(t^3 + 1)^2}\mathrm{d}t.$ 代人得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt [ 3 ]{x ^ {2} (1 - x)}} = - 3 \int \frac {t}{t ^ {3} + 1} \mathrm {d} t = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{t + 1} - \int \frac {t + 1}{t ^ {2} - t + 1} \mathrm {d} t \\ = \ln | t + 1 | - \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {2 t - 1}{t ^ {2} - t + 1} d t - \frac {3}{2} \int \frac {d t}{t ^ {2} - t + 1} = \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {(t + 1) ^ {2}}{t ^ {2} - t + 1} - \sqrt {3} \arctan \left(\frac {2 t - 1}{\sqrt {3}}\right) + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $t = \sqrt[3]{\frac{1 - x}{x}}.$
