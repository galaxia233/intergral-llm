【1943】 $\int \frac{x^3}{\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $\int \frac{x^3}{\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}}\mathrm{d}x = (ax^2 +bx + c)\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2} +\lambda \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{1 + 2x - x^2}},$

两边对 $x$ 求导数，得

$$
\frac {x ^ {3}}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} = (2 a x + b) \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} + \frac {(a x ^ {2} + b x + c) (1 - x)}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} + \frac {\lambda}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}}.
$$

从而有

$$
x ^ {3} \equiv (2 a x + b) (1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}) + (a x ^ {2} + b x + c) (1 - x) + \lambda .
$$

比较等式两端 $x$ 的同次幂系数，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} x ^ {3} \left| - 3 a = 1, \right. \\ x ^ {2} \left| 5 a - 2 b = 0, \right. \\ x ^ {1} \left| 2 a + 3 b - c = 0, \right. \\ x ^ {0} \left| b + c + \lambda = 0. \right. \end{array}
$$

由此， $a = -\frac{1}{3},b = -\frac{5}{6},c = -\frac{19}{6},\lambda = 4.$ 于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {x ^ {3}}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} d x = - \frac {1 9 + 5 x + 2 x ^ {2}}{6} \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} + 4 \int \frac {d x}{\sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}}} \\ = - \frac {1 9 + 5 x + 2 x ^ {2}}{6} \sqrt {1 + 2 x - x ^ {2}} + 4 \arcsin \left(\frac {x - 1}{\sqrt {2}}\right) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
