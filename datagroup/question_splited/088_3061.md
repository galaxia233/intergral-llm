【3061】 $\prod_{n=3}^{\infty} \frac{n^{2}-4}{n^{2}-1}$ .

证 由于部分乘积

$$
P _ {n} = \frac {1 \cdot 5}{2 \cdot 4} \cdot \frac {2 \cdot 6}{3 \cdot 5} \cdot \frac {3 \cdot 7}{4 \cdot 6} \dots \frac {(n - 4) n}{(n - 3) (n - 1)} \cdot \frac {(n - 3) (n + 1)}{(n - 2) n} \cdot \frac {(n - 2) (n + 2)}{(n - 1) (n + 1)} = \frac {n + 2}{4 (n - 1)} \rightarrow \frac {1}{4} \quad (\text {当} n \rightarrow \infty \text {时}),
$$

故无穷乘积 $\prod_{n=3}^{\infty} \frac{n^{2}-4}{n^{2}-1}$ 收敛，且其值为 $\frac{1}{4}$ .
