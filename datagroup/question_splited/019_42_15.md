42.15 If $z$ is defined as a function of $x$ and $y$ by $xy - yz + xz = 0$ , find $\partial z / \partial x$ and $\partial z / \partial y$ .

By implicit differentiation with respect to $x$ ,

$$
y - y \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} + x \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} + z = 0 \quad \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} (x - y) = - (y + z) \quad \frac {\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac {y + z}{y - x}
$$

By implicit differentiation with respect to $y$ ,

$$
x - \left(y \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} + z\right) + x \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = 0 \quad \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} (x - y) = z - x \quad \frac {\partial z}{\partial y} = \frac {z - x}{x - y}
$$
