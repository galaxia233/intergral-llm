34.10 Sketch the curve with the parametric equations $x = x_0 + at, y = y_0 + bt,$ where $a$ and $b$ are not both 0.

$bx = bx_{0} + abt, ay = ay_{0} + abt.$ Subtracting the second equation from the first, we get $bx - ay = bx_{0} - ay_{0}$ . This is a line through the point $(x_0,y_0)$ and parallel to the vector $(a,b)$ , since $(x,y) - (x_0,y_0) = t(a,b)$ . See Fig. 34-10.
