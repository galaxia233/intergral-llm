26.29 A bacterial culture, growing exponentially, increases from 200 to 500 grams in the period from 6 a.m. to 9 a.m. How many grams will be present at noon?

$y = 200e^{Kt}$ , where $t = 0$ at 6 a.m. Then, $500 = 200e^{3K}$ , $e^{3K} = \frac{5}{2}$ . At noon, $t = 6$ and $y = 200e^{6K} = 200(e^{3K})^2 = 200(\frac{5}{2})^2 = 1250$ grams.
