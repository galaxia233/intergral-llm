32.39 Find the volume of the solid obtained by revolving the region of Problem 32.38 about the $x$ -axis.

By the disk formula, $V = \pi \int_{0}^{\infty}(e^{-2x})^{2}dx = \pi \int_{0}^{\infty}e^{-4x}dx = \pi \lim_{v\to +\infty}\int_{0}^{v}e^{-4x}dx = \pi \lim_{v\to +\infty}\left(-\frac{1}{4} e^{-4x}\right)\Big|_{0}^{v} = -(\pi /4)\lim_{v\to +\infty}\left(e^{-4v} - 1\right) = \pi /4.$
