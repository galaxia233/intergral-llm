【2235】求 $\lim_{x\to +0}\frac{\int_{0}^{\sin x}\sqrt{\tan x}\mathrm{d}x}{\int_{0}^{\tan x}\sqrt{\sin x}\mathrm{d}x}.$

解 $\lim_{x\to +0}\frac{\int_{0}^{\sin x}\sqrt{\tan x}\mathrm{d}x}{\int_{0}^{\tan x}\sqrt{\sin x}\mathrm{d}x} = \lim_{x\to +0}\frac{\sqrt{\tan(\sin x)}(\sin x)^{\prime}}{\sqrt{\sin(\tan x)}(\tan x)^{\prime}} = \lim_{x\to +0}\sqrt{\frac{\tan(\sin x)}{\sin x}\frac{\sin x}{\tan x}\frac{\tan x}{\sin x(\tan x)}}\lim_{x\to +0}\cos^{3}x = 1.$
