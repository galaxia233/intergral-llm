【4145】 $\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2} = \frac{z^2}{c^2},z = c.$

解 若令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = brsin\varphi$ ，则有

$$
I _ {x y} = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} a b r d r \int_ {\sigma} ^ {e} z ^ {2} d z = \frac {1}{5} \pi a b c ^ {3},
$$

$$
I _ {y z} = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} a b r d r \int_ {\sigma} ^ {c} (a r \cos \varphi) ^ {2} d z = a ^ {3} b c \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \cos^ {2} \varphi d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} (1 - r) r ^ {3} d r = \frac {1}{2 0} \pi a ^ {3} b c.
$$

利用对称性可得 $I_{zr} = \frac{1}{20}\pi ab^3 c.$
