【1797】 $\int x^{3} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} \mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $x^{3} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{3}} \mathrm{d} x = -\frac{1}{2} x^{2} \mathrm{~d}\left(\mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}}\right)$ .

解 $\int x^{3} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} \mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{2} \int x^{2} \mathrm{~d}\left(\mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}}\right) = -\frac{1}{2} x^{2} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} + \frac{1}{2} \int \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} \mathrm{~d}\left(x^{2}\right) = -\frac{x^{2} + 1}{2} \mathrm{e}^{-x^{2}} + C.$
