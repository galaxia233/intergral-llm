【2478】 $x^{2} - xy + y^{2} = a^{2}$ 绕 $Ox$ 轴.

解 原方程即 $y^{2} - xy + x^{2} - a^{2} = 0$ ，从而，

$$
y = \frac {x \pm \sqrt {4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}}}{2},
$$

函数的定义域为 $\left[-\frac{2}{\sqrt{3}} a, \frac{2}{\sqrt{3}} a\right]$ . 与 $Ox$ 轴的交点分别为 $x = -a$ 与 $x = a$ . 于是，所求的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V _ {x} = 2 \left\{\pi \int_ {0} ^ {a} \frac {1}{4} (x + \sqrt {4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}}) ^ {2} d x + \pi \int_ {a} ^ {\frac {2}{3} a} \left[ \frac {1}{4} (x + \sqrt {4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}}) ^ {2} - \frac {1}{4} (x - \sqrt {4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}}) ^ {2} \right] d x \right\} \\ = \frac {\pi}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {a} (4 a ^ {2} - 2 x ^ {2} + 2 x \sqrt {4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}}) d x + 2 \pi \int_ {a} ^ {\frac {2}{3} a} x \sqrt {4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}} d x \\ = \pi \left[ 2 a ^ {3} - \frac {1}{3} a ^ {3} - \frac {1}{9} (4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \left| _ {0} ^ {a} - \frac {2}{9} (4 a ^ {2} - 3 x ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \right| _ {a} ^ {\frac {2}{\sqrt {3}} a} \right] = \frac {8}{3} \pi a ^ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$
