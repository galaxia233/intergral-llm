30.30 $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt[3]{x} + \sqrt{x}}.$   
Let $x = z^6$ . (In general, let $x = z^m$ , where $m$ is the least common multiple of the radicals.) $dx = 6z^5 dz$ . Then $\int_{\sqrt[3]{x} + \sqrt{x}} dx = \int_{\sqrt{z^2 + z^3}} 6 \int_{\frac{z^3}{1 + z}} 6 \int_{\frac{z^3}{1 + z}} 6 \left( \frac {1}{3} z ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2} z ^ {2} + z - \ln |1 + z| \right) + C = 2\sqrt{x} - 3\sqrt[3]{x} + \sqrt[6]{x} - \ln (1 + \sqrt[6]{x}) + C.$
