【1927】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x(1 + 2\sqrt{x} + \sqrt[3]{x})}$

解设 $\sqrt[6]{x} = t$ ，则 $x = t^6$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = 6t^{5}\mathrm{dt}$ .代入得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} r}{x (1 + 2 \sqrt {x} + \sqrt [ 3 ]{x})} = 6 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{t (1 + 2 t ^ {3} + t ^ {2})} = 6 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{t (1 + t) (2 t ^ {2} - t + 1)} = 6 \int \left[ \frac {1}{t} - \frac {1}{4 (1 + t)} - \frac {6 t - 1}{4 (2 t ^ {2} - t + 1)} \right] \mathrm {d} t \\ = 6 \left\{\ln t - \frac {1}{4} \ln | 1 + t | - \frac {3}{8} \int \frac {4 t - 1}{2 t ^ {2} - t + 1} d t - \frac {1}{1 6} \int \frac {d (t - \frac {1}{4})}{(t - \frac {1}{4}) ^ {2} + \frac {7}{1 6}} \right\} \\ = 6 \left\{\ln | t | - \frac {1}{4} \ln | 1 + t | - \frac {3}{8} \ln (2 t ^ {2} - t + 1) - \frac {1}{4 \sqrt {7}} \arctan \frac {4 t - 1}{\sqrt {7}} \right\} + C \\ = \frac {3}{4} \ln \frac {t ^ {8}}{(1 + t) ^ {2} (2 t ^ {2} - t + 1) ^ {3}} - \frac {3}{2 \sqrt {7}} \arctan \frac {4 t - 1}{\sqrt {7}} + C \\ = \frac {3}{4} \ln \frac {x \sqrt [ 3 ]{x}}{(1 + \sqrt [ 6 ]{x}) ^ {2} (2 \sqrt [ 3 ]{x} - \sqrt [ 6 ]{x} + 1) ^ {3}} - \frac {3}{2 \sqrt {7}} \arctan \frac {4 \sqrt [ 6 ]{x} - 1}{\sqrt {7}} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
