【3903】用一组顶点 $A_{ij}$ 位于整数点的正方形作为积分域的近似域，并取被积函数在每个正方形距原点最远的顶点之值，近似地计算积分 $\iint_{r^2 + y^2 \leqslant 25} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{24 + x^2 + y^2}}$ 并与精确值加以比较.

解 由题意知，应取的正方形顶点为 $(1,1),(1,2),(1,3),(1,4),(2,1),(2,2),(2,3),(2,4),(3,1),(3,2),(3,3),(3,4),(4,1),(4,2),(4,3)$ ，故利用对称性知

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{4} \iint_ {r ^ {2} \cdot y ^ {2} \cdot 2 5} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{\sqrt {2 4 + x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} \approx \frac {1}{\sqrt {2 6}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {2 9}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {3 4}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {4 1}} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {3 2}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {3 7}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {4 4}} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {4 2}} + \frac {2}{\sqrt {4 9}} \\ \approx 0. 1 9 6 + 0. 3 7 1 + 0. 3 4 3 + 0. 3 1 2 + 0. 1 7 7 + 0. 3 2 9 + 0. 3 0 2 + 0. 1 5 4 + 0. 2 8 5 \approx 2. 4 7 0, \\ \end{array}
$$

即 $\iint_{x^2 + y^2 < 0.25} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{24 + x^2 + y^2}} \approx 9.880.$

下面计算积分的精确值：

$$
\iint_ {t ^ {2} \cdot y ^ {2} \cdot 2 5} \frac {\mathrm {d} r \mathrm {d} y}{\sqrt {2 4 + x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {3} \ln (y + \sqrt {2 4 + x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}) \left| _ {0} ^ {\sqrt {2 5 - x ^ {2}}} \mathrm {d} x = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {5} \ln (\sqrt {2 5 - x ^ {2}} + 7) \mathrm {d} x - 2 \int_ {0} ^ {5} \ln (2 4 + x ^ {2}) \mathrm {d} x. \right.
$$

由于 $\int \ln (24 + x^2)\mathrm{d}x = x\ln (24 + x^2) - \int \frac{2x^2}{24 + x^2}\mathrm{d}x = x\ln (24 + x^2) - 2x + \frac{24}{\sqrt{6}}\arctan \frac{x}{\sqrt{24}} +C.$

从而， $2\int_{0}^{5}\ln (24 + x^{2})\mathrm{d}x = \left[2x\ln (24 + x^{2}) - 4x + \frac{48}{\sqrt{6}}\arctan \frac{x}{\sqrt{24}}\right]\Bigg|_{0}^{5} = 20\ln 7 - 20 + 8\sqrt{6}\arctan \frac{5}{\sqrt{24}};$

又 $4\int_{0}^{5}\ln (\sqrt{25 - x^2} +7)\mathrm{d}x = 4[x\ln (\sqrt{25 - x^2} +7)]\Big|_{0}^{5} + 4\int_{0}^{5}\frac{x^2\mathrm{d}x}{(\sqrt{25 - x^2} + 7)\sqrt{25 - x^2}}$

$$
= 2 0 \ln 7 + 4 \int_ {0} ^ {5} \frac {x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} x}{(\sqrt {2 5 - x ^ {2}} + 7) \sqrt {2 5 - x ^ {2}}},
$$

再令 $x = 5\sin t$ ，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {5} \frac {x ^ {2} \mathrm {d} x}{(\sqrt {2 5 - x ^ {2}} + 7) \sqrt {2 5 - x ^ {2}}} = \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {- 2 5 \cos^ {2} t + 2 5}{5 \cos t + 7} \mathrm {d} t = - \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (5 \cos t - 7) \mathrm {d} t - \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {2 4}{5 \cos t + 7} \mathrm {d} t \\ = (7 t - 5 \sin t) \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} - 2 4 \left[ \frac {1}{\sqrt {6}} \arctan \left(\frac {1}{\sqrt {6}} \tan \frac {t}{2}\right) \right] \right| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} = \frac {7 \pi}{2} - 5 - 4 \sqrt {6} \arctan \frac {2}{\sqrt {2 4}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

从而， $4\int_{0}^{5}\ln (\sqrt{25 - x^2} +7)\mathrm{d}x = 20\ln 7 + 14\pi -20 - 16\sqrt{6}\arctan \frac{2}{\sqrt{24}}.$

注意到 $2\arctan \frac{2}{\sqrt{24}} + \arctan \frac{5}{\sqrt{24}} = \frac{\pi}{2}$ ,

最后便得到 $\iint_{r^2 - y^2 \leqslant 25} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{24 + x^2 + y^2}} = 14\pi - 4\sqrt{24}\left(2\arctan \frac{2}{\sqrt{24}} + \arctan \frac{5}{\sqrt{24}}\right) = 2\pi (7 - \sqrt{24}) \approx 13.19.$

将精确值与近似值作比较，显见误差较大，其原因在于有不少不是正方形的区域都被忽略，因而产生较大的绝对误差4.31及较大的相对误差 $\frac{4.31}{13.19} \approx 32.7\%$

注意求 $\iint_{x^2 + y^2 \leqslant 25} \frac{\mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{24 + x^2 + y^2}}$ 的精确值若采用极坐标则较为简单：

$$
\iint_ {r ^ {2} + x ^ {2} \leqslant 2 5} \frac {\mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y}{\sqrt {2 4 + x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}} = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \mathrm {d} \theta \int_ {0} ^ {5} \frac {r \mathrm {d} r}{\sqrt {2 4 + r ^ {2}}} = 2 \pi (7 - \sqrt {2 4}).
$$

但按原习题集的安排，似应在3937题以后才开始使用极坐标，故本题仍用直角坐标进行计算.
