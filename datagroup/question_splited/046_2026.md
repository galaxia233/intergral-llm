【2026】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(2 + \cos x)\sin x}$

解设 $t = \tan \frac{x}{2}$ ，同2025题，得

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{(2 + \cos x) \sin x} = \int \frac {1 + t ^ {2}}{t (3 + t ^ {2})} \mathrm {d} t = \int \left[ \frac {1}{3 t} + \frac {2 t}{3 (3 + t ^ {2})} \right] \mathrm {d} t = \frac {1}{3} \ln | t (3 + t ^ {2}) | + C _ {1}
$$

$\text{串}$ 由于 $t(3 + t^2) = \tan \frac{x}{2}\left(2 + \sec^2\frac{x}{2}\right) = \frac{\sin\frac{x}{2}}{\cos^3\frac{x}{2}}\left(1 + 2\cos^2\frac{x}{2}\right)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{6} \ln \frac {(1 - \cos x) (2 + \cos x) ^ {2}}{(1 + \cos x) ^ {3}} + C. \\ = \frac {\left(\frac {1 - \cos x}{2}\right) ^ {\frac {1}{2}}}{\left(\frac {1 + \cos x}{2}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} (\cos x + 2) = 2 \left[ \frac {(1 - \cos x) (\cos x + 2) ^ {2}}{(1 + \cos x) ^ {3}} \right] ^ {\frac {1}{2}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

因而， $\ln |t(3 + t^2)| = \ln 2 + \frac{1}{2}\ln \frac{(1 - \cos x)(2 + \cos x)^2}{(1 + \cos x)^3}.$
