【2991】 $\frac{1}{1\cdot 2\cdot 3} +\frac{1}{3\cdot 4\cdot 5} +\frac{1}{5\cdot 6\cdot 7} +\dots .$

解 由 $\frac{1}{(2n - 1)2n(2n + 1)} = \frac{1}{2}\left[\frac{1}{(2n - 1)2n} -\frac{1}{2n(2n + 1)}\right]$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{1 \cdot 2 \cdot 3} + \frac {1}{3 \cdot 4 \cdot 5} + \dots + \frac {1}{(2 n - 1) 2 n (2 n + 1)} + \dots \\ = \frac {1}{2} \left[ \frac {1}{1 \cdot 2} - \frac {1}{2 \cdot 3} + \frac {1}{3 \cdot 4} - \frac {1}{5 \cdot 6} + \dots + \frac {1}{(2 n - 1) 2 n} - \frac {1}{2 n (2 n + 1)} + \dots \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2} (2 \ln 2 - 1) ^ {*}) = \ln 2 - \frac {1}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 注意原级数的绝对收敛性，并利用2988题的结果.
