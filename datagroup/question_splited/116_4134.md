【4134】 $z = x^{2} + y^{2},x + y = a,x = 0,y = 0,z = 0.$

解 物体的质量为 $M = \int_{0}^{a}\mathrm{d}x\int_{0}^{a - x}\mathrm{d}y\int_{0}^{x^2 +y^2}\mathrm{d}z = \frac{1}{6} a^4.$

质心的横坐标为 $x_0 = \frac{1}{M}\int_0^a x\mathrm{d}x\int_0^{a - x}\mathrm{d}y\int_0^{x^2 +y^2}\mathrm{d}z = \frac{6}{a^4}\cdot \frac{a^5}{15} = \frac{2a}{5},$

同理可求得 $y_0 = \frac{2a}{5}$ 而

$$
\begin{array}{l} z _ {0} = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {a} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {0} ^ {a - x} \mathrm {d} y \int_ {0} ^ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} z \mathrm {d} z = \frac {1}{M} \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left(\frac {a ^ {5}}{1 0} - \frac {1}{2} a ^ {4} x + \frac {4}{3} a ^ {3} x ^ {2} - 2 a ^ {2} x ^ {3} + 2 a x ^ {4} - \frac {1 4}{1 5} x ^ {5}\right) \mathrm {d} x \\ = \frac {6}{a ^ {4}} \cdot \frac {7}{1 8 0} a ^ {6} = \frac {7}{3 0} a ^ {2}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，质心的坐标为 $x_0 = y_0 = \frac{2}{5} a, z_0 = \frac{7}{30} a^2.$
