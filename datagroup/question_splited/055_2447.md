【2447】 $r = ae^{mp}$ （ $m > 0$ ）当 $0 < r < a$ .

解 $0 < r < a, -\infty < \varphi < 0$ 所求的弧长为

$$
s = \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} \sqrt {a ^ {2} e ^ {2 m _ {\varphi}} + a ^ {2} m ^ {2} e ^ {2 m _ {\varphi}}} d \varphi = a \sqrt {m ^ {2} + 1} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {0} e ^ {m _ {\varphi}} d \varphi = \frac {a \sqrt {1 + m ^ {2}}}{m}.
$$
