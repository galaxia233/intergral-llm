42.108 Find an equation of the tangent plane to the surface $z = 2x^{2} - y^{2}$ at the point $(1,1,1)$ .

$\partial z / \partial x = 4x = 4, \quad \partial z / \partial y = -2y = -2.$ Hence, a normal vector to the tangent plane is $(4, -2, -1)$ , and an equation of the plane is $4(x - 1) - 2(y - 1) - (z - 1) = 0$ , or, equivalently, $4x - 2y - z = 1$ .
