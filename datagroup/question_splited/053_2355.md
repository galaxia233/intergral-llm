【2355】证明等式： $\int_{0}^{+\infty}f\left(ax + \frac{b}{x}\right)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{a}\int_{0}^{+\infty}f(\sqrt{x^2 + 4ab})\mathrm{d}x.$

其中 $a > 0, b > 0$ （假定等式左端的积分有意义）

证设 $ax - \frac{b}{x} = t$ ，则当 $0 < x < +\infty$ 时， $-\infty < t < +\infty$ ， $ax + \frac{b}{x} = \sqrt{t^2 + 4ab}$

将此二式相加得 $x = \frac{1}{2a} (t + \sqrt{t^2 + 4ab}).$

从而有 $\mathrm{dx} = \frac{1}{2a}\frac{t + \sqrt{t^2 + 4ab}}{\sqrt{t^2 + 4ab}}\mathrm{dt}.$

代入要证的等式左端，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} \left(a x + \frac {b}{x}\right) d x = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {+ \infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) \frac {t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}} d t \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {- \infty} ^ {\infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) \frac {t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}} d t + \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) \frac {t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}} d t \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) \frac {\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b} - t}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}} d t + \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) \frac {t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}} d t \\ = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) \frac {\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b} - t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b} + t}{\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}} d t \\ = \frac {1}{a} \int_ {0} ^ {+ \infty} f (\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 4 a b}) d t, \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\int_0^{+\infty}f\left(ax + \frac{b}{x}\right)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{a}\int_0^{+\infty}f(\sqrt{x^2 + 4ab})\mathrm{d}x.$
