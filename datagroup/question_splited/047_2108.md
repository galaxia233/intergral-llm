【2108】 $\int \arcsin \sqrt{x} \, \mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \arcsin \sqrt{x} \, \mathrm{d}x = x \arcsin \sqrt{x} - \frac{1}{2} \int \frac{\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{1 - x}} \, \mathrm{d}x.$

对于积分 $\int \frac{\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{1 - x}}\mathrm{d}x$ ，设 $\sqrt{x} = t$ ，则 $\mathrm{dx} = 2t\mathrm{dt}$ ，于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\sqrt {x}}{\sqrt {1 - x}} d x = 2 \int \frac {t ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}}} d t = - 2 \int \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} d t + 2 \int \frac {d t}{\sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}}} \\ = - t \sqrt {1 - t ^ {2}} - \arcsin t + 2 \arcsin t + C _ {1} = \arcsin \sqrt {x} - \sqrt {x - x ^ {2}} + C _ {1}. \\ \end{array}
$$

因而， $\int \arcsin \sqrt{x}\mathrm{d}x = \left(x - \frac{1}{2}\right)\arcsin \sqrt{x} +\frac{1}{2}\sqrt{x - x^2} +C.$
