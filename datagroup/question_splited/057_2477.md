【2477】 $x^{2} + (y - b)^{2} = a^{2}$ $(0 < a\leqslant b)$ 绕 $Ox$ 轴.

解 $y_{1} = b + \sqrt{a^{2} - x^{2}}, y_{2} = b - \sqrt{a^{2} - x^{2}} (-a \leqslant x \leqslant a)$ . 所求的体积为

$$
V _ {x} = \pi \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \left(y _ {1} ^ {2} - y _ {2} ^ {2}\right) d x = 8 b \pi \int_ {0} ^ {a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = 2 \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} b.
$$
