【4035】 $\left(\frac{x}{a} +\frac{y}{b}\right)^n +\left(\frac{z}{c}\right)^m = 1,x = 0,y = 0,z = 0(n > 0,m > 0).$

提示 令 $x = \arccos^2\varphi, y = brsin^2\varphi (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2})$

解 令 $x = \arccos^2\varphi, y = brsin^2\varphi (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2})$ ，则曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = c \iint_ {\Omega} \sqrt [ m ]{1 - \left(\frac {x}{a} + \frac {y}{b}\right) ^ {n}} d x d y = 2 a b c \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt [ m ]{1 - r ^ {n}} r d r \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos \varphi \sin \varphi d \varphi = a b c \int_ {0} ^ {1} \sqrt [ m ]{1 - r ^ {n}} r d r \\ = \frac {a b c}{n} B \left(\frac {1}{m} + 1, \frac {2}{n}\right) = \frac {a b c}{n} \frac {\Gamma \left(\frac {1}{m} + 1\right) \Gamma \left(\frac {2}{n}\right)}{\Gamma \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {2}{n} + 1\right)} = \frac {a b c}{n + 2 m} \frac {\Gamma \left(\frac {1}{m}\right) \Gamma \left(\frac {2}{n}\right)}{\Gamma \left(\frac {1}{m} + \frac {2}{n}\right)}. \\ \end{array}
$$
