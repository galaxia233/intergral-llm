26.25 If the population of Latin America has a doubling time of 27 years, by what percent does it grow per year?

The population $y = y_0 e^{Kt}$ . By the given information, $2y_0 = y_0 e^{27K}, \quad 2 = e^{27K}, \quad e^K = \sqrt[27]{2} \approx 1.0234$ . By the solution to Problem 26.6, the percentage increase per year $r = 100(e^K - 1) \approx 100(1.0234 - 1) = 2.34$ .
