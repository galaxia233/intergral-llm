45.4 Find a tangent vector, the tangent line, the speed, and the normal plane to the helical curve $\mathbf{R}(t) = (a\cos 2\pi t,a\sin 2\pi t,bt)$ at $t = 1$

A tangent vector is $\mathbf{R}'(t) = (-2\pi a\sin 2\pi t, 2\pi a\cos 2\pi t, b) = (0, 2\pi a, b)$ . The tangent line is $(a, 0, b) + u(0, 2\pi a, b)$ . The speed is $|\mathbf{R}'(t)| = \sqrt{4\pi^2a^2 + b^2}$ . The normal plane has the equation $(0)(x - a) + (2\pi a)(y - 0) + (b)(z - b) = 0$ , or equivalently, $2\pi ay + bz = b^2$ .
