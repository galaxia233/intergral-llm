【4168】证明：尽管累次积分

$$
\int_ {1} ^ {\cdot \cdot \cdot} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {1} ^ {\cdot \cdot \cdot} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} y \quad \text {及} \quad \int_ {1} ^ {\cdot \cdot \cdot} \mathrm {d} y \int_ {1} ^ {\cdot \cdot \cdot} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} x
$$

收敛，但积分 $\iint_{x\geqslant 1,y\geqslant 1}\frac{x^2 - y^2}{(x^2 + y^2)^2}\mathrm{d}x\mathrm{d}y$

发散.

证 先证两个累次积分收敛. 我们有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} d y = \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 y} \cdot \frac {2 y d y}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} - \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {y}{2} \cdot \frac {2 y d y}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \\ = - \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 y \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right)} \left| _ {y = 1} ^ {y = - \infty} - \int_ {1} ^ {- \infty} \frac {x ^ {2} d y}{2 y ^ {2} \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right)} + \frac {y}{2 \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right)} \right| _ {y = 1} ^ {y = - \infty} - \int_ {1} ^ {- \infty} \frac {d y}{2 \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right)} \\ = \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 \left(x ^ {2} + 1\right)} - \frac {1}{2} \int_ {1} ^ {\infty} \left(\frac {1}{y ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}\right) d y - \frac {1}{2 \left(x ^ {2} + 1\right)} - \frac {1}{2} \int_ {1} ^ {\infty} \frac {d y}{x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}} = \frac {x ^ {2} - 1}{2 \left(x ^ {2} + 1\right)} - \frac {1}{2} = - \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 1}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故 $\int_{1}^{+\infty}\mathrm{d}x\int_{1}^{+\infty}\frac{x^2 - y^2}{(x^2 + y^2)^2}\mathrm{d}y = -\int_{1}^{+\infty}\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^2 + 1} = -\frac{\pi}{4};$

同理（利用已算得的结果）

$$
\int_ {1} ^ {- \infty} d y \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} d x = - \int_ {1} ^ {- \infty} d y \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {y ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} d x = - \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \left(- \frac {1}{y ^ {2} + 1}\right) d y = \frac {\pi}{4},
$$

故两个累次积分都收敛.

次证积分

$$
\iint_ {x \geqslant 1, y \geqslant 1} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} d x d y \tag {1}
$$

发散. 为此只要证积分

$$
\iint_ {r \geqslant 1. 1 \leqslant y \leqslant r} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} d. r d y \tag {2}
$$

发散即可（因为如果积分 (1) 收敛，则绝对值积分

$$
\iint_ {x \geqslant 1, y \geqslant 1} \left| \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \right| d x d y \tag {3}
$$

必收敛. 从而，在小一点的区域上的积分

$$
\iint_ {x \geqslant 1, 1 \leqslant y \leqslant r} \left| \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \right| d x d y
$$

更收敛. 由此可知，积分 (2) 收敛). 由于

$$
I _ {n} = \iint_ {\substack {1 \leqslant x \leqslant n \\ 1 \leqslant y \leqslant r}} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {1} ^ {n} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {1} ^ {x} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} y,
$$

仿上，利用部分积分法，容易算得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {1} ^ {x} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} d y = - \frac {x ^ {2}}{2 y (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} \Bigg | _ {y - 1} ^ {y - r} - \int_ {1} ^ {x} \frac {x ^ {2} d y}{2 y ^ {2} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} + \frac {y}{2 (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} \Bigg | _ {y - 1} ^ {y = x} - \int_ {1} ^ {r} \frac {d y}{2 (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} \\ = - \frac {1}{x ^ {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{2 x}, \\ \end{array}
$$

故 $I_{n} = \int_{1}^{n}\left(-\frac{1}{x^{2} + 1} +\frac{1}{2x}\right)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi}{4} -\arctan n + \frac{1}{2}\ln n\rightarrow +\infty (n\rightarrow \infty),$

由此可知积分（2）发散

注意，也可用反证法证明积分 (1) 发散。假定积分 (1) 收敛。于是，积分 (3) 收敛。但恒有

$$
\iint_ {x \neq 1, y \geqslant 1} \left| \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \right| d x d y = \int_ {1} ^ {\infty} d x \int_ {1} ^ {- \infty} \left| \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \right| d y = \int_ {1} ^ {\infty} d y \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \left| \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) ^ {2}} \right| d x. \tag {4}
$$

故 (4) 式中两个累次积分都收敛。又由前面已证不取绝对值的两个累次积分

$$
\int_ {1} ^ {\cdot \cdot \cdot}   \mathrm {d} x   \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty}   \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} y \quad \text {与} \quad \int_ {1} ^ {\cdot \cdot \cdot}   \mathrm {d} y   \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty}   \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} x
$$

都收敛，故知 $\iint_{C} \frac{x^2 - y^2}{(x^2 + y^2)^2} \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y = \int_{1}^{+\infty} \, \mathrm{d}x \int_{1}^{+\infty} \frac{x^2 - y^2}{(x^2 + y^2)^2} \, \mathrm{d}y = -\frac{\pi}{4},$

$$
\iint_ {x \geqslant 1, y \geqslant 1} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} d x d y = \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} d y \int_ {1} ^ {+ \infty} \frac {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}{(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) ^ {2}} d x = \frac {\pi}{4},
$$

这是不可能的。证毕.
