【2242】+ $\int_{\frac{1}{e}}^{e}|\lg x|\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 将区间 $\left[\frac{1}{e}, e\right]$ 分成 $\left[\frac{1}{e}, 1\right]$ 及 $\left[1, \frac{1}{e}\right]$ .

解 $\int_{\frac{1}{e}}^{e}|\lg x|\mathrm{d}x = \int_{\frac{1}{e}}^{1}(-\lg x)\mathrm{d}x + \int_{1}^{e}\lg x\mathrm{d}x = \left(-x\lg x\Bigg{|}_{\frac{1}{e}}^{1} + \int_{\frac{1}{e}}^{1}\frac{1}{\ln 10}\mathrm{d}x\right) + x\lg x\Bigg{|}_{1}^{e} - \int_{1}^{e}\frac{1}{\ln 10}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= 2 \left(1 - \frac {1}{e}\right) l g e.
$$
