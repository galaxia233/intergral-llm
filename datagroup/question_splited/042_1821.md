【1821】 $\int x\sin^2 x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x\sin^2 x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int x(1 - \cos 2x)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int x\mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{2}\int x\cos 2x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{4} x^2 -\frac{1}{4}\int x\mathrm{d}(\sin 2x)$

$$
= \frac {1}{4} x ^ {2} - \frac {1}{4} x \sin 2 x + \frac {1}{4} \int \sin 2 x d x = \frac {1}{4} x ^ {2} - \frac {x}{4} \sin 2 x - \frac {1}{8} \cos 2 x + C.
