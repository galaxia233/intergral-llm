【2188】 $\int_0^x\cos t\mathrm{d}t.$

解 将区间 $[0, x]n$ 等分，得 $h = \frac{x}{n}$ 。取 $\xi_i = ih (i = 0, 1, \dots, n-1)$ 。与 2187 题类似，可得

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {i = 0} ^ {n - 1} h \cos i h = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {h}{2 \sin \frac {h}{2}} \left(\sin \frac {h}{2} + \sin \frac {2 n - 1}{2} h\right) = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {\frac {h}{2}}{\sin \frac {h}{2}} \left[ \sin \frac {x}{2 n} + \sin \frac {(2 n - 1) x}{2 n} \right] = \sin x,
$$

即 $\int_0^x\cos t\mathrm{d}t = \sin x.$
