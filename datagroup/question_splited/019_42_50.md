42.51 If $z = g(x)h(y)$ , show that $\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \frac{\partial z}{\partial y}$ .

$\partial z / \partial x = g'(x)h(y), \quad \partial z / \partial y = g(x)h'(y), \quad \partial^2 z / (\partial x \partial y) = g'(x)h'(y). \quad \text{So}, \quad z \frac{\partial^2z}{\partial x \partial y} = g(x)h(y)g'(x)h'(y),$ while $\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} \frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = g'(x)h(y)g(x)h'(y)$ .
