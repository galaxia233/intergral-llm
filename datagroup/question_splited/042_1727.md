【1727】 $\int \frac{x^2}{(1 - x)^{100}} dx.$

提示 注意 $x^{2} = [(x - 1) + 1]^{2}$ .

解 $\int \frac{x^2}{(1 - x)^{100}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{(x - 1 + 1)^2}{(1 - x)^{100}}\mathrm{d}x = \int [(1 - x)^{-98} - 2(1 - x)^{-99} + (1 - x)^{-100}]\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{9 7 (1 - x) ^ {9 7}} - \frac {1}{4 9 (1 - x) ^ {9 8}} + \frac {1}{9 9 (1 - x) ^ {9 9}} + C.
