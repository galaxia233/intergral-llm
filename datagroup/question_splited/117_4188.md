【4188】 $\int_0^a\mathrm{d}x\int_0^x\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{(a - x)(x - y)}} (a > 0).$

解 $\int_{0}^{a}\mathrm{d}x\int_{0}^{x}\frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\sqrt{(a - x)(x - y)}} = \int_{0}^{a}\frac{2\sqrt{x}}{\sqrt{a - x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

作变量代换 $x = au$ ，则

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {u} \frac {2 \sqrt {x}}{\sqrt {a - x}} d x = 2 a \int_ {0} ^ {1} u ^ {\frac {1}{2}} (1 - u) ^ {- \frac {1}{2}} d u = 2 a B \left(\frac {3}{2}, \frac {1}{2}\right) = 2 a \frac {\Gamma \left(\frac {3}{2}\right) \Gamma \left(\frac {1}{2}\right)}{\Gamma (2)} = 2 a \cdot \frac {1}{2} \left[ \Gamma \left(\frac {1}{2}\right) \right] ^ {2} = \pi a.
$$
