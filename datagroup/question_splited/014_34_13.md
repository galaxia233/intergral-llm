34.15 Sketch the curve given by the parametric equations $x = \cosh t, y = \sinh t$ .

We know that $\cosh^2 t - \sinh^2 t = 1$ . Hence, we have $x^2 - y^2 = 1$ . Since $x = \cosh t > 0$ , we have only one branch of the hyperbola (Fig. 34-11).
