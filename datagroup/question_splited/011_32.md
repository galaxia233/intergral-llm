32.1 Determine whether the area in the first quadrant under the curve $y = 1 / x$ , for $x \geq 1$ , is finite.

This is equivalent to determining whether the improper integral $\int_{1}^{\infty}(1 / x)dx$ is convergent. $\int_1^\infty (1 / x)dx = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\int_1^v (1 / x)dx = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\ln v\int_1^v = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\ln v = +\infty$ . Thus, the integral diverges and the area is infinite.
