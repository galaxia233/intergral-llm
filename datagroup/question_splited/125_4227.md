【4227】 $\int_{C} |y| \mathrm{d}s$ 其中 $C$ 为双纽线 $(x^{2} + y^{2})^{2} = a^{2}(x^{2} - y^{2})$ 的弧.

解 双纽线的极坐标方程为 $r^2 = a^2\cos 2\varphi$ ，弧长的微分为

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {r ^ {2} + r ^ {\prime 2}} \mathrm {d} \varphi = \frac {a}{\sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi}} \mathrm {d} \varphi .
$$

于是， $\int_{C}|y|\mathrm{d}s = 4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{4}}a\sqrt{\cos2\varphi}\sin \varphi \frac{a}{\sqrt{\cos2\varphi}}\mathrm{d}\varphi = 4a^{2}(-\cos \varphi)\left| \begin{array}{l}\frac{\pi}{4}\\ 0 \end{array} \right. = 2a^{2}(2 - \sqrt{2}).$
