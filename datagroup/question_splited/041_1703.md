【1703】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x}$

提示 注意 $\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x} = \frac{\frac{1}{2\cos^2\frac{x}{2}}\mathrm{d}x}{\tan\frac{x}{2}} = \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(\tan\frac{x}{2}\right)}{\tan\frac{x}{2}}.$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin x} = \int \frac{\frac{1}{2\cos^2\frac{x}{2}}}{\tan\frac{x}{2}}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(\tan\frac{x}{2}\right)}{\tan\frac{x}{2}} = \ln \left|\tan \frac{x}{2}\right| + C.$
