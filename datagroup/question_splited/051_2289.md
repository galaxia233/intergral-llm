【2289】证明： $\int_{a}^{b}\mathrm{e}^{(\alpha +\mathrm{i}\beta)x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{\mathrm{e}^{b(\alpha + \mathrm{i}\beta)} - \mathrm{e}^{a(\alpha + \mathrm{i}\beta)}}{\alpha + \mathrm{i}\beta}$ （α及β为常数）.

证 $\int_{a}^{b}\mathrm{e}^{(\alpha +\mathrm{i}\beta)x}\mathrm{d}x = \int_{a}^{b}\mathrm{e}^{\alpha x}\cos \beta x\mathrm{d}x + \mathrm{i}\int_{a}^{b}\mathrm{e}^{\alpha x}\sin \beta x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{\mathrm{e}^{\alpha x}\left[\alpha\cos\beta x + \beta\sin\beta x + \mathrm{i}(\alpha\sin\beta x - \beta\cos\beta x)\right]}{\alpha^{2} + \beta^{2}}\Bigg{|}_{a}^{b}$

$$
= \frac {e ^ {\alpha x} (\alpha - i \beta) (\cos \beta x + i \sin \beta x)}{(\alpha + i \beta) (\alpha - i \beta)} \left| _ {a} ^ {b} = \frac {e ^ {(a + i \beta) x}}{\alpha + i \beta} \right| _ {a} ^ {b} = \frac {e ^ {(a + i \beta) b} - e ^ {(a + i \beta) a}}{\alpha + i \beta}.
$$
