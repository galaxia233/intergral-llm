【4403】给定标量场
$$
u = \ln \frac {1}{r},
$$
其中 $r = \sqrt{(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 + (z - c)^2}$ . 在空间 $(x, y, z)$ 的哪些点成立等式
$$
| \operatorname {g r a d} u | = 1?
$$
解 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = -\frac{x - a}{r^2}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = -\frac{y - b}{r^2}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = -\frac{z - c}{r^2}$ .

于是，
$$
| \operatorname {g r a d} u | = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial x}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial y}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial u}{\partial z}\right) ^ {2}} = \sqrt {\frac {1}{r ^ {4}} \left[ (x - a) ^ {2} + (y - b) ^ {2} + (z - c) ^ {2} \right]} = \frac {1}{r}.
$$

要 $\left|\operatorname{grad} u\right| = 1$ ，只要 $r = 1$ ，即在以点 $(a, b, c)$ 为中心，1为半径的球面上，均有
$$
\left| \operatorname {g r a d} \left(\ln \frac {1}{r}\right) \right| = 1.
$$
其中 $r = \sqrt{(x - a)^2 + (y - b)^2 + (z - c)^2}$
