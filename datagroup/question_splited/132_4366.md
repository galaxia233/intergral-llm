【4366】 $\iint_{S} x^{2} \mathrm{d}y \mathrm{~d}z + y^{2} \mathrm{~d}z \mathrm{~d}x + z^{2} \mathrm{~d}x \mathrm{~d}y$ ，式中 $S$ 为球面 $(x - a)^{2} + (y - b)^{2} + (x - c)^{2} = R^{2}$ 的外侧.

解 根据轮换对称知，只要计算 $\iint_{S} z^{2} \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y.$ 注意到 $z - c = \pm \sqrt{R^{2} - (x - a)^{2} - (y - b)^{2}}$ ，并利用极坐标，即得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {S} z ^ {2} \mathrm {d} x \mathrm {d} y \\ = \iint_ {(x - a) ^ {2} + (y - b) ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} [ c + \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2} - (y - b) ^ {2}} ] ^ {2} d x d y - \iint_ {(x - a) ^ {2} + (y - b) ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} [ c - \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2} - (y - b) ^ {2}} ] ^ {2} d x d y \\ = 4 c \iint_ {(x - a) ^ {2} + (y - b) ^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} \sqrt {R ^ {2} - (x - a) ^ {2} - (y - b) ^ {2}} d x d y = 4 c \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {R} \sqrt {R ^ {2} - r ^ {2}} r d r \\ = 8 \pi c \left[ - \frac {1}{3} \left(R ^ {2} - r ^ {2}\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {R} = \frac {8}{3} \pi R ^ {3} c. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，我们有 $\iint_{S} x^{2} \mathrm{d}y \mathrm{d}z + y^{2} \mathrm{d}z \mathrm{d}x + z^{2} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y = \frac{8}{3} \pi R^{3}(a + b + c).$
