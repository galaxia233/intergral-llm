34.23 Find $dy / dx$ and $d^2 y / dx^2$ for $x = a\cos^3\theta, y = a\sin^3\theta.$

$\begin{array}{l} \frac{d}{d\theta}\left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right) = -\sec^2\theta ,\\  \frac{d}{d\theta}\left(\frac{dy}{dx}\right) = -\sin^2\theta , \end{array}$ $dy / d\theta = 3a\sin^2\theta \cos \theta .$ So, $\frac{dy}{dx} = \frac{dy / d\theta}{dx / d\theta} = -\frac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta} = -\tan \theta .$ Further,

$$
\frac {d ^ {2} y}{d x ^ {2}} = \frac {d}{d \theta} \left(\frac {d y}{d x}\right) / \frac {d x}{d \theta} = \frac {- \sec^ {2} \theta}{- 3 a \cos^ {2} \theta \sin \theta} = \frac {1}{3 a} \sec^ {4} \theta \csc \theta
$$
