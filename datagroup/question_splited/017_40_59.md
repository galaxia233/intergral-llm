40.59 Prove $(\mathbf{A} - \mathbf{B})\times (\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}) = 2(\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{B}).$

$\mathbf{(A - B)}\times (\mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B}) = (\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{A}) + (\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{B}) - (\mathbf{B}\times \mathbf{A}) - (\mathbf{B}\times \mathbf{B}).$ Since $\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{A} = \mathbf{B}\times \mathbf{B} = \mathbf{0}$ and $\mathbf{B}\times \mathbf{A} =$ $-(A\times B)$ ，the result is $2(A\times B)$
