46.15 Solve $\frac{dy}{dx} = 1 + y + x^2 +yx^2.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {d y}{d x} = y \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) + 1 + x ^ {2} = (y + 1) \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right). \quad \int \frac {d y}{y + 1} = \int \left(1 + x ^ {2}\right) d x, \quad \ln | y + 1 | = x + \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} + C _ {1}, \quad | y + 1 | = \\ e ^ {C _ {1}} \cdot e ^ {x + x ^ {2} / 3}, \quad y = C e ^ {x + x ^ {2} / 3} - 1. \\ \end{array}
