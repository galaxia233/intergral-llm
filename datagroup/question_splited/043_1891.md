【1891】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(x - 1)^2(x + 1)^3}.$

解 $Q = (x - 1)^{2}(x + 1)^{3}, Q_{1} = (x - 1)(x + 1)^{2} = x^{3} + x^{2} - x - 1, Q_{2} = (x - 1)(x + 1) = x^{2} - 1.$

设 $\frac{x}{(x - 1)^2(x + 1)^3} = \left(\frac{Ax^2 + Bx + C}{x^3 + x^2 - x - 1}\right)' + \frac{Dx + E}{x^2 - 1}$ ，从而，

$$
x \equiv (2 A x + B) (x - 1) (x + 1) - (3 x - 1) \left(A x ^ {2} + B x + C\right) + (D x + E) (x - 1) (x + 1) ^ {2}.
$$

比较等式两端 $x$ 的同次幂系数，得

$$
\begin{array}{l l} x ^ {4} & D = 0, \\ x ^ {3} & - A + D + E = 0, \\ x ^ {2} & A - 2 B - D + E = 0, \\ x ^ {1} & - 2 A + B - 3 C - D - E = 1, \\ x ^ {0} & - B + C - E = 0. \end{array}
$$

由此， $A = -\frac{1}{8}$ ， $B = -\frac{1}{8}$ ， $C = -\frac{1}{4}$ ， $D = 0$ ， $E = -\frac{1}{8}$ 于是，

$$
\int \frac {x d x}{(x - 1) ^ {2} (x + 1) ^ {3}} = - \frac {x ^ {2} + x + 2}{8 (x - 1) (x + 1) ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{8} \int \frac {d x}{x ^ {2} - 1} = - \frac {x ^ {2} + x + 2}{8 (x - 1) (x + 1) ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{1 6} \ln \left| \frac {x + 1}{x - 1} \right| + C.
$$
