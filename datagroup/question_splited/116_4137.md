【4137】 $x^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2},y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}(z > 0).$

解 物体的质量为 $M = \int_{0}^{a} \mathrm{d}z \int_{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}^{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}} \mathrm{d}y \int_{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}^{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}} \mathrm{d}x = 4 \int_{0}^{a} (a^2 - z^2) \mathrm{d}z = \frac{8a^3}{3}.$

于是， $x_{0} = \frac{1}{M}\int_{0}^{u}\mathrm{d}z\int_{\sqrt{a^{2} - z^{2}}}\sqrt{a^{2} - z^{2}}\mathrm{d}y\int_{\sqrt{a^{2} - z^{2}}}^{\sqrt{a^{2} - z^{2}}}x\mathrm{d}x = 0.$

同理可得 $y_0 = 0$ ，而 $z_0 = \frac{1}{M}\int_0^a z\mathrm{d}z\int_{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}^{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}\mathrm{d}y\int_{-\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}^{\sqrt{a^2 - z^2}}\mathrm{d}x = a^4\cdot \frac{3}{8a^3} = \frac{3}{8} a.$

于是，质心的坐标为 $x_0 = y_0 = 0$ ， $z_0 = \frac{3}{8} a$
