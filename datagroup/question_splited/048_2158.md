【2158】 $\int \sqrt{1 - x^2}\arcsin x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \sqrt{1 - x^2}\arcsin x\mathrm{d}x = x\sqrt{1 - x^2}\arcsin x - \int x\left(1 - \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\arcsin x\right)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = x \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \arcsin x - \frac {x ^ {2}}{2} - \int \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \arcsin x d x + \int \frac {\arcsin x}{\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} d x \\ = x \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \arcsin x - \frac {x ^ {2}}{2} + \frac {1}{2} (\arcsin x) ^ {2} - \int \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \arcsin x d x. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\int \sqrt{1 - x^2}\arcsin x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{x}{2}\sqrt{1 - x^2}\arcsin x - \frac{x^2}{4} +\frac{1}{4} (\arcsin x)^2 +C(|x| < 1).$
