34.12 Find parametric equations for the hyperbola $\frac{x^2}{a^2} -\frac{y^2}{b^2} = 1.$

Let $x = at + a / 4t$ , $y = bt - b / 4t$ . Then $(x / a)^2 = t^2 + \frac {1}{2} + 1 / 16t^2$ , $(y / b)^2 = t^2 - \frac {1}{2} + 1 / 16t^2$ . Hence, $(x / a)^2 - (y / b)^2 = 1$ . Another possibility (cf. Problem 34.6) would be $x = a \sec u$ , $y = b \tan u$ .
