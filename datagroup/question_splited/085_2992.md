шзг $\sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{2}-1}=\lim _{N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^{2}-1}=\lim _{N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_{n=2}^{\infty} \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{n-1}-\frac{1}{n+1}\right)=\frac{1}{2} \lim _{N \rightarrow \infty}\left(1+\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{N+1}\right)$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} \left(1 + \frac {1}{2}\right) = \frac {3}{4}.
$$
