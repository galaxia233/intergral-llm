40.38 For a unit cube, find the angle $\psi$ between a diagonal $\overrightarrow{OP}$ and an adjacent edge $\overrightarrow{OR}$ . (See Fig. 40-5.)

$\overrightarrow{OP} = (1, 1, 1), \overrightarrow{OR} = (0, 0, 1). \quad \overrightarrow{OP} \cdot \overrightarrow{OR} = |\overrightarrow{OP}| |\overrightarrow{OR}| \cos \psi, \quad 1 = \sqrt{3} \cos \psi = \sqrt{3} / 3, \quad \psi \approx 54^\circ 44'.$ [By geometry, $\psi = 90^\circ - \theta$ .]
