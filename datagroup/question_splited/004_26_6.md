26.6 If a quantity $y$ grows exponentially with a growth constant $K$ and if during each unit of time there is an increase in $y$ of $r$ percent, find the relationship between $K$ and $r$ .

$y = y_0e^{Kt}$ . When $t = 1,\quad y = (1 + r / 100)y_0.$ Hence, $(1 + r / 100)y_{0} = y_{0}e^{K},\quad 1 + r / 100 = e^{K}.$ So $K = \ln (1 + r / 100)$ and $r = 100(e^{\kappa} - 1)$
