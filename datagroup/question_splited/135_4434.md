【4434】设 $x = f(u, v, w), y = g(u, v, w), z = h(u, v, w)$
用正交曲线坐标 $u, v, w$ 表示 $\operatorname{div} a(x, y, z)$ . 作为特殊的情形，求用柱坐标和球坐标表示 $\operatorname{div} a$ 的表示式.
提示 研究向量 $\pmb{a}$ 通过以曲面 $u =$ 常数， $v =$ 常数， $\omega =$ 常数为界的小立体（接近于长方体） $V$ 的表面 $S$ 的通量.
解 考虑向量 $\pmb{a}$ 通过由曲面 $u =$ 常数， $v =$ 常数， $\omega =$ 常数所界的小立体（接近于长方体） $V$ 的表面 $S$ 的通量（图8.72），我们有 $\pmb{a} = a_{n}e_{1} + a_{3}e_{2} + a_{4}e_{3}$ ，在 $\pmb{u}$ 曲线上，只有 $\pmb{u}$ 变化（ $v$ 和 $w$ 都是常数），故
$$
\mathrm {d} \boldsymbol {r} = \frac {\partial x}{\partial u} \mathrm {d} u \boldsymbol {i} + \frac {\partial y}{\partial u} \mathrm {d} u \boldsymbol {j} + \frac {\partial z}{\partial u} \mathrm {d} u \boldsymbol {k},
$$
从而， $\mathrm{ds}_1 = |\mathrm{d}\boldsymbol {r}| = L\mathrm{d}\boldsymbol{u}$ ，其中
$$
L = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial u}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial y}{\partial u}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial u}\right) ^ {2}} = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial f}{\partial u}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial g}{\partial u}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial h}{\partial u}\right) ^ {2}},
$$

ds为u曲线上的弧元素.同理可得 $\mathrm{ds}_2 = M\mathrm{dv},\mathrm{ds}_3 = N\mathrm{dw},$
其中 $\mathrm{ds}_2, \mathrm{ds}_3$ 分别为 $v, w$ 曲线上的弧元素，而
$$
M = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial f}{\partial v}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial g}{\partial v}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial h}{\partial v}\right) ^ {2}}. \quad N = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial f}{\partial w}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial g}{\partial w}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial h}{\partial w}\right) ^ {2}}.
$$

由于坐标曲线互相垂直， $\Delta s_1, \Delta s_2, \Delta s_3$ 都很小，故 $V$ 接近于长方体。因此，其体积为
$$
V \approx \Delta s _ {1} \Delta s _ {2} \Delta s _ {3} \approx d s _ {1} d s _ {2} d s _ {3} = L M N d u d v d w.
$$

现计算 $\pmb{a}$ 通过 $V$ 的表面 $S$ 向外的通量 $\iint_{S} a_{n} \mathrm{d}S, S$ 共包括六块小曲面（图8.72），记垂直于 $\pmb{e}_{1}$ 方向的两块

为 $S_{1}$ 与 $S_{2}$ （即图中的 $MM_{2}N_{1}M_{3}$ 与 $M_{1}N_{3}NN_{2}$ ），垂直于 $\pmb{e}_{2}$ 方向的两块为 $S_{3}$ 与 $S_{4}$ ，垂直于 $\pmb{e}_{3}$ 方向的两块为 $S_{5}$ 与 $S_{6}$ 。显然，由于曲面很小，有
$$
\begin{array}{l} \iint_ {S _ {2}} a _ {n} \mathrm {d} S + \iint_ {S _ {1}} a _ {n} \mathrm {d} S \approx a _ {u} \Delta S _ {2} \Delta S _ {3} \left| _ {(u - \Delta u, v, w)} - a _ {u} \Delta S _ {2} \Delta S _ {3} \right| _ {(u, v, w)} \\ \approx a _ {u} M N \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w \left| _ {(u - 3 u, 1, w)} - a _ {u} M N \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w \right| _ {(u, v, u)} \approx \frac {\partial (a _ {u} M N \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w)}{\partial u} \mathrm {d} u = \frac {\partial (M N a _ {u})}{\partial u} \mathrm {d} u \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w. \\ \end{array}
$$

同理可得
$$
\iint_ {S _ {1}} a _ {n} \mathrm {d} S + \iint_ {S _ {3}} a _ {n} \mathrm {d} S \approx \frac {\partial (N L a _ {v})}{\partial v} \mathrm {d} u \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w. \quad \iint_ {S _ {6}} a _ {n} \mathrm {d} S + \iint_ {S _ {5}} a _ {n} \mathrm {d} S \approx \frac {\partial (L M a _ {w})}{\partial w} \mathrm {d} u \mathrm {d} v \mathrm {d} w.
$$

相加即得 $\iint_{S} a_{n} \mathrm{d} S \approx \left[ \frac{\partial (M N a_{n})}{\partial u} + \frac{\partial (N L a_{n})}{\partial v} + \frac{\partial (L M a_{w})}{\partial w} \right] \mathrm{d} u \mathrm{~d} v \mathrm{~d} w.$
于是， $\frac{\iint_{S}a_{n}\mathrm{d}S}{V}\approx \frac{1}{LMN}\left[\frac{\partial}{\partial u} (MN a_{n}) + \frac{\partial}{\partial v} (NL a_{v}) + \frac{\partial}{\partial w} (LMa_{w})\right].$
显然，当小立体 $V$ 愈缩向点 $M(V)$ 愈小）时，上述各近似等式都愈精确。于是，令 $V$ 缩向 $M$ （即 $S$ 的直径 $d(S)$ 趋于零）取极限，利用4422题的结果，得
$$
\operatorname {d i v} \boldsymbol {a} = \lim  _ {d (S) \rightarrow 0} \frac {\iint_ {a _ {n}} d S}{V} = \frac {1}{L M N} \left[ \frac {\partial}{\partial u} \left(M N a _ {u}\right) + \frac {\partial}{\partial v} \left(N L a _ {v}\right) + \frac {\partial}{\partial w} \left(L M a _ {w}\right)\right].
$$

特别是在柱坐标情形下，有 $x = r\cos \varphi, y = r\sin \varphi, z = z (u = r, v = \varphi, w = z)$
从而，
$$
\begin{array}{l} L = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial r}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial y}{\partial r}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial r}\right) ^ {2}} = 1, \quad M = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial \varphi}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial y}{\partial \varphi}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial \varphi}\right) ^ {2}} = r, \\ N = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial z}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial y}{\partial z}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial z}\right) ^ {2}} = 1. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\operatorname{div} a = \frac{1}{r} \left[ \frac{\partial}{\partial r} (ra_r) + \frac{\partial a_\varphi}{\partial \varphi} + r \frac{\partial a_z}{\partial z} \right].$
在球坐标情形下，有
$$
x = r \sin \theta \cos \varphi , \quad y = r \sin \theta \sin \varphi , \quad z = r \cos \theta \quad (u = r, v = \theta , w = \varphi).
$$

从而， $L = \sqrt{\left(\frac{\partial x}{\partial r}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\partial y}{\partial r}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial r}\right)^2} = 1,$ $M = \sqrt{\left(\frac{\partial x}{\partial\theta}\right)^{2} + \left(\frac{\partial y}{\partial\theta}\right)^{2} + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial\theta}\right)^{2}} = r,$
$$
N = \sqrt {\left(\frac {\partial x}{\partial \varphi}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial y}{\partial \varphi}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {\partial z}{\partial \varphi}\right) ^ {2}} = r \sin \theta .
$$

于是，
$$
\operatorname {d i v} a = \frac {1}{r ^ {2} \sin \theta} \left[ \frac {\partial}{\partial r} \left(a _ {r} r ^ {2} \sin \theta\right) + \frac {\partial}{\partial \theta} \left(a _ {\theta} r \sin \theta\right) + \frac {\partial}{\partial \varphi} \left(a _ {\varphi} r\right) \right] = \frac {1}{r ^ {2} \sin \theta} \left[ \sin \theta \frac {\partial}{\partial r} \left(a _ {r} r ^ {2}\right) + r \frac {\partial}{\partial \theta} \left(a _ {\theta} \sin \theta\right) + r \frac {\partial a _ {\varphi}}{\partial \varphi} \right].
$$
