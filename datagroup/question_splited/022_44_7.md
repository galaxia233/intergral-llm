44.7 Evaluate $\int_1^2\int_0^y x\sqrt{y^2 - x^2} dxdy.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} I \int_ {0} ^ {y} x \sqrt {y ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = - \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {2}{3} (y ^ {2} - x ^ {2}) ^ {3 / 2} ] _ {0} ^ {y} = - \frac {1}{3} (y ^ {2} - x ^ {2}) ^ {3 / 2} ] _ {0} ^ {y} = - \frac {1}{3} [ - (y ^ {2}) ^ {3 / 2} ] = \frac {1}{3} y ^ {3}. \\ I = \int_ {1} ^ {2} \frac {1}{3} y ^ {3} d y = \frac {1}{1 2} y ^ {4} ] _ {1} ^ {2} = \frac {1}{1 2} (1 6 - 1) = \frac {1 5}{1 2} = \frac {5}{4}. \end{array}
$$
