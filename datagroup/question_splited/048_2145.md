【2145】+ $\int \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\ln \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\ln \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x}}\mathrm{d}x = -\int \ln \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x}}\mathrm{d}(\sqrt{1 - x^2})$

$$
= - \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \ln \frac {x}{\sqrt {1 - x}} + \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} (2 - x)}{x (1 - x)} d x.
$$

右端的积分

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} (2 - x)}{x (1 - x)} d x = \int \frac {(1 - x ^ {2}) (2 - x)}{x (1 - x) \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} d x = \int \frac {2 + x - x ^ {2}}{x \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} d x \\ = 2 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} + \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} - \int \frac {x \mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}} = - 2 \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(\frac {1}{x}\right)}{\sqrt {\left(\frac {1}{x}\right) ^ {2} - 1}} + \arcsin x + \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} \\ = - 2 \ln \left| \frac {1}{x} + \sqrt {\frac {1}{x ^ {2}} - 1} \right| + \arcsin x + \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} + C _ {1} = - 2 \ln \frac {1 + \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}}}{x} + \arcsin x + \sqrt {1 - x ^ {2}} + C _ {1}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是， $\int \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}}\ln \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x}}\mathrm{d}x = \left(\frac{1}{2} -\ln \frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x}}\right)\sqrt{1 - x^2} -\ln \frac{1 + \sqrt{1 - x^2}}{x} +\frac{1}{2}\arcsin x + C$ （0<x<1).
