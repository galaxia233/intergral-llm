45.11 Let $\mathbf{F}(t) = \mathbf{G}(t)\times \mathbf{H}(t)$ . Prove $\mathbf{F}' = (\mathbf{G}\times \mathbf{H}') + (\mathbf{G}'\times \mathbf{H})$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \mathbf {F} ^ {\prime} (t) = \lim  _ {\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac {\mathbf {F} (t + \Delta t) - \mathbf {F} (t)}{\Delta t} = \lim  _ {\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac {[ \mathbf {G} (t + \Delta t) \times \mathbf {H} (t + \Delta t) ] - [ \mathbf {G} (t) \times \mathbf {H} (t) ]}{\Delta t} \\ = \lim  _ {\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \frac {\left\{\mathbf {G} (t + \Delta t) \times [ \mathbf {H} (t + \Delta t) - \mathbf {H} (t) ] \right\} + \left\{\left[ \mathbf {G} (t + \Delta t) - \mathbf {G} (t) \right] \times \mathbf {H} (t) \right\}}{\Delta t} \\ = \lim  _ {\Delta t \rightarrow 0} \left[ \mathbf {G} (t + \Delta t) \times \frac {\mathbf {H} (t + \Delta t) - \mathbf {H} (t)}{\Delta t} + \frac {\mathbf {G} (t + \Delta t) - \mathbf {G} (t)}{\Delta t} \times \mathbf {H} (t) \right] = [ \mathbf {G} (t) \times \mathbf {H} ^ {\prime} (t) ] + [ \mathbf {G} ^ {\prime} (t) \times \mathbf {H} (t) ]. \\ \end{array}
$$
