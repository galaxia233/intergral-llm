【3936】 $\iint_{\Omega} y^2 \, \mathrm{d}x \, \mathrm{d}y$ ，其中 $\Omega$ 是被横轴和摆线

$$
x = a (t - \sin t), \quad y = a (1 - \cos t) \quad (0 \leqslant t \leqslant 2 \pi)
$$

的第一拱所包围的区域.

提示 利用2281题及2282题的结果，

解 $\iint_{\Omega} y^{2} \mathrm{d}x \mathrm{d}y = \int_{0}^{2\pi a} \mathrm{d}x \int_{0}^{y} y^{2} \mathrm{d}x = \frac{a^{4}}{3} \int_{0}^{2\pi} (1 - \cos t)^{4} \mathrm{d}t = \frac{2^{4} a^{4}}{3} \int_{0}^{2\pi} \sin^{8} \frac{t}{2} \mathrm{d}t = \frac{2^{5} a^{4}}{3} \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin^{8} u \mathrm{d}u$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {2 ^ {5} a ^ {4}}{3} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {8} u d u + \int_ {\frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\pi} \sin^ {8} u d u \right\} = \frac {2 ^ {5} a ^ {4}}{3} \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {8} u d u + \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {8} u d u \right\} = \frac {2 ^ {5} a ^ {4}}{3} \cdot 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {8} u d u * \\ = \frac {2 ^ {5} a ^ {4}}{3} \cdot 2 \cdot \frac {1 \cdot 3 \cdot 5 \cdot 7}{2 \cdot 4 \cdot 6 \cdot 8} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} ^ {* *}) = \frac {3 5}{1 2} \pi a ^ {4}. \\ \end{array}
$$

*）利用2282题的结果.

$\text{水} ^ { \text{水} }$ ）利用2281题的结果.
