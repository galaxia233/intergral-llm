【2237】 求：
(1) $\int_{0}^{2} f(x) \mathrm{d}x$ ，设 $f(x) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} x^2, & 0 \leqslant x \leqslant 1, \\ 2 - x, & 1 < x \leqslant 2; \end{array} \right.$ (2) $\int_{0}^{1} f(x) \mathrm{d}x$ ，设 $f(x) = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll} x, & 0 \leqslant x \leqslant t, \\ t \frac{1 - x}{1 - t}, & t \leqslant x \leqslant 1. \end{array} \right.$

解 (1) $\int_{0}^{2} f(x) \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{1} x^{2} \mathrm{d}x + \int_{1}^{2} (2 - x) \mathrm{d}x = \frac{5}{6}$ .
