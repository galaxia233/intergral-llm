【4186】证明：若 1) 函数 $\varphi (x,y)$ 在有界区域 $a\leqslant x\leqslant A,b\leqslant y\leqslant B$ 内连续；2) 函数 $f(x)$ 在闭区间 $a\leqslant x\leqslant$

$A$ 上连续；3) $p < 1$ ，则积分 $\int_{a}^{A}\mathrm{d}x\int_{b}^{B}\frac{\varphi(x,y)}{|f(x) - y|^{p}}\mathrm{d}y$ 收敛.

证 首先注意，由于 $p < 1$ , 故积分 $\int_{b}^{B} \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{|f(x) - y|^p}$ 对每个固定的 $x \in [a, A]$ 恒收敛， (若 $f(x) \in [b, B]$ 此为瑕积分，点 $f(x)$ 是瑕点，由于 $p < 1$ , 它收敛；若 $f(x) \in [b, B]$ , 则为常义积分，当然收敛). 再根据 $\varphi(x, y)$ 的有界性，即知：对每个固定的 $x \in [a, A]$ , 积分 $\int_{b}^{B} \frac{\varphi(x, y)}{|f(x) - y|^p} \mathrm{d}y$ 都收敛。令

$$
F (x) = \int_ {b} ^ {B} \frac {\varphi (x , y)}{| f (x) - y | ^ {p}} d y \quad (a \leqslant x \leqslant A).
$$

下面我们证明 $F(x)$ 是 $a \leqslant x \leqslant A$ 上的连续函数。若已获证，则积分

$$
\int_ {a} ^ {A} \mathrm {d} x \int_ {b} ^ {B} \frac {\varphi (x , y)}{| f (x) - y | ^ {p}} \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {a} ^ {A} F (x) \mathrm {d} x
$$

显然是收敛的（右端为常义积分），于是本题获证。令 $c = \max_{a\leqslant x\leqslant A}|f(x)|$ . 今将函数 $\varphi (x,y)$ 连续地延拓到有界闭矩形 $R(a\leqslant x\leqslant A,b - 2c\leqslant y\leqslant B + 2c)$ 上（只要规定

$$
\varphi (x, y) = \left\{ \begin{array}{l} \varphi (x, B), a \leqslant x \leqslant A, B <   y \leqslant B + 2 c, \\ \varphi (x, b), a \leqslant x \leqslant A, b - 2 c \leqslant y <   b \end{array} \right.
$$

即可）。延拓后的函数仍记为 $\varphi(x, y)$ . 由于 $\varphi(x, y)$ 及 $|f(x) - y|^p$ 都在 $R$ 上连续，故有界且一致连续：存在常数 $M$ , 使对一切 $(x, y) \in R$ , 有

$$
\left| \varphi (x, y) \right| \leqslant M, \quad \left| f (x) - y \right| ^ {1 - p} \leqslant M. \tag {1}
$$

任给 $\varepsilon > 0$ ，存在 $\delta_1 > 0$ （取 $\delta_1 < \left(\frac{\varepsilon}{2}\right)^{\frac{1}{1 - p}}$ ），使当 $|x_1 - x_2| < \delta_1, |y_1 - y_2| < \delta_1 ((x_1, y_1) \in R, (x_2, y_2) \in R)$ 时，恒有

$$
\left| \varphi \left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}\right) - \varphi \left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}\right) \right| <   \varepsilon , \tag {2}
$$

$$
\left| \left| f \left(x _ {1}\right) - y _ {1} \right| ^ {1 - p} - \left| f \left(x _ {2}\right) - y _ {2} \right| ^ {1 - p} \right| <   \epsilon . \tag {3}
$$

又由 $f(x)$ 在 $[a,A]$ 上的一致连续性可知，存在 $\delta_2 > 0$ ，使当 $|x_{1} - x_{2}| < \delta_{2}$ （ $x_{1},x_{2}\in [a,A])$ ，恒有

$$
\left| f \left(x _ {1}\right) - f \left(x _ {2}\right) \right| <   \delta_ {1}. \tag {4}
$$

令 $\delta = \min \{\delta_1, \delta_2\}$ . 于是，由 (2) 式可知：当 $|x_1 - x_2| < \delta$ ( $x_1, x_2 \in [a, A]$ ) 时，对一切 $b - c \leqslant y \leqslant B + c$ ，恒有

$$
\left| \varphi \left(x _ {1}, y + f \left(x _ {1}\right)\right) - \varphi \left(x _ {2}, y + f \left(x _ {2}\right)\right) \right| <   \varepsilon . \tag {5}
$$

现设 $|x_{1} - x_{2}| < \delta, (x_{1}, x_{2} \in [a, A])$ . 不失一般性，设 $f(x_{1}) \geqslant f(x_{2})$ ，我们有

$$
\begin{array}{l} F \left(x _ {1}\right) - F \left(x _ {2}\right) = \int_ {b} ^ {B} \frac {\varphi \left(x _ {1} , y\right)}{\left| f \left(x _ {1}\right) - y \right| ^ {p}} d y - \int_ {b} ^ {B} \frac {\varphi \left(x _ {2} , y\right)}{\left| f \left(x _ {2}\right) - y \right| ^ {p}} d y \\ = \int_ {b f (x _ {1})} ^ {B - f (x _ {1})} \frac {\varphi (x _ {1} , u + f (x _ {1}))}{| u | ^ {p}} d u - \int_ {b f (x _ {2})} ^ {B - f (x _ {2})} \frac {\varphi (x _ {2} , u + f (x _ {2}))}{| u | ^ {p}} d u \\ = \int_ {b - f (x _ {1})} ^ {B - f (x _ {2})} \frac {\varphi (x _ {1} , u + f (x _ {1})) - \varphi (x _ {2} , u + f (x _ {2}))}{| u | ^ {p}} d u - \int_ {B - f (x _ {1})} ^ {B - f (x _ {2})} \frac {\varphi (x _ {1} , u + f (x _ {1}))}{| u | ^ {p}} d u \\ + \int_ {b - f (x _ {1})} ^ {b ^ {f (x _ {2})}} \frac {\varphi (x _ {2} , u + f (x _ {2}))}{| u | ^ {p}} d u \\ = I _ {1} - I _ {2} + I _ {3}, \tag {6} \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $I_{1}, I_{2}, I_{3}$ 分别表上式中的三个积分。易知 $(p < 1)$

$$
\int_ {a} ^ {\beta} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{| u | ^ {p}} = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \frac {1}{1 - p} \left[ \beta^ {1 - p} - a ^ {1 - p} \right], & 0 \leqslant \alpha \leqslant \beta , \\ \frac {1}{1 - p} \left[ (- \alpha) ^ {1 - p} - (- \beta) ^ {1 - p} \right], & \alpha \leqslant \beta \leqslant 0, \\ \frac {1}{1 - p} \left[ \beta^ {1 - p} + (- \alpha) ^ {1 - p} \right], & \alpha <   0 <   \beta . \end{array} \right.
$$

从而，在任何情形下均有

$$
\int_ {\alpha} ^ {\beta} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{| u | ^ {p}} \leqslant \frac {1}{1 - p} \left(| \beta | ^ {1 - p} + | \alpha | ^ {1 - p}\right); \tag {7}
$$

而当 $\alpha, \beta$ 同号时，有

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\beta} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{| u | ^ {p}} = \frac {1}{1 - p} \left| | \beta | ^ {1 - p} - | \alpha | ^ {1 - p} \right|. \tag {8}
$$

于是，由（5）式、（1）式及（7）式，得

$$
\left| I _ {1} \right| <   \varepsilon \int_ {b} ^ {B} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{| u | ^ {p}} \leqslant \frac {\varepsilon}{1 - p} \left(\left| B - f \left(x _ {2}\right) \right| ^ {1 - p} + \left| b - f \left(x _ {1}\right) \right| ^ {1 - p}\right) \leqslant \frac {2 M \varepsilon}{1 - p}. \tag {9}
$$

下面估计 $I_{2}$ ：若 $B - f(x_{2})$ 与 $B - f(x_{1})$ 同号，则由（1）式、（8）式及（3）式，有

$$
\left| I _ {2} \right| \leqslant M \int_ {B (r _ {1})} ^ {B - f (x _ {2})} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{| u | ^ {p}} = \frac {M}{1 - p} \left| | B - f (x _ {2}) | ^ {1 - p} - | B - f (x _ {1}) | ^ {1 - p} \right| <   \frac {M \epsilon}{1 - p};
$$

若 $B - f(x_{2})$ 与 $B - f(x_{1})$ 异号，即 $B - f(x_{1}) < 0 < B - f(x_{2})$ . 由于 $[B - f(x_{2})] - [B - f(x_{1})] = f(x_{1}) - f(x_{2}) < \delta_{1}$ ，故有 $|B - f(x_{1})| < \delta_{1}, |B - f(x_{2})| < \delta_{1}$ . 于是，由 (7) 式并注意到 $\delta_{1} < \left(\frac{\varepsilon}{2}\right)^{\frac{1}{1 - \rho}}$ ，即得

$$
\left| I _ {2} \right| \leqslant M \int_ {B f (x _ {1})} ^ {B f (x _ {2})} \frac {\mathrm {d} u}{| u | ^ {p}} \leqslant \frac {M}{1 - p} \left(\left| B - f \left(x _ {2}\right) \right| ^ {1 - p} + \left| B - f \left(x _ {1}\right) \right| ^ {1 - p}\right) <   \frac {M}{1 - p} \left(\delta_ {1} ^ {1 - p} + \delta_ {1} ^ {1 - p}\right) <   \frac {M \epsilon}{1 - p}.
$$

所以，在任何情况下均有

$$
\left| I _ {2} \right| <   \frac {M \epsilon}{1 - p}. \tag {10}
$$

同理，可得（在任何情况下）

$$
\left| I _ {3} \right| <   \frac {M _ {\epsilon}}{1 - p}. \tag {11}
$$

于是，由（6）式、（9）式、（10）式及（11）式，即得

$$
\left| F \left(x _ {1}\right) - F \left(x _ {2}\right) \right| <   \left| I _ {1} \right| + \left| I _ {2} \right| + \left| I _ {3} \right| <   \frac {4 M _ {\varepsilon}}{1 - p}.
$$
