【1700】+ $\int \frac{\sin x\cos x}{\sqrt{a^2\sin^2x + b^2\cos^2x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 分别就 $|a| = |b| \neq 0$ 及 $|a| \neq |b|$ 两种情况求解.

解 当 $|a| = |b| \neq 0$ 时，

$$
\int \frac {\sin x \cos x}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} x + b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} x}} d x = \frac {1}{| a |} \int \sin x \cos x d x = \frac {1}{2 | a |} \sin^ {2} x + C;
$$

当 $|a| \neq |b|$ 时，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\sin x \cos x}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} x + b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} x}} d x = \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {d (\sin^ {2} x)}{\sqrt {(a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}) \sin^ {2} x + b ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}} \sqrt {(a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}) \sin^ {2} x + b ^ {2}} + C \\ = \frac {\sqrt {a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} x + b ^ {2} \cos^ {2} x}}{a ^ {2} - b ^ {2}} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
