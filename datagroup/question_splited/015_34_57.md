34.99 Find the curvature of the hyperbola $xy = 1$ at $(1,1)$ .

$y^\prime = -1 / x^2$ and $y^{\prime \prime} = 2 / x^{3}$ .By Problem 34.97,

$$
\kappa = \frac {2 / x ^ {3}}{\left[ 1 + \left(1 / x ^ {4}\right) \right] ^ {3 / 2}} = \frac {2}{x ^ {3}} \frac {x ^ {6}}{\left(x ^ {4} + 1\right) ^ {3 / 2}} = \frac {2 x ^ {3}}{\left(x ^ {4} + 1\right) ^ {3 / 2}}
$$

When $x = 1, \kappa = 2 / 2\sqrt{2} = \sqrt{2} / 2.$
