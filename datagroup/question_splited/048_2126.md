【2126】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^6(1 + x^2)}.$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^6(1 + x^2)} = \int \frac{(x^2 + 1) - x^2}{x^6(1 + x^2)}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^6} -\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^4(1 + x^2)} = -\frac{1}{5x^5} -\int \frac{(x^2 + 1) - x^2}{x^4(1 + x^2)}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {1}{5 x ^ {5}} - \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {4}} + \int \frac {x ^ {2}}{x ^ {4} (1 + x ^ {2})} \mathrm {d} x = - \frac {1}{5 x ^ {5}} + \frac {1}{3 x ^ {3}} + \int \left(\frac {1}{x ^ {2}} - \frac {1}{1 + x ^ {2}}\right) \mathrm {d} x \\ = - \frac {1}{5 x ^ {5}} + \frac {1}{3 x ^ {3}} - \frac {1}{x} - \arctan x + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
