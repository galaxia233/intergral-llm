【2257】证明：若函数 $f(x)$ 在闭区间 $[0,1]$ 上连续，则
(2) $\int_{0}^{\pi} x f(\sin x) \, \mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi}{2} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(\sin x) \, \mathrm{d}x.$

证（2）设 $\pi - t = x$ , 则 $\mathrm{d}x = -\mathrm{d}t$ , 且 $xf(\sin x) = (\pi - t)f(\sin t)$ . 代入得

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x f (\sin x) d x = - \int_ {\pi} ^ {0} (\pi - t) f (\sin t) d t = \pi \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} f (\sin t) d t - \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} t f (\sin t) d t,
$$

即 $\int_{0}^{\pi} x f(\sin x) \mathrm{d}x = \frac{\pi}{2} \int_{0}^{\pi} f(\sin x) \mathrm{d}x.$
