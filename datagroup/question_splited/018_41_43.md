41.43 Describe the level curves of $f(x, y) = \frac{y - 1}{x + 2}$ .

See Fig. 41-27. The level curves are $\frac{y - 1}{x + 2} = k$ , or $y = kx + (2k + 1)$ . This is a family of punctured straight lines. All these lines pass through the point $(-2, 1)$ , which is excluded from them. There is a level curve through every point not on the vertical line $x = -2$ .
