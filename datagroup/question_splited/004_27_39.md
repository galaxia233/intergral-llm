27.39 $\int \frac{1}{4 + x^2} dx.$

Let $x = 2u$ , $dx = 2du$ . Then $\int \frac{dx}{4 + x^2} = \frac{1}{2} \int \frac{du}{1 + u^2} = \frac{1}{2} \tan^{-1} u + C = \frac{1}{2} \tan^{-1} \frac{x}{2} + C$ . This is a special case of the formula $\int \frac{dx}{a^2 + x^2} = \frac{1}{a} \tan^{-1} \frac{x}{a} + C$ for $a > 0$ .
