40.16 Find an equation of the sphere with center in the $xz$ -plane and passing through the points $P(0,8,0)$ , $Q(4,6,2)$ , and $R(0,12,4)$ .

Let $C(a,0,c)$ be the center. Then $\overline{PC} = \overline{QC} = \overline{RC}$ . So, $\sqrt{a^2 + 64 + c^2} = \sqrt{(a - 4)^2 + 36 + (c - 2)^2} = \sqrt{a^2 + 144 + (c - 4)^2}$ . Hence, $a^2 + 64 + c^2 = (a - 4)^2 + 36 + (c - 2)^2 = a^2 + 144 + (c - 4)^2$ . Equate the first and third terms: $64 + c^2 = 144 + (c - 4)^2$ , $c^2 = 80 + c^2 - 8c + 16$ , $8c = 96$ , $c = 12$ . Substitute 12 for $c$ in the first equation: $a^2 + 64 + 144 = (a - 4)^2 + 36 + 100$ , $a^2 + 72 = a^2 - 8a + 16$ , $8a = -56$ , $a = -7$ . So, the center is $(-7,0,12)$ . The radius $\overline{PC} = \sqrt{49 + 64 + 144} = \sqrt{257}$ .
