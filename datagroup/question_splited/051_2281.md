【2281】 $I_{n} = \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sin^{n}x\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 $I_{n} = \frac{n - 1}{n} I_{n - 2}$

解 $I_{n} = -\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin^{n-1} x \mathrm{~d}(\cos x) = -\sin^{n-1} x \cos x \left| \begin{array}{c} \frac{\pi}{2} \\ 0 \end{array} + (n-1) \int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}} \sin^{n-2} x \cos^{2} x \mathrm{~d}x \right|$ .

$$
= (n - 1) \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {n - 2} x d x - (n - 1) \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {n} x d x,
$$

移项合并得

$$
I _ {n} = \frac {n - 1}{n} I _ {n - 2}.
$$

利用上述递推公式即可求得

$$
I _ {n} = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} \frac {(2 k - 1) ! !}{(2 k) ! !} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2}, & n = 2 k, \\ \frac {(2 k) ! !}{(2 k + 1) ! !}, & n = 2 k + 1. \end{array} \right.
$$
