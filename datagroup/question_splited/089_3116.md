【3116】 $\int_0^1 (1 - x^2)^{50}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int_0^1 (1 - x^2)^{50}\mathrm{d}x = \int_0^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\cos^{101}t\mathrm{d}t = \frac{(100)!!}{(101)!!} = \frac{2^{100}\cdot(50!)^2}{101!} = \frac{2^{100}\cdot 100\pi\cdot 50^{100}\mathrm{e}^{-100}\mathrm{e}^{\frac{\theta_1}{300}}}{101\sqrt{2\pi\cdot 100}\cdot 100^{100}\mathrm{e}^{-100}\mathrm{e}^{\frac{\theta_2}{1200}}}$

$$
= \frac {1 0 \sqrt {\pi}}{1 0 1 \sqrt {2}} e ^ {\frac {\theta}{3 0 0}} = 0. 1 2 4 1 e ^ {\frac {\theta}{3 0 0}} \approx 0. 1 2 4 1 \left(1 + \frac {\theta}{3 0 0}\right),
$$

其中 $|\theta| < 1$ $(0 < \theta_{1} < 1, 0 < \theta_{2} < 1)$ .
