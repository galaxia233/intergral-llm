【1682】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^2 + 1}}.$

提示 注意 $\frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} = \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x|x|\sqrt{1 + \frac{1}{x^2}}} = -\frac{\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{|x|}\right)}{\sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{1}{|x|}\right)^2}}.$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x|x|\sqrt{1 + \frac{1}{x^2}}} = -\int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{|x|}\right)}{\sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{1}{|x|}\right)^2}} = -\ln \left(\frac{1}{|x|} +\sqrt{1 + \frac{1}{x^2}}\right) + C$

$$
= - \ln \left| \frac {1 + \sqrt {x ^ {2} + 1}}{x} \right| + C.
$$
