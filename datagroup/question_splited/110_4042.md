【4042】求曲面 $z = \sqrt{x^2 - y^2}$ 包含在柱面 $(x^{2} + y^{2})^{2} = a^{2}(x^{2} - y^{2})$ 内那部分的面积.

解 注意到 $\sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\right)^2} = \sqrt{1 + \left(\frac{x}{\sqrt{x^2 - y^2}}\right)^2 + \left(\frac{-y}{\sqrt{x^2 - y^2}}\right)^2} = \frac{\sqrt{2}x}{\sqrt{x^2 - y^2}},$

又积分域由双纽线 $r^2 = a^2\cos 2\varphi$ 所围成.于是，利用对称性知，所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \iint_ {\Omega} \frac {\sqrt {2} x}{\sqrt {x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} d x d y = 4 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a \sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi}} \sqrt {2} r \frac {r \cos \varphi}{r \sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi}} d r = 2 \sqrt {2} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} a ^ {2} \cos \varphi \sqrt {\cos 2 \varphi} d \varphi \\ = 2 a ^ {2} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \sqrt {1 - 2 \sin^ {2} \varphi} d (\sqrt {2} \sin \varphi) = 2 a ^ {2} \left[ \frac {\sqrt {2} \sin \varphi}{2} \sqrt {1 - 2 \sin^ {2} \varphi} + \frac {1}{2} \arcsin (\sqrt {2} \sin \varphi) \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} = \frac {\pi a ^ {2}}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
