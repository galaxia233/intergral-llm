【4433】求由极坐标 $r$ 与 $\varphi$ 表示的平面向量 $a = a(r,\varphi)$ 之散度的表示式.
解设极坐标的 $r$ 线与 $\varphi$ 线的单位向量为 $\pmb{e}_{r}$ 与 $\pmb{e}_{\varphi}$ ，且
$$
\boldsymbol {a} (r, \varphi) = a _ {r} (r, \varphi) \boldsymbol {e} _ {r} + a _ {\varphi} (r, \varphi) \boldsymbol {e} _ {\varphi}.
$$

这里自然假定 $a_r, a_\varphi$ 都具有连续的偏导数，取面积元素 $\Delta S = r\Delta \varphi \Delta r$ ，记其围线为 $\Delta C$ . 首先，推导向量 $a$ 经过围线 $\Delta C$ 的通量，即矢流. 通量可分两部分：一部分是经过 $r$ 线的；另一部分是经过 $\varphi$ 线的. 它们分别是
$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {r} ^ {r + \Delta r} a _ {\varphi} (r, \varphi + \Delta \varphi) d r - \int_ {r} ^ {r + \Delta r} a _ {\varphi} (r, \varphi) d r = \int_ {r} ^ {r + \Delta r} \left[ a _ {\varphi} (r, \varphi + \Delta \varphi) - a _ {\varphi} (r, \varphi) \right] d r \\ = \int_ {r} ^ {r - \Delta} \frac {\partial a _ {\varphi} (r , \varphi)}{\partial \varphi} \Delta \varphi d r = \frac {\partial a _ {\varphi} (r , \varphi)}{\partial \varphi} \Delta \varphi \Delta r, \\ \int_ {\xi} ^ {\xi - \Delta r} a _ {r} (r + \Delta r, \varphi) (r + \Delta r) d \varphi - \int_ {\xi} ^ {\xi - \Delta r} a _ {r} (r, \varphi) r d \varphi = \int_ {\xi} ^ {\xi - \Delta r} [ a _ {r} (r + \Delta r, \varphi) (r + \Delta r) - a _ {r} (r, \varphi) r ] d \varphi \\ \approx \int_ {c} ^ {r - \Delta \varphi} \frac {\partial [ a _ {r} (r , \varphi) r ]}{\partial r} \Delta r d \varphi \approx \frac {\partial [ a _ {r} (r , \varphi) r ]}{\partial r} \Delta r \Delta \varphi . \\ \end{array}
$$

且由于 $a_{r}, a_{\varphi}$ 的偏导数的连续性，当 $\Delta r, \Delta \varphi$ 取得愈小时，上述近似等式愈精确。于是，向量 $\pmb{a}$ 经过 $\Delta C$ 的通量为
$$
\oint_ {\Delta C} \boldsymbol {a} \cdot \boldsymbol {n} d s \approx \left\{\frac {\partial a _ {\varphi} (r , \varphi) ]}{\partial \varphi} + \frac {\partial [ a _ {r} (r , \varphi) r ]}{\partial r} \right\} \Delta \varphi \Delta r,
$$

其中 $\pmb{n}$ 为曲线 $\Delta C$ 的外法向量，而且当 $\Delta r, \Delta \varphi$ 愈小时此近似等式愈精确.
于是，根据散度的定义，并注意到 $\Delta S$ 收缩为一点 $(r, \varphi)$ 与 $\Delta r \to 0, \Delta \varphi \to 0$ 等价，从而，即得
$$
\begin{array}{l} \operatorname {d i v} a = \lim  _ {\Delta S \rightarrow 0} \frac {\oint_ {\Delta C} \boldsymbol {a} \cdot \boldsymbol {n} \mathrm {d} s}{\Delta S} = \lim  _ {\frac {\Delta r}{\Delta S} \rightarrow 0} \frac {\left\{\frac {\partial a _ {\varphi} (r , \varphi)}{\partial \varphi} + \frac {\partial [ a _ {r} (r , \varphi) r ]}{\partial r} \right\} \Delta r \Delta \varphi}{r \Delta r \Delta \varphi} \\ = \frac {1}{r} \left\{\frac {\partial a _ {\varphi} (r , \varphi)}{\partial \varphi} + \frac {\partial [ a _ {r} (r , \varphi) r ]}{\partial r} \right\} = \frac {1}{r} \left[ \frac {\partial (r a _ {r})}{\partial r} + \frac {\partial a _ {\varphi}}{\partial \varphi} \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
