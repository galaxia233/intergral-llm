【3055】 $\prod_{n = 1}^{\infty}\cos \frac{\pi}{2^{n + 1}} = \frac{2}{\pi}.$

证 由于部分乘积

$$
\begin{array}{l} P _ {n} = \cos \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {2}} \cos \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {3}} \dots \cos \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}} = \frac {1}{2 \sin \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}}} \cos \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {2}} \cos \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {3}} \dots \left(\cos \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}} \cdot 2 \sin \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}}\right) = \dots \\ = \frac {\sin \frac {\pi}{2}}{2 ^ {n} \sin \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}}} = \frac {\frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}}}{\sin \frac {\pi}{2 ^ {n + 1}}} \cdot \frac {2}{\pi} \rightarrow \frac {2}{\pi} \quad (\text {当} n \rightarrow \infty \text {时}), \\ \end{array}
$$

故 $\prod_{n=1}^{\infty} \cos \frac{\pi}{2^{n+1}} = \frac{2}{\pi}$ .
