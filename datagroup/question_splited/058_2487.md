【2487】 $y = a\cos \frac{\pi x}{2b}$ （ $|x|\leqslant b$ ）绕 $Ox$ 轴

解 $y^\prime = -\frac{\pi a}{2b}\sin \frac{\pi x}{2b},\quad \sqrt{1 + y^{\prime 2}} = \frac{1}{2b}\sqrt{4b^2 + \pi^2a^2\sin^2\frac{\pi x}{2b}}.$

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} P _ {x} = 2 \pi \int_ {- b} ^ {b} y \sqrt {1 + y ^ {\prime 2}} d x = 2 \pi \int_ {- b} ^ {b} \frac {a}{2 b} \cos \frac {\pi x}{2 b} \sqrt {4 b ^ {2} + \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} \frac {\pi x}{2 b}} d x \\ = \frac {4}{\pi} \left[ \frac {1}{2} \pi a \sin \frac {\pi x}{2 b} \sqrt {4 b ^ {2} + \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} \frac {\pi x}{2 b}} + \frac {4 b ^ {2}}{2} \ln \left| \pi a \sin \frac {\pi x}{2 b} + \sqrt {4 b ^ {2} + \pi^ {2} a ^ {2} \sin^ {2} \frac {\pi x}{2 b}} \right| \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {b} \\ = 2 a \sqrt {\pi^ {2} a ^ {2} + 4 b ^ {2}} + \frac {8 b ^ {2}}{\pi} \ln \frac {\pi a + \sqrt {4 b ^ {2} + \pi^ {2} a ^ {2}}}{2 b}. \\ \end{array}
$$
