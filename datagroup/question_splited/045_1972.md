【1972】 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x^3)\sqrt{1 - x^2}}.$

提示 令 $\frac{1 + x}{1 - x} = z$ ，并利用 1884 题的结果.

解设 $\frac{1 + x}{1 - x} = z$ ，则 $x = \frac{z - 1}{z + 1},\mathrm{d}x = \frac{2}{(z + 1)^2}\mathrm{d}z.$

代入得 $\int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x^3)\sqrt{1 - x^2}} = \frac{1}{2}\int \frac{(z^2 - 1)\mathrm{d}z}{\sqrt{z}(3z^2 + 1)} = \int \frac{(z^2 - 1)\mathrm{d}(\sqrt{z})}{3z^2 + 1} = \int \left[\frac{1}{3} -\frac{4}{3(3z^2 + 1)}\right]\mathrm{d}(\sqrt{z})$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {\sqrt {z}}{3} - \frac {4}{3} \cdot \frac {1}{\sqrt [ 4 ]{3}} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (\sqrt [ 4 ]{3 z ^ {2}})}{(\sqrt [ 4 ]{3 z ^ {2}}) ^ {4} + 1} = \frac {\sqrt {z}}{3} - \frac {4}{3 \sqrt [ 4 ]{3}} \left[ \frac {1}{4 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {z \sqrt {3} + \sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2 z ^ {2}} + 1}{z \sqrt {3} - \sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2 z ^ {2}} + 1} + \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {2}} \arctan \left(\frac {\sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2 z ^ {2}}}{1 - z \sqrt {3}}\right) \right] ^ {*}) + C \\ = \frac {\sqrt {z}}{3} - \frac {1}{3 \sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2}} \left[ \ln \frac {z \sqrt {3} + \sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2 z ^ {2}} + 1}{z \sqrt {3} - \sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2 z ^ {2}} + 1} - 2 \arctan \left(\frac {\sqrt [ 4 ]{1 2 z ^ {2}}}{z \sqrt {3} - 1}\right) \right] + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $z = \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}$

\*)利用 1884 题的结果.
