29.2 Find $\int \sin^2 ax dx$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {U s i n g P r o b l e m 2 9 . 1 ,} \int \sin^ {2} a x d x = \int (1 - \cos^ {2} a x) d x = x - \frac {1}{2} \left(x + \frac {1}{a} \sin a x \cos a x\right) + C = \\ \frac {1}{2} \left(x - \frac {1}{a} \sin a x \cos a x\right) + C. \end{array}
$$
