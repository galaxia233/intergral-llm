【2544】近似地求出半轴为 $a = 10$ 及 $b = 6$ 的椭圆的周长.

解 设椭圆的参数方程为

$$
x = 1 0 \cos t, \quad y = 6 \sin t.
$$

于是有 $\mathrm{ds} = \sqrt{x_t'^2 + y_t'^2}\mathrm{dt} = 10\sqrt{1 - \frac{16}{25}\sin^2t}\mathrm{dt},$

从而得椭圆的周长为 $s = 4\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\mathrm{d}s = 40\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sqrt{1 - \frac{16}{25}\sin^{2}t}\mathrm{d}t.$

现取 $n = 2k = 6$ 近似计算积分

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \sin^ {2} t} d t.
$$

注意到 $\sin^2\frac{\pi}{12} = \frac{2 - \sqrt{3}}{4}$ , $\sin^2\frac{5\pi}{12} = \frac{2 + \sqrt{3}}{4}$ , $h = \frac{\pi}{12}$ , 即有

$$
t _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 1;
$$

$$
t _ {1} = \frac {\pi}{1 2}, \quad 4 y _ {1} = 4 \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \cdot \frac {1}{4} (2 - \sqrt {3})} = 3. 9 1 3;
$$

$$
t _ {2} = \frac {\pi}{6}, \quad 2 y _ {2} = 2 \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \cdot \frac {1}{4}} = 1. 8 3 3;
$$

$$
t _ {3} = \frac {\pi}{4}, \quad 4 y _ {3} = 4 \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \cdot \frac {1}{2}} = 3. 2 9 3;
$$

$$
t _ {4} = \frac {\pi}{3}, \quad 2 y _ {4} = 2 \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \cdot \frac {3}{4}} = 1. 4 4 2;
$$

$$
t _ {5} = \frac {5 \pi}{1 2}, \quad 4 y _ {5} = 4 \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \cdot \frac {1}{4} (2 + \sqrt {3})} = 2. 5 3 9;
$$

$$
t _ {6} = \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad y _ {6} = 4 \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5}} = 0. 6.
$$

按辛普森公式，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {1 - \frac {1 6}{2 5} \sin^ {2} t} d t \approx \frac {h}{3} \left[ \left(y _ {0} + y _ {6}\right) + 4 \left(y _ {1} + y _ {3} + y _ {5}\right) + 2 \left(y _ {2} + y _ {4}\right) \right] \\ = \frac {\pi}{3 6} (1 + 0. 6 + 3. 9 1 3 + 3. 2 9 3 + 2. 5 3 9 + 1. 8 3 3 + 1. 4 4 2) \\ \approx 1. 2 7 6, \\ \end{array}
$$

所以，椭圆周长的近似值为 $s = 40\int_{0}^{\frac{\pi}{2}}\sqrt{1 - \frac{16}{25}\sin^{2}t}\mathrm{d}t\approx 40\times 1.276 = 51.04.$
