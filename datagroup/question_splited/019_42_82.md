42.82 If $z = 2x^{2} - 3xy + 7y^{2}, x = \sin t, y = \cos t,$ find $dz / dt$

By the chain rule, $\frac{dz}{dt} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial x}\frac{dx}{dt} +\frac{\partial z}{\partial y}\frac{dy}{dt} = (4x - 3y)\cos t + (-3x + 14y)(-\sin t) = (4\sin t - 3\cos t)\cos t - (14\cos t - 3\sin t)\sin t = 3\sin^2 t - 10\sin t\cos t - 3\cos^2 t.$
