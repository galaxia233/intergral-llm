【2965】 $\cos^{2m}x$ （ $m$ 为正整数）.

解 $\cos^{2m}x = \left(\frac{e^{ix} + e^{-ix}}{2}\right)^{2m} = \frac{1}{2^{2m}}\sum_{l = 0}^{2m}C_{2m}^{\prime}e^{(2m - l)ir}e^{-lir} = \frac{1}{2^{2m}} C_{2m}^{m} + \frac{1}{2^{2m}}\left(\sum_{l = 0}^{m - 1} + \sum_{l = m + 1}^{2m}\right)C_{2m}^{l}e^{2(m - l)ir}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m}} C _ {2 m} ^ {m} + \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m}} \left[ \sum_ {l = 0} ^ {m - 1} C _ {2 m} ^ {l} e ^ {2 (m - l) i x} + \sum_ {l ^ {\prime} = 0} ^ {m - 1} C _ {2 m} ^ {2 m - l ^ {\prime}} e ^ {- 2 (m - l ^ {\prime}) i x} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m}} C _ {2 m} ^ {m} + \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m}} \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {m - 1} C _ {2 m} \left[ e ^ {2 (m - s) i x} + e ^ {- 2 (m - s) i x} \right] = \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m}} C _ {2 m} ^ {m} + \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m - 1}} \sum_ {s = 0} ^ {m - 1} C _ {2 m} ^ {s} \cos 2 (m - s) x \\ = \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m}} C _ {2 m} ^ {m} + \frac {1}{2 ^ {2 m - 1}} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {m} C _ {2 m} ^ {m - k} \cos 2 k x. \\ \end{array}
$$

由于上述表达式为一三角多项式，故在 $(-\infty, +\infty)$ 内的傅里叶级数展开式即为它本身.
