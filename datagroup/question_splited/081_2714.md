【2714】证明：下面二级数

$$
\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}} (\alpha > 0) \quad \text {及} \quad \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\beta}} (\beta > 0)
$$

的积当 $\alpha + \beta > 1$ 时是收敛级数，而当 $\alpha + \beta < 1$ 时是发散级数。

证记

$$
\sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\beta}} = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} c _ {n} \quad (\alpha > 0, \beta > 0).
$$

按乘法法则应有

$$
c _ {n} = \sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \left[ \frac {(- 1) ^ {i - 1}}{i ^ {\alpha}} \cdot \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - i}}{(n - i + 1) ^ {\beta}} \right] = (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \sum_ {\substack {1 \leqslant r \leqslant n \\ i \dots j - n - 1}} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} j ^ {\beta}} = (- 1) ^ {n - 1} d _ {n},
$$

其中 $d_{n} = \sum_{1\leqslant i\leqslant n}\frac{1}{i^{*}(n - i + 1)^{\beta}} (n = 1,2,\dots)$

（1）当 $\alpha +\beta \leqslant 1$ 时，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} d _ {n} \geqslant \sum_ {1 \leqslant i \leqslant \frac {n}{2}} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} (n - i + 1) ^ {\beta}} \geqslant \frac {1}{\left(\frac {n}{2}\right) ^ {\alpha}} \sum_ {1 \leqslant i \leqslant \frac {n}{2}} \frac {1}{(n - i + 1) ^ {\beta}} = \frac {1}{\left(\frac {n}{2}\right) ^ {\alpha}} \sum_ {\frac {n}{2} <   j <   n} \frac {1}{j ^ {\beta}} \geqslant \frac {1}{\left(\frac {n}{2}\right) ^ {\alpha}} \int_ {\frac {n}{2}} ^ {n} \frac {d t}{t ^ {\beta}} \\ = 2 ^ {\alpha} \cdot \frac {1}{1 - \beta} \left(1 - \frac {1}{2 ^ {1 - \beta}}\right) n ^ {1 - (a - \beta)}. \\ \end{array}
$$

于是，当 $\alpha + \beta < 1$ 时， $d_{n} \to \infty$ （当 $n \to \infty$ 时）；当 $\alpha + \beta = 1$ 时， $d_{n} \geqslant 2^{\alpha} \cdot \frac{1}{1 - \beta} (1 - \frac{1}{2^{1 - \beta}}) > 0$ ，即当 $\alpha + \beta \leqslant 1$ 时， $d_{n}$ 不趋于零（当 $n \to \infty$ 时），从而知 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} c_{n} = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (-1)^{n-1} d_{n}$ 为发散级数。

（2）当 $\alpha +\beta >1$ 时，有

$$
d _ {n} = \sum_ {1 \leqslant i <   n} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} (n - i + 1) ^ {\beta}} = \sum_ {1 \leqslant i <   \frac {n}{2}} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} (n - i + 1) ^ {\beta}} + \sum_ {\frac {n}{2} <   i \leqslant n} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} (n - i + 1) ^ {\beta}} = \sum_ {1} + \sum_ {2},
$$

其中 $\sum_{1} = \sum_{1\leqslant i\leqslant \frac{n}{2}}\frac{1}{i^{o}(n - i + 1)^{\beta}}\leqslant \frac{1}{\left(\frac{n}{2} + 1\right)^{\beta}}\sum_{1\leqslant i\leqslant \frac{n}{2}}\frac{1}{i^{o}}\leqslant \frac{1}{\left(\frac{n}{2} + 1\right)^{\beta}}\left(1 + \int_{1}^{\frac{n}{2}}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{t^{o}}\right)$

$$
= O \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {\beta}}\right) + () \left(n ^ {\beta} \cdot \int_ {1} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {d t}{t ^ {\alpha}}\right) = O (n ^ {\beta}) + O \left(n ^ {1 - (\alpha - \beta)}\right),
$$

同理有 $\sum_{2} \leqslant () (n^{-\alpha}) + () (n^{1 - (\alpha - \beta)})$

由于 $\alpha > 0, \beta > 0, 1 - (\alpha + \beta) < 0$ ，故有 $d_{n}$ 趋于零：

$$
d _ {n} \leqslant O (n ^ {\alpha}) + O (n ^ {- \beta}) + O (n ^ {1 - \{\alpha + \beta \}}) \rightarrow 0 \quad (\text {当} n \rightarrow \infty \text {时}).
$$

记 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} c_n$ 的部分和为 $S_n = c_1 + c_2 + \cdots + c_n$. 考虑原两级数的部分和

$$
A _ {n} = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k - 1}}{k ^ {\alpha}}, \quad B _ {n} = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k - 1}}{k ^ {\beta}}.
$$

今考察下列差数

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta_ {n} = A _ {n} B _ {n} - S _ {n} = \left(\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {i - 1}}{i ^ {\alpha}}\right) \left(\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {j - 1}}{j ^ {\beta}}\right) - S _ {n} \\ = \left(\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {i - 1}}{i ^ {\alpha}}\right) \left(\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {j - 1}}{j ^ {\beta}}\right) - \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} (- 1) ^ {k - 1} \sum_ {\substack {1 \leqslant i \leqslant k \\ i + j - k + 1}} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} j ^ {\beta}} \\ = \left(\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {i}}{i ^ {\alpha}}\right) \left(\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {j}}{j ^ {\beta}}\right) - \sum_ {\substack {2 \leqslant i - j \leqslant n - 1 \\ 1 \leqslant i, j \leqslant n}} \left(\frac {(- 1) ^ {\alpha}}{i ^ {\alpha}}\right) \left(\frac {(- 1) ^ {j}}{j ^ {\beta}}\right) \\ = \sum_ {s = 1} ^ {2 n - 1} \sum_ {\substack {1 \leqslant i \leqslant s \\ i + j = s + 1}} \frac {(- 1) ^ {s + 1}}{i ^ {\alpha} j ^ {\beta}} - \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \sum_ {\substack {1 \leqslant i \leqslant k \\ i + j = k + 1}} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k - 1}}{i ^ {\alpha} j ^ {\beta}} = \sum_ {s = n - 1} ^ {2 n - 1} (- 1) ^ {s - 1} \sum_ {\substack {1 \leqslant i \leqslant s \\ i + j = n + 1}} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} j ^ {\beta}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

为估计上述差数各项，可看下列乘法表（图 5.1). $A_{n}B_{n}$ 表示下列乘法表正方形各交点上乘积的全部和，而 $S_{n}$ 表示下列乘法表对角线左上角各交点上乘积项 (圆点号处的乘积项) 的总和。于是，剩下的差数实指下列乘法表对角线的右下部分各交点处乘积项 (打 $\times$ 号处的乘积项) 的总和.

于是，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \Delta_ {n} = \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\beta}} \left[ \frac {(- 1) ^ {1}}{2 ^ {\alpha}} + \frac {(- 1) ^ {2}}{3 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}} \right] + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 2}}{(n - 1) ^ {\beta}} \left[ \frac {(- 1) ^ {2}}{3 ^ {\alpha}} + \frac {(- 1) ^ {3}}{4 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}} \right] \\ + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {1}}{2 ^ {\beta}} \left[ \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}} \right] \\ = (- 1) ^ {n} \left\{\frac {1}{n ^ {\beta}} \left(\frac {1}{2 ^ {\alpha}} - \frac {1}{3 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n}}{n ^ {\alpha}}\right) + \frac {1}{(n - 1) ^ {\beta}} \left(\frac {1}{3 ^ {\alpha}} - \frac {1}{4 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}}\right) + \dots + \frac {1}{2 ^ {\beta}} \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {\alpha}}\right) \right\}. \\ \end{array}
$$

因此，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left| \Delta_ {n} \right| = \frac {1}{n ^ {\beta}} \left(\frac {1}{2 ^ {\alpha}} - \frac {1}{3 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n}}{n ^ {\alpha}}\right) + \frac {1}{(n - 1) ^ {\beta}} \left(\frac {1}{3 ^ {\alpha}} - \frac {1}{4 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{n ^ {\alpha}}\right) + \dots + \frac {1}{2 ^ {\beta}} \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {\alpha}}\right) \\ \leqslant \frac {1}{n ^ {\beta}} \cdot \frac {1}{2 ^ {\alpha}} + \frac {1}{(n - 1) ^ {\beta}} \cdot \frac {1}{3 ^ {\alpha}} + \dots + \frac {1}{2 ^ {\beta}} \cdot \frac {1}{n ^ {\alpha}} \\ = \sum_ {\substack {i \rightarrow j = n - 2\\2 \leqslant i, j \leqslant n}} \frac {1}{j ^ {\beta} i ^ {\alpha}} \leqslant \sum_ {\substack {1 \leqslant i, j \leqslant n + 1\\i \rightarrow j = n + 2}} \frac {1}{i ^ {\alpha} j ^ {\beta}} = d ^ {n + 1}. \\ \end{array}
$$

由前已证：当 $\alpha +\beta >1$ 时， $d_{n}\rightarrow 0$ （当 $n\to \infty$ 时），故有 $\Delta_{n}\rightarrow 0$ （当 $n\to \infty$ 时).于是，

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} S _ {n} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} (A _ {n} B _ {n} - \Delta_ {n}) = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} A _ {n} \cdot \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} B _ {n} = \left(\sum_ {i = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {i - 1}}{i ^ {\alpha}}\right)\left(\sum_ {j = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {j - 1}}{j ^ {\beta}}\right),
$$

其中右端两级数的收敛性是由 $\alpha > 0, \beta > 0$ ，按莱布尼茨判别法获得的。于是，当 $\alpha + \beta > 1$ 时，级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n^{\alpha}} (\alpha > 0)$ 与级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(-1)^{n-1}}{n^{\beta}} (\beta > 0)$ 的积 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} c_n$ 为收敛级数。
