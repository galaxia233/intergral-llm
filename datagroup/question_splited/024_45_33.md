45.33 For any scalar function $f(x, y, z)$ with continuous mixed second partial derivatives, show that $\operatorname{curl} \nabla f = 0$ .

$\nabla f = (f_x, f_y, f_z)$ . Then $\operatorname{curl} \nabla f = (f_{zy} - f_{yz}, f_{xz} - f_{zx}, f_{yx} - f_{xy}) = (0, 0, 0) = \mathbf{0}$ . Here, we used the equality of the mixed second partial derivatives.
