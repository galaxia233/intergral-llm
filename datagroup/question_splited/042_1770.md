【1770】 $\int x^{5}(2 - 5x^{3})^{\frac{2}{3}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $2 - 5x^{3} = t$ ，则 $x^{3} = \frac{1}{5} (2 - t)$ ，从而，

$$
x ^ {5} \mathrm {d} x = \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3} \mathrm {d} (x ^ {3}) = - \frac {1}{7 5} (2 - t) \mathrm {d} t,
$$

故得 $\int x^{5}(2 - 5x^{3})^{\frac{2}{3}}\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{1}{75}\int t^{\frac{2}{3}}(2 - t)\mathrm{d}t = -\frac{1}{75}\int (2t^{\frac{2}{3}} - t^{\frac{5}{3}})\mathrm{d}t = -\frac{2}{125} t^{\frac{5}{3}} + \frac{1}{200} t^{\frac{8}{3}} + C$

$$
= - \frac {6 + 2 5 x ^ {3}}{1 0 0 0} (2 - 5 x ^ {3}) ^ {\frac {5}{3}} + C.
$$
