【2330】 $\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x\quad (0 <   a <   b).$

解设 $x = \sqrt{t}$ 则 $\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int_{a^{2}}^{b^{2}}\frac{\sin t}{\sqrt{t}}\mathrm{d}t.$

其次，设 $f(t) = \sin t$ ， $\varphi (t) = (\sqrt{t})^{-1}$ ，则 $\varphi (t)$ 单调下降，且 $\varphi (t) > 0.$ 于是，

$$
\frac {1}{2} \int_ {a ^ {2}} ^ {b ^ {2}} \frac {\sin t}{\sqrt {t}} d t = \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {a ^ {2}} ^ {\xi} \sin t d t = \frac {1}{2 a} (\cos a ^ {2} - \cos \xi) = \frac {1}{a} \sin \frac {\xi + a ^ {2}}{2} \sin \frac {\xi - a ^ {2}}{2} = \frac {1}{a} \theta ,
$$

其中 $a^2 \leqslant \xi \leqslant b^2, |\theta| \leqslant 1.$

所以， $\int_{a}^{b}\sin x^{2}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{\theta}{a} (|\theta |\leqslant 1).$
