32.15 Evaluate $\int_{2}^{\infty}\frac{dx}{x\ln^{2}x}.$

$\int \frac{1}{x\ln^2x} dx = \int (\ln x)^{-2}\cdot \left(\frac{1}{x}\right)dx = -(\ln x)^{-1}.$ Hence,

$$
\int_ {2} ^ {\infty} \frac {d x}{x \ln^ {2} x} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \int_ {2} ^ {v} \frac {1}{x \ln^ {2} x} d x = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} - (\ln x) ^ {- 1} ] _ {2} ^ {v} = \lim  _ {v \rightarrow + \infty} \left(- \frac {1}{\ln v} + \frac {1}{\ln 2}\right) = \frac {1}{\ln 2}
$$
