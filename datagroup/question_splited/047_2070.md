【2070】 $\int x^5\sin 5x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x^{5}\sin 5x\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{\cos 5x}{5}\left(x^{5} - \frac{20x^{3}}{25} +\frac{120x}{625}\right) + \frac{\sin 5x}{25}\left(5x^{4} - \frac{60x^{2}}{25} +\frac{120}{625}\right)^{*}) + C$

$$
= - \frac {\cos 5 x}{5} \left(x ^ {3} - \frac {4 x ^ {3}}{5} + \frac {2 4 x}{1 2 5}\right) + \frac {\sin 5 x}{2 5} \left(5 x ^ {4} - \frac {1 2 x ^ {2}}{5} + \frac {2 4}{1 2 5}\right) + C.
$$

\*) 利用 2067 题的结果.
