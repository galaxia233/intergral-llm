【4036】求曲面 $a z = x y$ 包含在圆柱 $x^{2} + y^{2} = a^{2}$ 内那部分的面积.

解 所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2}} \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {y}{a}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {x}{a}\right) ^ {2}} d x d y = \iint_ {r ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant r ^ {2}} \sqrt {\frac {a ^ {2} + (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})}{a ^ {2}}} d x d y \\ = \frac {1}{a} \iint_ {r ^ {2} - y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2}} \sqrt {a ^ {2} + (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2})} d x d y = \frac {4}{a} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a} r \sqrt {a ^ {2} + r ^ {2}} d r = \frac {2 \pi a ^ {2}}{3} (2 \sqrt {2} - 1). \\ \end{array}
$$
