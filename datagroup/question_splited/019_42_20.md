42.20 If $z = xe^{y / x}$ , show that $x\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = z$ .

In general, if $z = xf(y/x)$ , where $f$ is differentiable, $x\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} + y\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = x[xf'(y/x)(-y/x^2) + f(y/x)] + xyf'(y/x)(1/x) = xf(y/x) = z$ .
