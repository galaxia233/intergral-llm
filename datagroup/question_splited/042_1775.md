【1775】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}} + \mathrm{e}^{x}}.$

提示 令 $e^{\frac{x}{2}} = t$ .

解设 $\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}} = t$ ，则 $\mathrm{e}^{x} = t^{2},\mathrm{d}x = \frac{2\mathrm{d}t}{t}$

故得 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}} + \mathrm{e}^{x}} = 2\int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{t^2(1 + t)} = 2\int \left(\frac{1 - t}{t^2} +\frac{1}{1 + t}\right)\mathrm{d}t = -\frac{2}{t} -2\ln t + 2\ln (1 + t) + C$

$$
= - 2 \mathrm {e} ^ {- \frac {x}{2}} - x + 2 \ln \left(1 + \mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {x}{2}}\right) + C.
$$
