44.40 Find the volume of the solid cut out from the sphere $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} \leq 4$ by the cylinder $x^{2} + y^{2} = 1$ (see Fig. 44-24).

It suffices to multiply by 8 the volume of the solid in the first octant. Use cylindrical coordinates. The sphere is $r^2 + z^2 = 4$ and the cylinder is $r = 1$ . Thus, we have $V = 8\int_{0}^{\pi /2}\int_{0}^{1}\sqrt{4 - r^2} drd\theta = 8\int_{0}^{\pi /2}\left[-\frac{1}{2}\cdot \frac{2}{3} (4 - r^2)^{3 / 2}\right]_0^1 d\theta = -\frac{8}{3}\int_{0}^{\pi /2}\left[(3)^{3 / 2} - 8\right]d\theta = \frac{8}{3} (8 - 3\sqrt{3})\int_{0}^{\pi /2}d\theta = \frac{4\pi}{3} (8 - 3\sqrt{3}).$
