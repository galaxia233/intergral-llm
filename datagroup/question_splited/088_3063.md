【3063】 $\prod_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{(2n + 1)(2n + 7)}{(2n + 3)(2n + 5)}.$

证 由于部分乘积

$$
\begin{array}{l} P _ {n} = \frac {3 \cdot 9}{5 \cdot 7} \cdot \frac {5 \cdot 1 1}{7 \cdot 9} \cdot \frac {7 \cdot 1 3}{9 \cdot 1 1} \dots \frac {(2 n - 3) (2 n + 3)}{(2 n - 1) (2 n + 1)} \cdot \frac {(2 n - 1) (2 n + 5)}{(2 n + 1) (2 n + 3)} \cdot \frac {(2 n + 1) (2 n + 7)}{(2 n + 3) (2 n + 5)} \\ = \frac {3}{7} \cdot \frac {2 n + 7}{2 n + 3} \rightarrow \frac {3}{7} \quad (\text {当} n \rightarrow \infty \text {时}), \\ \end{array}
$$

故无穷乘积 $\prod_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{(2n+1)(2n+7)}{(2n+3)(2n+5)}$ 收敛，且其值为 $\frac{3}{7}$ .
