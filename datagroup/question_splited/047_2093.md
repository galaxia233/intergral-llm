解 $\int \left(1 - \frac{2}{x}\right)^{2} \mathrm{e}^{x} \mathrm{~d} x = \int \left(1 - \frac{4}{x} + \frac{4}{x^{2}}\right) \mathrm{e}^{x} \mathrm{~d} x = \mathrm{e}^{x} - 4 \operatorname{li}(\mathrm{e}^{x}) - 4 \int \mathrm{e}^{x} \mathrm{~d} \left(\frac{1}{x}\right)$

$$
= \mathrm {e} ^ {x} - 4 \operatorname {l i} \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {x}\right) - \frac {4}{x} \mathrm {e} ^ {x} + 4 \int \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{x} \mathrm {d} x = \mathrm {e} ^ {x} \left(1 - \frac {4}{x}\right) + C.
