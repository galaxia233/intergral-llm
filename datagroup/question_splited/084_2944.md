【2944】在区间 $(-\pi, \pi)$ 内展开 $f(x) = \pi^2 - x^2$ .

解 因为 $f(x) = \pi^2 - x^2$ 为偶函数，从而， $b_n = 0$ ，且

$$
a _ {0} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} (\pi^ {2} - x ^ {2}) d x = \frac {2}{\pi} \left(\pi^ {2} x - \frac {1}{3} x ^ {3}\right) \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\pi} = \frac {4 \pi^ {2}}{3},
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {n} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \left(\pi^ {2} - x ^ {2}\right) \cos n x d x = - \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x ^ {2} \cos n x d x = - \frac {2}{n \pi} x ^ {2} \sin n x \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} + \frac {4}{n \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} x \sin n x d x \right. \\ = - \frac {4}{n ^ {2} \pi} x \cos n x \left| _ {0} ^ {\pi} + \frac {4}{n ^ {2} \pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \cos n x d x = \frac {4}{n ^ {2}} (- 1) ^ {n + 1}, \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

故按展开定理， $f(x)$ 可展开为 $\frac{2\pi^2}{3} + 4\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{(-1)^{n + 1}}{n^2}\cos nx = \pi^2 - x^2$ （ $-\pi < x < \pi$ ）
