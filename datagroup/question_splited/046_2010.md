【2010】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt[3]{\tan x}}.$

解设 $\sqrt[3]{\tan x} = t$ ，则 $x = \arctan t^3$ ， $\mathrm{dx} = \frac{3t^2}{1 + t^6}\mathrm{dt}$ 代人得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt [ 3 ]{\tan x}} = 3 \int \frac {t \mathrm {d} t}{1 + t ^ {6}} = \frac {3}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(t ^ {2}\right)}{1 + \left(t ^ {2}\right) ^ {3}} = \frac {3}{2} \left[ \frac {1}{6} \ln \frac {\left(t ^ {2} + 1\right) ^ {2}}{t ^ {4} - t ^ {2} + 1} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \frac {2 t ^ {2} - 1}{\sqrt {3}} \right] ^ {*}) + C \\ = \frac {1}{4} \ln \frac {(t ^ {2} + 1) ^ {2}}{t ^ {4} - t ^ {2} + 1} + \frac {\sqrt {3}}{2} \arctan \frac {2 t ^ {2} - 1}{\sqrt {3}} + C, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $t = \sqrt[3]{\tan x}$

\*) 利用1881题的结果.
