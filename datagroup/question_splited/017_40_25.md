40.25 Find the vector $\overrightarrow{PQ}$ from $P = (1, -2, 4)$ to $Q = (3, 4, 3)$ .

In general, the vector $\overrightarrow{PQ}$ from $P(x_{1},y_{1},z_{1})$ to $Q(x_{2},y_{2},z_{2})$ is $\overrightarrow{PQ} = (x_2 - x_1,\quad y_2 - y_1,\quad z_2 - z_1)$ . In this case, $\overrightarrow{PQ} = (2,6, - 1)$ .
