40.48 Verify the identity $|\mathbf{A}\times \mathbf{B}|^2 = |\mathbf{A}|^2 |\mathbf{B}|^2 -(\mathbf{A}\cdot \mathbf{B})^2.$

Let $\theta$ be the angle between $\mathbf{A}$ and $\mathbf{B}$ . Then, $|\mathbf{A}|^2 |\mathbf{B}|^2 - (\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B})^2 = |\mathbf{A}|^2 |\mathbf{B}|^2 - |\mathbf{A}|^2 |\mathbf{B}|^2 \cos^2 \theta = |\mathbf{A}|^2 |\mathbf{B}|^2 (1 - \cos^2 \theta) = |\mathbf{A}|^2 |\mathbf{B}|^2 \sin^2 \theta = |\mathbf{A} \times \mathbf{B}|^2$ . [Compare this result with Cauchy's inequality, Problem 33.28.]
