【2867】 $\ln (1 + x + x^2 +x^3)$

解 $\ln (1 + x + x^2 +x^3) = \ln [(1 + x)(1 + x^2)] = \ln (1 + x) + \ln (1 + x^2).$

但

$$
\ln (1 + x) = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {x ^ {n}}{n} \quad (- 1 <   x \leqslant 1),
$$

$$
\ln (1 + x ^ {2}) = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {x ^ {2 n}}{n} \quad (- 1 \leqslant x \leqslant 1),
$$

故当 $-1 < x \leqslant 1$ 时，有

$$
\begin{array}{l} \ln (1 + x + x ^ {2} + x ^ {3}) = \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {x ^ {n}}{n} + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n - 1} \frac {x ^ {2 n}}{n} \\ = \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {m - 1} \frac {x ^ {m}}{m} + \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {\left[ \frac {m}{2} \right] - 1} [ 1 + (- 1) ^ {m} ] \frac {x ^ {m}}{m} \\ = \sum_ {m = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {(- 1) ^ {m - 1} + (- 1) ^ {\left[ \frac {m}{2} \right] - 1} [ 1 + (- 1) ^ {m} ]}{m} x ^ {m}. \\ \end{array}
$$
