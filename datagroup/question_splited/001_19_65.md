19.65 Find $\int \frac{(x - 2)(x + 3)}{\sqrt{x}} dx.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {(x - 2) (x + 3)}{\sqrt {x}} d x = \int \frac {x ^ {2} + x - 6}{\sqrt {x}} d x = \int \left(x ^ {3 / 2} + x ^ {1 / 2} - 6 x ^ {- 1 / 2}\right) d x = \frac {2}{5} x ^ {5 / 2} + \frac {2}{3} x ^ {3 / 2} - 1 2 x ^ {1 / 2} + C \\ = \frac {2}{1 5} x ^ {1 / 2} (3 x ^ {2} + 5 x - 9 0) + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
