解 $\int \frac{\arctan^{\frac{x}{2}}}{\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}}(1 + \mathrm{e}^{x})}\mathrm{d}x = \int \left(\mathrm{e}^{-\frac{x}{2}} - \frac{\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}}}{1 + \mathrm{e}^{x}}\right)\arctan^{\frac{x}{2}}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
