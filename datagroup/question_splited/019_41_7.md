41.73 Find rectangular coordinates for the point with cylindrical coordinates $(1 / \sqrt{3}, 7\pi / 6, 4)$ .

$$
x = r \cos \theta = \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \cos \frac {7 \pi}{6} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \left(- \frac {\sqrt {3}}{2}\right) = - \frac {1}{2} \quad y = r \sin \theta = \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \sin \frac {7 \pi}{6} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {3}} \left(- \frac {1}{2}\right) = - \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {3}} = - \frac {\sqrt {3}}{6}
$$
