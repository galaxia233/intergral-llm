【4288】 $\int_{(x_1,y_1,z_1)}^{(x_2,y_2,z_2)}f(x + y + z)(\mathrm{d}x + \mathrm{d}y + \mathrm{d}z)$ ，其中 $f$ 为连续函数.

解 令 $F(x, y, z) = \int_{0}^{x + y + z} f(u) \mathrm{d}u$ . 由于 $f(u)$ 是连续函数，故

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = f (x + y + z), \quad F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = f (x + y + z), \quad F _ {z} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = f (x + y + z),
$$

并且这些偏导数都是连续的。因此，$F(x, y, z)$ 可微，且

$$
\mathrm {d} F (x, y, z) = F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) \mathrm {d} x + F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) \mathrm {d} y + F _ {z} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) \mathrm {d} z = f (x + y + z) (\mathrm {d} x + \mathrm {d} y + \mathrm {d} z).
$$

于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {\left(r _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right)} ^ {\left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right)} f (x + y + z) (\mathrm {d} x + \mathrm {d} y + \mathrm {d} z) = F (x, y, z) \left| \begin{array}{l} \left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right) \\ \left(r _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right) \end{array} = F \left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right) - F \left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right) \right. \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {2} + y _ {2} + z _ {2}} f (u) d u - \int_ {0} ^ {x _ {1} + y _ {1} + z _ {1}} f (u) d u = \int_ {x _ {1} + y _ {1} + z _ {1}} ^ {x _ {2} + y _ {2} + z _ {2}} f (u) d u. \\ \end{array}
$$
