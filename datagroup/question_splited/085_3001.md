【3001】设 $P(x) = a_0 + a_1x + \dots +a_mx^m$ .求级数 $\sum_{n = 0}^{\infty}\frac{P(n)}{n!} x^n$ 的和.

解令 $P(n) = a_0 + a_1n + \dots +a_m n^m\equiv \alpha_0 + \alpha_1n + \dots +\alpha_m n(n - 1)\dots (n - m + 1),$

其中 $\alpha_{i}(i = 0,1,\dots ,m)$ 可由上述恒等式求出，则

$$
\begin{array}{l} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {P (n)}{n !} x ^ {n} = \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {\alpha_ {0} + \alpha_ {1} n + \cdots + \alpha_ {m} n (n - 1) \cdots (n - m + 1)}{n !} x ^ {n} \\ = \alpha_ {0} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} \frac {x ^ {n}}{n !} + \alpha_ {1} x \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {x ^ {n - 1}}{(n - 1) !} + \dots + \alpha_ {m} x ^ {m} \sum_ {n = m} ^ {\infty} \frac {x ^ {n - m}}{(n - m) !} \\ = \alpha_ {0} e ^ {x} + \alpha_ {1} x e ^ {x} + \dots + \alpha_ {m} x ^ {m} e ^ {x} = e ^ {x} (\alpha_ {0} + \alpha_ {1} x + \dots + \alpha_ {m} x ^ {m}). \\ \end{array}
$$
