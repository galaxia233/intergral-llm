【4229】 $\int_{C} \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \, \mathrm{d}s$ ，其中 $C$ 为圆周 $x^2 + y^2 = ax$ .

解 对于上半圆周，弧长的微分为

$$
\mathrm {d} s = \sqrt {1 + \left(\frac {a - 2 x}{2 y}\right) ^ {2}} \mathrm {d} x = \frac {a}{2 y} \mathrm {d} x = \frac {a}{2 \sqrt {a x - x ^ {2}}} \mathrm {d} x \cdot (0 \leqslant x \leqslant a).
$$

于是， $\int_{c} \sqrt{x^2 + y^2} \, \mathrm{d}s = 2\int_{a}^{a} \sqrt{ax} \frac{a}{2\sqrt{ax - x^2}} \, \mathrm{d}x = a\sqrt{a}\int_{a}^{a} \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{a - x}} = 2a^2.$
