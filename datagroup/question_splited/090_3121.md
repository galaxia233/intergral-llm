【3121】求在给定点按下表取值的最低次的 n 次多项式 P_n(x)

<table><tr><td>x</td><td>-2</td><td>0</td><td>4</td><td>5</td></tr><tr><td>y</td><td>5</td><td>1</td><td>-3</td><td>1</td></tr></table>

$P_{n}(-1), P_{n}(1), P_{n}(6)$ 近似地等于什么？

解 $x_0 = -2, x_1 = 0, x_2 = 4, x_3 = 5;$
$$
y _ {0} = 5, y _ {1} = 1, y _ {2} = - 3, y _ {3} = 1.
$$
$$
\begin{array}{l} P _ {3} (x) = \frac {(x - x _ {1}) (x - x _ {2}) (x - x _ {3})}{(x _ {0} - x _ {1}) (x _ {0} - x _ {2}) (x _ {0} - x _ {3})} y _ {0} + \frac {(x - x _ {0}) (x - x _ {2}) (x - x _ {3})}{(x _ {1} - x _ {0}) (x _ {1} - x _ {2}) (x _ {1} - x _ {3})} y _ {1} \\ + \frac {(x - x _ {0}) (x - x _ {1}) (x - x _ {3})}{(x _ {2} - x _ {0}) (x _ {2} - x _ {1}) (x _ {2} - x _ {3})} y _ {2} + \frac {(x - x _ {0}) (x - x _ {1}) (x - x _ {2})}{(x _ {3} - x _ {0}) (x _ {3} - x _ {1}) (x _ {3} - x _ {2})} y _ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$
以 $(x_{i},y_{i})(i = 0,1,2,3)$ 代人上式，化简即得
$$
\begin{array}{l} P _ {3} (x) = 1 - \frac {5 5}{2 1} x - \frac {1}{1 4} x ^ {2} + \frac {5}{4 2} x ^ {3}. \\ P _ {3} (- 1) = 1 + \frac {5 5}{2 1} - \frac {1}{1 4} - \frac {5}{4 2} \approx 3. 4 3, \\ P _ {3} (1) = 1 - \frac {5 5}{2 1} - \frac {1}{1 4} + \frac {5}{4 2} \approx - 1. 5 7, \\ P _ {3} (6) = 1 - \frac {1 1 0}{7} - \frac {1 8}{7} + \frac {1 8 0}{7} \approx 8. 4 3. \\ \end{array}
$$
