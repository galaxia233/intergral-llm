42.40 For $f(x, y) = 3x^4 - 2x^3y^2 + 7y$ , find $f_{xx}, f_{xy}, f_{yx}$ , and $f_{yy}$ .

$$
f _ {x} = 1 2 x ^ {3} - 6 x ^ {2} y ^ {2}, \quad f _ {y} = - 4 x ^ {3} y + 7, \quad f _ {x x} = 3 6 x ^ {2} - 1 2 x y ^ {2}, \quad f _ {y y} = - 4 x ^ {3}, \quad f _ {x y} = - 1 2 x ^ {2} y, \quad f _ {y x} = - 1 2 x ^ {2} y.
$$
