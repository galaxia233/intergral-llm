【2862】 $\frac{1}{1 + x + x^2}$

解 $\frac{1}{1 + x + x^2} = \frac{1}{\sqrt[3]{x + \frac{1 - \sqrt{3}}{2}}} - \frac{1}{x + \frac{1 + \sqrt{3}}{2}}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{i \sqrt {3}} \left[ \frac {1 + i \sqrt {3}}{2} \left(1 + \frac {1 + i \sqrt {3}}{2} x\right) ^ {- 1} - \frac {1 - i \sqrt {3}}{2} \left(1 + \frac {1 - i \sqrt {3}}{2} x\right) ^ {- 1} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{i \sqrt {3}} \left[ \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \left(\frac {1 + i \sqrt {3}}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} x ^ {n} - \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \left(\frac {1 - i \sqrt {3}}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} x ^ {n} \right] \\ = \frac {1}{i \sqrt {3}} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} (- 1) ^ {n} \left[ \left(\frac {1 + i \sqrt {3}}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} - \left(\frac {1 - i \sqrt {3}}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} \right] x ^ {n}. \\ \end{array}
$$

由于

$$
\begin{array}{l} (- 1) ^ {n} \left[ \left(\frac {1 + i \sqrt {3}}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} - \left(\frac {1 - i \sqrt {3}}{2}\right) ^ {n + 1} \right] \\ = (- 1) ^ {n} \left[ \left(\cos \frac {\pi}{3} + i \sin \frac {\pi}{3}\right) ^ {n + 1} - \left(\cos \frac {\pi}{3} - i \sin \frac {\pi}{3}\right) ^ {n + 1} \right] \\ = (- 1) ^ {n} \left[ \left(\cos \frac {n + 1}{3} \pi + i \sin \frac {n + 1}{3} \pi\right) - \left(\cos \frac {n + 1}{3} \pi - i \sin \frac {n + 1}{3} \pi\right) \right] \\ = (- 1) ^ {n} \cdot 2 i \sin \frac {n + 1}{3} \pi = 2 i \cdot (- 1) ^ {n} \sin \left[ (n + 1) \pi - \frac {2 (n + 1)}{3} \pi \right] \\ = 2 \mathrm {i} \cdot (- 1) ^ {n} \left[ - \cos (n + 1) \pi \sin \frac {2 (n + 1)}{3} \pi \right] \\ = 2 \mathrm {i} \cdot (- 1) ^ {n} \cdot (- 1) \cdot (- 1) ^ {n + 1} \sin \frac {2 (n + 1)}{3} \pi = 2 \mathrm {i} \sin \frac {2 (n + 1)}{3} \pi , \\ \end{array}
$$

故得

$$
\frac {1}{1 + 1 + x ^ {2}} = \frac {2}{\sqrt {3}} \sum_ {n = 0} ^ {\infty} x ^ {n} \sin \frac {2 (n + 1)}{3} \pi ,
$$

其中 $|x| < \min \left(\frac{2}{|1 + \mathrm{i}\sqrt{3}|},\frac{2}{|1 - \mathrm{i}\sqrt{3}|}\right) = 1$ ，即 $|x| < 1$
