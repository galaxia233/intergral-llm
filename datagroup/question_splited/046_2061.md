【2061】 $\int \frac{\sin^2x}{\cos^2x\sqrt{\tan x}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{\sin^2 x}{\cos^2 x \sqrt{\tan x}} \mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{\sin^2 x \mathrm{d}(\tan x)}{\sqrt{\tan x}} = 2 \int \sin^2 x \mathrm{d}(\sqrt{\tan x}) = 2 \int (1 - \cos^2 x) \mathrm{d}(\sqrt{\tan x})$

$$
= 2 \sqrt {\tan x} - 2 \int \frac {d (\sqrt {\tan x})}{1 + \tan^ {2} x} = 2 \sqrt {\tan x} - \frac {1}{2 \sqrt {2}} \ln \frac {\tan x + \sqrt {2 \tan x} + 1}{\tan x - \sqrt {2 \tan x} + 1} + \frac {1}{\sqrt {2}} \arctan \frac {\sqrt {2 \tan x}}{\tan x - 1} *) + C (\tan x > 0).
$$

\*) 利用1884题的结果.
