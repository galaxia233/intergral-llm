27.58 Find an equation of the tangent line to the graph of $y = \sin^{-1}\frac{x}{3}$ at the origin.

$y' = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - (x/3)^2}} \left( \frac{1}{3} \right)$ , which is $\frac{1}{3}$ when $x = 0$ . Hence, the slope of the tangent line is $\frac{1}{3}$ , and, since it goes through the origin, its equation is $y = \frac{1}{3} x$ .
