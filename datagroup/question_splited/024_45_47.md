45.47 State Green's theorem in the plane.

If $\mathcal{C}$ is a closed plane curve bounding a region $\mathcal{R}$ and $\mathbf{n}$ denotes the exterior unit normal vector along $\mathcal{C}$ , then $\int_{\mathcal{C}} \mathbf{F} \cdot \mathbf{n} ds = \iint_{\mathcal{R}} \operatorname{div} \mathbf{F} dA$ for any vector field $\mathbf{F}$ on $\mathcal{R}$ .
