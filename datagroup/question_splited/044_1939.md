【1939】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x)^2\sqrt{1 - x^2}}.$

提示 令 $\sqrt{\frac{1 - x}{1 + x}} = t.$

解 设 $\sqrt{\frac{1 - x}{1 + x}} = t$ ，则 $x = \frac{1 - t^2}{1 + t^2}, \mathrm{d}x = -\frac{4t}{(1 + t^2)^2}\mathrm{d}t, 1 - x = \frac{2t^2}{1 + t^2}, \sqrt{1 - x^2} = \frac{2t}{1 + t^2}$ .

代入得 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(1 - x)^2\sqrt{1 - x^2}} = -\frac{1}{2}\int \frac{1 + t^2}{t^4}\mathrm{d}t = \frac{1}{6t^3} +\frac{1}{2t} +C = \frac{2 - x}{3(1 - x)^2}\sqrt{1 - x^2} +C.$
