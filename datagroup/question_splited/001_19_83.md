19.83 Find the family of curves for which the slope of the tangent line at $(x,y)$ is $(1 + x) / (1 - y)$ .

\(
dy / dx = (1 + x) / (1 - y). \quad \text{Then} \quad \int (1 - y) dy = \int (1 + x) dx, \quad y - \frac{1}{2} y^2 = x + \frac{1}{2} x^2 + C, \quad x^2 + y^2 + 2x - 2y + C = 0, \quad (x + 1)^2 + (y - 1)^2 = C_1. \quad \text{With} \quad C_1 > 0, \quad \text{this is a family of circles with center at} (-1, 1).
\)
