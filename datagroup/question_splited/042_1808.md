【1808】 $\int x\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x}\mathrm{d}(x^2) = \frac{x^2}{2}\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x} -\int \frac{x^2}{1 - x^2}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{x^2}{2}\ln \frac{1 + x}{1 - x} +\int \left(1 - \frac{1}{1 - x^2}\right)\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= x - \frac {1 - x ^ {2}}{2} \ln \frac {1 + x}{1 - x} + C.
$$
