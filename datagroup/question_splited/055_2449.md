【2449】 $r = \frac{p}{1 + \cos\varphi}$ （ $|\varphi |\leqslant \frac{\pi}{2})$

解 $r' = \frac{p\sin\varphi}{(1 + \cos\varphi)^2}, \sqrt{r^2 + r'^2} = \frac{2p\cos\frac{\varphi}{2}}{(1 + \cos\varphi)^2}$ . 所求的弧长为

$$
\begin{array}{l} s = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {2 p \cos \frac {\varphi}{2}}{(1 + \cos \varphi) ^ {2}} d \varphi = \frac {p}{2} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sec^ {3} \frac {\varphi}{2} d \varphi = \frac {p}{2} \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sec \frac {\varphi}{2} (1 + \tan^ {2} \frac {\varphi}{2}) d \varphi \\ = p \left\{\int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \frac {\mathrm {d} \varphi}{\cos \frac {\varphi}{2}} + 2 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sqrt {\sec^ {2} \frac {\varphi}{2} - 1} \mathrm {d} \left(\sec \frac {\varphi}{2}\right) \right\} \\ = 2 p \left\{\ln \tan \left(\frac {\pi}{4} + \frac {\varphi}{4}\right) + \frac {\sec \frac {\varphi}{2}}{2} \sqrt {\sec^ {2} \frac {\varphi}{2} - 1} - \frac {1}{2} \ln \left(\sec \frac {\varphi}{2} + \tan \frac {\varphi}{2}\right) \right\} \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \\ = p \left\{\sqrt {2} + \ln (\sqrt {2} + 1) \right\}. \\ \end{array}
$$
