43.15 In what direction should one initially travel, starting at the origin, to obtain the most rapid rate of increase of the function $f(x,y,z) = (3 - x + y)^2 + (4x - y + z + 2)^3$ ?

The appropriate direction is that of $\nabla f = (f_x, f_y, f_z) = (2(3 - x + y)(-1) + 3(4x - y + z + 2)^2(4), 2(3 - x + y) + 3(4x - y + z + 2)^2(-1), 3(4x - y + z + 2)^2) = (42, -6, 12) = 6(7, -1, 2)$ .
