【1753】 $\int \sin^2 3x\sin^3 2x\mathrm{d}x.$

提示 注意 $\sin^2 3x\sin^3 2x = \frac{3}{8}\sin 2x + \frac{3}{16}\sin 4x - \frac{1}{8}\sin 6x - \frac{3}{16}\sin 8x + \frac{1}{16}\sin 12x.$

解因为 $\sin^2 3x\sin^3 2x = \frac{1}{2} (1 - \cos 6x)\cdot \frac{1}{4} (3\sin 2x - \sin 6x).$

$$
= \frac {1}{8} (3 \sin 2 x - 3 \cos 6 x \sin 2 x - \sin 6 x + \sin 6 x \cos 6 x)
$$

$$
= \frac {3}{8} \sin 2 x + \frac {3}{1 6} \sin 4 x - \frac {1}{8} \sin 6 x - \frac {3}{1 6} \sin 8 x + \frac {1}{1 6} \sin 1 2 x,
$$

所以， $\int \sin^2 3x\sin^3 2x\mathrm{d}x = -\frac{3}{16}\cos 2x - \frac{3}{64}\cos 4x + \frac{1}{48}\cos 6x + \frac{3}{128}\cos 8x - \frac{1}{192}\cos 12x + C.$
