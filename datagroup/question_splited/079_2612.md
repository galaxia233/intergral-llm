【2612】 $a_{n} = \left[\mathrm{e} - \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^{n}\right]^{p}$

解 $\left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)^n = e^{\pi \ln \left(1 + \frac{1}{n}\right)} = e^{n\left[\frac{1}{n} - \frac{1}{2n^2} + O^* \left(\frac{1}{n^3}\right)\right]} = e^{1 - \frac{1}{2n} + O^* \left(\frac{1}{n^2}\right)}.$

由于 $a_{n} > 0$ ，且

$$
a _ {n} = \left[ e \left(1 - e ^ {- \frac {1}{2 n} + O ^ {*} \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right)}\right) \right] ^ {p} \sim e ^ {p} \left[ \frac {1}{2 n} + O ^ {*} \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {2}}\right) \right] ^ {p} = O ^ {*} \left(\frac {1}{n ^ {p}}\right),
$$

故仅当 $p > 1$ 时，级数收敛
