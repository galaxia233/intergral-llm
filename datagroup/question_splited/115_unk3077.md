【 4 1 1 4 】 $\quad \frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} + \frac {z ^ {4}}{c ^ {4}} = 1.$

解 令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi, z = z$ ，则得体积

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} a b r d r \int_ {- c (1 - r ^ {2}) \frac {1}{4}} ^ {c (1 - r ^ {2}) \frac {1}{4}} d z = 4 \pi a b c \int_ {0} ^ {1} r (1 - r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {1}{4}} d r = 4 \pi a b c \left[ - \frac {2}{5} (1 - r ^ {2}) ^ {\frac {5}{4}} \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {8}{5} \pi a b c. \\ \left(\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}\right) ^ {2} + \frac {z ^ {4}}{c ^ {4}} = 1. \tag {4115} \\ \end{array}
$$
