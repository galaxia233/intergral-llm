【1846】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{a + bx^2}} (b\neq 0).$

解 当 $b > 0$ 时，

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {a + b x ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {b}} \ln | x \sqrt {b} + \sqrt {a + b x ^ {2}} | + C;
$$

当 $a > 0$ 及 $b < 0$ 时，

$$
\int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\sqrt {a + b x ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {- b}} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (\sqrt {- b} x)}{\sqrt {(\sqrt {a}) ^ {2} - (\sqrt {- b} x) ^ {2}}} = \frac {1}{\sqrt {- b}} \arcsin \left(x \sqrt {- \frac {b}{a}}\right) + C.
$$
