【2423】 $r = a\cos \varphi, r = a(\cos \varphi + \sin \varphi)\left[M\left(\frac{a}{2}, 0\right) \in S\right].$

解如图 4.32 所示，

$$
\mid O A \mid = a, \quad \alpha = - \frac {\pi}{4},
$$

阴影部分即为所求的面积。

曲线 $L_{1}:r = a\cos \varphi ,L_{2}:r = a(\cos \varphi +\sin \varphi)$ .所求的面积为

$$
S = \frac {\pi}{2} \left(\frac {a}{2}\right) ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} \int_ {\frac {- \pi}{4}} ^ {0} a ^ {2} (\cos \varphi + \sin \varphi) ^ {2} d \varphi = \frac {a ^ {2} (\pi - 1)}{4}.
$$
