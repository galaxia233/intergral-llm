【2954】 $f(x) = \arcsin (\cos x)$

解 $f(x)$ 是以 $2\pi$ 为周期的连续周期函数，又 $f(x)$ 为偶函数，从而， $b_{n} = 0$ ，且

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {0} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \arcsin (\cos x) d x = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) d x = 0, \\ a _ {n} = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \arcsin (\cos x) \cos n x d x = \frac {2}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi} \left(\frac {\pi}{2} - x\right) \cos n x d x \\ = \frac {2}{\pi} \left[ \frac {\pi}{2 n} \sin n x - \frac {x}{n} \sin n x - \frac {1}{n ^ {2}} \cos n x \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\pi} = \frac {2}{\pi} \frac {1}{n ^ {2}} [ 1 - (- 1) ^ {n} ] \\ = \left\{ \begin{array}{l l} 0, & n = 2 k (k = 1, 2, 3, \dots), \\ \frac {4}{(2 k + 1) ^ {2} \pi}, & n = 2 k + 1 (k = 0, 1, 2, \dots), \end{array} \right. \\ \end{array}
$$

故按展开定理， $f(x)$ 可展开为 $\frac{4}{\pi}\sum_{k = 0}^{\infty}\frac{\cos(2k + 1)x}{(2k + 1)^2} = \arcsin (\cos x)\quad (-\infty < x < + \infty).$
