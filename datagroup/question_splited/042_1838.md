【1838】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{3x^2 - 2x - 1}$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{3x^2 - 2x - 1} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^2 - \frac{2}{3}x - \frac{1}{3}} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(x - \frac{1}{3}\right)}{\left(x - \frac{1}{3}\right)^2 - \left(\frac{2}{3}\right)^2}$

$$
= - \frac {1}{3} \cdot \frac {3}{4} \ln \left| \frac {\frac {2}{3} + \left(x - \frac {1}{3}\right)}{\frac {2}{3} - \left(x - \frac {1}{3}\right)} \right| + C _ {1} = \frac {1}{4} \ln \left| \frac {x - 1}{3 x + 1} \right| + C.
$$
