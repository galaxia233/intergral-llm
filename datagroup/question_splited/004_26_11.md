26.11 If an amount of money earning 8 percent per year is compounded 10 times per year, what is the equivalent yearly rate of return?

By Problem 26.8, the amount after 1 year will be $y_0(1 + \frac{8}{1000})^{10} = y_0(1.008)^{10} \approx 1.0829y$ . Thus, the equivalent yearly rate is 8.29 percent.
