44.10 Evaluate $I = \int_0^1\int_{y^4}^{y^2}\sqrt{y / x}  dx  dy.$

$\int_{y^4}^{y^2}\sqrt{y / x} dx = 2\sqrt{y}\sqrt{x} \left]_{y^4}^{y^2} = 2\sqrt{y} (y - y^2) = 2(y^{3 / 2} - y^{5 / 2})\right.$ . Therefore, $I = 2\int_{0}^{1}(y^{3 / 2} - y^{5 / 2})dy = 2(\frac{2}{5} y^{5 / 2} - \frac{2}{7} y^{7 / 2})\big]_0^1 = 2(\frac{2}{5} -\frac{2}{7}) = \frac{8}{35}$ .
