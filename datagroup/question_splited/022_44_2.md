44.2 Evaluate the iterated integral $I = \int_0^1\int_{x^2}^{x^3}(x^2 +y^2)dydx.$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {x ^ {2}} ^ {x ^ {3}} \left(x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}\right) d y = \left(x ^ {2} y + \frac {1}{3} y ^ {3}\right) \bigg ] _ {x ^ {2}} ^ {x ^ {3}} = \left(x ^ {5} + \frac {1}{3} x ^ {9}\right) - \left(x ^ {4} + \frac {1}{3} x ^ {6}\right). \\ \frac {1}{6} x ^ {6} + \frac {1}{3 0} x ^ {1 0} - \frac {1}{5} x ^ {5} - \frac {1}{2 1} x ^ {7} \bigg ] _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {1}{6} + \frac {1}{3 0} - \frac {1}{5} - \frac {1}{2 1} = - \frac {1}{2 1}. \end{array}
