【2000】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos^3 x}$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos^3 x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)}{\sin^3\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)} = -\frac{\cos\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)}{2\sin^2\left(x + \frac{\pi}{2}\right)} + \frac{1}{2}\ln \left|\tan \left(\frac{x + \frac{\pi}{2}}{2}\right)\right|^*) + C$

$$
= \frac {\sin x}{2 \cos^ {2} x} + \frac {1}{2} \ln \left| \tan \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\pi}{4}\right) \right| + C.
$$

\*）利用1999题的结果.
