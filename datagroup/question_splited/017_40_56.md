40.56 Find the volume of the parallelepiped whose edges are $\overrightarrow{OA}$ , $\overrightarrow{OB}$ , $\overrightarrow{OC}$ , where $O = (0, 0, 0)$ , $A = (1, 2, 3)$ , $B = (1, 1, 2)$ , $C = (2, 1, 1)$ .

The volume is $|\overrightarrow{OA} \cdot (\overrightarrow{OB} \times \overrightarrow{OC})| = |(1,2,3) \cdot ((1,1,2) \times (2,1,1))| = |(1,2,3) \cdot (-1,3,-1)| = 1 + 6 - 3 = 2.$
