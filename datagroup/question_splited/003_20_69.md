20.69 Apply Simpson's rule with $n = 4$ to approximate $\int_0^1 x^4 dx$ .

We obtain, with $\Delta x = \frac{1}{4}$ , $\frac{1}{12} [0^4 + 4(\frac{1}{4})^4 + 2(\frac{1}{2})^4 + 4(\frac{3}{4})^4 + 1^4] = \frac{1}{12} (\frac{1}{64} + \frac{1}{8} + \frac{81}{64} + 1) = \frac{154}{768} \approx 0.2005$ , which is close to the exact value $\int_0^1 x^4 dx = \frac{1}{5} x^5\bigg]_0^1 = \frac{1}{5} = 0.2$ .
