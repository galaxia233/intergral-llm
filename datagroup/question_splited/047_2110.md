【2110】 $\int \arcsin \frac{2\sqrt{x}}{1 + x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \arcsin \frac{2\sqrt{x}}{1 + x}\mathrm{d}x = \int \arcsin \frac{2\sqrt{x}}{1 + x}\mathrm{d}(x + 1) = (x + 1)\arcsin \frac{2\sqrt{x}}{1 + x} -\operatorname {sgn}(1 - x)\int \frac{1}{\sqrt{x}}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= (x + 1) \arcsin \frac {2 \sqrt {x}}{1 + x} - 2 \sqrt {x} \operatorname {s g n} (1 - x) + C,
$$

其中用到了

$$
\left(\arcsin \frac {2 \sqrt {x}}{1 + x}\right) ^ {\prime} = \frac {\frac {1}{(1 + x) ^ {2}} \left[ (1 + x) \frac {1}{\sqrt {x}} - 2 \sqrt {x} \right]}{\sqrt {1 - \frac {4 x}{(1 + x) ^ {2}}}} = \frac {1}{1 + x} \cdot \frac {1 - x}{\sqrt {(1 - x) ^ {2}} \sqrt {x}} = \frac {1}{1 + x} \operatorname {s g n} (1 - x) \frac {1}{\sqrt {x}}.
$$
