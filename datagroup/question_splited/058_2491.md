【2491】 $x^{2} + (y - b)^{2} = a^{2}$ （ $b \geqslant a$ ）绕 $Ox$ 轴.

解此圆分成两单值支

$$
y = b + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} \quad \text {及} \quad y = b - \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}.
$$

于是，所求的表面积为

$$
P _ {x} = 2 \pi \int_ {- a} ^ {a} (b + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}) \frac {a}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} d x + 2 \pi \int_ {- a} ^ {a} (b - \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}) \frac {a}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}}} d x = 4 \pi^ {2} a b.
$$
