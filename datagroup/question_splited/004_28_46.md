28.46 Find $\int x\sqrt{1 + x} dx$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \text {L e t} u = x, d v = \sqrt {1 + x} d x, d u = d x, v = \frac {2}{3} (1 + x) ^ {3 / 2}: \int x \sqrt {1 + x} d x = \frac {2}{3} x (1 + x) ^ {3 / 2} - \frac {2}{3} \int (1 + x) ^ {3 / 2} d x \\ = \frac {2}{3} x (1 + x) ^ {3 / 2} - \frac {2}{3} \cdot \frac {2}{5} (1 + x) ^ {5 / 2} + C = \frac {2}{1 5} (1 + x) ^ {3 / 2} [ 5 x - 2 (1 + x) ] + C = \frac {2}{1 5} (1 + x) ^ {3 / 2} (3 x - 2) + C. \end{array}
$$
