【4002】 求椭圆 $\frac{x^2}{\mathrm{ch}^2u} +\frac{y^2}{\mathrm{sh}^2u} = c^2 (u = u_1,u_2)$

和双曲线 $\frac{x^2}{\cos^2 v} - \frac{y^2}{\sin^2 v} = c^2 \quad (v = v_1, v_2) \quad (0 < u_1 < u_2; 0 < v_1 < v_2; x > 0, y > 0)$

所围的面积.

提示 作变换 $x = c \operatorname{ch} u \cos v, y = c \operatorname{sh} u \sin v$ ，则有 $|I| = |c^2 \operatorname{ch}^2 u - c^2 \cos^2 v|$ 。

解 作变换 $x = c\mathrm{ch}u\mathrm{cos}v$ ， $y = c\mathrm{sh}u\mathrm{sin}v$ ，则有 $\left|I\right| = \left|c^{2}\mathrm{ch}^{2}u - c^{2}\mathrm{cos}^{2}v\right|$

因为 $\mathrm{ch}^2 u\geqslant 1\geqslant \cos^2 v$ ，故所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = c ^ {2} \int_ {u _ {1}} ^ {u _ {2}} \int_ {v _ {1}} ^ {v _ {2}} \left(\operatorname {c h} ^ {2} u - \cos^ {2} v\right) \mathrm {d} u \mathrm {d} v = c ^ {2} \left[ \left(v _ {2} - v _ {1}\right) \int_ {u _ {1}} ^ {u _ {2}} \frac {1 + \operatorname {c h} 2 u}{2} \mathrm {d} u - \left(u _ {2} - u _ {1}\right) \int_ {v _ {1}} ^ {v _ {2}} \cos^ {2} v \mathrm {d} v \right] \\ = \frac {c ^ {2}}{4} \left[ \left(v _ {2} - v _ {1}\right) \left(\operatorname {s h} 2 u _ {2} - \operatorname {s h} 2 u _ {1}\right) - \left(u _ {2} - u _ {1}\right) \left(\sin 2 v _ {2} - \sin 2 v _ {1}\right) \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
