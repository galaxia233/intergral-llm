【2288】利用欧拉公式 $\mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i}\pi} = \cos x + \mathrm{i}\sin x,$

证明： $\int_0^{2\pi}\mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i}nx}\mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i}nx}\mathrm{d}x = \left\{ \begin{array}{ll}0, & m\neq n,\\ 2\pi , & m = n \end{array} \right.$ （n及m为整数）.

证 当 $m = n$ 时， $\int_0^{2\pi}\mathrm{e}^{\mathrm{i}nx}\mathrm{e}^{-\mathrm{i}nx}\mathrm{d}x = \int_0^{2\pi}\mathrm{d}x = 2\pi .$

当 $m \neq n$ 时，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} e ^ {i m x} e ^ {- i m x} d x = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} (\cos n x + i \sin n x) (\cos m x - i \sin m x) d x \\ = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \cos (m - n) x d x - i \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} \sin (m - n) x d x = 0. \\ \end{array}
$$
