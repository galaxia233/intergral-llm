40.44 Find the distance $d$ from the origin to the plane of Problem 40.42.

Let $X$ denote any point on the plane. The distance $d$ is the magnitude of the scalar projection of $\overrightarrow{OX}$ on the vector $\mathbf{N} = (8, 4, 4)$ . Choosing $X = P(1, -1, 4)$ , we have

$$
d = \frac {\left| \mathbf {N} \cdot \overrightarrow {O P} \right|}{\left| \mathbf {N} \right|} = \frac {\left| (8 , 4 , 4) \cdot (1 - 1 , 4) \right|}{4 \sqrt {6}} = \frac {2 0}{4 \sqrt {6}} = \frac {5}{\sqrt {6}} = \frac {5 \sqrt {6}}{6}
$$
