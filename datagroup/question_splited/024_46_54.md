46.54 Find the general solution of $y^{\prime \prime} + y^{\prime} + y = 0$

Solve the auxiliary equation $u^2 + u + 1 = 0$ , $u = \frac{-1 \pm \sqrt{-3}}{2} = -\frac{1}{2} \pm \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} i$ . By Problem 46.48, the general solution is $\left(C_1 \cos \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} x + C_2 \sin \frac{\sqrt{3}}{2} x\right) e^{-x / 2}$ .
