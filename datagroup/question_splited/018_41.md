41.1 Sketch the cylinder $\frac{x^2}{16} +\frac{y^2}{9} = 1.$

See Fig. 41-1. The surface is generated by taking the ellipse $\frac{x^2}{16} + \frac{y^2}{9} = 1$ in the $xy$ -plane and moving it parallel to the $z$ -axis ( $z$ is the missing variable).
