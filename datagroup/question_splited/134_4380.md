【4380】 $\iint_{S}\left[\left(\frac{\partial R}{\partial y} -\frac{\partial Q}{\partial z}\right)\cos \alpha +\left(\frac{\partial P}{\partial z} -\frac{\partial R}{\partial x}\right)\cos \beta +\left(\frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} -\frac{\partial P}{\partial y}\right)\cos \gamma \right]\mathrm{d}S.$

解 记 $P^{\star} = \frac{\partial R}{\partial y} - \frac{\partial Q}{\partial z}, Q^{\star} = \frac{\partial P}{\partial z} - \frac{\partial R}{\partial x}, R^{\star} = \frac{\partial Q}{\partial x} - \frac{\partial P}{\partial y}$ ，则易知 $\frac{\partial P^{\star}}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial Q^{\star}}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial R^{\star}}{\partial z} = 0.$

于是，原曲面积分等于零.
