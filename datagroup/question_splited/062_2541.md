【2541】 计算 $\int_0^1\mathrm{e}^{x^2}\mathrm{d}x$ ，精确到 0.001.

解采用辛普森公式计算，则其误差为

$$
R _ {n} (x) = - \frac {1}{1 8 0 n ^ {4}} 2 e ^ {\xi^ {2}} (8 \xi^ {4} + 2 4 \xi^ {2} + 6) \quad (0 <   \xi <   1),
$$

故有 $|R_{n}(x)| < \frac{1}{180n^{4}} 2\mathrm{e} \cdot 38.$

要 $|R_{n}(x)| < 10^{-3}$ ，只要 $\frac{2 \cdot 38\mathrm{e}^{1}}{180n^{4}} < 10^{-3}$ ，即只要取 $n = 6$

现取 $n = 6$ ，则有

$$
x _ {0} = 0, \quad y _ {0} = 1 \quad x _ {1} = \frac {1}{6}, \quad y _ {1} = e ^ {\frac {1}{3 6}} = 1. 0 2 8 2;
$$

$$
x _ {2} = \frac {1}{3}, \quad y _ {2} = e ^ {\frac {1}{9}} = 1. 1 1 7 5; \quad x _ {3} = \frac {1}{2}, \quad y _ {3} = e ^ {\frac {1}{4}} = 1. 2 8 4 0
$$

$$
x _ {4} = \frac {2}{3}, \quad y _ {4} = e ^ {\frac {4}{9}} = 1. 5 5 9 6; \quad x _ {5} = \frac {5}{6}, \quad y _ {5} = e ^ {\frac {2 5}{3 6}} = 2. 0 0 2 6;
$$

$$
x _ {6} = 1, \quad y _ {6} = e = 2. 7 1 8 3.
$$

于是，

$$
\int_ {0} ^ {1} e ^ {x ^ {2}} d x \approx \frac {1}{1 8} \left[ \left(y _ {0} + y _ {6}\right) + 4 \left(y _ {1} + y _ {3} + y _ {5}\right) + 2 \left(y _ {2} + y _ {4}\right) \right] \approx 1. 4 6 3.
$$
