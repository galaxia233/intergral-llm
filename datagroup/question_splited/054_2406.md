【2406】 $Ax^{2} + 2Bxy + Cy^{2} = 1$ （ $AC - B^{2} > 0$ )

解 解方程，得

$$
y _ {1} = \frac {- B x - \sqrt {B ^ {2} x ^ {2} - C (A x ^ {2} - 1)}}{C} \quad \text {及} \quad y _ {2} = \frac {- B x + \sqrt {B ^ {2} x ^ {2} - C (A x ^ {2} - 1)}}{C},
$$

当 $B^{2}x^{2} - C(Ax^{2} - 1)\geqslant 0$ ，即 $|x|\leqslant \sqrt{\frac{C}{AC - B^2}}$ 时， $y_{1}$ 及 $y_{2}$ 才有实数值.设 $a = \sqrt{\frac{C}{AC - B^2}}$ ，则所求的面积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \left(y _ {2} - y _ {1}\right) d x = \frac {2}{C} \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \sqrt {C ^ {2} - (A C - B ^ {2}) x ^ {2}} d x = \frac {2}{C} \sqrt {A C - B ^ {2}} \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x \\ = \frac {2}{C} \sqrt {A C - B ^ {2}} \frac {\pi}{2} a ^ {2} = \frac {\pi}{\sqrt {A C - B ^ {2}}}. \\ \end{array}
$$
