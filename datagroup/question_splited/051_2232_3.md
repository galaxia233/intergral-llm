【2232】求：(3) $\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{\sin x}^{\cos x}\cos (\pi t^2)\mathrm{d}t.$

解 (3) $\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{\sin x}^{\cos x}\cos (\pi t^{2})\mathrm{d}t = \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{\sin x}^{0}\cos (\pi t^{2})\mathrm{d}t + \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{0}^{\cos x}\cos (\pi t^{2})\mathrm{d}t$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {d (\sin x)}{d x} \cdot \frac {d}{d (\sin x)} \int_ {0} ^ {\sin x} \cos (\pi t ^ {2}) d t + \frac {d (\cos x)}{d x} \cdot \frac {d}{d (\cos x)} \int_ {0} ^ {\cos x} \cos (\pi t ^ {2}) d t \\ = - \cos x \cos (\pi \sin^ {2} x) - \sin x \cos (\pi \cos^ {2} x) ^ {*}) = (\sin x - \cos x) \cos (\pi \sin^ {2} x). \\ \end{array}
$$

$\cos (\pi \cos^2 x) = \cos (\pi -\pi \sin^2 x) = -\cos (\pi \sin^2 x).$
