27.38 What identity is implied by the result of Problem 27.35?

Two functions with the same derivative differ by a constant. Hence, $\tan^{-1}[(a + x) / (1 - ax)] = \tan^{-1}x + C$ . When $x = 0$ , $\tan^{-1}a = \tan^{-1}0 + C = 0 + C = C$ . Thus, the identity is $\tan^{-1}[(a + x) / (1 - ax)] = \tan^{-1}x + \tan^{-1}a$ .
