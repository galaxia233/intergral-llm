【3113】 $\frac{1\cdot 3\cdot 5\cdots 99}{2\cdot 4\cdot 6\cdots 100}$

解 $\frac{1\cdot 3\cdot 5\cdots 99}{2\cdot 4\cdot 6\cdots 100} = \frac{100!}{2^{100}(50!)^2} = \frac{\sqrt{2\pi\cdot 100}\cdot 100^{100}e^{-100}e^{\frac{\theta_1}{1200}}}{2^{100}\cdot 100\pi\cdot 50^{100}e^{-100}e^{\frac{\theta_2}{300}}} = 0.0798e^{\frac{\theta}{300}}$

$$
\approx 0. 0 7 9 8 \left(1 + \frac {\theta}{3 0 0}\right),
$$

其中 $|\theta| < 1$ $(0 < \theta_{1} < 1, 0 < \theta_{2} < 1)$ .
