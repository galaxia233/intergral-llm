【2086】 $\int \frac{1 + \mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{2}}}{(1 + \mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{4}})^{2}}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $\mathrm{e}^{\frac{x}{4}} = t$ ，则 $x = 4\ln t$ ， $\mathrm{d}x = \frac{4}{t}\mathrm{d}t$ ，代人得

$$
\int \frac {1 + e ^ {\frac {x}{2}}}{(1 + e ^ {\frac {x}{4}}) ^ {2}} d x = 4 \int \frac {1 + t ^ {2}}{t (1 + t) ^ {2}} d t = 4 \int \left[ \frac {1}{t} - \frac {2}{(1 + t) ^ {2}} \right] d t = 4 \ln t + \frac {8}{1 + t} + C = x + \frac {8}{1 + e ^ {\frac {x}{4}}} + C.
$$
