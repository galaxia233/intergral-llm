【4287】 $\int_{(x_1,y_1,z_1)}^{(x_2,y_2,z_2)}\varphi (x)\mathrm{d}x + \psi (y)\mathrm{d}y + \chi (z)\mathrm{d}z$ ，式中 $\varphi ,\psi ,\chi$ 为连续函数.

解 因为 $\varphi(x) \mathrm{d}x + \psi(y) \mathrm{d}y + \chi(z) \mathrm{d}z = \mathrm{d}\left(\int_{x_1}^x \varphi(u) \mathrm{d}u + \int_{y_1}^y \psi(v) \mathrm{d}v + \int_{z_1}^z \chi(w) \mathrm{d}w\right)$ ,

故有 $\int_{(x_1,y_1,z_1)}^{(x_2,y_2,z_2)}\varphi (x)\mathrm{d}x + \psi (x)\mathrm{d}x + \chi (z)\mathrm{d}z = \left(\int_{x_1}^x\varphi (u)\mathrm{d}u + \int_{y_1}^y\psi (v)\mathrm{d}v + \int_{z_1}^z\chi (w)\mathrm{d}w\right)\Bigg{|}_{(x_1,y_1,z_1)}^{(x_2,y_2,z_2)}$

$$
= \int_ {x _ {1}} ^ {x _ {2}} \varphi (u) d u + \int_ {x _ {1}} ^ {x _ {2}} \psi (v) d v + \int_ {z _ {1}} ^ {z _ {2}} \chi (w) d w.
$$
