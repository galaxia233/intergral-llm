20.72 Find the area inside the ellipse $x^{2} / a^{2} + y^{2} / b^{2} = 1$

The area is twice the area above the $x$ -axis and under the ellipse, which is given by

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {- a} ^ {a} y d x = \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \frac {b}{a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = \frac {b}{a} \int_ {- a} ^ {a} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2}} d x = \frac {b}{a} \frac {\pi a ^ {2}}{2} \quad [ b y P r o b l e m 2 0. 7 1 ] \\ = \frac {\pi a b}{2} \\ \end{array}
$$

Hence, the total area inside the ellipse is $\pi ab$ .
