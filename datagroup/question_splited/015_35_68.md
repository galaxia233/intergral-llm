35.68 Find the area bounded by the curve $r = 2\cos \theta, 0 \leq \theta \leq \pi$ .

$A = \frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{\pi}4\cos^{2}\theta d\theta = 2\int_{0}^{\pi}\frac{1}{2} (1 + \cos 2\theta)d\theta = (\theta +\frac{1}{2}\sin 2\theta)\big]_{0}^{\pi} = \pi .$ This could have been obtained more easily by noting that the region is a circle of radius 1.
