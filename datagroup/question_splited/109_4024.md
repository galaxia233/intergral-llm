【4024】 $\left(\frac{x^2}{a^2} +\frac{y^2}{b^2}\right)^2 +\frac{z}{c} = 1,z = 0.$

提示 令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi$ ，则曲面方程化为

$$
z = c (1 - r ^ {4}) \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant 2 \pi , 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1).
$$

解 若令 $x = \arccos \varphi, y = b r \sin \varphi$ ，则曲面方程化为

$$
z = c (1 - r ^ {1}) \quad (0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant 2 \pi , 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1).
$$

于是，曲面所围区域的体积为

$$
V = \iint_ {\frac {r ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} \cdot \frac {v ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}} \leqslant 1} c \left[ 1 - \left(\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}\right) \right] d x d y = \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {1} a b c r (1 - r ^ {3}) d r = \frac {2}{3} \pi a b c.
$$
