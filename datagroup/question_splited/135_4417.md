【4417】求场 $u = \frac{1}{r}$ （其中 $r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}$ ）在方向 $l\{\cos \alpha, \cos \beta, \cos \gamma\}$ 上的导数.
在怎样的情况下，此导数等于零？
解 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial x} = -\frac{x}{r^3}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial y} = -\frac{y}{r^3}, \frac{\partial u}{\partial z} = -\frac{z}{r^3}$ . 于是，
$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial u}{\partial l} = - \frac {x}{r ^ {3}} \cos \alpha - \frac {y}{r ^ {3}} \cos \beta - \frac {z}{r ^ {3}} \cos \gamma = - \frac {1}{r ^ {2}} [ \cos (r, x) \cos \alpha + \cos (r, y) \cos \beta + \cos (r, z) \cos \gamma ] \\ = - \frac {\cos (l , r)}{r ^ {2}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

要 $\frac{\partial u}{\partial l} = 0$ ，只要 $\cos (l,r) = 0$ ，即 $l\bot r$ ，此即所求的解.
