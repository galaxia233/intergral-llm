【2960】+ 把函数 $f(x) = \sec x\left(-\frac{\pi}{4} < x < \frac{\pi}{4}\right)$ 展开为傅里叶级数.

解 显然 $f(x) = \sec x$ 在 $(-\frac{\pi}{4}, \frac{\pi}{4})$ 内连续，而且是偶函数，故 $b_n = 0$ ，且

$$
a _ {0} = \frac {8}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \sec x d x = \frac {8}{\pi} \left[ \ln \left| \tan \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\pi}{4}\right) \right| \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}}
$$

$$
= \frac {8}{\pi} \left[ \ln \left| \frac {\sin \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\pi}{4}\right)}{\cos \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\pi}{4}\right)} \right| \right] \left| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} = \frac {8}{\pi} \left[ \ln \left| \frac {1 - \cos \left(x + \frac {x}{2}\right)}{\sin \left(x + \frac {x}{2}\right)} \right| \right] \right| _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}}
$$

$$
= \frac {8}{\pi} \left[ \ln \left| \frac {1 + \sin x}{\cos x} \right| \right] \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} = \frac {8}{\pi} \ln (1 + \sqrt {2}),
$$

$$
a _ {n} = \frac {8}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \frac {\cos 4 n x}{\cos x} d x \quad (n = 1, 2, \dots).
$$

由于

$$
\begin{array}{l} \cos 4 n x - \cos (4 n x - 4 x) = - 2 \sin (4 n x - 2 x) \sin 2 x = - 4 \sin (4 n x - 2 x) \sin x \cos x \\ = 2 \left[ \cos (4 n x - x) - \cos (4 n x - 3 x) \right] \cos x, \\ \end{array}
$$

故

$$
\begin{array}{l} a _ {n} = \frac {8}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \frac {\cos 4 n x}{\cos x} d x \\ = \frac {1 6}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} [ \cos (4 n - 1) x - \cos (4 n - 3) x ] d x + \frac {8}{\pi} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{4}} \frac {\cos 4 (n - 1) x}{\cos x} d x \\ = \frac {1 6}{\pi} \left[ \frac {1}{4 n - 1} \sin \left(n \pi - \frac {\pi}{4}\right) - \frac {1}{4 n - 3} \sin \left(n \pi - \frac {3}{4} \pi\right) \right] + a _ {n - 1} \\ = \frac {1 6}{\pi} \left[ \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{4 n - 1} \sin \frac {\pi}{4} - \frac {(- 1) ^ {n - 1}}{4 n - 3} \sin \frac {3 \pi}{4} \right] + a _ {n - 1} \\ = \frac {1 6}{\pi} (- 1) ^ {n} \frac {\sqrt {2}}{2} \left(\frac {1}{4 n - 3} - \frac {1}{4 n - 1}\right) + a _ {n - 1} \\ = \frac {1 6}{\pi} \frac {\sqrt {2} (- 1) ^ {n}}{(4 n - 3) (4 n - 1)} + a _ {n - 1} \quad (n = 1, 2, \dots). \\ \end{array}
$$

由此递推公式，得

$$
a _ {n} = \frac {1 6 \sqrt {2}}{\pi} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k}}{(4 k - 3) (4 k - 1)} + a _ {0} = \frac {1 6 \sqrt {2}}{\pi} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k}}{(4 k - 3) (4 k - 1)} + \frac {8}{\pi} \ln (1 + \sqrt {2}) \quad (n = 1, 2, \dots).
$$

于是，下面的展式成立：

$$
\sec x = \frac {4}{\pi} \ln (1 + \sqrt {2}) + \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {\infty} \left[ \frac {8}{\pi} \ln (1 + \sqrt {2}) - \frac {1 6 \sqrt {2}}{\pi} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {(- 1) ^ {k - 1}}{(4 k - 3) (4 k - 1)} \right] \cos 4 n x   (- \frac {\pi}{4} < x < \frac {\pi}{4}).
$$
