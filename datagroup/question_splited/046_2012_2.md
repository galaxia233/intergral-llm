【2012】(2) 推出下列积分的递推公式：

(1) $I_{n} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^{n}x}; \quad (2) K_{n} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos^{n}x} \quad (n > 2).$

利用这些公式计算 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^5 x}$ 和 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos^7 x}$ .

提示 利用分部积分法及 $1 = \sin^2 x + \cos^2 x$ ，易得

(1) $I_{n} = -\frac{\cos x}{(n - 1)\sin^{n - 1}x} + \frac{n - 2}{n - 1} I_{n - 2}$ ; (2) $K_{n} = \frac{\sin x}{(n - 1)\cos^{n - 1}x} + \frac{n - 2}{n - 1} K_{n - 2}$ .

解 (2) $K_{n} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos^{n}x} = \int \frac{\sin^{2}x + \cos^{2}x}{\cos^{n}x}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{n - 1}\int \sin x\mathrm{d}\left(\frac{1}{\cos^{n - 1}x}\right) + K_{n - 2}$

$$
= \frac {\sin x}{(n - 1) \cos^ {n - 1} x} - \frac {1}{n - 1} K _ {n - 2} + K _ {n - 2} = \frac {\sin x}{(n - 1) \cos^ {n - 1} x} + \frac {n - 2}{n - 1} K _ {n - 2};
$$

利用此公式及 $K_{1} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\cos x} = \ln \left|\tan \left(\frac{x}{2} + \frac{\pi}{4}\right)\right| + C$ ，即得

$$
K _ {7} = \int \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{\cos^ {7} x} = \frac {\sin x}{6 \cos^ {6} x} + \frac {5}{6} K _ {5} = \dots = \frac {\sin x}{6 \cos^ {6} x} + \frac {5 \sin x}{2 4 \cos^ {4} x} + \frac {5 \sin x}{1 6 \cos^ {2} x} + \frac {5}{1 6} \ln \left| \tan \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\pi}{4}\right) \right| + C.
$$
