【2233】 求：
（1） $\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\int_0^x\cos x^2dx}{x};$

解 (1) $\lim_{x\to 0}\frac{\int_{0}^{x}\cos x^{2}dx}{x} = \lim_{x\to 0}\cos x^{2} = 1;$   
(2) $\lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{\int_{0}^{x} (\arctan x)^{2} \mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x^{2} + 1}} = \lim_{x \to +\infty} \frac{(\arctan x)^{2}}{\frac{x}{\sqrt{1 + x^{2}}}} = \frac{\pi^{2}}{4}$ ;   
(3) $\lim_{x\to +\infty}\frac{\left(\int_0^x\mathrm{e}^{x^2}\mathrm{d}x\right)^2}{\int_0^x\mathrm{e}^{2x^2}\mathrm{d}x} = \lim_{x\to +\infty}\frac{2\mathrm{e}^{x^2}\int_0^x\mathrm{e}^{x^2}\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{2x^2}} = \lim_{x\to +\infty}\frac{2\int_0^x\mathrm{e}^{x^2}\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{x^2}} = \lim_{x\to +\infty}\frac{2\mathrm{e}^{x^2}}{2x\mathrm{e}^{x^2}} = \lim_{x\to +\infty}\frac{1}{x} = 0.$
