【3920】 $\Omega -$ 圆 $x^{2} + y^{2}\leqslant y.$

提示 积分域 $\Omega$ 为 $x^{2} + \left(y - \frac{1}{2}\right)^{2} \leqslant \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^{2}$ .

解如图8.3所示.积分域 $\Omega$ 为 $x^{2} + (y - \frac{1}{2})^{2}\leqslant (\frac{1}{2})$ ，其围线为

$$
x ^ {2} + \left(y - \frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2} = \left(\frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2}.
$$

于是，

$$
I = \int_ {- \frac {1}{2}} ^ {\frac {1}{2}} d x \int_ {\frac {1}{2} - \sqrt {\frac {1}{4} - x ^ {2}}} ^ {\frac {1}{2} + \sqrt {\frac {1}{4} - x ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d y = \int_ {0} ^ {1} d y \int_ {- \sqrt {y - y ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {y - y ^ {2}}} f (x, y) d x.
$$
