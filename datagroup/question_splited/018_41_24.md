41.24 Identify the graph of $25x^{2} - y^{2} - z^{2} = 25$ .

This is equivalent to $x^{2} - (y^{2} / 25) - (z^{2} / 25) = 1$ , which is a circular hyperboloid of two sheets; see Problem 41.15. Each cross section $x = k$ is a circle $y^{2} + z^{2} = 25(k^{2} - 1)$ . (We must have $|k| \geq 1$ .) Each cross section $y = k$ is a hyperbola $x^{2} - (z^{2} / 25) = 1 + (k^{2} / 25)$ , and each cross section $z = k$ is a hyperbola $x^{2} - (y^{2} / 25) = 1 + (k^{2} / 25)$ . The axis of the hyperboloid is the $x$ -axis.
