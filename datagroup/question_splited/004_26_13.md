26.13 A sum of money, compounded continuously, is multiplied by 5 in 8 years. If it amounts to $10,000 after 24 years, what was the initial sum of money?

By the formula of Problem 26.9, $y = y_0 e^{0.01r_k}$ , where $r$ is the rate of interest and $k$ is the number of years. Hence, $5y_0 = y_0 e^{0.08r}$ , $5 = e^{0.08r}$ . After 24 years, $10,000 = y_0 e^{0.024r} = y_0 (e^{0.08r})^3 = y_0 (5)^3 = 125y_0$ . Hence, the initial quantity $y_0$ was $\frac{10,000}{125} = 80$ dollars.
