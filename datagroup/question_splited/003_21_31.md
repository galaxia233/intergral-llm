21.31 Find the length of the arc of $24xy = x^4 + 48$ from $x = 2$ to $x = 4$ .

$y = (x^{4} + 48) / 24x = \frac{1}{24}(x^{3} + 48x^{-1})$ . Then $y' = \frac{1}{24}(3x^2 - 48/x^2) = (3x^4 - 48)/24x^2 = (x^4 - 16)/8x^2$ . Hence,

$$
1 + \left(y ^ {\prime}\right) ^ {2} = 1 + \frac {x ^ {8} - 3 2 x ^ {4} + 2 5 6}{6 4 x ^ {4}} = \frac {6 4 x ^ {4} + x ^ {8} - 3 2 x ^ {4} + 2 5 6}{6 4 x ^ {4}} = \frac {x ^ {8} + 3 2 x ^ {4} + 2 5 6}{6 4 x ^ {4}} = \left(\frac {x ^ {4} + 1 6}{8 x ^ {2}}\right) ^ {2}
$$

So, $L = \frac{1}{8}\int_{2}^{4}(x^{2} + 16x^{-2})dx = \frac{1}{8} (\frac{1}{3} x^{3} - 16x^{-1})]_{2}^{4} = \frac{1}{8} [(64\frac{1}{3} -4) - (\frac{8}{3} -8)] = \frac{17}{6}$ .
