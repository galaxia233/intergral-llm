27.42 $\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{25 - 16x^2}}.$   
$\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{25 - 16x^2}} = \int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{16(\frac{25}{16} - x^2)}} = \frac{1}{4}\int \frac{dx}{\sqrt{(\frac{5}{4})^2 - x^2}}.$ By the formula given in Problem 27.41, this is equal to $\frac{1}{4}\sin^{-1}\frac{x}{\frac{5}{4}} +C = \frac{1}{4}\sin^{-1}\frac{4x}{5} +C.$
