【2141】 $\int x\ln (4 + x^4)\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x\ln (4 + x^4)\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \ln (4 + x^4)\mathrm{d}(x^2) = \frac{1}{2} x^2\ln (4 + x^4) - 2\int \frac{x^5}{4 + x^4}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} \ln (4 + x ^ {4}) - 2 \int \left(x - \frac {4 x}{4 + x ^ {4}}\right) d x = \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} \ln (4 + x ^ {4}) - x ^ {2} + 2 \arctan \left(\frac {x ^ {2}}{2}\right) + C.
$$
