45.10 If $\mathbf{G}(3) = (1, 1, 2)$ and $\mathbf{G}'(3) = (3, -2, 5)$ , find $\frac{d}{dt} [t^2\mathbf{G}(t)]$ at $t = 3$ .

$\frac{d}{dt} [t^2 \mathbf{G}(t)] = t^2 \mathbf{G}'(t) + 2t \mathbf{G}(t)$ . Hence, at $t = 3$ , $\frac{d}{dt} [t^2 \mathbf{G}(t)] = 9(3, -2, 5) + 6(1, 1, 2) = (33, -12, 57)$ .
