【4259】 $\int_{(0,1)}^{(3, - 1)}x\mathrm{d}x + y\mathrm{d}y.$

解 显然， $x\mathrm{d}x + y\mathrm{d}y = \mathrm{d}\left(\frac{x^2 + y^2}{2}\right)$ 是全微分.于是，

$$
\int_ {(0, 1)} ^ {(3, - 4)} x \mathrm {d} x + y \mathrm {d} y = \int_ {(0, 1)} ^ {(3, - 4)} \mathrm {d} \left(\frac {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}{2}\right) = \frac {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}}{2} \Bigg | _ {(0, 1)} ^ {(3, - 4)} = 1 2.
$$
