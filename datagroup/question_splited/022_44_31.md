44.31 Find the volume in the first octant bounded by $2x + 2y - z + 1 = 0, y = x,$ and $x = 2$

See Fig. 44-19. $V = \iint_{D} (2x + 2y + 1) \, dA = \int_{0}^{2} \int_{0}^{x} (2x + 2y + 1) \, dy \, dx = \int_{0}^{2} (2xy + y^2 + y) \big]_{0}^{x} \, dx = \int_{0}^{2} (3x^2 + x) \, dx = (x^3 + \frac{1}{2} x^2) \big]_{0}^{2} = 8 + 2 = 10.$
