34.71 If $\mathbf{R}''(u) = \mathbf{0}$ for all $u$ , show that $\mathbf{R}(u) = \mathbf{A} + u\mathbf{B}$ , either a constant function or a straight line.

By Problem 34.68, $\mathbf{R}'(u)$ is a constant, $\mathbf{B}$ . If $\mathbf{B} = \mathbf{0}$ , then $\mathbf{R}(u)$ is a constant, again by Problem 34.68. If $\mathbf{B} \neq \mathbf{0}$ , then $\mathbf{R}(u)$ has the form $\mathbf{A} + u\mathbf{B}$ , by Problem 34.70.
