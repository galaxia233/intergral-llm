【4421】用直接计算的方法证明：向量 $\pmb{a}$ 的散度与直角坐标系的选择无关.
证 设除直角坐标系 $Oxyz$ （坐标轴方向的单位向量为 $i, j, k$ ）外，另有直角坐标系 $O'x'y'z'$ （坐标轴方向的单位向量为 $i', j', k'$ ）。我们要证
$$
\frac {\partial a _ {x}}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial a _ {y}}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial a _ {z}}{\partial z} = \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}},
$$

i' = cosα1 i + cosβj + cosγk, 设 j' = cosα2 i + cosβj + cosγ2 k, k' = cosα3 i + cosβj + cosγ3 k.
又设 $r_{0} = 00^{\prime} = ai + bj + ck$ . 于是，空间一点 $P$ 在两个坐标系中的坐标 $(x, y, z)$ 与 $(x^{\prime}, y^{\prime}, z^{\prime})$ 之间的关系为（令 $r = OP, r' = O'P$ ）：
$$
\begin{array}{l} x ^ {\prime} = r ^ {\prime} \cdot i ^ {\prime} = (r - r _ {0}) \cdot i ^ {\prime} = (x - a) \cos \alpha_ {1} + (y - b) \cos \beta_ {1} + (z - c) \cos \gamma_ {1}, \\ y ^ {\prime} = r ^ {\prime} \cdot j ^ {\prime} = (r - r _ {0}) \cdot j ^ {\prime} = (x - a) \cos \alpha_ {2} + (y - b) \cos \beta_ {2} + (z - c) \cos \gamma_ {2}, \\ z ^ {\prime} = r ^ {\prime} \cdot k ^ {\prime} = (r - r _ {0}) \cdot k ^ {\prime} = (x - a) \cos \alpha_ {3} + (y - b) \cos \beta_ {3} + (z - c) \cos \gamma_ {3}, \\ \end{array}
$$

我们有
$$
\begin{array}{l} \dot {a} = a _ {x ^ {\prime}} \mathbf {i} ^ {\prime} + a _ {y ^ {\prime}} \mathbf {j} ^ {\prime} + a _ {z ^ {\prime}} \mathbf {k} ^ {\prime} \\ = a _ {r ^ {\prime}} \left(\cos \alpha_ {1} i + \cos \beta_ {1} j + \cos \gamma_ {1} k\right) + a _ {y ^ {\prime}} \left(\cos \alpha_ {2} i + \cos \beta_ {2} j + \cos \gamma_ {2} k\right) + a _ {z ^ {\prime}} \left(\cos \alpha_ {3} i + \cos \beta_ {3} j + \cos \gamma_ {3} k\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

由此可知
$$
a _ {x} = a _ {x ^ {\prime}} \cos \alpha_ {1} + a _ {y ^ {\prime}} \cos \alpha_ {2} + a _ {z ^ {\prime}} \cos \alpha_ {3} \quad a _ {y} = a _ {x ^ {\prime}} \cos \beta_ {1} + a _ {y ^ {\prime}} \cos \beta_ {2} + a _ {z ^ {\prime}} \cos \beta_ {3} \quad a _ {z} = a _ {x ^ {\prime}} \cos \gamma_ {1} + a _ {y ^ {\prime}} \cos \gamma_ {2} + a _ {z ^ {\prime}} \cos \gamma_ {3}.
$$

于是，
$$
\frac {\partial a _ {x}}{\partial x} = \frac {\partial a _ {r}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} \frac {\partial x ^ {\prime}}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial a _ {r}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} \frac {\partial y ^ {\prime}}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial a _ {r}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} \frac {\partial z ^ {\prime}}{\partial x} = \left(\cos \alpha_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \cos \alpha_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \cos \alpha_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \alpha_ {1}
$$

$$
+ \left(\cos \alpha_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \cos \alpha_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \cos \alpha_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \alpha_ {2} + \left(\cos \alpha_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \cos \alpha_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \cos \alpha_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \alpha_ {3}.
$$

同理，可得
$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial a _ {y}}{\partial y} = \left(\cos \beta_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \cos \beta_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \cos \beta_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \beta_ {1} + \left(\cos \beta_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \cos \beta_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \cos \beta_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \beta_ {2} \\ + \left(\cos \beta_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \cos \beta_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \cos \beta_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \beta_ {3}, \\ \end{array}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial a _ {z}}{\partial z} = \left(\cos \gamma_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \cos \gamma_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \cos \gamma_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \gamma_ {1} + \left(\cos \gamma_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \cos \gamma_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \cos \gamma_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \gamma_ {2} \\ + \left(\cos \gamma_ {1} \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \cos \gamma_ {2} \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \cos \gamma_ {3} \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}}\right) \cos \gamma_ {3}. \\ \end{array}
$$

将这三式相加，得
$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {\partial a _ {x}}{\partial x} + \frac {\partial a _ {y}}{\partial y} + \frac {\partial a _ {z}}{\partial z} = \left(i ^ {\prime} \cdot i ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {r ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \left(j ^ {\prime} \cdot i ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \left(k ^ {\prime} \cdot i ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \left(i ^ {\prime} \cdot j ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \left(j ^ {\prime} \cdot j ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \left(k ^ {\prime} \cdot j ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} \\ + \left(i ^ {\prime} \cdot k ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \left(j ^ {\prime} \cdot k ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} + \left(k ^ {\prime} \cdot k ^ {\prime}\right) \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}} \\ = \frac {\partial a _ {x ^ {\prime}}}{\partial x ^ {\prime}} + \frac {\partial a _ {y ^ {\prime}}}{\partial y ^ {\prime}} + \frac {\partial a _ {z ^ {\prime}}}{\partial z ^ {\prime}}. \\ \end{array}
$$

证毕.
