【2084】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{2x} + \mathrm{e}^x - 2}$

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\mathrm{e}^{2x} + \mathrm{e}^x - 2} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{(e^x + 2)(e^x - 1)} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{1}{e^x - 1}\mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{1}{e^x + 2}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = - \frac {1}{3} \int \left(1 - \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{\mathrm {e} ^ {x} - 1}\right) \mathrm {d} x - \frac {1}{6} \int \left(1 - \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {x}}{\mathrm {e} ^ {x} + 2}\right) \mathrm {d} x = - \frac {x}{3} + \frac {1}{3} \ln | \mathrm {e} ^ {x} - 1 | - \frac {x}{6} + \frac {1}{6} \ln (\mathrm {e} ^ {x} + 2) + C \\ = - \frac {x}{2} + \frac {1}{3} \ln | e ^ {x} - 1 | + \frac {1}{6} \ln (e ^ {x} + 2) + C. \\ \end{array}
