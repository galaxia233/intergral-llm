42.58 Let $f(x, t) = u(x + at) + v(x - at)$ , where $u$ and $v$ are assumed to have continuous second partial derivatives. In generalization of Problems 42.55-42.57, show that $f$ satisfies the wave equation $a^2 f_{xx} = f_{tt}$ .

$$
\begin{array}{l} f _ {x} = u ^ {\prime} (x + a t) + v ^ {\prime} (x - a t), \quad f _ {x x} = u ^ {\prime \prime} (x + a t) + v ^ {\prime \prime} (x - a t). \quad f _ {t} = a u ^ {\prime} (x + a t) - a v ^ {\prime} (x - a t), \quad f _ {t t} = a ^ {2} u ^ {\prime \prime} (x + a t) + a ^ {2} v ^ {\prime \prime} (x - a t) = a ^ {2} f _ {x x}. \end{array}
$$
