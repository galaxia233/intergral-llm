45.39 Prove that $\operatorname{div}(\nabla f \times \nabla g) = 0$ for any scalar functions $f(x, y, z)$ and $g(x, y, z)$ with continuous mixed second partial derivatives.

By Problems 45.33 and 45.37, $\operatorname{div}(\nabla f \times \nabla g) = \mathbf{0} \cdot \nabla g - \nabla f \cdot \mathbf{0} = 0$ .
