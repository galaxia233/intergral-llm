【4158】半径为 $R$ 质量为 $M$ 的均匀球体 $\xi^2 +\eta^2 +\zeta^2\leqslant R^2$ 以怎样的力吸引质量为 $m$ 的质点 $P(0,0,a)$ ?

解引力在 $Ox$ 轴和 $Oy$ 轴上的投影为零，即 $X = Y = 0$，而在 $Oz$ 轴上的投影为

$$
Z = k \rho_ {0} m \iiint_ {\xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} + \zeta^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2}} \frac {(\zeta - a) d \xi d \eta d \zeta}{\left[ \xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} + (\zeta - a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} = k m \rho_ {0} \int_ {- R} ^ {R} (\zeta - a) d \zeta \iint_ {\xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} \leqslant R ^ {2} - \zeta^ {2}} \frac {d \xi d \eta}{\left[ \xi^ {2} + \eta^ {2} + (\zeta - a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {\frac {3}{2}}}
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = k m \rho_ {0} \int_ {R} ^ {R} (\zeta - a) d \zeta \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\sqrt {R ^ {2} - \zeta^ {2}}} \frac {r d r}{\left[ r ^ {2} + (\zeta - a) ^ {2} \right] ^ {\frac {3}{2}}} = 2 \pi k m \rho_ {0} \int_ {- R} ^ {R} (\zeta - a) \left(\frac {1}{| \zeta - a |} - \frac {1}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta + a ^ {2}}}\right) d \zeta \\ = 2 \pi k m \rho_ {0} \int_ {R} ^ {R} \operatorname {s g n} (\zeta - a) d \zeta - 2 \pi k m \rho_ {0} \int_ {- R} ^ {R} \frac {(\zeta - a) d \zeta}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta + a ^ {2}}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

其中 $\rho_{0} = \frac{3M}{4\pi R^{3}}$

分别求上述两个积分：

当 $a \geqslant R$ 时， $\int_{-R}^{R} \operatorname{sgn}(\zeta - a) \mathrm{d}\zeta = -\int_{-R}^{R} \mathrm{d}\zeta = -2R,$

当 $a < R$ 时， $\int_{-R}^{R}\operatorname{sgn}(\zeta - a)\mathrm{d}\zeta = -\int_{-R}^{a}\mathrm{d}\zeta + \int_{a}^{R}\mathrm{d}\zeta = -2a;$

而

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {R} ^ {R} \frac {(\zeta - a) d \zeta}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta + a ^ {2}}} = - \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {R} ^ {R} \frac {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta - (R ^ {2} + a ^ {2})}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta + a ^ {2}}} d \zeta - a \int_ {- R} ^ {R} \frac {d \zeta}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta + a ^ {2}}} \\ = - \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {- R} ^ {R} \sqrt {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta} d \zeta + \left(\frac {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2}}{2 a} - a\right) \int_ {- R} ^ {R} \frac {d \zeta}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta}} \\ = - \frac {1}{2 a} \int_ {- R} ^ {R} \sqrt {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta} d \zeta + \frac {R ^ {2} - a ^ {2}}{2 a} \int_ {- R} ^ {R} \frac {d \zeta}{\sqrt {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta}}, \\ \end{array}
$$

当 $a \geqslant R$ 时，将上式右端分别积分，得结果：

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left[ \frac {1}{4 a ^ {2}} \left(R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta\right) ^ {\frac {3}{2}} \cdot \frac {2}{3} + \frac {R ^ {2} - a ^ {2}}{2 a} \left(- \frac {1}{2 a}\right) \cdot 2 \sqrt {R ^ {2} + a ^ {2} - 2 a \zeta} \right] \Bigg | _ {- R} ^ {R} \\ = \frac {1}{6 a ^ {2}} \left[ (a - R) ^ {3} - (a + R) ^ {3} \right] - \frac {R ^ {2} - a ^ {2}}{2 a ^ {2}} \left[ (a - R) - (a + R) \right] = \frac {2 R ^ {4}}{3 a ^ {2}} - 2 R; \\ \end{array}
$$

当 $a < R$ 时，积分得结果：

$$
\frac {1}{6 a ^ {2}} \left[ (R - a) ^ {3} - (a + R) ^ {3} \right] - \frac {R ^ {2} - a ^ {2}}{2 a ^ {2}} \left[ (R - a) - (R + a) \right] = - \frac {4 a}{3}.
$$

于是，当 $a \geqslant R$ 时，则 $Z = 2\pi km\rho_0\left(-2R - \frac{2R^3}{3a^2} + 2R\right) = -\frac{4}{3a^2}\pi km\rho_0R^3 = -\frac{kMm}{a^2}$

当 $a < R$ 时，则 $Z = 2\pi km\rho_0\left(-2a + \frac{4a}{3}\right) = -\frac{4}{3}\pi akm\rho_0 = -\frac{kMm}{R^3} a.$

从以上结果可以得到下面两个推论：

（1）位于球外的一点 $(a\geqslant R)$ 因球体而受到的引力相当于将球体的全部质量 $M = \frac{4}{3}\pi R^3\rho_0$ 集中在它的中心处时受到的引力，引力的方向朝向球心；  
（2）对于在球里面的一点 $(a < R)$ 来说，引力与 $R$ 无关，其大小与 $R = a$ 时的情况一样，即在点 $P$ 外面的球壳部分对 $P$ 点的引力为零。
