20.62 The velocity $v$ of an object moving on the $x$ -axis is $\cos 3t$ , and the object is at the origin at $t = 0$ . Find the average value of the position $x$ over the interval $0 \leq t \leq \pi / 3$ .

$x = \int vdt = \int \cos 3tdt = \frac{1}{3}\sin 3t + C.$ But $x = 0$ when $t = 0$ . Hence, $C = 0$ and $x = \frac{1}{3}\sin 3t$ The average value of $x$ on $[0,\pi /3]$ is

$$
\left. \frac {1}{\pi / 3} \int_ {0} ^ {\pi / 3} \frac {1}{3} \sin 3 t d t = \frac {1}{\pi} \left(- \frac {1}{3} \cos 3 t\right) \right] _ {0} ^ {\pi / 3} = - \frac {1}{3 \pi} (\cos \pi - \cos 0) = - \frac {1}{3 \pi} (- 1 - 1) = \frac {2}{3 \pi}
$$
