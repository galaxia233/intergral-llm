【2232】求：(2) $\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{x^2}^{x^3}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{1 + t^4}};$

解 (2) $\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{x^2}^{x^3}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{1 + t^4}} = \frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{x^2}^{0}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{1 + t^4}} +\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}x}\int_{0}^{x^3}\frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{1 + t^4}}$

$$
= \frac {\mathrm {d}}{\mathrm {d} x} \left(x ^ {3}\right) \frac {\mathrm {d}}{\mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {3}\right)} \int_ {0} ^ {x ^ {3}} \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{\sqrt {1 + t ^ {4}}} - \frac {\mathrm {d}}{\mathrm {d} x} \left(x ^ {2}\right) \frac {\mathrm {d}}{\mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {2}\right)} \int_ {0} ^ {x ^ {2}} \frac {\mathrm {d} t ^ {\prime}}{\sqrt {1 + t ^ {4}}} = \frac {3 x ^ {2}}{\sqrt {1 + x ^ {1 2}}} - \frac {2 x}{\sqrt {1 + x ^ {8}}};
$$
