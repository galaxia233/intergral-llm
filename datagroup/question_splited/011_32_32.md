32.33 Find the area under the curve $y = x / \sqrt{1 - x^2}$ for $0 \leq x \leq 1$ .

$A = \int_{0}^{1}\frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} dx = \lim_{v\to 1^{-}}\int_{0}^{v}\frac{x}{\sqrt{1 - x^2}} dx = \lim_{v\to 1^{-}}\left(-\left(1 - x^2\right)^{1 / 2}\right]_0^v = \lim_{v\to 1^{-}}[-\left(1 - v^2\right)^{1 / 2} + 1] = 1.$
