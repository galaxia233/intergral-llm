【2262】计算积分 $\int_{\mathrm{e}^{-2\pi n}}^{1}\left|\left[\cos \left(\ln \frac{1}{x}\right)\right]^{\prime}\right|\mathrm{d}x$ （ $n$ 为正数）.

解 $\left[\cos \left(\ln \frac{1}{x}\right)\right]' = \frac{\sin(-\ln x)}{x}.$ 设 $x = e^{-t}$ , 则 $\mathrm{d}x = -e^{-t}\mathrm{d}t, \frac{\sin(-\ln x)}{x} = \frac{\sin t}{e^{-t}} = e^t\sin t.$ 代入得 $\int_{e^{-2\pi n}}^{1} \left|\left[\cos \left(\ln \frac{1}{x}\right)\right]\right'| \mathrm{d}x = \int_{0}^{2\pi n} |\sin t| \mathrm{d}t = \sum_{k=1}^{2n} \int_{(k-1)\pi}^{k\pi} |\sin t| \mathrm{d}t = \sum_{k=1}^{2n} \int_{0}^{\pi} \sin t \mathrm{d}t = 2 \cdot 2n = 4n.$
