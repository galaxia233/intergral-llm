【3122】写出经过三点： $A(x_0 - h,y_1)$ ， $B(x_{0},y_{0})$ ， $C(x_0 + h,y_1)$ 的抛物线方程 $y = ax^{2} + bx + c.$

解 将三点的坐标代入拉格朗日插值公式，即得
$$
\begin{array}{l} y = \frac {(x - x _ {0}) (x - x _ {0} - h)}{[ (x _ {0} - h) - x _ {0} ] [ (x _ {0} - h) - (x _ {0} + h) ]} y _ {1} + \frac {(x - x _ {0} + h) (x - x _ {0} - h)}{[ x _ {0} - (x _ {0} - h) ] [ x _ {0} - (x _ {0} + h) ]} y _ {0} \\ + \frac {(x - x _ {0} + h) (x - x _ {0})}{[ (x _ {0} + h) - (x _ {0} - h) ] [ (x _ {0} + h) - x _ {0} ]} y _ {1} \\ = y _ {0} + \frac {y _ {1} - y _ {- 1}}{2 h} (x - x _ {0}) + \frac {y _ {1} - 2 y _ {0} + y _ {- 1}}{2 h ^ {2}} (x - x _ {0}) ^ {2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
