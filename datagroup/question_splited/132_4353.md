【4353】求密度为 $\rho_0$ 的均质球面壳 $x^{2} + y^{2} + z^{2} = a^{2}$ （ $z \geq 0$ ）对于 $Oz$ 轴的转动惯量.

解转动惯量为

$$
\begin{array}{l} I _ {z} = \iint_ {S} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) \rho_ {0} d S = \rho_ {0} \iint_ {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} \leqslant a ^ {2}} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) \frac {a}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - x ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}} d x d y = a \rho_ {0} \int_ {0} ^ {2 \pi} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {a} \frac {r ^ {3}}{\sqrt {a ^ {2} - r ^ {2}}} d r \\ = 2 \pi a ^ {4} \rho_ {0} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {3} \theta d \theta = \frac {4}{3} \pi a ^ {4} \rho_ {0}. \\ \end{array}
$$
