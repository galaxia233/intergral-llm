45.14 Derive the converse of Problem 45.13: If $|\mathbf{G}(t)|$ is constant, then $\mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}'(t) = 0$ .

Let $|\mathbf{G}(t)| = c$ for all $t$ . Then $\mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}(t) = |\mathbf{G}(t)|^2 = c^2$ . So $\frac{d}{dt}[\mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}(t)] = 0$ . Hence, $\mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}'(t) + \mathbf{G}'(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}(t) = 0$ . Thus, $2\mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}'(t) = 0$ , and, therefore, $\mathbf{G}(t) \cdot \mathbf{G}'(t) = 0$ .
