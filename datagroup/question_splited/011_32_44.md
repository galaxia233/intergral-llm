32.45 Evaluate $\int_{1 / \pi}^{\infty}\frac{1}{x^2}\sin \frac{1}{x} dx.$

$\int_{1 / \pi}^{\infty}\frac{1}{x^{2}}\sin \frac{1}{x} dx = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\int_{1 / \pi}^{v}\frac{1}{x^{2}}\sin \frac{1}{x} dx = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\cos \frac{1}{x}\Bigg]_{1 / \pi}^{v} = \lim_{v\to +\infty}\left(\cos \frac{1}{v} +1\right) = 1 + 1 = 2.$
