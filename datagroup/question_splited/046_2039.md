【2039】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^6 x + \cos^6 x}$   

解 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{\sin^6 x + \cos^6 x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 - 3\sin^2 x\cos^2 x} = \int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{1 - \frac{3}{4}\sin^2 2x} = \int \frac{2\mathrm{d}(\tan 2x)}{4\sec^2 2x - 3\tan^2 2x} = \arctan \left(\frac{\tan 2x}{2}\right) + C.$
