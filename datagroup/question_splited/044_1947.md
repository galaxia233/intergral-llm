【1947】 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^3\sqrt{x^2 + 1}}.$

解设 $x = \frac{1}{t}$ ，则 $\mathrm{dx} = -\frac{1}{t^2}\mathrm{dt}$ ，这里碰到二次无理式 $\sqrt{x^2 + 1}$ 需引用 $\operatorname {sgn}t$ 的问题，不妨设

$$
\sqrt {x ^ {2} + 1} = \frac {\sqrt {t ^ {2} + 1}}{t} (t > 0).
$$

代入得 $\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x^3\sqrt{x^2 + 1}} = -\int \frac{t^2}{\sqrt{t^2 + 1}}\mathrm{d}t = -\int \frac{(t^2 + 1) - 1}{\sqrt{t^2 + 1}}\mathrm{d}t = -\int \sqrt{t^2 + 1}\mathrm{d}t + \int \frac{\mathrm{d}t}{\sqrt{t^2 + 1}}$

$$
= - \frac {t}{2} \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 1} - \frac {1}{2} \ln | t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 1} | + \ln | t + \sqrt {t ^ {2} + 1} | + C = - \frac {\sqrt {x ^ {2} + 1}}{2 x ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {1 + \sqrt {x ^ {2} + 1}}{| x |} + C.
$$
