【1916】 $\int \frac{x^4 - 1}{x(x^4 - 5)(x^5 - 5x + 1)}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{x^4 - 1}{x(x^4 - 5)(x^5 - 5x + 1)}\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{5}\int \frac{\mathrm{d}(x^5 - 5x)}{(x^5 - 5x)(x^5 - 5x + 1)}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{5} \int \left(\frac {1}{x ^ {5} - 5 x} - \frac {1}{x ^ {5} - 5 x + 1}\right) d (x ^ {5} - 5 x) = \frac {1}{5} \int \frac {d (x ^ {5} - 5 x)}{x ^ {5} - 5 x} - \frac {1}{5} \int \frac {d (x ^ {5} - 5 x + 1)}{x ^ {5} - 5 x + 1} \\ = \frac {1}{5} \ln \left| \frac {x (x ^ {4} - 5 x)}{x ^ {5} - 5 x + 1} \right| + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
