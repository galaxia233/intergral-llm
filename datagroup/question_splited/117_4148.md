【4148】 $z = x^{2} + y^{2},x + y = \pm 1,x - y = \pm 1,z = 0.$

提示 注意 $I_{z} = I_{zx} + I_{xy}$，并令 $x + y = u$，$x - y = v$

解曲面所界的均匀物体对于 $Oz$ 轴的转动惯量记作 $I_{z}$，则 $I_{z} = I_{zx} + I_{zy}$

若令 $x + y = u$，$x - y = v$，则有 $x = \frac{u + v}{2},y = \frac{u - v}{2},z = \frac{u^2 + v^2}{2}$ 且 $|I| = \frac{1}{2}$ .于是，

$$
I _ {z} = \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {- 1} ^ {1} d v \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {u ^ {2} - v ^ {2}}{2}} \frac {1}{2} \left\{\left(\frac {u - v}{2}\right) ^ {2} + \left(\frac {u + v}{2}\right) ^ {2} \right\} d z = \int_ {1} ^ {1} d u \int_ {1} ^ {1} \frac {(u ^ {2} + v ^ {2}) ^ {2}}{8} d v = \frac {1 4}{4 5}.
$$
