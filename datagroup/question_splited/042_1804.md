【1804】 $\int x\arctan x\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int x\arctan x\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \arctan x\mathrm{d}(x^2) = \frac{1}{2} x^2\arctan x - \frac{1}{2}\int \frac{x^2}{1 + x^2}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} x ^ {2} \arctan x - \frac {1}{2} \int \left(1 - \frac {1}{1 + x ^ {2}}\right) d x = \frac {1 + x ^ {2}}{2} \arctan x - \frac {x}{2} + C.
$$
