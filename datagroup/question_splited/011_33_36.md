33.35 Find $k$ so that the angle between $\mathbf{A} = (3, -2)$ and $\mathbf{B} = (1, k)$ is $60^{\circ}$ .

$\mathbf{A} \cdot \mathbf{B} = |\mathbf{A}| |\mathbf{B}| \cos \theta, 3 - 2k = \sqrt{13} \sqrt{1 + k^2} \cdot \frac{1}{2}, 9 - 12k + 4k^2 = \frac{13}{4} (1 + k^2), 36 - 48k + 16k^2 = 13 + 13k^2, 3k^2 - 48k + 23 = 0, k = \frac{48 \pm \sqrt{2028}}{6} = \frac{24 \pm \sqrt{507}}{3}.$
