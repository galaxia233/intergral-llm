21.24 Find the area of the region between the $x$ -axis and $y = (x - 1)^3$ from $x = 0$ to $x = 2$ .

As shown in Fig. 21-19, the region consists of two pieces, one below the $x$ -axis from $x = 0$ to $x = 1$ , and the other above the $x$ -axis from $x = 1$ to $x = 2$ . Hence, the total area is $\int_0^1 -(x - 1)^3 dx + \int_1^2 (x - 1)^3 dx = -\frac{1}{4} (x - 1)^4 ]_0^1 +\frac{1}{4} (x - 1)^4 ]_1^2 = [-\frac{1}{4} (0 - 1)] + [\frac{1}{4} (1 - 0)] = \frac{1}{2}$ .
