42.41 If $f(x, y) = e^{xy}y^2 + \frac{x}{y}$ , find $f_{xx}, f_{xy}, f_{yx}$ , and $f_{yy}$ .

$$
f _ {x} = y e ^ {x y} y ^ {2} + \frac {1}{y} = e ^ {x y} y ^ {3} + \frac {1}{y} \quad f _ {y} = 2 e ^ {x y} y + x e ^ {x y} y ^ {2} - \frac {x}{y ^ {2}} = e ^ {x y} (2 y + x y ^ {2}) - \frac {x}{y ^ {2}}
$$

$$
f _ {x x} = e ^ {x y} y ^ {3} (y) = e ^ {x y} y ^ {4} \quad f _ {y y} = e ^ {x y} (2 + 2 x y) + x e ^ {x y} (2 y + x y ^ {2}) + \frac {2 x}{y ^ {3}} = e ^ {x y} (2 + 4 x y + x ^ {2} y ^ {2}) + \frac {2 x}{y ^ {3}}
$$

$$
f _ {x y} = 3 e ^ {x y} y ^ {2} + x e ^ {x y} y ^ {3} - \frac {1}{y ^ {2}} \quad f _ {y x} = e ^ {x y} \left(y ^ {2}\right) + y e ^ {x y} \left(2 y + x y ^ {2}\right) - \frac {1}{y ^ {2}} = 3 e ^ {x y} y ^ {2} + x e ^ {x y} y ^ {3} - \frac {1}{y ^ {2}}
$$
