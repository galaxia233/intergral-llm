32.11 Evaluate $\int_0^\infty \frac{dx}{x^2 + a^2}.$

$\int_0^v\frac{dx}{x^2 + a^2} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\tan^{-1}\frac{x}{a}\Bigg]_0^v = \frac{1}{\sqrt{a}}\tan^{-1}\frac{v}{a}.$ But, $\lim_{v\to +\infty}\tan^{-1}\frac{v}{a} = \frac{\pi}{2}.$ Hence, $\int_0^\infty \frac{dx}{x^2 + a^2} = \frac{\pi}{2\sqrt{a}}.$
