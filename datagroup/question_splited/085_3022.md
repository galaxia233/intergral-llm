【3022】 $\sum_{n = 1}^{\infty}\frac{\sin(2n - 1)x}{2n - 1}.$

解 记 $I(x) = \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{\sin nx}{n}$ , 利用 3018 题的结果，有

$$
I (x) = (\operatorname {s g n} x) \sum_ {n - 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\sin n | x |}{n} = (\operatorname {s g n} x) \frac {\pi - | x |}{2} \quad (| x | <   2 \pi).
$$

又记

$$
I _ {1} (x) = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\sin (2 k - 1) x}{2 k - 1}, \quad I _ {2} (x) = \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\sin 2 k x}{2 k},
$$

则有

$$
I _ {2} (x) = (\operatorname {s g n} x) \frac {1}{2} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {\infty} \frac {\sin k (2 | x |)}{k} = \frac {1}{2} (\operatorname {s g n} x) \frac {\pi - 2 | x |}{2} \quad (| x | <   \pi).
$$

由 $I(x) = I_{1}(x) + I_{2}(x)$ ，当 $|x| < \pi$ 时，有

$$
(\operatorname {s g n} x) \frac {\pi - | x |}{2} = I _ {1} (x) + (\operatorname {s g n} x) \frac {\pi - 2 | x |}{4}.
$$

于是，最后得 $I_{1}(x) = (\operatorname{sgn} x)\left(\frac{\pi - |x|}{2} - \frac{\pi - 2|x|}{4}\right) = \frac{\pi}{4} \operatorname{sgn} x$ （ $|x| < \pi$ ）
