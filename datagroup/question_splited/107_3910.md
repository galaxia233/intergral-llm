【3910】设 $f(x,y) = F_{xy}''(x,y)$ ，计算 $I = \int_{a}^{A}\mathrm{d}x\int_{b}^{B}f(x,y)\mathrm{d}y.$

解 不妨按先对 $y$ 后对 $x$ 积分的顺序计算，即得

$$
I = \int_ {a} ^ {A} \left[ F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, B) - F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, b) \right] d x = F (x, B) \left| _ {a} ^ {A} - F (x, b) \right| _ {a} ^ {A} = F (A, B) - F (a, B) - F (A, b) + F (a, b).
$$
