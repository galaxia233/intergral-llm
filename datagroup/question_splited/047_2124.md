【2124】 $\int \mathrm{sh}ax\sin bx\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \mathrm{sh}ax\sin bx\mathrm{d}x = \frac{1}{2}\int \mathrm{e}^{ax}\sin bx\mathrm{d}x - \frac{1}{2}\int \mathrm{e}^{-ax}\sin bx\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= \frac {1}{2} e ^ {a x} \frac {a \sin b x - b \cos b x ^ {*})}{a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} e ^ {- a x} \frac {a \sin b x + b \cos b x ^ {*})}{a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} + C = \frac {a c h a x \sin b x - b s h a x \cos b x}{a ^ {2} + b ^ {2}} + C.
$$

\*) 利用 1829 题的结果.
