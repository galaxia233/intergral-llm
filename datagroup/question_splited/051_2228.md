【2228】 $\lim_{n\to \infty}\sin{\frac{\pi}{n}}\sum_{k = 1}^{n}\frac{1}{2 + \cos{\frac{k\pi}{n}}}$

解 由于 $\sin \frac{\pi}{n} = \frac{\pi}{n} (1 + \alpha_{n})$ ，式中 $\lim_{n\to \infty}\alpha_n = 0$ 。于是，

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \sin \frac {\pi}{n} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {1}{2 + \cos \frac {k \pi}{n}} = \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} (1 + \alpha_ {n}) \frac {\pi}{n} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {1}{2 + \cos \frac {k \pi}{n}} = \left(\lim  _ {n \rightarrow 1} \frac {\pi}{n} \sum_ {k = 1} ^ {n} \frac {1}{2 + \cos \frac {k \pi}{n}}\right) \lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} (1 + \alpha_ {n})
$$

$$
= \pi \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {\mathrm {d} x}{2 + \cos \pi x} = \frac {2}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {\tan \frac {\pi x}{2}}{\sqrt {3}}\right) \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {1} = \frac {\pi}{\sqrt {3}}.
$$
