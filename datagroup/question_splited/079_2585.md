【2585】 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (\sqrt{2} - \sqrt[3]{2})(\sqrt{2} - \sqrt[5]{2}) \cdots (\sqrt{2} - \sqrt[2n+1]{2}).$

提示同2580题并利用63题的结果.

解由于

$$
\lim  _ {n \rightarrow \infty} \frac {a _ {n + 1}}{a _ {n}} = \lim  _ {n = 1} (\sqrt {2} - \sqrt [ 2 n + 3 ]{2}) = \sqrt {2} - 1 <   1,
$$

故级数 $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} (\sqrt{2} - \sqrt[3]{2})(\sqrt{2} - \sqrt[5]{2}) \cdots (\sqrt{2} - \sqrt[2n+1]{2})$ 收敛.
