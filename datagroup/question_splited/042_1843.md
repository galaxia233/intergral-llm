【1843】 $\int \frac{x^5\mathrm{d}x}{x^6 - x^3 - 2}$

提示 如果本题不化成标准形式来做，则可有更简单的做法，只需注意

$$
\frac {x ^ {5} \mathrm {d} x}{x ^ {6} - x ^ {3} - 2} = \frac {1}{3} \cdot \frac {x ^ {3} \mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {3}\right)}{\left(x ^ {3} - 2\right) \left(x ^ {3} + 1\right)} = \frac {1}{9} \left(\frac {2}{x ^ {3} - 2} + \frac {1}{x ^ {3} + 1}\right) \mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {3}\right),
$$

即易获解.

解 $\int \frac{x^5\mathrm{d}x}{x^6 - x^3 - 2} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{x^3\mathrm{d}(x^3)}{\left(x^3 - \frac{1}{2}\right)^2 - \frac{9}{4}} = \frac{1}{3}\int \frac{\left(x^3 - \frac{1}{2}\right) + \frac{1}{2}}{\left(x^3 - \frac{1}{2}\right)^2 - \frac{9}{4}}\mathrm{d}\left(x^3 -\frac{1}{2}\right)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{6} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2}}{\left(x ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2} - \frac {9}{4}} - \frac {1}{6} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2}\right)}{\left(\frac {3}{2}\right) ^ {2} - \left(x ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {1}{6} \ln | x ^ {6} - x ^ {3} - 2 | - \frac {1}{1 8} \ln \left| \frac {\frac {3}{2} + \left(x ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2}\right)}{\frac {3}{2} - \left(x ^ {3} - \frac {1}{2}\right)} \right| + C = \frac {1}{9} \ln \left\{\left| x ^ {3} + 1 \right| (x ^ {3} - 2) ^ {2} \right\} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
