【4015】 $z = x^{2} + y^{2},x^{2} + y^{2} = x,x^{2} + y^{2} = 2x,z = 0,$

解令 $x = r\cos \varphi$ ， $y = r\sin \varphi$ ，则方程 $x^{2} + y^{2} = x$ ， $x^{2} + y^{2} = 2x$ 及 $z = x^{2} + y^{2}$ 变为

$$
r = \cos \varphi , \quad r = 2 \cos \varphi \quad \text {及} \quad z = r ^ {2}.
$$

于是，所求的体积为

$$
V = \iint_ {\Omega} (x ^ {2} + y ^ {2}) d x d y = \int_ {- \frac {\pi}{2}} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {\cos \varphi} ^ {2 \cos \varphi} r ^ {3} d r = \frac {2}{4} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} (1 6 \cos^ {4} \varphi - \cos^ {4} \varphi) d \varphi = \frac {1 5}{2} \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \cos^ {4} \varphi d \varphi
$$

$$
= \frac {1 5}{2} \cdot \frac {3}{4} \cdot \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {\pi}{2} = \frac {4 5}{3 2} \pi .
$$
