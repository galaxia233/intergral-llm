【2054】 $\int \frac{2\sin x - \cos x}{3\sin^2x + 4\cos^2x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解 $\int \frac{2\sin x - \cos x}{3\sin^2x + 4\cos^2x}\mathrm{d}x = \int \frac{2\sin x}{3\sin^2x + 4\cos^2x}\mathrm{d}x - \int \frac{\cos x}{3\sin^2x + 4\cos^2x}\mathrm{d}x$

$$
= - 2 \int \frac {d (\cos x)}{3 + \cos^ {2} x} - \int \frac {d (\sin x)}{1 - \sin^ {2} x} = - \frac {2}{\sqrt {3}} \arctan \left(\frac {\cos x}{\sqrt {3}}\right) - \frac {1}{4} \ln \frac {2 + \sin x}{2 - \sin x} + C.
$$
