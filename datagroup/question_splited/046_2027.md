【2027】 $\int \frac{\sin^2x}{\sin x + 2\cos x}\mathrm{d}x.$

解设 $\tan \frac{x}{2} = t$ ，同2025题，得

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int \frac {\sin^ {2} x}{\sin x + 2 \cos x} d x = 4 \int \frac {t ^ {2} d t}{(1 + t ^ {2}) ^ {2} (1 + t - t ^ {2})} = \frac {4}{5} \int \left[ \frac {1}{1 + t ^ {2}} + \frac {- 2 + t}{(1 + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{1 + t - t ^ {2}} \right] d t \\ = \frac {4}{5} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{1 + t ^ {2}} - \frac {8}{5} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} t}{(1 + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} + \frac {2}{5} \int \frac {2 t \mathrm {d} t}{(1 + t ^ {2}) ^ {2}} + \frac {4}{5} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} (t - \frac {1}{2})}{\frac {5}{4} - (t - \frac {1}{2}) ^ {2}} \\ = \frac {4}{5} \arctan t - \frac {8}{5} \left[ \frac {t}{2 \left(1 + t ^ {2}\right)} + \frac {1}{2} \arctan t \right] ^ {*}) - \frac {2}{5} \cdot \frac {1}{1 + t ^ {2}} + \frac {4}{5 \sqrt {5}} \ln \left| \frac {\frac {\sqrt {5}}{2} + (t - \frac {1}{2})}{\frac {\sqrt {5}}{2} - (t - \frac {1}{2})} \right| + C _ {1} \\ = - \frac {2}{5} \cdot \frac {1 + 2 t}{1 + t ^ {2}} + \frac {4}{5 \sqrt {5}} \ln \left| \frac {\frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2} + t}{\frac {\sqrt {5} + 1}{2} - t} \right| + C _ {1} \\ = - \frac {1}{5} (\cos x + 2 \sin x) ^ {* *}) + \frac {4}{5 \sqrt {5}} \ln \left| \tan \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) \right| ^ {* * *} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$

\*) 利用1817题的结果.

$$
* *) \quad - \frac {2}{5} \cdot \frac {1 + 2 t}{1 + t ^ {2}} = - \frac {2}{5} \cdot \frac {1 + 2 \tan \frac {x}{2}}{\sec^ {2} \frac {x}{2}} = - \frac {2}{5} \cdot \frac {1 + 2 \cdot \frac {\sin x}{1 + \cos x}}{\frac {2}{1 + \cos x}} = - \frac {1}{5} (\cos x + 2 \sin x) - \frac {1}{5}.
$$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \left. \right.\left. * * *\right) \ln \left| \frac {\sqrt {5} - 1}{2} + t{\frac {\sqrt {5} + 1}{2} - t} \right| = \ln \left| \frac {\tan \left(\frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) + \tan \frac {x}{2}}{\cot \left(\frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) - \tan \frac {x}{2}} \right| \\ = \ln \left| \frac {\tan \left(\frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) + \tan \frac {x}{2}}{1 - \tan \left(\frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) \tan \frac {x}{2}} \right| + \ln \frac {1}{\cot \left(\frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right)} \\ = \ln \left| \tan \left(\frac {x}{2} + \frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) \right| - \ln \left[ \cot \left(\frac {\arctan 2}{2}\right) \right]. \\ \end{array}
$$
