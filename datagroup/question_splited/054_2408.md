【2408】 $x = a\ln \frac{a + \sqrt{a^2 - y^2}}{y} -\sqrt{a^2 - y^2}$ （曳物线）， $y = 0$

提示 所求面积为

$$
S = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left(a \ln \frac {a + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}}{y} - \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}\right) d y,
$$

并注意 $y = 0$ 为瑕点。

解 如图 4.24 所示，所求的面积为

![](images/0d601505598ec6e33636bc9e55fb4311ab0eb483e1b2d3961dc856d228553e99.jpg)  
图 4.24

$$
\begin{array}{l} S = 2 \int_ {0} ^ {a} \left(a \ln \frac {a + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}}{y} - \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}\right) d y \\ = 2 a \lim  _ {c \rightarrow + 0} \int_ {c} ^ {a} \ln \frac {a + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}}{y} d y - 2 \left(\frac {y}{2} \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}} + \frac {a ^ {2}}{2} \arcsin \frac {y}{a}\right) \Bigg | _ {0} ^ {a} \\ = 2 a \lim  _ {c \rightarrow + 0} \left(y \ln \frac {a + \sqrt {a ^ {2} - y ^ {2}}}{y} + a \arcsin \frac {y}{a}\right) \Bigg | _ {c} ^ {a} - \frac {\pi a ^ {2}}{2} = \pi a ^ {2} - \frac {\pi a ^ {2}}{2} = \frac {\pi a ^ {2}}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
