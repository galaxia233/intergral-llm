【2986】 $\frac{1}{1\cdot 3} +\frac{1}{3\cdot 5} +\frac{1}{5\cdot 7} +\dots .$

提示 注意 $\frac{1}{(2n - 1)(2n + 1)} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{2n - 1} -\frac{1}{2n + 1}\right).$

解 由 $\frac{1}{(2n - 1)(2n + 1)} = \frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{2n - 1} -\frac{1}{2n + 1}\right)$

$$
\begin{array}{l} \frac {1}{1 \cdot 3} + \frac {1}{3 \cdot 5} + \dots + \frac {1}{(2 n - 1) (2 n + 1)} + \dots \\ = \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{(2 n - 1) (2 n + 1)} = \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \sum_ {n = 1} ^ {N} \frac {1}{2} \left(\frac {1}{2 n - 1} - \frac {1}{2 n + 1}\right) = \lim  _ {N \rightarrow \infty} \left(\frac {1}{2} - \frac {1}{2} \cdot \frac {1}{2 N + 1}\right) = \frac {1}{2}. \\ \end{array}
$$
