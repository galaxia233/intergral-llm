$\left(\frac {x ^ {2}}{a ^ {2}} + \frac {y ^ {2}}{b ^ {2}}\right) ^ {2} + \frac {z ^ {4}}{c ^ {4}} = 1. \tag {4115}$

解题思路 作变量代换 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos^{\frac{1}{2}}\psi, y = b r \sin \varphi \cos^{\frac{1}{2}}\psi, z = c r \sin^{\frac{1}{2}}\psi,$

则有 $|I| = \frac{1}{2} abcr^2\sin^{-\frac{1}{2}}\psi$ 且 $V$ 的 $\frac{1}{8}$ 部分（第一卦限内）为 $0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2}, 0 \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac{\pi}{2}, 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1$ ，并利用 3856 题的结果及延拓公式.

解 令 $x = \arccos \varphi \cos^{\frac{1}{2}}\psi, y = brsin\varphi \cos^{\frac{1}{2}}\psi, z = cr\sin^{\frac{1}{2}}\psi$ ，则有 $|I| = \frac{1}{2} abcr^{2}\sin^{-\frac{1}{2}}\psi$ 且 $\frac{1}{8}$ 区域 $V$ （第一卦限内）为

$$
0 \leqslant \varphi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant \psi \leqslant \frac {\pi}{2}, \quad 0 \leqslant r \leqslant 1.
$$

于是，体积为

$$
\begin{array}{l} V = 8 \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \varphi \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} d \psi \int_ {0} ^ {1} \frac {1}{2} a b c r ^ {2} \sin^ {- \frac {1}{2}} \psi d r = \frac {2}{3} \pi a b c \int_ {0} ^ {\frac {\pi}{2}} \sin^ {- \frac {1}{2}} \psi d \psi = \frac {2}{3} \pi a b c \cdot \frac {1}{2} B \left(\frac {1}{2}, \frac {1}{4}\right) ^ {*}) \\ = \frac {2}{3} \pi a b c \frac {1}{2} \frac {\Gamma (\frac {1}{2}) \Gamma (\frac {1}{4})}{\Gamma (\frac {3}{4})} = \frac {1}{3} \pi a b c \frac {\sqrt {\pi} \Gamma^ {2} (\frac {1}{4})}{\Gamma (\frac {1}{4}) \Gamma (\frac {3}{4})} = \frac {1}{3} \pi a b c \frac {\sqrt {\pi} \Gamma^ {2} (\frac {1}{4})}{\sqrt {2} \pi} ^ {\dots} \\ = \frac {1}{3} a b c \sqrt {\frac {\pi}{2}} \Gamma^ {2} \left(\frac {1}{4}\right). \\ \end{array}
$$

$\star$ ）利用 3856 题的结果.  
$\star \star)$ 利用延拓公式： $\Gamma (x)\Gamma (1 - x) = \frac{\pi}{\sin\pi x}.$
