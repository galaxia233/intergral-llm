【3131】对于函数 $f(x) = \mathrm{e}^{kx}(a\leqslant x\leqslant b)$ 写出伯恩斯坦多项式 $B_{n}(x)$
解 $B_{n}(x) = \sum_{j = 0}^{n}\mathrm{e}^{k(a + (b - a)\frac{j}{n})}C_{n}^{j}\frac{(x - a)^{j}(b - x)^{n - j}}{(b - a)^{n}}$
$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {k a}}{(b - a) ^ {n}} \sum_ {j = 0} ^ {n} \mathrm {e} ^ {k \frac {(b - a) j}{n}} C _ {n} ^ {j} (x - a) ^ {j} (b - x) ^ {n} j = \frac {\mathrm {e} ^ {k a}}{(b - a) ^ {n}} \left[ \mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {b - a}{n} k} (x - a) + (b - x) \right] ^ {n} \\ = \mathrm {e} ^ {k a} \left[ \mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {b - a _ {k}}{n} \frac {x - a}{b - a} + \frac {b - x}{b - a}} \right] ^ {n} = \mathrm {e} ^ {k a} \left[ (\mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {b - a _ {k}}{n}} - 1) \frac {x - a}{b - a} + \frac {b - x + x - a}{b - a} \right] ^ {n} \\ = \mathrm {e} ^ {k u} \left[ 1 + \left(\mathrm {e} ^ {\frac {b - a}{n} k} - 1\right) \frac {x - a}{b - a} \right] ^ {n}. \\ \end{array}
$$
