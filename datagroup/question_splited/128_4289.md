【4289】 $\int_{(x_1,y_1,z_1)}^{(r_2,y_2,z_2)}f(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2})(xdx + ydy + zdz),$

式中 $f$ 为连续函数.

解题思路 令 $F(x,y,z) = \frac{1}{2}\int_{0}^{x^2 +y^2 +z^2}f(\sqrt{v})\mathrm{d}v.$ 注意

$$
F _ {r} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = x f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right), F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = y f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right), F _ {z} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = z f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right),
$$

及 $\mathrm{d}F(x,y,z) = f(\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2})(x\mathrm{d}x + y\mathrm{d}y + z\mathrm{d}z),$

再令 $\sqrt{v} = u$ ，即可得结果 $\int \frac{\sqrt{x_2^2 - y_2^2 - z_2^2}}{\sqrt{x_1^2 + y_1^2 + z_1^2}} u f(u) \, \mathrm{d}u.$

解 令 $F(x, y, z) = \frac{1}{2} \int_{0}^{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} f(\sqrt{v}) \, \mathrm{d}v$ . 由于 $f$ 是连续函数，故

$$
F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = x f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right), F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = y f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right), F _ {z} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) = z f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right),
$$

并且这些偏导数都是连续的。因此，$F(x, y, z)$ 可微，且

$$
\mathrm {d} F (x, y, z) = F _ {x} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) \mathrm {d} x + F _ {y} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) \mathrm {d} y + F _ {z} ^ {\prime} (x, y, z) \mathrm {d} z = f (\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}) (x \mathrm {d} x + y \mathrm {d} y + z \mathrm {d} z).
$$

于是，

$$
\begin{array}{l} \int_ {\left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right)} ^ {\left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right)} f \left(\sqrt {x ^ {2} + y ^ {2} + z ^ {2}}\right) \left(x d x + y d y + z d z\right) = F \left(x _ {2}, y _ {2}, z _ {2}\right) - F \left(x _ {1}, y _ {1}, z _ {1}\right) = \frac {1}{2} \int_ {x _ {1} ^ {2} + y _ {1} ^ {2} + z _ {1} ^ {2}} ^ {x _ {2} ^ {2} + y _ {2} ^ {2} + z _ {2} ^ {2}} f (\sqrt {v}) d v ^ {*}) \\ = \int_ {\sqrt {x _ {1} ^ {2} - y _ {1} ^ {2} - z _ {1} ^ {2}}} ^ {\sqrt {x _ {2} ^ {2} + y _ {2} ^ {2} + z _ {2} ^ {2}}} u f (u) d u, \\ \end{array}
$$

*) 这里已作代换 $\sqrt{v} = u$ （ $v = u^2$ ， $\mathrm{d}v = 2u\mathrm{d}u)$
