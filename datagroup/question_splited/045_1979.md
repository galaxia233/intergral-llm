【1979】 $\int \frac{(x^2 + 1)\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^4 + x^2 + 1}}.$

解 $\int \frac{(x^2 + 1)\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^4 + x^2 + 1}} = \int \frac{x\mathrm{d}x}{\sqrt{x^4 + x^2 + 1}} +\int \frac{\mathrm{d}x}{x\sqrt{x^4 + x^2 + 1}}$

$$
\begin{array}{l} = \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2}\right)}{\sqrt {\left(x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2} + \frac {3}{4}}} - \frac {1}{2} \int \frac {\mathrm {d} \left(\frac {1}{x ^ {2}}\right)}{\sqrt {\left(\frac {1}{x ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2}\right) ^ {2} + \frac {3}{4}}} = \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {x ^ {2} + \frac {1}{2} + \sqrt {x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + 1}}{\frac {1}{x ^ {2}} + \frac {1}{2} + \sqrt {\frac {x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + 1}{x ^ {4}}}} + C. \\ = \frac {1}{2} \ln \frac {x ^ {2} (1 + 2 x ^ {2} + 2 \sqrt {x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + 1})}{2 + x ^ {2} + 2 \sqrt {x ^ {4} + x ^ {2} + 1}} + C. \\ \end{array}
$$
