33.17 Given $O(0,0)$ , $A(3,1)$ , and $B(1,5)$ as vertices of the parallelogram $OAPB$ , find the coordinates of $P$ (see Fig. 33-4).

Let $\mathbf{A} = (3,1)$ and $\mathbf{B} = (1,5)$ . Then, by the parallelogram law, $\overrightarrow{OP} = \mathbf{A} + \mathbf{B} = (3,1) + (1,5) = (4,6)$ . Hence, $P$ has coordinates $(4,6)$ .
