44.38 Describe the planar region $\mathcal{R}$ whose area is given by the iterated integral $I = \int_{\pi}^{2\pi}\int_{1}^{1 - \sin \theta}rdrd\theta .$

$r = 1 - \sin \theta$ is a cardioid, and $r = 1$ is the unit circle. Between $\theta = \pi$ and $\theta = 2\pi$ , $1 < 1 - \sin \theta$ and the cardioid is outside the circle. Therefore, $\mathcal{R}$ is the region outside the unit circle and inside the cardioid (Fig. 44-22).
